ENHANCEMENT New! \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} estimates weekly milk totals. \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}now calculates estimated weekly milk totals based on your monthly \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.t-estday}} data. You can view the **Weekly Milk Production by Lactation** graph on the [Lactations](<../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Manage Your Animals/lactations.mdx>) tab of your animals.

<hint type="info">
  **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x49;f you have a parlor integration configured to capture [Daily Milk](<../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Manage Your Animals/daily-milk.mdx>) data, your graph will continue to show **actual** weekly milk totals.
</hint>

## Bug Fixes

- \{\{Partners.Zoetis}} samples with duplicate sample numbers import failure. We identified an issue where \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} would try to import \{\{Partners.Zoetis}} samples even if the samples had duplicate numbers. The import would fail. \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} now shows an error message if you attempt to import duplicate sample numbers.
- Errors while editing existing reports or \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}s. \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} now allows you to edit existing reports or \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}s without errors.
