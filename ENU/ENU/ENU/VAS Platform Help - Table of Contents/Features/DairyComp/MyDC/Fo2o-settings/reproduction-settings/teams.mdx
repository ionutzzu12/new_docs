The \{\{Buttons-UI.FeaturesTertiary}} page allows you to view and manage groups of \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} technicians by the types of tasks they typically perform. This allows you to control the types of events each team can enter in your [Event Definitions](<../../../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/DairyComp/MyDC/Settings/General Settings/event-definitions.mdx>). From this page you can see all of your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}'s teams, if they are active, and which technicians are assigned to each team. You can find this feature under the :Link[DairyComp/MyDC menu]{label="DairyComp/MyDC menu" overridedLabel="DairyComp/MyDC menu" spaceId docId="WFLX7SPMpZfy7zoMyrvRh" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Menu-DairyComp" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../../../NavPlatform/Menu.htm#Menu-DairyComp" href="../../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/navigate-pulse/menu-options.mdx"} > :Link[Settings]{label="Settings" overridedLabel="Settings" spaceId docId="WFLX7SPMpZfy7zoMyrvRh" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Menu-DairyComp" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../../../NavPlatform/Menu.htm#Menu-DairyComp" href="../../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/navigate-pulse/menu-options.mdx"} option on the \{\{Buttons-UI.FeaturesSecondary}} or \{\{Buttons-UI.FeaturesSecondary}} tabs. Your updates will apply to the \{\{Buttons-UI.FeaturesTertiary}} list regardless of which tab you use to access it.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Edit Teams

  1. Click on the applicable team in the list and then make your changes in the side panel on the right.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/Y_trqAP5iWjZ8JrOdRmnd_dc-teams-edit.png" alt caption size="73"}
  2. Click in any of the following fields to change their values:\* **Active Team**: Toggle the team between active and inactive.
     - **Team name**: Edit the name of the team.
     - **Technicians**: To add or remove a \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} technician to the team, check or uncheck the box next to the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} technician's name.To revert back to the original values for the technician, click **Reset**.
  3. When you are done editing the team, click **Save**.The system displays a message at the bottom of the page, confirming that your changes were saved.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Add Teams

  1. Click the **Add New Team** button at the top of the page to open a window to add a new team.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/W3tVr-MpMM0b6Xp9JY5UE_dc-teams-add.png" alt caption size="33"}
  2. Toggle the **Active Team** field to indicate whether the team is active or inactive.
  3. In the **Team name** field, enter the name of the team.The name must not exceed 45 characters.
  4. In the **Technicians** drop-down list, check the boxes next to the technicians that you want to add to the team.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/dWqAxlKBo7pxVBmiWJp8f_dc-teams-add-techs.png" alt caption size="22"}
  5. Click **Save**.The list now displays your new team.
</ExpandableHeading>
