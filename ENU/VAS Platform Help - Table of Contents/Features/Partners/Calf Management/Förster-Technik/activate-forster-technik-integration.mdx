<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Required Permissions

  Only users who are assigned a Role with Integrations Management permissions can connect partner integrations. See [Edit Dairy User Permissions](../../Platform/Features/Config/UserMgmtRoles/EditUserPermissions.htm) and [Manage Dairy Roles](../../Platform/Features/Config/UserMgmtRoles/ManageRoles.htm) for more information.
</ExpandableHeading>

Prior to being able to activate your \{\{Partners.Forster}} integration, the following are required: 

- \{\{Partners.Forster}} feeding and scale equipment installed at your facility.
- An existing Calf Cloud account and API key. Contact \{\{Partners.Forster}} to inquire about an account, or go to [https://www2.calf-cloud.com](https://www2.calf-cloud.com/) to verify your account status. The API key is accessible from your Calf Cloud General Profile page, in the **API release** section. \{\{Commands.Click}}**Start release** to show the key.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/L_khANTqBFnUXaNiJryl4_calf-cloud-api-access.png" alt caption size="37"}

Once your equipment and Calf Cloud account are confirmed, follow these steps to activate the integration and present data on the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}} Partners tab in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}:

1. In \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, navigate to **Partners&#x20;**>**&#xA0;Integrations Gallery&#x20;**>**&#xA0;**\{\{Partners.PartnerName}}.
2. \{\{Commands.Click}}**See Details** or **See Requirements** on the \{\{Partners.PartnerName}}integration card.
3. Follow the Connection Instructions on the right side of the page to establish the connection:1) Enter your API key:\* \{\{Commands.Click}} the edit icon

```javascript
 ![edit icon](../../Resources/Images/icons/pencil-blue-on-white.png "edit icon")

 and paste your API key into the field that opens.
```
