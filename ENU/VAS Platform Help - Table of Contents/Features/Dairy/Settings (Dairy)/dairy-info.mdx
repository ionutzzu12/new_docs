<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Required Permissions

  Only users who are assigned to a Role with \{\{RolesPermissions.PermissionNames}} permissions can use this feature. See [Edit Dairy User Permissions](../Config/UserMgmtRoles/EditUserPermissions.htm) and [Manage Dairy Roles](../Config/UserMgmtRoles/ManageRoles.htm) for more information.
</ExpandableHeading>

Use the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}}**&#x20;Info** page to edit general information for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}, such as the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} name, \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} type, and \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} description. All required fields are marked with the **\*** (asterisk) symbol. See the topics below to learn more:

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/Kndej1Kr3cksWlboAxxDR_dairy-settings-dairy-info-tab.png" size="39" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/getting-started/dairy-settings-dairy-info-tab.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/getting-started/dairy-settings-dairy-info-tab.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

1. \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}} Name: The name of the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}.
2. \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}} Type: The \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} type. Select Milk Producer, Calf Ranch, or Heifer Grower from the list.
3. Description: A description of the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}.
4. \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}} Image: Use the **Resizing Slider** to resize the image that is displayed on the [Home (Dairy Profile)](../Dairy/DairyHome/DairyProfile.htm) page to the preferred size in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}. Drag and drop an image, or click **Add Image** to select an image.
5. Timezone: The time \{\{zone}} where the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} is located.
6. Is Discoverable: Select whether you want your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} to be visible or not visible to other users. If you want to prevent users from requesting access, hide your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}.:::hint\{type="info"}
   **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x57;hen you hide your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}, the only way new users can join is for you to invite them. See [Invite a User to Your Dairy](../../GetStarted/InviteUserToDairy.htm) for instructions, or see [Who has access to my data?](<../../../FAQs/Data Access and Security/who-has-access-to-my-data.mdx>) for more details on hiding your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} from \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} searches.
   \:::
7. Click **Save** to apply your changes.
8. Click the **Address** tab to edit the address, phone number, email, or website for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}.. See [Dairy Address](DairyAddress.htm) for more information.
9. Click the \{\{Buttons-UI.PerformanceIndicators}} tab to set alert thresholds for common health events that occur across your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}, send notifications to your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} team when your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} exceeds those thresholds, and set the upper and lower limits used to track other \{\{KPIs}} across your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}. See [Performance Indicators](PerformanceIndicators.htm) for more information.
