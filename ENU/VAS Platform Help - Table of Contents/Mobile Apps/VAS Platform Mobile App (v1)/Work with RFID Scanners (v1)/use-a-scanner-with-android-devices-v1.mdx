This list displays all available scanners, even if they are not currently paired or connected.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/SuDUiGvp35HpXqhXw6E-O_rfid-connections.png" alt caption size="66"}

If the scanner you want to use is already paired with your device but shows a status of **Not Connected**, make sure that the scanner is powered on and that the Bluetooth connection is enabled in both your mobile device and the scanner. For any scanner that shows a status of **Connected**, you can use it to look up animals in the mobile app and process them in your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}'s [Tasks & Worklists (v1)](../Worklists/MobLists.htm) and [Events](../MobEventsIntro.htm).

<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Show me how to pair a new scanner in RFID Connections

  When using the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.radiofrequencyID-short}}**&#x20;Connections** menu to pair with available scanners, simply tap any scanner in the list that is **Not Connected**. If the scanner has not paired with your device yet, you may be prompted to enter a PIN to complete the pairing.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/Gg634G9Egl2APALj4NuOX_rfid-pairing.png" size="17" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/mobile-app/rfid-pairing.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/mobile-app/rfid-pairing.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  If your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} has multiple scanners and wands, you can pair each of them with your device and then select the one you want to use within the \{\{General.ProductName}} mobile app.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Look for the Scanner Shortcut!

  In the \{\{MorphHelp.AndroidOS-TM}} version of the mobile app, features that support scanners, such as [Tasks & Worklists (v1)](../Worklists/MobLists.htm) and [Events](../MobEventsIntro.htm), offer a shortcut for using scanners.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/amnX8QLhIJsW4NVY8dnyo_rfid-switch.png" size="50" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/mobile-app/rfid-switch.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/mobile-app/rfid-switch.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  Toggle the **Scanner** connection switch on or off to connect or disconnect from a paired scanner. When you toggle this switch on, the mobile app detects which scanner was last used under your account, and it connects you to it. Once the scanner finishes connecting, the mobile app displays a pop-up message to confirm that it is ready to use.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/DuL9q1GTlrfYZZf5Emyhv_scanner-switch-confirm.png" size="26" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/mobile-app/scanner-switch-confirm.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/mobile-app/scanner-switch-confirm.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  Note that in the \{\{MorphHelp.AppleOS-TM}} version of the mobile app, the **Scanner** indicator is not a switch that you can toggle. Instead, it is a status indicator that shows green when a scanner is connected and gray when it is not.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## I don't see the More button. How do I find it?

  :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/oKazFYT_2nVAqkeaJfHEH_back-button.png" alt caption size="19"}:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/1ZxRnXQSdCm_IDaw6O5wy_home-buttons.png" alt caption size="16"}

  Follow the steps below if you do not see the \{\{Mobile-UI.Feature-Names}} button at the bottom of your screen.

  1. Tap the **Back** arrow at the top left of the screen until you return to one of the main feature screens.
  2. From there, you'll find the feature buttons at the bottom of the screen.
</ExpandableHeading>
