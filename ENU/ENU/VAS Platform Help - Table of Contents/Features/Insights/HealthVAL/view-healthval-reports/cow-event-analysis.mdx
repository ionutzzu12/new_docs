  ### Required Permissions

  To use this feature, your \{\{General.ProductName}} user account must be assigned to a Role with \{\{RolesPermissions.PermissionNames}} permissions. See [Edit Dairy User Permissions](../../Config/UserMgmtRoles/EditUserPermissions.htm) and [Manage Dairy Roles](<../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Manage Dairy Users and Roles/manage-dairy-roles.mdx>) for more information.
</ExpandableHeading>

Cow \{\{Insights.EventAnalysis}} graphs allow you to focus on individual events for specific lactations or days in milk, and are an invaluable resource for tracking and investigating your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}'s unique health profile in greater detail. The graphs are located on the [Cows](cows.mdx) page, below the main trends graph.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/sLSXqrsNjLyXPYxDawIY7_health-summaries-event-analysis.png" size="59" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/reports/health-summaries-event-analysis.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/reports/health-summaries-event-analysis.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

Follow the guidelines below when working with \{\{Insights.EventAnalysis}} graphs.

5. Click **Occurrence** to view the reference data in occurrence percentages and include threshold values on the graph, or click **Count** to view the reference data in total counts.
6. Threshold: The red dashed line

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/w0rPGvJHFy-t4OWetQBte_threshold.png" size="10" caption="threshold line" alt="threshold line" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/insights/threshold.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/insights/threshold.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="true" indent="2"></Image>

7. Hover over a bar on the graph to view additional data.
8. If the graph includes data for the current month, the bar is shaded to indicate that data for the month is incomplete.
