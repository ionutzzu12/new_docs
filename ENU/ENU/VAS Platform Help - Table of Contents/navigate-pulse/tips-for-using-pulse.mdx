See the sections below for ways to make the most of \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}. For a description of \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} interface, see [Navigate PULSE](<../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/navigate-pulse.mdx>).

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## View an Animal

  You can use the \{\{Buttons-UI.Search}} field at the top left of the screen to search for an animal at your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}. See [View Animal Details on CowCards](../Features/CowCards/WorkingWithCowCards.htm) for more information.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/ubN3Lz_13OUqPUtMweG_r_search-for-cow.png" size="36" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Platform/search-for-cow.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/search-for-cow.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## View Platform Status and Shortcuts

  At the top right of the screen, you will find buttons that allow you to view [synchronization status](<../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/FAQs/Data Access and Security/when-was-my-data-last-synchronized.mdx>) between the \{\{General.ProductName}} and \{\{DC-UI.DC-ProductName}},\{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedWatchPlatform}}, and \{\{Partners.Parlor}}. You can also send feedback about \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} to \{\{masterVAS.CompanyName-short}} and find help for using \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Manage Your User Account

  Your user name is displayed in the top right corner of the screen, and it provides a drop‑down menu where you can edit your user account or log out of \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}. See [Edit Your User Account](../Features/Config/EditUserAcct.htm) for more information.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/6_uXlHhbreQ7oM89r-AQq_top-nav-bar.png" size="19" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Platform/navigating/top-nav-bar.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/navigating/top-nav-bar.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Customize Away!

  \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} interface is highly customizable, allowing you to edit the content on many screens. Always look for the **Options** button

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/P1VEAfaQ-pf8f-496DyI8_options24x24.png" size="10" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/icons/options_24x24.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/icons/options_24x24.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  and use the **Edit Card** option to organize the information that is displayed on each screen. You can also edit section titles to make them easier to find.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/S2G5UBx4VlUfwcPE2yPsZ_edit-card-option.png" size="10" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Platform/cowcards/edit-card-option.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/cowcards/edit-card-option.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  Customization is allowed only through the web version of \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, but many customizations can be saved for use in the mobile app as well. See [View Animal Details on CowCards](../Features/CowCards/WorkingWithCowCards.htm) and [Customize Dashboards](<../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Dairy/Dashboard/customize-dashboards.mdx>) for examples of how you can edit your view.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Scroll to See All of Your Information

  \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} is designed to fit screens of many sizes, including computer monitors, tablets, and mobile devices. Each screen displays a lot of information and groups it into logical sections. Be sure to scroll to the side as well as up and down to see all of your data.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Expand and Condense to See More or Less

  Take advantage of the "accordion" style display to view all high‑level information within one screen, while still being able to expand different areas to see more detail.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/hUji-fIRR8hJnpbEnc5n4_expand-collapse.png" size="71" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Platform/features-icons-examples/expand-collapse.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/features-icons-examples/expand-collapse.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Switch Views within Certain Screens

  Do you prefer to see all events in one list, or to see more information all on one screen? Would you rather see your data in a chart than a table? Look over to the far right in the title bar on certain screens to switch between dual table or single table view, or switch between table view and graph view.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/S5HpbZUQ2SSn6qvIZbsmu_dual-single-table-view.png" size="42" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Platform/navigating/dual-single-table-view.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/navigating/dual-single-table-view.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>
