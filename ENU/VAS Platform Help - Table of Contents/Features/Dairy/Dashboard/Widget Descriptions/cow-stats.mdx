  ### What permissions do I need to view this widget?

  To view this widget on a Dashboard or \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.O-verview-}} Page, your user account must be assigned to a Role with \{\{RolesPermissions.PermissionNames}} permissions. The \{\{Widgets.CowStats}} widget provides a snapshot view of the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} by current animal status: milking, \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.f-resh-cow}}, dry, or hospital, as well as close-up \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} information for cows and heifers. This widget is useful for quickly identifying animal counts and determining how many cows need to be transitioned to dry in preparation for calving.

Follow these guidelines when reviewing the data in this widget:

- Color-coded areas contain milking cow (blue), hospital \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} (red), dry cow (gray), and heifer (green) data.
- View the **Updated** timestamp to determine the date and time of the last data sync with \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-simple}}. This data is updated on a daily basis.
- The **All&#x20;****Milking Cows** total includes all To Tank and Hospital cows.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/uqRX8BvmYmthHToPXIaEj_wg-cowstats.png" size="64" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/dashboard/widget-gallery/wg-cowstats.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/dashboard/widget-gallery/wg-cowstats.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

**Milking Cow** data includes the following: 

- \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.F-resh-cow-}}: Recently calved
- \{\{Due}} Dry: Animals to be dried off prior to calving
- To Tank: Animals contributing milk to tank
- Hospital: Animals in the hospital \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}}

**Dry Cow** data includes the following: 

- Close-Up: Animals in the close-up \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} near calving
- Far Off: All other dry animals not in the close-up \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}}

**Heifer** data includes the following: 

- Close-Up Heifers: Heifers in the close-up \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} near calving
