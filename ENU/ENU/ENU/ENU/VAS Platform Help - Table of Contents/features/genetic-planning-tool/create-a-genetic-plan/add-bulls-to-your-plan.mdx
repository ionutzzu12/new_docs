\{\{UDS-general.UDSBrand}}\{\{UDS-general.UDSProduct}} supports the manual addition of bulls to your genetic plan. This allows you to hand-select bulls that are not currently included in the \{\{UDS-general.UDSBrand}} marketing list, or to make use of remaining inventory from another semen supplier.

<hint type="info">
  **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x57;hile you may add \{\{UDS-general.OutBull-lower}}s to your plan, the system will not allow you to activate a genetic plan comprised entirely of \{\{UDS-general.OutBull-lower}}s.
</hint>

See the sections below for details on adding, setting inventory limits, and pricing for Additional Bulls: 

- **Add Bulls:** During [Step 3: Choose Your Bulls](step-3-choose-your-bulls.mdx), you have the option to search for bulls by \{\{NAAB}} code, Name, or International Registration Number (IntRegNum), and then add them to your plan. See :Link[Include Additional Bulls]{label="Include Additional Bulls" overridedLabel="Include Additional Bulls" spaceId docId="6GDG6vfN8_CVeBmQ-ng_r" version="v2" docAnchorId="#AddBulls" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="GeneticPlanStep3.htm#AddBulls" href="step-3-choose-your-bulls.mdx"} for detailed instructions.
- **Set Pricing:&#xA0;**&#x54;o include price details for the doses of Additional Bulls in your plan, you can add it during [Step 4: Create Your Strategy](<../../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/GENEX Genetic Planning Tool/Create a Genetic Plan/step-4-create-your-strategy.mdx>). See :Link[Set Third-Party Bull Prices]{label="Set Third-Party Bull Prices" overridedLabel="Set Third-Party Bull Prices" spaceId docId="Q6qb0xcUw4LyE67OtGMPX" version="v2" docAnchorId="#AddBulls" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="GeneticPlanStep4.htm#AddBulls" href="../../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/GENEX Genetic Planning Tool/Create a Genetic Plan/step-4-create-your-strategy.mdx"} for detailed instructions.
- **Manually Add or Edit Bulls:** You can manually add bulls if you \{\{did}} not find them in \{\{UDS-general.UDSBrand}}\{\{UDS-general.UDSProduct}}, as well as edit existing bulls that you have manually added or view existing bulls that others have manually added.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Manually Add a Bull

  If you have the correct role and permissions, you can add bulls that the \{\{UDS-general.CommonProductName}} cannot recognize or find:

  <WorkflowBlock>
    <WorkflowBlockItem>
      In \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, click on your user name in the upper right corner, then select \{\{UDS-general.UDSBrand}}**&#x20;Management** from the drop-down list to open the \{\{UDS-general.UDSBrand}}**&#x20;Management** page.
    </WorkflowBlockItem>

    <WorkflowBlockItem>
      Open the **Bull List** tab, then click **Add Bull** in the upper right corner.
    </WorkflowBlockItem>

    <WorkflowBlockItem>
      Enter the bull's **Sire Information**, then select **Continue**. The following fields are required:\* **IntRegNum:** Animal's identification number. An IntRegNum must consist of 18 characters in this order: a 2-digit breed code, a 3-digit country code, a 1-digit gender code, followed by an identification number. For example, `HO840M000123456789` represents a Holstein (`HO`) from the United States (`840`) that is male (`M`) with an identification number of `000123456789`.

      - **NAAB Code(s):** Animal's identification code assigned by the National Association of Animal Breeders (NAAB). \{\{UDS-general.UDSBrand}}\{\{UDS-general.UDSProduct}} can support up to 6 NAAB codes for a single animal.
      - **Sire:** Animal's sire.
      - **Breed:** Animal's breed. If you don't select a dam, you must select a breed from the drop-down list.
    </WorkflowBlockItem>

    <WorkflowBlockItem>
      Enter the bull's **Genetic Values** information, then select **Continue**. The genetic values are automatically populated based on the animal's parent averages, but you can edit the traits to be more accurate. Use the USA scale to configure trait values in this step.
    </WorkflowBlockItem>

    <WorkflowBlockItem>
      <ExpandableHeading>
        ### Required Fields

        - Milk (lbs)
        - Protein (lbs)
        - Fat (lbs)
        - NM$: Net Merit Index
        - CM$: Cheese Merit Index
        - FM$: Fluid Merit Index
        - Productive Life
        - Daughter Pregnancy Rate
        - Somatic Cell Score
        - Sire Calving \{\{Ease}}
        - Daughter Calving Ease
        - Sire Still Birth
        - Daughter Still Birth
        - Cow Conception Rate
        - Heifer Conception Rate
        - Livability
        - DWP$: Dairy Wellness Profit
      </ExpandableHeading>

      Enter the bull's **Genes & Haplotype** information about its recessive genes and haplotypes, then select **Continue**. Although this information isn't required, adding any known information helps the \{\{UDS-general.CommonProductName}} eliminate poor matches that could promote recessive genes and haplotypes.
    </WorkflowBlockItem>

    <WorkflowBlockItem>
      Review the bull's summary, then click **Confirm** at the bottom right side of the page to add the bull to your Bulls List.
    </WorkflowBlockItem>
  </WorkflowBlock>
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Manually Edit a Bull

  Follow the steps below to edit bulls that you have manually added to the Bull List:

  1. In \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, click on your user name in the top-right corner, then select \{\{UDS-general.UDSBrand}}**&#x20;Management** from the drop-down list to open the \{\{UDS-general.UDSBrand}} \{\{UDS-general.UDSProduct}} page.
  2. Open the **Bull List** tab to view your added bulls. Note that the list only shows bulls added in the last 7 days by default. To view more bulls, clear or update the **Filters**.
  3. To edit a bull, click its **IntRegNum** to open the animal's information in a side panel.
  4. Select **Edit Bull** in the bottom right corner of the side panel.
  5. Make your edits, review the bull's summary, then click **Confirm** at the bottom right side of the page to save your changes.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## View Added Bull Information

  Follow the steps below to view details about a manually added bull:

  1. In \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, click on your user name in the top-right corner, then select \{\{UDS-general.UDSBrand}}**Management** from the drop-down list to open the \{\{UDS-general.UDSBrand}}\{\{UDS-general.UDSProduct}} page.
  2. Open the **Bull List** tab to view your added bulls. Note that the list only shows bulls added in the last 7 days by default. To view more bulls, clear or update the **Filters**.
  3. To view a bull's details, click its **IntRegNum** to open the animal's information in a side panel and click **See More Info** at the bottom right corner of the side panel.
</ExpandableHeading>
