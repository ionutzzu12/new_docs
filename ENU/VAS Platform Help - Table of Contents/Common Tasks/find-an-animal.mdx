Use the Search bar or the Animal List to find an animal in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}:

- Search Bar: You can use the Search bar at any time by clicking the \{\{Buttons-UI.Search}} field at the top of the page or pressing **F2** on your keyboard. On the \{\{Buttons-UI.DairyProfile}} page, you can also click the \{\{Buttons-UI.Search}} button to open the Search bar.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  Animal List: To access the Animal List, select \{\{Buttons-UI.FeaturesMain}}**&#x20;> \*\*\*\***\{\{Buttons-UI.FeaturesSecondary}}> \{\{Buttons-UI.FeaturesTertiary}} from the [menu bar](<../../ENU/VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/navigate-pulse/menu-options.mdx>). Click**Filters** to search for specific animals or to limit the animals shown by \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}}, lactation, or reproductive code (\{\{RC}}). You can then sort the Animals table by clicking any of the column headers.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/6FeqrD7Ip9Bz83JO9v7wh_animal-list-new.png" alt caption size="66"}In the side panel, click **Go To&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}} to view the animal's [CowCard](<../Features/Manage Your Animals/cowcards-at-a-glance.mdx>).
