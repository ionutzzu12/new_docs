<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Required Permissions

  Only users who are assigned to a Role with \{\{RolesPermissions.PermissionNames}} permissions can use this feature. See [Edit Dairy User Permissions](../Config/UserMgmtRoles/EditUserPermissions.htm) and [Manage Dairy Roles](<../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Manage Dairy Users and Roles/manage-dairy-roles.mdx>) for more information.
</ExpandableHeading>

\{\{Buttons-UI.PerformanceIndicators}} in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} allow you to set alert thresholds for common health events that occur across your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}, send notifications to your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} team when your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} exceeds those thresholds, and set the upper and lower limits used to track other \{\{KPIs}} across your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}.

<hint type="info">
  **NOTE:&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} does not currently support alerts and notifications for all available indicator types.
</hint>

By tracking and examining health events for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}, you can implement new strategies and protocols for avoiding or treating specific illnesses. Over time, you can lower treatment and production loss costs, and have fewer milk and beef withholds.

The \{\{Buttons-UI.PerformanceIndicators}} page contains two settings, and both must be configured before \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} will track performance indicators and send notifications:

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Indicators

  Use the [Indicators](<../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Dairy/Settings (Dairy)/Performance Indicators/working-with-indicators.mdx>) page to view or adjust health and reproduction indicators for the animals in your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}.

  \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} supports various indicator types. Select an option to view or edit an indicator type.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Alert Groups

  Use the [Alert Groups](AlertGroupsTab.htm) page to select the events you want \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} to track and the team members you want to notify when your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} exceeds the alert thresholds.
