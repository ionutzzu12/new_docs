Tap \{\{Mobile-UI.MoreTab}} at the bottom right of the app to access additional options in the mobile app.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## My Account

  Tap \{\{Mobile-UI.AppMyAccount}} to view your account information, update your user settings, or log out of the mobile app.

  Tap \{\{Mobile-UI.AppUserSettings}}, and then tap \{\{Mobile-UI.AppPinFreq}} to update how often the mobile app requests your \{\{General.PersonalIdentificationNumber}} when logging in to the app. You can choose to always request your \{\{General.PersonalIdentificationNumber}} during login, after 8 hours have elapsed, or only once a day.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Refresh Herd Data in Online Mode

  When the mobile app has an active WiFi or data connection, you can refresh your data. If you go outside of data range, the app will continue to work in offline \{\{mode}}. Once you are back in data range, you can refresh your data and view the latest updates.

  <hint type="info">
    **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x57;hile the mobile app allows you to continue working in offline mode, some features in the mobile app require you to be online to see data.
  </hint>

  See the topics below to learn more:

  - [Does the mobile app use my cell phone data?](<../../FAQs/Data Access and Security/does-the-mobile-app-use-my-cell-phone-data.mdx>)
  - [What if I have poor or limited internet access in the barn? Can I still use PULSE?](<../../FAQs/Data Access and Security/what-if-i-have-poor-or-limited-internet-access-in-the-barn-can-i-still-use-pulse.mdx>)
</ExpandableHeading>
