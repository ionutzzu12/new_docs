  ### Required Permissions

  Only users who are assigned to a Role with \{\{RolesPermissions.PermissionNames}} permissions can use this feature. See [Edit Dairy User Permissions](../Config/UserMgmtRoles/EditUserPermissions.htm) and [Manage Dairy Roles](<../../../../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Manage Dairy Users and Roles/manage-dairy-roles.mdx>) for more information.

The [Alert Groups](<../../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Dairy/settings-dairy/performance-indicators/working-with-alert-groups.mdx>) page lists all alert groups added to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}.

See the topics below to learn more:

- Click the \{\{Buttons-UI.Indicators}} tab to view or adjust health and reproduction indicators for the animals in your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}. See [Working with Indicators](<../../../../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Dairy/Settings (Dairy)/Performance Indicators/working-with-indicators.mdx>) for more information.
- Click the **Add Alert Group** button to open a side panel and add a new alert group for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}. See [Add Alert Groups](add-alert-groups.mdx) for instructions.
- Alert Group Name: The name of the alert group.
- \{\{Buttons-UI.Indicators}}: The \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-EventIndicator}}s being tracked across your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} by the alert group.
- Users: The users being notified by the alert group.
- Time Set: The time of day when \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} examines \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-ThresholdDifferential}}s for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} and notifies your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} team.
- Click an alert group in the table to open a side panel and edit or delete an existing alert group for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}. See [Edit Alert Groups](AlertGroupsEdit.htm) and [Delete Alert Groups](delete-alert-groups.mdx) for instructions.
