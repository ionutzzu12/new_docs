The **Heifer Count by&#x20;**\{\{Age}}**&#x20;Groupings** widget displays a breakdown of the number of heifers by age groupings of years/months. The first grouping displays the number of heifers up to 2 months of age. After that, the age groupings display the number of heifers in 3-month intervals (e.g, 3 to 5 months, 9 to 11 months, etc.).

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/9wD0MiVRZTGjcapItmW1s_age-count-heifers.png" size="56" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/dashboard/age-count-heifers.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/dashboard/age-count-heifers.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

Follow these guidelines when reviewing the data in this widget:

- Age groupings appear on the left side of the chart. Each grouping can be expanded to view details for each month of the grouping.
- Age groupings display in the format YR-MO (Year-Month).
- Percentages for each age grouping (and their sub-groupings) appear down the center of the chart. For example, the percentage PCT12 in the **By Age 0-06…0-08** grouping means that 12% of the total number of heifers are within that grouping.
- The total number of heifers appears at the top-right of the chart. Below that are total heifer counts for each age grouping.
