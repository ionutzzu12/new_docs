Prior to being able to activate your \{\{Partners.AgSource}} integration, you must first have an existing MyAgsource account that includes \{\{Partners.Keto}} management tool. [Contact AgSource](https://dairy.agsource.com/contact-us/) to inquire about an account, or go to [https://myagsource.com/](https://myagsource.com/) to verify your account status.

After you confirm your MyAgSource account, complete the following steps to activate the integration and add the widget to your \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} dashboard:

1. Contact [VAS Support](http://platform-docs.vas.com/default.htm#cshid=1004) to activate the \{\{Partners.AgSource}} integration. Be sure to have your \{\{General.ProductName}}\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}} Name prior to contacting \{\{General.CompanyName-short}} Support.
2. View the dashboard to which you'd like to add \{\{Partners.KetoMonitor}} widget. Select the \{\{MorphHelp.CustomizePage}} button and select the \{\{Partners.KetoMonitor}} widget.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/VAEj7qELqoTTSWQCgAe8P_keto-page-customization.png" alt caption size="40"}See [Customize Dashboards](../../Platform/Features/Dashboards/DairyDashboard/CustomizeDashboard.htm) for detailed instructions.
