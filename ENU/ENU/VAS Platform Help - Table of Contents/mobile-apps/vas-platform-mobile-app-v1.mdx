### Download the mobile app and see how herd, feed, and parlor management can be faster and easier than ever before!

Access your data when you need it most – anyplace, anytime – from your mobile device. The mobile app gives you access to the valuable data you need to make important decisions from your phone or tablet.

The mobile app provides a subset of the features that are available in the [web version of PULSE](https://platform.vas.com/login) so that you have the tools you need most while you're out on the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}. Mobile app data remains synchronized as long as your device has an active data connection. This helps to ensure that your data is as recent as possible while you are out with the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}.
