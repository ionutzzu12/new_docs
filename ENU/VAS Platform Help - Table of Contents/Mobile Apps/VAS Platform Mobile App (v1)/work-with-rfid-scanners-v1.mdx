    ### Learn more about the Standards for Low Frequency RFID Scanners

    The International Organization for Standardization (\{\{ISO}}) provides standards for the identification of animals using radio frequencies. See [ISO 11784 and ISO 11785](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ISO_11784_and_ISO_11785) to view these standards.

You can scan animals with the mobile app after you pair and connect your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.radiofrequencyID-short}} scanners to your device. The mobile app supports Bluetooth connections with \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.radiofrequencyID-short}} wands and scanners. However, the \{\{MorphHelp.AppleOS-TM}} and \{\{MorphHelp.AndroidOS-TM}} versions of the mobile app each work a bit differently with Bluetooth wands/scanners.
