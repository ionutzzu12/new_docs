  ### Required Permissions

  Only users who are assigned to a Role with \{\{RolesPermissions.PermissionNames}} permissions can use this feature. See [Edit Dairy User Permissions](EditUserPermissions.htm) and [Manage Dairy Roles](<../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Manage Dairy Users and Roles/manage-dairy-roles.mdx>) for more information.

\{\{MorphHelp.User\_Management\_UI\_item}} features in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} allow you to manage user accounts and roles by company and \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}. \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}}**User** permissions are controlled entirely through **Role** assignments. User connections at the company level differ from \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}-level connections.
