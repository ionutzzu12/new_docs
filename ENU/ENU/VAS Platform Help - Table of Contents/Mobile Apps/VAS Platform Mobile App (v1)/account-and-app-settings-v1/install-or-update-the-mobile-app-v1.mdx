  The mobile app offers many of the same features as the web version and can be used on \{\{MorphHelp.AndroidOS-TM}} and Apple \{\{MorphHelp.AppleOS-TM}} devices. Review the [Minimum Requirements](https://vas.com/technology-specifications/) on the \{\{masterVAS.CompanyName-short}} website before installing the mobile app on \{\{MorphHelp.AppleOS-TM}} and \{\{MorphHelp.AndroidOS-TM}} devices.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Update the Mobile App

  Updates are typically controlled by the options you choose in your mobile device settings, which are independent of \{\{General.ProductName}} settings. The most recent release of the mobile app is always available in the \{\{Mobile-UI.AppStore}}. If your device does not have the current version installed, please visit Google Play or the \{\{Mobile-UI.AppStore}} and update it from there.

  [<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/N3itM7lhAnOOCdmTAzo2g_applestore.png" size="10" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Platform/mobile-app/applestore.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/mobile-app/applestore.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>](https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/vas-platform/id1279026784?mt=8)[<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/qOXaHTptZyt8ToGaETyn2_google-play-badge.png" size="10" alt="Download from Google Play" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Platform/mobile-app/google-play-badge.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/mobile-app/google-play-badge.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.vas.mobile)
