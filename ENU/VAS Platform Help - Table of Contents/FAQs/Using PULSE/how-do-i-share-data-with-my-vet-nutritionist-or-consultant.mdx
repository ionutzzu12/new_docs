Any user inside or outside of your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} can use \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}. You can either [invite them to view your dairy](<../../Features/Manage Dairy Users and Roles/invite-a-user-to-your-dairy.mdx>), or they can [request access to your dairy](<../../Get Started/request-access-to-a-dairy.mdx>) on their own. When sharing your data with your vet, nutritionist, or any other outside \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.c-onsultant}}, you can use Role assignments to set their Platform permissions.

Within the default roles that are available, you will find one for **Vet&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.C-onsultant-}} that provides the typical permissions usually required by your vet (see example below). If you want to create additional roles or edit permissions for an existing one, see [Manage Dairy Users and Roles](<../../../ENU/VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/features/manage-dairy-users-and-roles.mdx>).

\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}}**User** permissions are controlled entirely through **Role** assignments. To edit \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} user permissions, open the :Link[Dairy menu]{label="Dairy menu" overridedLabel="Dairy menu" spaceId docId="WFLX7SPMpZfy7zoMyrvRh" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Menu-Dairy" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../NavPlatform/Menu.htm#Menu-Dairy" href="../../../ENU/VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/navigate-pulse/menu-options.mdx"}, navigate to \{\{Buttons-UI.UserMgmtMenu}}, and select the **Users** or **Roles** option.
