<ExpandableHeading>
  ## View Account Information, Update User Settings, or Sign Out

  Tap \{\{Mobile-UI.MoreTab}} at the bottom right of the screen, and then tap \{\{Mobile-UI.AppMyAccount}} to view your account information, update your user settings, or log out of the mobile app.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Install or Update the Mobile App

  Follow the guidelines below to \{\{Mobile-UI.AppUpdateInstall}}:

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Install the Mobile App

    The mobile app offers many of the same features as the web version and can be used on \{\{MorphHelp.AndroidOS-TM}} and Apple \{\{MorphHelp.AppleOS-TM}} devices. Review the [Minimum Requirements](https://vas.com/technology-specifications/) on the \{\{masterVAS.CompanyName-short}} website before installing the mobile app on \{\{MorphHelp.AppleOS-TM}} and \{\{MorphHelp.AndroidOS-TM}} devices.

    Follow the steps below to \{\{Mobile-UI.AppInstall}}:

    1. Visit Google Play or the \{\{Mobile-UI.AppStore}} on your device to download and install the app.
    2. Launch the app and log in with your \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} username and password.If you have not created your \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} user account, tap **New to&#x20;**\{\{General.ProductName}}**? Sign Up** (see [Sign Up](../GetStarted/SignUpForVASAcct.htm) for help). Complete the sign-up process and log in to the mobile app.
    3. Set a 4-digit PIN number that you will enter each time you open the mobile app.After you log in, the **Select a&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}} screen is displayed.
    4. Select a \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} and start using the mobile app to manage your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}!
  </ExpandableHeading>

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Update the Mobile App

    Updates are typically controlled by the options you choose in your mobile device settings, which are independent of \{\{General.ProductName}} settings. The most recent release of the mobile app is always available in the \{\{Mobile-UI.AppStore}}. If your device does not have the current version installed, please visit Google Play or the \{\{Mobile-UI.AppStore}} and update it from there.

    <ExpandableHeading>
