If this is the **first time** you're using \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, you'll be required to [sign up for an account](sign-up.mdx).

You can then log in to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} at [https://platform.vas.com/login](https://platform.vas.com/login).

Check the **Remember Me** option to save the last user email entered. This feature does not save your password.

<hint type="info">
  **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x54;he "auto sign‑in" settings in your browser will override the system's **Remember Me** option. If the Remember Me checkbox appears to have no effect on retaining or dismissing your email address, check to see whether your browser auto sign‑in is enabled or disabled.
</hint>
