Follow these steps to activate the \{\{Partners.parlor}} integration and view data in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}:

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Step 1: Connect the Parlor Integration in PULSE

  Follow these steps to configure and integrate your parlor with \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}:

  1. In \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, navigate to **Partners&#x20;**>**&#xA0;Integrations Gallery&#x20;**>**&#xA0;**\{\{Partners.PartnerName}}.
  2. \{\{Commands.Click}}**See Details** or **See Requirements** on the \{\{Partners.PartnerName}} card.
  3. Follow the Connection Instructions on the right side of the page to establish the connection:1) Click the **Connection Status** toggle and verify the status switches to **Active**.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/yVoLb7NAnbX1zs-j2jcYx_integration-connected.png" alt="Connection Status: Active" caption size="22"}
     2\) \{\{Commands.Click}}**Save**.

  <hint type="warning">
    **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**&#x41;s noted in the Connection Instructions, make sure you have TeamViewer installed on your PC. If necessary, you can download and install it [here](https://www.teamviewer.com/en-us/download/windows/).
  </hint>
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Step 2: Configure Milking Shifts

  Once the data connection is active, you are directed to the shift scheduling page. Follow the guidelines below when adding your \{\{Partners.parlor}} details:  

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/wuhF58Qt8tepLfULOVOsI_parlor-shift-schedule-drawer.png" size="56" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Platform/parlorcomp/parlor-shift-schedule-drawer.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/parlorcomp/parlor-shift-schedule-drawer.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  - Click **Add New Shift** at the top of the page to add each of your milking shifts and associated details.::::ExpandableHeading

  ### For help filling out the shift details, click here!

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    **Shift Type:** Select either a **Milking** or **Cleaning** shift.
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    **Start and End Hours/Minutes:** Enter the times at which you want the new shift to start and end. The system warns you if you attempt to create a shift at a time that already has a shift scheduled.:::hint\{type="info"}
    **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x54;he format of the start and end times can be changed from standard time to 24-hour time in your \{\{General.ProductName-short}} account settings. See [Edit Your User Account](<../../User Menu/edit-your-user-account.mdx>) to learn more.
    \:::
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    **Shift Management:** This section displays the number of \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}} currently assigned to the shift as well as the total number of animals in those \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}}. You can click the **Clear All&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.P-ens-}} link to remove all of the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}} assigned to the shift.
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    **Add&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.P-ens-}}**:** Enter the numbers of the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}} you want assigned to the shift, separated by commas, or use a dash to indicate a range of \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}}. Click the "x" beside a \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} number to delete it from the shift. You can also hold the shift key and check multiple pens from the drop-down.:::hint\{type="info"}
    **NOTE:&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.P-ens-}} do not need to be added in milking order.
    \:::
    \::::
  </Paragraph>

  - When complete, check the **Day Schedule** at the top of the page for a quick overview of your daily scheduled shifts: :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/13Azwd-otd0OsurK9_yMz_parlor-shift-schedule.png" alt caption size="56"}
