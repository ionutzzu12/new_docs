To access the \{\{Partners.smaXtec}} sensor data for a specific animal, first access the CowCard for that animal. Then, navigate to the \{\{Partners.PartnersUIoption}} tab. For more details on accessing a CowCard, see [CowCards at a Glance](<../../../Manage Your Animals/cowcards-at-a-glance.mdx>).

## Alerts

When new alerts are available, a red badge displays on the Partners tab for the animal's CowCard.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/Mm3bxdJP0twwAROmV5KB-_smaxtec-alerts.png" size="71" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Partners/smaxtec-alerts.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Partners/smaxtec-alerts.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

All alerts received in the last 24 hours display at the top of the page. After a 24-hour period, alerts automatically clear.

## Graph

Depending on the bolus type (standard or premium), the graph for each animal displays either two or three types of data.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/HGq2fZbQBK9Kbsqk1gT-u_smaxtec-graph-main.png" size="89" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Partners/smaxtec-graph-main.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Partners/smaxtec-graph-main.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/XqKZuFfMkn9YbnOAmaX39_smaxtec-graph-prem-main.png" size="89" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Partners/smaxtec-graph-prem-main.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Partners/smaxtec-graph-prem-main.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

1. **View:&#x20;**&#x53;elect to view animal data for the Last 24 Hours, 7 Days, or 30 Days.
2. **Event icon:&#x20;**&#x49;dentifies animal events. Hover over the icon to view more details on the diagnosis, or health or reproduction-related event. See [smaXtec/VAS Data Details](smaxtecvas-data-details.mdx) for a complete list of \{\{Partners.smaXtec}} and \{\{masterVAS.CompanyName-short}} alerts and other other shared data.
3. **pH:&#x20;**(premium bolus only) Rumen acidity level. Normal rumen pH range is between 5.7 and 7.3.:::hint\{type="info"}
   **NOTE:&#x20;**\{\{Partners.smaXtec}} sends alerts for animals with a pH level below 5.8, which is an indicator of \{\{acidosis}}, and for animals with a large \{\{variance}} in pH levels, which may be a sign of poor feed composition.
   \:::
4. **Temperature:&#x20;**&#x41;nimal temperature.
5. **Activity:&#x20;**&#x54;he activity level for an animal. The higher the value, the greater the amount of activity.
6. **Tooltip:&#x20;**&#x48;over over the graph data to view the bolus reading for a specific date and time.

## Messages

The Messages area below the graph provides more information on recent alerts, and includes the following information.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/5c3weQq15WW4eWpJywN6S_smaxtec-messgs-main.png" size="64" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Partners/smaxtec-messgs-main.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Partners/smaxtec-messgs-main.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

1. **Message:&#x20;**&#x49;dentifies the diagnosis event, or health or reproduction-related alert. See [smaXtec/VAS Data Details](smaxtecvas-data-details.mdx) for a complete list of \{\{Partners.smaXtec}} and \{\{masterVAS.CompanyName-short}} alerts and other other shared data.
2. **Date and Time:&#x20;**&#x54;he date and time of the alert or event.
3. **Observations:&#x20;**&#x41; description of the event or alert, as well as possible indications.
