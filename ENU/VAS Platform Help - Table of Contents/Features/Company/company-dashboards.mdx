<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Where do I find my Company View?

  **Company features are only available when viewing a company in&#x20;**\{\{General.ProductName-short}}**, not when viewing a&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}**.** To access company features, a member of the company must invite you to connect with it. See [Invite a User to Your Dairy](../../../GetStarted/InviteUserToDairy.htm) for more information.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/-1RWzJXwq_CihZI38KrJF_nav-co-dashboard.png" size="45" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/dashboard/company/nav-co-dashboard.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/dashboard/company/nav-co-dashboard.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>
</ExpandableHeading>

\{\{Buttons-UI.D-ashboardsCompany-}} provide a snapshot view of essential information for all \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}} within your company. As illustrated below, key animal data, such as Cow Stats, are available for all company \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}}, or can be viewed in a graphical format by individual \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/2f1T7W_3K2BAfz1ZgVIDQ_view-co-dashboard.png" size="58" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/dashboard/company/view-co-dashboard.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/dashboard/company/view-co-dashboard.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

You can add both company-level and \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}-level widgets to optimize your company dashboard. Expand the sections below to learn more:

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Company-Level Widgets

  The company-level \{\{Widgets.CowStats}} widgets provide a snapshot view of the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}s by current animal status: milking, \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.f-resh-cow}}, dry, or hospital, as well as close-up \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} information for cows and heifers.

  The \{\{Widgets.ProjectedCalves}} widget provides important details regarding calf parentage and the expected number of heifer calves to be added to the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}. Note that these widgets are only available on \{\{Buttons-UI.D-ashboardsCompany-}}; they cannot be added to \{\{Buttons-UI.Dashboard}}s.

  See the topics below to learn more:

  - [Cow Stats for All Dairies](CowStats-AllDairies.htm)
  - [Cow Stats by Dairy](<Company Dashboards/cow-stats-by-dairy.mdx>)
  - [Projected Calves by Sire & Semen Type for All Dairies](ProjectedCalves-AllDairies.htm)
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Dairy-Level Widgets

  You can still add \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}-level widgets to your \{\{Buttons-UI.D-ashboardsCompany-}}. \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}}-level widgets are collapsed below their respective \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} on the Page Customization screen to help you choose widgets for the correct \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}.
