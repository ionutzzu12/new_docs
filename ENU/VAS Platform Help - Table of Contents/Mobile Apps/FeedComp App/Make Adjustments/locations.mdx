<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Reactivate Location

  1. \{\{Commands.Click}} the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} to \{\{Commands.UserActions}}.
  2. Tap `Reactivate` at the bottom of the screen.

  <hint type="info">
    **TIP:&#x20;**&#x44;on't see the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}? Be sure to tap the \{\{Mobile-UI.InactiveMobile}} filter at the top of the page.
  </hint>
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Transfer Ingredients to Other Locations

  1. \{\{Commands.Click}} the location from which the ingredient needs to be moved.
  2. \{\{Commands.UserActions}}`Edit Location` at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Tap `Move Ingredients` at the bottom of the screen.
  4. Select the ingredient(s) to transfer (or use the **Select All** option), and then tap `Continue` at the bottom of the screen.
  5. Select the new location, and then tap `Verify Ingredients` at the bottom of the screen.
  6. Update the \{\{DM%}} and the **Cost Unit**, and then tap `Move Ingredients` at the bottom of the screen.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Search and Filter the Locations List

  Options to search and filter the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} list are available at the top of the \{\{General.UI-gui}}.
