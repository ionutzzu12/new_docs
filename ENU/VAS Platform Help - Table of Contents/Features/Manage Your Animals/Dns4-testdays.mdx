The \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.T-estdays-}} tab on an animal's CowCard provides information about your animal's \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.t-estday}} results by lactation. On this page you can easily switch between the **Table View** or the **Graph View**.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/1d4h0rcCZLR596HUxt0TR_testdays-graph.png" size="60" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/cowcards/testdays-graph.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/cowcards/testdays-graph.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.T-estday-}}data is collected by your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}'s \{\{DHI}} (\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}}\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.H-erd-}} Improvement) processing center. The date of this collection is commonly known as the "\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.t-est-day}}." \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.T-estday-}}data includes such things as milk weight, fat percentage, and somatic cell count (\{\{SCC}}), and is displayed on the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.T-estdays-}} tab of each \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}}.

Click on any \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.t-estday}} to open the [Testdays](../DairyComp/MyDC/testdays.mdx) page, where you can view straightforward, easy-to-follow graphs and summaries about your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}'s \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.t-estday}} results.
