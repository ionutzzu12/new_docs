Follow these steps to activate the \{\{Partners.parlor}} integration and view data in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}:

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Step 1: Connect the DairyComp and ParlorBoss Integration

  \{\{masterVAS.CompanyName-short}} will connect the integration between \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-simple}} and \{\{masterVAS-Products.ParlorBoss}} at your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} as part of the installation process.

  <hint type="warning">
    **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Products.ParlorBoss}} version 2.8.9 or higher must be installed and integrated with \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-simple}} at your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} for reporting to be available.
  </hint>
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Troubleshoot Connection Issues

  If you notice missing data (indicated by a hyphen "-") in your \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}\{\{masterVAS-Products.ParlorBoss}} reports, try the following troubleshooting steps: 

  1. Verify that \{\{masterVAS-Products.ParlorBoss}} is running and wasn't closed accidentally.
  2. Confirm the connection between \{\{masterVAS-Products.ParlorBoss}} and \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-simple}} is active by checking your \{\{masterVAS-Products.ParlorBoss}} logs and making sure that data is refreshing and sending to \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-simple}} as expected.
  3. Check the \{\{masterVAS-Products.ParlorBoss}} Autoupdate option to make sure it is turned on and set to a time outside of your standard milking shifts to avoid data interruptions. See [Configure Auto Update in ParlorBoss](https://dc-help.vas.com/default.htm#cshid=1106) for instructions.
  4. Restart the \{\{masterVAS-Products.ParlorBoss}} server.
  5. Restart the \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-simple}} server.

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Learn more about how ParlorBoss sends data to PULSE!

    \{\{masterVAS-Products.ParlorBoss}} pulls required animal tasks from \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-simple}} every 15 minutes. \{\{masterVAS-Products.ParlorBoss}} also pulls \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} milk shift configuration details to determine when shifts are scheduled to start and end. At the end of each milking shift, \{\{masterVAS-Products.ParlorBoss}} sends raw \{\{Partners.parlor}} data to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} for reporting purposes. \{\{masterVAS-Products.ParlorBoss}} checks for shift configuration changes in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} every 10 minutes.
  </ExpandableHeading>
