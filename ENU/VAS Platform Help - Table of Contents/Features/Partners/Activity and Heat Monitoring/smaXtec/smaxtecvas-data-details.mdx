\{\{Partners.smaXtec}} and the \{\{General.ProductName}} exchange animal data every 6 hours, and all alerts or events are categorized as a diagnosis, health-related, or reproduction-related. Health and reproduction items are used to populate \{\{Partners.smaXtec}} worklists. See the sections below for more details on available \{\{General.CompanyName-short}} and \{\{Partners.smaXtec}} data and alerts.
