  ## Settings

  \{\{Commands.UserActions}}

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/rP-qMV9X16MAXHwAs6biW_settingsoutlined25x25.png" size="10" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/feedcomp-img/icons/settings_outlined_25x25.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/feedcomp-img/icons/settings_outlined_25x25.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  **&#x20;Settings** to update your \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} user settings. Expand the sections below to learn more:

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Choose Equipment to Connect to a Scale

    <hint type="warning">
      **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**&#x54;his feature is only available when you’re using this device for feeding. If you change your mind about using a device for feeding, :Link[log out of the app]{label="log out of the app" overridedLabel="log out of the app" spaceId docId="sK8Esbx0pVQzqZ5wDAiMP" version="v2" docAnchorId="#logout" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="account-settings.mdx"} and select **Yes** or **No** when asked if the device will be used for feeding.
    </hint>

    1. Select the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Upper}} to connect.
    2. Tap Connect to Scale and select the scale to connect. After a scale is connected to your mobile device, the Bluetooth icon

    <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/Fy-5LzSrJIbpfrYH9QBhr_bluetoothconnected20x25.png" size="10" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/feedcomp-img/icons/bluetooth_connected_20x25.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/feedcomp-img/icons/bluetooth_connected_20x25.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false" indent="2"></Image>

    <Paragraph indent="1">
      displays at the top left of each screen in the \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp-upper}}. The Switch Scale button also becomes available and allows you to connect to a different scale if needed.
    </Paragraph>

    For more information about \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}, see [Scales](FCM-ScaleConnections.htm).
  </ExpandableHeading>

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Switch to Another Dairy

    <hint type="warning">
      **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**&#x54;his feature is only available when you’re **not** using this device for feeding. If you change your mind about using a device for feeding, :Link[log out of the app]{label="log out of the app" overridedLabel="log out of the app" spaceId docId="sK8Esbx0pVQzqZ5wDAiMP" version="v2" docAnchorId="#logout" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="account-settings.mdx"} and select **Yes** or **No** when asked if the device will be used for feeding.
    </hint>

    The current \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} selected is displayed in \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}}**&#x20;Name**. If you are not using this device for feeding, you can tap Switch \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}} to switch to another \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}, \{\{Commands.UserActions}}`Save` at the bottom of the screen.
  </ExpandableHeading>

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Enable Overnight Loads

    Activate the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompOvernightLoadToggle}} toggle to enable overnight loads for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}.

    See :Link[Mix Overnight Loads]{label="Mix Overnight Loads" overridedLabel="Mix Overnight Loads" spaceId docId="MRjA0M2P6Qw2XWBHnNeMC" version="v2" docAnchorId="#mixovernight" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="FCM-Feedings.htm#mixovernight" href="FCM-Feedings.htm#mixovernight"} to learn more.
  </ExpandableHeading>

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### View Measurement Setting

    View **Units** to see the unit of measurement used by your device. To change the unit of measurement system to the Imperial System or Metric System, you must update your account settings directly in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}:

    - Select **Imperial System** to display units in Pounds, Gallons, Fahrenheit, and Ounces.
    - Select **Metric System** to display units in Kilograms, Liters, Celsius, and Milligrams.

    See [Edit Your User Account](../../Config/EditUserAcct.htm)in the \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.H-elp-}}to learn more.
  </ExpandableHeading>

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Log out of Your User Profile

    <hint type="warning">
      **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**&#x54;his feature is only available when you’re using this device for feeding. If you change your mind about using a device for feeding, :Link[log out of the app]{label="log out of the app" overridedLabel="log out of the app" spaceId docId="sK8Esbx0pVQzqZ5wDAiMP" version="v2" docAnchorId="#logout" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="account-settings.mdx"} and select **Yes** or **No** when asked if the device will be used for feeding. See [Logging in to the FeedComp App](FCM-Login.htm) to learn more.
    </hint>

    Tap `Log out of Profile` at the bottom of the screen to log your user profile out of this device. The next person who opens \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} on this device will be prompted to sign in with their user profile.
  </ExpandableHeading>
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Refresh

  When the mobile app has an active WiFi or data connection, you can tap

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/sd7NFAD4IoD7KN6nrwloj_refresh.png" size="10" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/feedcomp-img/icons/refresh.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/feedcomp-img/icons/refresh.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  **&#x20;Refresh** to manually refresh your \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}}data. If you go outside of data range, the app will continue to work in offline \{\{mode}}. Once you are back in data range, you can refresh your data and view the latest updates.

  <hint type="info">
    **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x57;hile \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} allows you to continue working in offline mode, some features in \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} require you to be online to see data.
  </hint>
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Log out or Switch to Another User

  Tap

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/FSm7qalEviAqu9UHiM5f__logout.png" size="10" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/feedcomp-img/icons/logout.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/feedcomp-img/icons/logout.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  **&#x20;Sign-Out of Device** at the bottom of the screen to log your user profile out of this device. The next person who opens \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} on this device will be prompted to sign in with their user profile.

  <hint type="info">
    **TIP:&#x20;**&#x59;ou can also use this feature to choose whether you want to use this device for feeding. After you sign out, you will be asked if you're using this device for feeding during your next login. See [Logging in to the FeedComp App](FCM-Login.htm) to learn more.
