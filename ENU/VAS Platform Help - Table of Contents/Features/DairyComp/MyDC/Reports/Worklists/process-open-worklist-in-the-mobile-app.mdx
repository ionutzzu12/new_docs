  ### Required Permissions

  To use this feature, your \{\{General.ProductName}} user account must be assigned to a Role with \{\{RolesPermissions.PermissionNames}} permissions. See [Edit Dairy User Permissions](../../Config/UserMgmtRoles/EditUserPermissions.htm) and [Manage Dairy Roles](../../Config/UserMgmtRoles/ManageRoles.htm) for more information.
</ExpandableHeading>

\{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}\{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}s are available for processing on the go from the mobile app. To pull your latest \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}s down from \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} (and \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}} for \{\{masterVAS-Products.DCUnlimited-short}}\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}}), open the mobile app and tap **Refresh Lists** to get started.
