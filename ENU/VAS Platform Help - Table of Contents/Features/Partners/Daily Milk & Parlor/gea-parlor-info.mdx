  ### Can't find this feature? Make sure it's included in your subscription!

  This feature/option is available only for \{\{masterVAS-Products.DCP-users-short}}. \{\{masterVAS-Products.DCU-users-short}} will not see it in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} because this functionality is controlled in \{\{masterVAS-Products.OnPremise-lower}}\{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-simple}} rather than in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.

  Click [here](../../Platform/GetStarted/KnowYourPulse.htm) to learn more about the different versions of \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.
</ExpandableHeading>

\{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}\{\{Partners.Parlor}} integrations allow \{\{masterVAS-Products.MyDC-short}}\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}} to capture data from their milk sensors. This allows you to view daily milk and shift data details in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} for analysis tasks. See [Connect Parlor Integration and Configure Shifts (MyDC)](../Parlors/ParlorSetup-mydc.htm) to get started, and then [View Parlor Data and Build Reports](../Parlors/ParlorData.htm). After your \{\{Partners.Parlor}} data is received by \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, you can also view milk data on your animals' [Daily Milk](../../Platform/Features/CowCards/CC-DailyMilk.htm)\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}} tab.
