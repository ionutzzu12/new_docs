<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Required Permissions

  Only users who are assigned a Role with Integrations Management permissions can connect partner integrations. See [Edit Dairy User Permissions](../../Platform/Features/Config/UserMgmtRoles/EditUserPermissions.htm) and [Manage Dairy Roles](<../../../Manage Dairy Users and Roles/manage-dairy-roles.mdx>) for more information.
</ExpandableHeading>

Follow these steps to connect the \{\{Partners.PartnerName}} integration and view measurement data in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}:

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Step 1: Configure Items in PULSE

  <hint type="warning">
    **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}} version 25.2 is required to support mapping items from \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} for \{\{General.DCUnlimited}} dairies. See [Update DC305 to the Latest Release](https://dc-help.vas.com/FAQs/WebUpdater.htm) \{\{masterVAS-Phrases.RefsToOtherHelpProjects}} for update instructions.
  </hint>

  For data to be sent to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} (and \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}} for \{\{General.DCUnlimited}}\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}}), you must first add new items or configure existing items to map to the partner's data points.

  1. In \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, navigate to **Partners > Integrations Gallery** and locate your partner company.
  2. Click **See Requirements**.
  3. Click **Select Items** to begin adding new items or mapping to existing items. Expand :Link[Available Items]{label="Available Items" overridedLabel="Available Items" spaceId docId="H8FmjAUutBoKzyfTpqtch" version="v2" docAnchorId="#items" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="activate-nedap-smartsight-integration.mdx"} below to learn more about each item. :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/nW7Rzc1TymaFvPCI9O0a5_item-selection.png" alt caption size="50"}
  4. Check the boxes to add new items to your dairy for the specific data you are connecting to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.:::hint\{type="warning"}
     **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**&#x53;elect items for mapping carefully. If you make an error and need to change your item configuration, you will need to correct the items in \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}} (for \{\{General.DCUnlimited}} \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}}) or contact [VAS Support](http://platform-docs.vas.com/default.htm#cshid=1004).
     \:::
  5. If you have an existing \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-simple}} item you'd like to use, select it from the **DairyComp Item** drop-down.
  6. Click **Create** or **Done** in the bottom right corner to finish adding and/or mapping your items.
  7. Confirm the addition or mapping of your items and click **Save**.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Step 2: Connect Integration in PULSE

  Follow these steps to establish the connection to \{\{Partners.NedapSmartSight}}:

  1. Click the **Connection Status** toggle and verify the status switches to **Active**.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/yVoLb7NAnbX1zs-j2jcYx_integration-connected.png" alt="Connection Status: Active" caption size="22"}
  2. If necessary, \{\{Commands.click}}**Edit Integration** to make any adjustments or edits and save your changes.

  <hint type="warning">
    **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**&#x49;f your integration does not automatically connect, contact your partner company representative to connect the integration from their system.
  </hint>
</ExpandableHeading>

<hint type="info">
  **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x4F;nce the \{\{Partners.measurements}} system is connected and collecting data, the [CowCard](<../../../Manage Your Animals/cowcards-at-a-glance.mdx>) Partners tab will begin to populate measurement data. See [Nedap SmartSight CowCard Widget](smartsight-cowcard-widget.htm) to learn more.
</hint>
