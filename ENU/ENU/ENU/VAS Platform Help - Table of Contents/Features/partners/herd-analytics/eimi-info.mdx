[E.I. Medical Imaging](https://www.eimedical.com/) provides state-of-the-art, real-time ultrasound scanners to serve veterinarians and livestock producers worldwide. When you connect the integration from the \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}[Integrations Gallery](<../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/partners/integrations-gallery.mdx>), your raw herd data is sent to \{\{Partners.PartnerName}}. This is a one-way data share that you can turn on and off with a single click. See [General Integration Instructions](../integrations-gallery/general-integration-instructions.mdx) to learn more about connecting the intergration!
