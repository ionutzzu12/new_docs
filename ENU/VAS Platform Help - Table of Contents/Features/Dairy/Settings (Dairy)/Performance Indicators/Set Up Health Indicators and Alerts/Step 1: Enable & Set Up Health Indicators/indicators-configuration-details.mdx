If the **Weekly** option is selected in the **Period Preferences** area, the weekly schedule is displayed in the side panel. If the **Daily** option is selected, the side panel shows the daily schedule instead.

While the daily schedule shows only three days instead of four weeks, the color coding is essentially the same as the weekly schedule.

Periods on the schedule are color-coded as follows:

- Dark blue shows the prior period that \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} uses to determine \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-ThresholdDifferential}}s for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}.
- Light blue shows the period when \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} last examined \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-ThresholdDifferential}}s and sent alerts.
- Green shows the next day when \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} will examine \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-ThresholdDifferential}}s and send alerts.

For the weekly schedule, this means that \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} examines \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-ThresholdDifferential}}s and sends alerts on the day of the week you selected. In the example above, Wednesday (the day highlighted in green) is the one selected. If your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} exceeds an \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-AlertThresholdValue}} from the previous two periods (the previous week in dark blue and the last week in light blue), \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} notifies your team on that Wednesday.

In the daily schedule, yesterday's date is highlighted in light blue, and this shows you the day when \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} last examined \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-ThresholdDifferential}}s and sent alerts. The previous day that \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} uses to determine \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-ThresholdDifferential}}s for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} is highlighted in dark blue, and \{\{today}}'s date is highlighted in green.

This means that \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} examines \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-ThresholdDifferential}}s today using the data from yesterday and the day before yesterday as a base. When the \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-ThresholdDifferential}} examined today exceeds the \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-AlertThresholdValue}} entered for the \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-ThresholdDifferential}} examined from the previous two days, \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} notifies your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} team.
