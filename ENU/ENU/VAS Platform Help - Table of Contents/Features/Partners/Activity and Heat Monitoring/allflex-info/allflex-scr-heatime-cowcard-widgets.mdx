  ### Heat Alerts

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    A Heat Alert is triggered if the animal exhibits behavior that indicates it is in heat, like eating less and exhibiting more activity at the same time.
  </Paragraph>

  <ExpandableHeading indent="2">
