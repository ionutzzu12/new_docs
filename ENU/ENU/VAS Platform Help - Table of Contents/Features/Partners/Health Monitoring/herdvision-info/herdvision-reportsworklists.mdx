<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Required Setup

  To generate \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}s based on your \{\{Partners.PartnerName}} measurement values, your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} must first activate the integration. See [Activate HerdVision Integration](activate-herdvision-integration.mdx) to learn more.
</ExpandableHeading>

Use \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}'s [Report/Worklist Builder](<../../../../../ENU/VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/DairyComp/MyDC/reportsworklists/create-new-report-or-worklist/build-custom-report-or-worklist.mdx>) or \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}} to generate reports or \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}s based on locomotion or BCS values.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Sample Report to View Average BCS by Pen

  SUMPEN BCSFORBCS>0BYPEN
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Sample Worklist Command

  List of animals with a locomotion score of 50 or greater \{\{today}} or yesterday by \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}}:

  LIST ID PEN LMSFD LMSFT LMSVFOR LMSV>50 LMSFD>-2BYPEN
</ExpandableHeading>

<hint type="warning">
  **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**&#x49;f generating the \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}} in \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}}, be sure to add the `\K` switch to send the list to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.
</hint>

See [Create To-Do Lists](<../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/DairyComp/MyDC/Reports/Worklists/Learn More about To-Do Lists/create-to-do-lists.mdx>) to learn more about creating custom \{\{Worklists.ToDoCommonUse}}s. After you create your \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}, you'll then be able to [Complete Tasks (v1)](<../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Mobile Apps/VAS Platform Mobile App (v1)/Tasks & Worklists (v1)/complete-tasks-v1.mdx>) or [Complete Tasks (v2)](<../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Mobile Apps/DC on Mobile (v2)/complete-tasks-v2.mdx>).
