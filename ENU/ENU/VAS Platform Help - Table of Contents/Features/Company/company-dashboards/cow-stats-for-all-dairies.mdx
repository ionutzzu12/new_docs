  ### What permissions do I need to view this widget?

  To view this widget on your \{\{Buttons-UI.D-ashboardCompany-}}, your user account must be assigned to a Role with \{\{RolesPermissions.PermissionNames}} permissions. See [Edit Dairy User Permissions](../../Config/UserMgmtRoles/EditUserPermissions.htm) and [Manage Dairy Roles](<../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Manage Dairy Users and Roles/manage-dairy-roles.mdx>) for more information.

The \{\{Widgets.CowStats}} for All \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airies-}} widget is useful in quickly identifying animal counts by \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} and determining how many cows need to be transitioned to dry in preparation for calving at each location. This widget is only available on \{\{Buttons-UI.D-ashboardsCompany-}}.

Follow these guidelines when reviewing the data in this widget:

- The color-coded pie chart and detailed area to the right contain data for milking cows (blue), dry cows (green), and heifers (red).
- View the **Updated** timestamp to determine the date and time of the last data sync with \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-simple}}. This data is updated on a daily basis.
- The **Milking Cows** total includes all To Tank and Hospital cows.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/wu4vdS4x7TFnsn3B3r935_cow-stats-co-all-dairies-widget.png" alt caption size="55"}

**Milking Cow** data includes the following: 

- \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.F-resh-cow-}}: Recently calved
- \{\{Due}} Dry: Animals to be dried off prior to calving
- To Tank: Animals contributing milk to tank
- Hospital: Animals in the hospital \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}}

**Dry Cow** data includes the following: 

- Close Up: Animals in the close-up \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} near calving
- Far Off: All other dry animals not in the close-up \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}}

**Heifer** data includes the following: 

- Close Up: Heifers in the close-up \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} near calving
- Not Close Up: All other heifers not in the close-up \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}}
