For the most accurate and comprehensive \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} analysis, be sure to add parentage details for the animals in your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}. \{\{General.CompanyName-short}} recommends adding the data for all animals for the following standard \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}/\{\{DC-UI.DC-ProductName}} items: 

  - \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.S-ire-}} (\{\{SID}})
  - \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-am-}} (\{\{DID}})
  - Maternal \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.g-rands-ire}} (MGS)
  - Maternal great-\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.g-rands-ire}} (MGGS)

  This data is used to assess the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}'s genomic profile and is the basis of calculations made for specific animal traits and potential improvements.

  The breed average is also incorporated into the assessment calculations, but having specific parentage data for the animals in your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} greatly increases the accuracy of the analysis process:

  - **Three-generation parent average:** If a proof is not available for a female, trait data is used from three previous \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.s-ire}} generations (\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.s-ire}}, maternal \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.g-rands-ire}}, and maternal great-\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.g-rands-ire}}) to calculate the female's trait averages. \{\{UDS-general.UDSBrand}}\{\{UDS-general.UDSProduct}} calculates the trait values using weighted percentages of the three-generation parent averages and the breed average:\* 50% \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.s-ire}}
    - 25% maternal \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.g-rands-ire}}
    - 12.5% maternal great-\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.g-rands-ire}}
    - 12.5% breed average
  - **One-generation parent average:** To calculate a potential progeny's trait averages, trait data is used from the progeny's parents (\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.s-ire}} and \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-am}}).
  - **Breed average:** When parent data is unavailable, a progeny's trait averages are based on the breed average.

  See [View Animal Details on CowCards](../../Platform/Features/CowCards/WorkingWithCowCards.htm) to learn more about viewing and adding animal item data.
