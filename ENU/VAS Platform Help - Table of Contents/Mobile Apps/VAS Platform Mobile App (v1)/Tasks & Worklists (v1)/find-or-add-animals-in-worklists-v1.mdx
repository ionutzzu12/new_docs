All animals within the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} are displayed by their \{\{ID}} number. You have several options for working with animals in a list, including:

- Marking them \{\{Worklists.DoneUI}},
- Adding them to the queue (\{\{Worklists.VetListCommonUse}}s only),
- Searching for an animal,
- Scanning an animal,
- Adding more animals to a list, and
- Viewing animals that are already processed.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Search for an Animal

  The list search feature is helpful if you have located a specific animal and need to see if it is in your list. You can enter the animal ID in the search bar at the top of the screen, regardless of which \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} you are currently viewing.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/eG7cWVB-HCqxNNj3ULvaS_search-list.png" size="38" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/mobile-app/worklists/search-list.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/mobile-app/worklists/search-list.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  Animals matching your search are identified as needed or not needed:

  - `NEEDED`: The animal is in the list.
  - NOT NEEDED: The animal is not in the list, and you have the option to add it. See :Link[Add Animal to a Worklist]{label="Add Animal to a Worklist" overridedLabel="Add Animal to a Worklist" spaceId docId="7AC_g4VwJrfDfZvP9ry1W" version="v2" docAnchorId="#AddAnimal" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="find-or-add-animals-in-worklists-v1.mdx"} to learn more.

  <hint type="info">
    **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x49;f the animal is in a different \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} than the one you were originally viewing, you may need to go back to the main list screen to return to the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} you were working with prior to the search.
  </hint>
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Add an Animal to a Worklist

  If you want to add an animal to the list, tap the animal ID in :Link[your search results]{label="your search results" overridedLabel="your search results" spaceId docId="7AC_g4VwJrfDfZvP9ry1W" version="v2" docAnchorId="#SearchforAnimal" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="find-or-add-animals-in-worklists-v1.mdx"} or scan the animal. Select **Add to List** to add the animal.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/MuEfhEbU4lPOnPbjWcxo3_add-animal.png" size="24" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/mobile-app/worklists/add-animal.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/mobile-app/worklists/add-animal.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  <hint type="info">
    **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x54;he animal is added to the \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}} on your device only. Other users will not see the animal in the \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}} on their devices until you process the animal and select **Mark as Done**.
  </hint>
</ExpandableHeading>

Proceed to [Complete Tasks (v1)](CompleteTasks.htm) after you finish finding or adding animals.
