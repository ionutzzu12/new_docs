  ### Learn more about the information shown in the Sires list

  - \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.S-ire-}} \{\{ID}}**:** Animal's \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.s-ire}} ID
  - **Name:** Animal's common name
  - \{\{Reg}}**/**\{\{ISO}}**:** Animal's registration number or ISO ID number
  - **Active:** Indicates whether the animal is active or inactive
  - **Stud:** Semen company identification. For a listing of standard identifiers for the US, see [NAAB Stud Codes](https://www.naab-css.org/naab-icar-stud-codes).
  - **Breed:** Two-letter abbreviation for the breed of the animal's \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.s-ire}}. Common Cow breeds include the following: HO = Holstein; JE = Jersey; AY = Ayrshire; BS = Brown Swiss; GS = Guernsey; MS = Milking Shorthorn; XB = Beef cross-breed; XD = \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}} cross-breed.
  - **Type:** Identifies the type of semen used to conceive the calf:\* **Sexed**: Semen that has been analyzed and sorted based on the X/Y chromosome in the individual sperm cells. For \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} cows, this type of semen has mostly X-chromosome sperm cells to increase the likelihood of producing heifer calves.
    - **Unsexed**: Semen that has not been sorted based on the X/Y chromosome in the sperm cells.
</ExpandableHeading>

  To add a \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.s-ire}} on the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.S-ires-}} page, click **Add New&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.S-ire-}} or select \{\{Buttons-UI.FeaturesMain}}**&#x20;> \*\*\*\***\{\{Buttons-UI.FeaturesSecondary}}> \{\{Buttons-UI.FeaturesTertiary}} from the [menu bar](<../../../../ENU/VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/navigate-pulse/menu-options.mdx>).

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/pt7PZzhJXfurDfo2W4j_O_dc-sires-addbutton.png" size="49" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/dairycomp/dc-sires-addbutton.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/dairycomp/dc-sires-addbutton.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/Chl1OL0aMBrcP-wzynuU8_add-new-sire-pop.png" size="32" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/dairycomp/Add-New-Sire-Pop.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/dairycomp/Add-New-Sire-Pop.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  Verify or enter the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.S-ire-}} Information and click **Save**.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/kSXlxYRkG1IksA0i3uTQl_add-new-sire-info.png" size="29" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/dairycomp/Add-New-Sire-Info.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/dairycomp/Add-New-Sire-Info.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>
