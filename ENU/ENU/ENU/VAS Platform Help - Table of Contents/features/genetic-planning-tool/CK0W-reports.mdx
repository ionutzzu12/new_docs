On the :Link[Mating]{label="Mating" overridedLabel="Mating" spaceId docId="CK0W7y_f75QLHJAgjgi5d" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Report-Mating" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="CK0W-reports.mdx"} page, you can also create and download an :Link[Inbreeding]{label="Inbreeding" overridedLabel="Inbreeding" spaceId docId="CK0W7y_f75QLHJAgjgi5d" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Report-Inbreeding" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="CK0W-reports.mdx"} report. After you review the reports, you must :Link[activate the plan]{label="activate the plan" overridedLabel="activate the plan" spaceId docId="bOFIx0XLID5V8_6mI2RS6" version="v2" docAnchorId="#ActivatePlan" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="ActivateGeneticPlan.htm#ActivatePlan" href="../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/GENEX Genetic Planning Tool/activate-a-genetic-plan.mdx"} to finalize it and share it with the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}.

  ## Value of Proposal

  <hint type="info">
    **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x54;his is only generated for plans that use the Annual Plan Projection formula. To learn more about your plan's projection formula, see [Set Mating Options in Step 4](<../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/GENEX Genetic Planning Tool/Create a Genetic Plan/step-4-create-your-strategy.mdx>).
  </hint>

  Use this report to review the plan costs and genetic gains for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}.

  Click **Download&#x20;**\{\{UDS-general.Report-Value}}**&#x20;Report** in the upper right corner to export a PDF version of the report. Expand a section to include it in the PDF or collapse a section to exclude it from the PDF.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/bWtSDfqqgR5uflz_oek_k_value-main-new.png" size="38" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/GenPlanning/Reports/value-main-new.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/GenPlanning/Reports/value-main-new.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  1. **Inventory Projections Graph:** Displays the difference between the expected number of female calves being born monthly and compares that count to the number needed to meet plan goals.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/oai3jmry4tUaoYkx0KMix_value-inventory-graph.png" alt caption size="30"}The shaded area shows the difference between the monthly counts. Additional female calves not needed in the plan may be sold for profit.
  2. **Genetic ROI Graph:** Choose to view the ROI based on the cost per female or total cost for the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/iDqzyXi1Q7OCIa8V4T0BZ_value-roi-select.png" alt caption size="30"}The shaded area shows the value added based on the plan.
  3. **Economic Impact:** A summary of the various costs and returns that factor into the total economic gain of the plan. These gains include an increased \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} value based on genetic improvements of progeny and proceeds from the sale of dairy and \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}/beef calves, minus total semen costs.:::ExpandableHeading

  ### How is this data calculated?

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    Economic Impact = \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} genetic value gain + calf sale proceeds - current genetic value of the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} - semen cost
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.H-erd-}} genetic value gain:  Value gain by genetic improvement and the additional milking cows produced by the plan. Or, (NPV \* females produced in the plan) - current genetic value of the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}.
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    Calf sales: Proceeds from the sale of dairy bull calves and \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}/beef calves produced in the plan. Or, (dairy bull calves produced \* cost/head) + (beef calves produced \* cost/head). The total numbers of calves to be sold is based on matings with conventional, sexed, and bull semen, accounting for conception rates for cows and heifers, as well as losses \{\{due}} to abortions, pregnant culling, and stillbirths. The prices for \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} bull calves and beef calves is set in [Step 4](<../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/GENEX Genetic Planning Tool/Create a Genetic Plan/step-4-create-your-strategy.mdx>).
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    Current genetic value of the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}: To determine the actual financial gains from the animals produced by the plan, the value of the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}\{\{today}} is deducted from the total.
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    Semen cost: The :Link[Total Investment]{label="Total Investment" overridedLabel="Total Investment" spaceId docId="CK0W7y_f75QLHJAgjgi5d" version="v2" docAnchorId="#totalInvestment" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="CK0W-reports.mdx"} value noted below.
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    Example:
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    Replacements produced: 2250
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    NPV average: $275.85
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    Current genetic \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} value: $220,500.00
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    Calves to be sold: 400 dairy bulls, 300 beef
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    Average price/calf: $75 dairy bull/$105 beef
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    Total investment: $111,916.11
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    Calculation: (2250 \* $275.85) - $220,500.00 + (400 \* $75) + (300 \* $105) - $111,916.11 = $349,746.39
    \:::
  </Paragraph>

  4. **Doses per Female:** A summary of the semen doses per female needed in the plan.
  5. **AVG Cost per Dose:** A summary of the number of semen doses required for the plan, the average price per dose, and the total investment required by the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} to follow the plan.
  6. **Total Investment:** Total cost of the semen doses in your plan.::::ExpandableHeading

  ### How is this data calculated?

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    Total Investment = total cost of semen doses \* cost/dose calculated for each semen type (sexed, conventional, and beef semen)
    The quantity of doses required is based on the current conception rates for heifers and cows, as well as the semen type prices and percentages set in [Step 4](<../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/GENEX Genetic Planning Tool/Create a Genetic Plan/step-4-create-your-strategy.mdx>).
  </Paragraph>

  <hint type="info" indent="2">
    **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x49;f the Mate to High Fertility after (x) failed breedings :Link[Mating Option]{label="Mating Option" overridedLabel="Mating Option" spaceId docId="Q6qb0xcUw4LyE67OtGMPX" version="v2" docAnchorId="#mate" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../BuildGeneticPlan/GeneticPlanStep4.htm#mate" href="../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/GENEX Genetic Planning Tool/Create a Genetic Plan/step-4-create-your-strategy.mdx"} is enabled, additional doses of high fertility sexed semen may be required in your plan.
  </hint>

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    \::::
  </Paragraph>

  7. **Parameters:** A summary of the semen distribution among females, \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} growth and loss rates, and the market price (per head) of \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} and beef animals. Conception rates and the average \{\{age}} of heifers at first calving are also included, as these values directly affect the accuracy of culling projections and replacement heifer counts.
  8. **Projected Animals Produced:** Projected total number of calves and milking females that will serve as replacements in the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} as a result of the plan. See the :Link[Inventory & Projections Graph]{label="Inventory & Projections Graph" overridedLabel="Inventory & Projections Graph" spaceId docId="CK0W7y_f75QLHJAgjgi5d" version="v2" docAnchorId="#InventoryGraph" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="CK0W-reports.mdx"} for more details.
  9. **Genetic ROI:** Genetic return on investment compares the plan's semen costs to the projected value of the milking progeny in 4.5 years. The projection is based on a Net Present Value (NPV) calculation of the replacement milking cows generated in the plan. The NPV is the projected monetary value of the future daughter in current-day prices.:::ExpandableHeading

  ### How is this data calculated?

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    Genetic ROI = (NPV - investment per milking female) / investment per milking female
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    Investment per milking female takes into account the total semen cost, which is based on the :Link[Total Investment]{label="Total Investment" overridedLabel="Total Investment" spaceId docId="CK0W7y_f75QLHJAgjgi5d" version="v2" docAnchorId="#totalInvestment" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="CK0W-reports.mdx"} value noted above, as well as the number of replacements generated in the plan.
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    NPV: Average genetic value of daughters produced by the plan in current \* (discount of 8% per year for 4.5 years)
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    Example:
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    Replacements produced: 2550
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    Total cost: 3.55 doses \* $45.53 investment per female
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    NPV average: $210.85
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    Calculation: $210.85 - $45.53 / $45.53 = $3.63 per invested $, or a 363% increase
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    See the :Link[Genetic ROI Graph]{label="Genetic ROI Graph" overridedLabel="Genetic ROI Graph" spaceId docId="CK0W7y_f75QLHJAgjgi5d" version="v2" docAnchorId="#ROIgraph" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="CK0W-reports.mdx"} and values by female or \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}.
    \:::
  </Paragraph>
</ExpandableHeading>
