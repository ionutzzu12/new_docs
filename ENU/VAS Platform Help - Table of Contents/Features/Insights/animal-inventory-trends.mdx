  ### Required Permissions

  To use this feature, your \{\{General.ProductName}} user account must be assigned to a Role with \{\{RolesPermissions.PermissionNames}} permissions. See [Edit Dairy User Permissions](../Config/UserMgmtRoles/EditUserPermissions.htm) and [Manage Dairy Roles](../Config/UserMgmtRoles/ManageRoles.htm) for more information.
</ExpandableHeading>

The \{\{Insights.Inventory}} report provides a visual representation of recent animal inventory changes for cows and heifers by reproduction status, as well as heifers by \{\{age}}.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/fEMV1HhzFqUqGOI4xeII6_inventory-main.png" size="81" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/reports/inventory-main.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/reports/inventory-main.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

You can find this feature under the :Link[Insights menu]{label="Insights menu" overridedLabel="Insights menu" spaceId docId="WFLX7SPMpZfy7zoMyrvRh" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Menu-Insights" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../../NavPlatform/Menu.htm#Menu-Insights" href="../../NavPlatform/Menu.htm#Menu-Insights"}.

Use the following guidelines when viewing your \{\{Insights.Inventory}}:

- \{\{Commands.Click}} on the tabs to select one of three reports:\* Cows by \{\{RPRO}}
  - Heifers by RPRO
  - Heifers by Age
- By default, the reports show up to 12 months of data.
- Hover over the graph to view animal count details by month.
- \{\{Commands.Click}} the animal groups in the key to control which animal groups are included: :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/UigWJy3ehrfX_aiOKjjUj_click-key.png" alt caption size="74"}\* Cows by RPRO status key:
