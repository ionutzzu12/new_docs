This section lists all of our daily milk and \{\{Partners.parlor}} partners available for \{\{masterVAS-Products.MyDC-short}} dairies. Our partners' \{\{Partners.parlor\_systems}} provide milk weight and composition data for your herd's daily milking sessions.

After you activate an integration with one of the following partners, \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} displays animal data received from the partners' products.
