Pair a feed scale to \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp-upper}} using your mobile device's Bluetooth connection. Make sure that Bluetooth is enabled on your mobile device, and then connect it on the [Account Settings](account-settings.mdx) screen in the app. Note that some scales may require configuration before you connect it to the \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp-upper}}.

<hint type="warning">
  **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**&#x49;f you connect to equipment containing an overnight load, an overnight load notification with a red alert icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/zFuxFy12vBPd_L3Mh2reG_alert-triangle-red.png" alt caption size="10"} is displayed. You'll need to use other equipment until the overnight load is completed. See :Link[Mix Overnight Loads]{label="Mix Overnight Loads" overridedLabel="Mix Overnight Loads" spaceId docId="MRjA0M2P6Qw2XWBHnNeMC" version="v2" docAnchorId="#mixovernight" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="FCM-Feedings.htm#mixovernight" href="Feed/feed-using-the-feeding-schedule.mdx"} to learn more.
</hint>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Supported Scales

  - \{\{FeedComp.ScaleTec}} indicator
  - \{\{FeedComp.ScaleTopcon}} indicator (EZ2810 and EZ3410)

    ### Connect the Indicator to the FeedComp App

    After you confirm that the scale settings match the required settings, connect the indicator to your mobile device via Bluetooth:

    1. Open your mobile device's Bluetooth settings.
    2. Connect the indicator.
    3. After it is connected, shut down the app completely and make sure it is cleared from your list of background apps.
    4. Follow the instructions in :Link[Connect a Scale]{label="Connect a Scale" overridedLabel="Connect a Scale" spaceId docId="sFgc60g_uvqstXbp-5XWS" version="v2" docAnchorId="#connect" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="scales.mdx"} to connect the scale to the \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp-upper}}.
  </ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Troubleshoot a Scale Connection

  If the connection fails when you attempt to :Link[Connect a Scale]{label="Connect a Scale" overridedLabel="Connect a Scale" spaceId docId="sFgc60g_uvqstXbp-5XWS" version="v2" docAnchorId="#connect" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="scales.mdx"}, check the following, depending on your scale model:

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Topcon Digi-Star scale

    <WorkflowBlock>
      <WorkflowBlockItem>
        - Verify that no other devices are currently connected to the scale. Only one device can be connected to a \{\{FeedComp.ScaleTopcon}} at a time.
        - Check the \{\{FeedComp.ScaleTopcon}} ID and make sure it starts with "TMR-".:::ExpandableHeading

        ### Expand for steps to check or change the scale ID.

        <Paragraph listStyleType="decimal" indent="2">
          On the keypad, enter **1003** and press **Select**. The current ID displays.
        </Paragraph>

        <Paragraph listStyleType="decimal" listStart="2" indent="2">
          If the ID does not start with "TMR-", use the keypad to change it. Press **Enter** to save the ID.
          \:::
        </Paragraph>

        - Verify that Bluetooth is enabled on the \{\{FeedComp.ScaleTopcon}}.:::ExpandableHeading

        ### Expand for steps to enable Bluetooth.

        <Paragraph listStyleType="decimal" indent="2">
          On the keypad, enter **90022** and press **Select**.
        </Paragraph>

        <Paragraph listStyleType="decimal" listStart="2" indent="2">
          If the display shows an error message, press the **ZERO** button four times and then **ON**. The keypad shows the current Bluetooth setting as ON or OFF.
        </Paragraph>

        <Paragraph listStyleType="decimal" listStart="3" indent="2">
          If Bluetooth is OFF, press the **Function** button to select **ON** and **Enter** to save the setting.
          \:::
        </Paragraph>

        - If \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} crashed on your device the last time it was connected to the scale, the connection may still be active. Turn the scale off and back on. Then reconnect the scale from \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}}.
        - Ensure that the \{\{FeedComp.ScaleTopcon}} settings match the settings listed in :Link[Configure a Topcon Digi-Star scale]{label="Configure a Topcon Digi-Star scale" overridedLabel="Configure a Topcon Digi-Star scale" spaceId docId="sFgc60g_uvqstXbp-5XWS" version="v2" docAnchorId="#configuretopcon" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="scales.mdx"}.
        - If you use a \{\{FeedComp.ScaleTopcon}} and the \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp-upper}} will not complete a drop or automatically advance to the next \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}}, make sure that the mixer's Drop Tolerance is set to a minimum value of **1**. If you use **0** for the Drop Tolerance value, the \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp-upper}} may not complete drops correctly. To update your mixer's Drop Tolerance, tap \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompAdjustments}} at the bottom of the \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp-upper}} screen, tap \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompEquipment}}, and select the mixer to make your changes.
      </WorkflowBlockItem>
    </WorkflowBlock>
  </ExpandableHeading>

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Scale-Tec POINT scale

    - If \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} crashed on your device the last time it was connected to the scale, the connection may still be active. Turn the scale off and back on. Then reconnect the scale from \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}}.
    - Reference the [Scale-Tec Support Center](https://support.scale-tec.com/portal/en/kb/point) if you are having other issues.
