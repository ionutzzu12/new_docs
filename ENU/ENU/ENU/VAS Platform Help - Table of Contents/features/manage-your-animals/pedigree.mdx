Use the \{\{CowCards-UI.CC-tab-names}} tab on the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}} to learn more about an animal's \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-am}} and \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.s-ire}}, the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-am}}'s lactation history, and offspring information for the animal's calf.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/YRpG9igcM51PlZ_UtaefX_cowcardpedigree.png" size="75" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/cowcards/CowCardPedigree.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/cowcards/CowCardPedigree.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

\{\{CowCards-UI.CC-tab-names}} information can be used to predict or determine which important traits may be inherited by an animal. For example, you can use a \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-am}}'s lactation history to predict how much milk its calf may produce in its lifetime.

Expand the sections below to learn more:

  Use the **Update Pedigree** button to open a side panel that allows you to update the animal's \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-am}}, \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.s-ire}}, maternal \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.g-rands-ire}}, and great \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.g-rands-ire}}.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Dam's Lactations

  Use the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-am-}}**'s Lactations** table to determine how well the animal's \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-am}} performed in each lactation. It contains some of the most important milking-related statistics for each of the animal's lactations, allowing you to gauge its lifetime production rates at a glance.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/J8dNIA-PP85PTu2yuDwRL_cc-parentage-damlact.png" size="50" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/cowcards/cc-parentage-damlact.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/cowcards/cc-parentage-damlact.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  - Hover over any abbreviation to view the definition in a pop-up tooltip.
  - The **Total** row at the bottom of the table shows how much milk (\{\{TOTM}}), fat (\{\{TOTF}}), and protein (\{\{TOTP}}) the animal has produced across all lactations.
  - You can compare the animal's milk production to the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}'s average milking total (\{\{305ME}}).
</ExpandableHeading>

  <ExpandableHeading indent="2">
    ### Click here for pointers on customizing the view!

    Interactive column headers allow you to arrange the data into a way that's most useful to you. Depending on the features available for each column, you can add new columns of data, rearrange and reorder them, and filter some columns. To access these features, click on the column menu in the column you want to change.

    <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/qnuufb85hPEOhW9C5fPsS_columnmenuopen.png" size="42" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/dairycomp/ColumnMenuOpen.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/dairycomp/ColumnMenuOpen.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

    The top menu can contain three different options—one for resizing the column, one for filtering the data displayed in the column, and one for controlling which columns are displayed. Each menu contains various ways for manipulating what's shown on the page.

    Expand the sections below to learn more:

    <ExpandableHeading>
      ## Filter Columns

      Some columns that contain numeric values can be filtered to show only certain data. For example, to filter the **Lactation** column so that only animals with a lactation value of 3 display, click the filter option, and then use the fields to create your filter.

      <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/qj1RjbFtFHfdQMp1O0Yxl_herdfilter.png" size="37" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/dairycomp/HerdFilter.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/dairycomp/HerdFilter.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>
    </ExpandableHeading>

    <ExpandableHeading>
      ## Reorder Columns

      You can reorder the way columns display by clicking in the header of the column you want to move and dragging it to its new location.
    </ExpandableHeading>

    <ExpandableHeading>
      ## Change which Columns to Display

      To add or remove columns from the page, click the columns option and then check or uncheck the boxes to select which columns to display.

      <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/DQd5aSkjYd-ETqW95S84r_herdcolumns.png" size="35" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/dairycomp/HerdColumns.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/dairycomp/HerdColumns.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

      You can also remove columns by clicking on the header of the column you want to remove, holding down the mouse key, and dragging the column up and out of the table.
    </ExpandableHeading>
  </ExpandableHeading>
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Tip! View Calves List

  While the \{\{CowCards-UI.CC-tab-names}} tab primarily provides \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-am}} and \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.s-ire}} information, you can view a list of a \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-am}}'s calves in the [Events](../Events/Events.htm) tab.

  In the Events tab of a \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-am}}'s CowCard, click on a \{\{Events.1FRESH}} event to open a side panel and view its details.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/KTZz8RHtltWoCZjRGo1um_cc-parentage-calves.png" size="80" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/cowcards/cc-parentage-calves.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/cowcards/cc-parentage-calves.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  The event details include a list of calves that were delivered during the event. Click any calf's ID to go to its \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}}.

  For more information about the Events tab, see [Events](../Events/Events.htm).
