\{\{Worklists.HospitalListUI-Web}} in the mobile app allow you treat animals on active protocols, view current milk or beef withholds, and add any new events as needed. Replacing what has historically been a paper list with an electronic one in the mobile app offers better safekeeping of your records, captures data for future analysis, and presents the opportunity to use scanners to speed up animal identification and treatment.

The mobile app delivers the same audible cues and sounds that many customers are familiar with from the \{\{PCC}} product, which helps your veterinarian and \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} workers quickly identify the animals that require treatment.

Use \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} or \{\{DC-UI.DC-ProdNameShort}} to create your \{\{Worklists.HospListCommonUse}}s with a special type of LIST command. For \{\{DC-UI.DC-ProdNameShort}} lists, adding the \HK switch to the commands sends them to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.

Most \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}} schedule \{\{Worklists.HospListCommonUse}}s to run daily to ensure treatment protocols are properly followed, as drugs are usually administered on a daily basis.

Prior to being able to generate \{\{Worklists.HospListCommonUse}}s, your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} must first go through the process of defining treatment protocols, hospital items, and \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} types in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} or \{\{DC-UI.DC-ProdNameShort}}. See [Create Hospital Lists](<../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/DairyComp/MyDC/Reports/Worklists/Learn More about Hospital Lists/create-hospital-lists.mdx>) to learn more.

<hint type="info">
  **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x41;ll adjustments to your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}'s protocols must be made in \{\{DC-UI.DC-ProdNameShort}}. \{\{Protocol}} details are viewable from the [Protocols](<../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/DairyComp/MyDC/Settings/Health Settings/8k1I-protocols.mdx>) Settings page.
</hint>

\{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} provides a default set of \{\{Worklists.HospitalListSingular}} event shortcuts and sound files. In the current \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} release, you can customize up to 7 event shortcuts by using \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}\{\{Buttons-UI.FeaturesSecondary}} page. Hospital list sounds are not currently customizable. See [Configure PULSE Hospital Lists](<../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/DairyComp/MyDC/Reports/Worklists/Learn More about Hospital Lists/configure-pulse-hospital-lists.mdx>) to learn more.

You can then process your lists in the mobile app and [monitor them in the web version of PULSE](<../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Mobile Apps/VAS Platform Mobile App (v1)/Tasks & Worklists (v1)/view-animal-status-in-the-mobile-app-v1.mdx>).
