The \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompButtons}}\{\{General.UI-gui}} allows you to view historical feed data in \{\{masterVAS-Products.AllProductNames}} and supports analysis tasks to optimize your feed operations. Tap \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompReports}} at the bottom of the screen, and then tap a report name to get started:

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Feeding History Report

  \{\{Commands.UserActions}} the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompReportNames}}\{\{Controls.ControlTypes}} to view historical information and other relevant details for your feedings, including missed amounts, feeder names, aborts, and acknowledge amounts.

  ## Pen Feed History Report

  \{\{Commands.UserActions}} the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompReportNames}}\{\{Controls.ControlTypes}} to view historical feeding count and \{\{DM/HD}} details for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}}, along with a summary of the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} selected. To access the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.P-en-}}\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeedHistory}} reports, select the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} from the [Pens](<../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/FeedComp on PULSE/pens.mdx>) list.

  Tap

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/59zljCrwosLSjFigUn2M5_penhistory26x25.png" size="10" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/feedcomp-img/icons/pen_history_26x25.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/feedcomp-img/icons/pen_history_26x25.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  **&#x20;\*\*\*\***\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompPenHistory}}, select a \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}}, and then select the **Date Range** for the report. Options to view the report by **Pen Count History** or **DM History** are available at the bottom of the screen.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Load Error Report

  \{\{Commands.UserActions}} the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompReportNames}}\{\{Controls.ControlTypes}} to view load error amounts for feedings to monitor and help prevent load errors made during feedings.

  Tap

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/m0RGizzfQGDD5NY-xPCBL_loaderror25x25.png" size="10" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/feedcomp-img/icons/load_error_25x25.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/feedcomp-img/icons/load_error_25x25.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  **&#x20;\*\*\*\***\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompLoadError}}, select the **Date Range** for the report, select a **Feeder**, enter the **Load Error Tolerance (lbs)**, \{\{Commands.UserActions}}`Submit` at the bottom of the screen.

  <hint type="info">
    **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x54;he error tolerance sets the minimum error amount to include in the report. For instance, if you set the error tolerance to 100, the report includes errors of 100 pounds and more (or kilograms based on the unit of measure specified for your [user account](<../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/User Menu/edit-your-user-account.mdx>)).
  </hint>
</ExpandableHeading>
