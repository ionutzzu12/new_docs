The \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-simple}}\{\{Buttons-UI.FeaturesTertiary}} page only includes content for \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}} with an active \{\{Partners.Cubex}}\{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} integration.

For new \{\{Partners.Cubex}} integrations, \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} imports medicine details from \{\{Partners.Cubex}} and attempts to match each drug with its \{\{MorphHelp.CowCare}} equivalent based on the \{\{NADA}} identification number. In cases where the drugs cannot be automatically matched based on the NADA, you must complete a task to "map" (i.e., associate) medicines available in \{\{MorphHelp.CowCare}} to drugs available in \{\{Partners.Cubex}} inventory.

You can find this feature under the :Link[DairyComp/MyDC menu]{label="DairyComp/MyDC menu" overridedLabel="DairyComp/MyDC menu" spaceId docId="WFLX7SPMpZfy7zoMyrvRh" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Menu-DairyComp" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../../../NavPlatform/Menu.htm#Menu-DairyComp" href="../../../../../../ENU/VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/navigate-pulse/menu-options.mdx"} > :Link[Settings]{label="Settings" overridedLabel="Settings" spaceId docId="WFLX7SPMpZfy7zoMyrvRh" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Menu-DairyComp" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../../../NavPlatform/Menu.htm#Menu-DairyComp" href="../../../../../../ENU/VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/navigate-pulse/menu-options.mdx"} option on the \{\{Buttons-UI.FeaturesSecondary}} tab. Please note, however, that if you do not have an active \{\{Partners.Cubex}}\{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} integration, you will not be able to complete any actions on this page.
