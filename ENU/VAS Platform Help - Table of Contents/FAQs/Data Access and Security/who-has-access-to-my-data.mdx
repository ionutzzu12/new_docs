The only people who have access to your data are the ones you allow. You can add or remove access to outside advisors and \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} employees using a simple role-based system.

Hide My \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}} from Public Searches: You can edit the settings for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} so that it does not appear in the list when users search for \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}} to connect to. Change the **Is Discoverable** setting on the \{\{MorphHelp.D-airySettings}} page to **No,&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}**&#x20;not visible for other users**. See [Settings (Dairy)](../Features/DairySettings/Settings-Dairy.htm) for instructions.
