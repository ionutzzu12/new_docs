If you mistakenly mark an animal as \{\{Worklists.DoneUI}} and have not processed the update yet, follow these steps to undo it:

1. Open the list in the mobile app, select the \{\{Worklists.DoneUI}} tab, and locate the animal you want to undo. Follow either of these steps:1) Tap the animal and then choose **UNDO**.
   2\) :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/HtAC7QxGSIPVNf9f-xa0m_undo-select-vetlist.png" alt caption size="38"}
   Swipe left on the animal.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/EIXhO7uRKUUWHxVE_IJq0_undo-select-vetlist-option2.png" size="25" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/mobile-app/worklists/undo-select-vetlist-option2.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/mobile-app/worklists/undo-select-vetlist-option2.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false" indent="2"></Image>

2. Tap **Yes** to confirm the UNDO.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/_p291ikyF48wiwHzmbfTD_undo-select-vetlist-confirm.png" size="17" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/mobile-app/worklists/undo-select-vetlist-confirm.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/mobile-app/worklists/undo-select-vetlist-confirm.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false" indent="2"></Image>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  Once you confirm, the app moves the animal back to the \{\{Worklists.ToDoUI-Mob}} tab.
</Paragraph>

<hint type="info">
  **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x4F;nly animals with the status \{\{Worklists.Received}} can be undone. If an animal's status is \{\{Worklists.Processing}} or \{\{Worklists.Processed}}, the change has already been sent to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} and cannot be undone.
</hint>
