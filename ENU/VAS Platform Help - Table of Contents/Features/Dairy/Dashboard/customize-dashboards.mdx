  ### Required Permissions

  Only users who are assigned to a Role with \{\{RolesPermissions.PermissionNames}} permissions can use this feature. See [Edit Dairy User Permissions](../../Config/UserMgmtRoles/EditUserPermissions.htm) and [Manage Dairy Roles](<../../Manage Dairy Users and Roles/manage-dairy-roles.mdx>) for more information.

You can customize your dashboards based on the needs of your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}. You can add, remove, resize, or rearrange widgets to prioritize the data you see. (For a full list of available widgets and what they show, see [Widget Descriptions](WidgetDescriptions.htm).)

Follow these steps to customize your dashboards:

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Step 1: Select Widgets

  Use the checkboxes on the left to choose which widgets to display on the Dashboard or \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.O-verview-}} page. When choosing widgets, use the following guidelines:

  - The widgets are categorized under the following tabs: **General**, \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.H-erd-}}, **Feed**, and **Parlor**. Click each tab to view the widgets that are available in these categories. See [Widget Descriptions](WidgetDescriptions.htm) for more information.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/BeqIui7mA0ln-GYhbzMsP_co-page-customization.png" alt caption size="44"}
  - For the \{\{Buttons-UI.D-ashboardCompany-}}, \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}-level widgets are presented by \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} name.
  - The small number shown on the right edge of each tab indicates how many widgets are currently selected for that category.
  - Use the Widget Gallery button at the top right of the page to find other available widgets.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Step 2: Arrange Widgets

  Use the right side of the page to arrange how the widgets are displayed on the Dashboard or \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.O-verview-}} page:

  - To rearrange widgets, simply drag and drop them to where you want them located. As illustrated below, once you begin dragging an item, the page will display dotted boxes to indicate where you are able to drop them.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/gKlqAyj53ISzXJAnYBOCZ_move-widget.png" alt caption size="56"}
  - To remove all widgets from the Dashboard or \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.O-verview-}} page, click the **Clear All** button at the top right.
  - Click the **X** at the top right of any widget to remove it from the Dashboard or \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.O-verview-}} page. This also unchecks it from the list to the left.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Step 3 (Optional): Add New Widgets

  Click the **Widget Gallery** button at the top right of the customization page to open the Widget Gallery. In the Widget Gallery, you can view a complete list of widgets that you can use in your dashboards.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/sxrpx4ngOJk1RXMfPpu9M_wg-list-explained.png" size="86" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/dashboard/widget-gallery/wg-list-explained.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/dashboard/widget-gallery/wg-list-explained.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  Use follow these guidelines to select widgets:

  - Click the General, \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.H-erd-}}, Feed, and Parlor categories at the top of the Widget Gallery to preview widgets that are available in each category for Platform Dashboards and \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.O-verview-}} Pages.
  - Click a widget's **ADD** button to add it to the list of available widgets in the Page Customization options.
  - Newly-added widgets display a **Just Added** badge under Page Customization.
  - Widgets that require an integration with one of our [Partners](../../partners.mdx) include the option to view additional details on completing the integration.

  In the example, 11 \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.H-erd-}} widgets are included in the \{\{Buttons-UI.Dashboard}}, each of which shows as **ADDED** in the Widget Gallery.

  <hint type="info">
    **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x53;ome widgets require setup and activation in your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} or company \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} account before they will begin displaying data on your Dashboard and \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.O-verview-}} Pages. If you have added a widget and it is not displaying any data, please contact [VAS Support](http://platform-docs.vas.com/default.htm#cshid=1004) for help.
  </hint>
</ExpandableHeading>
