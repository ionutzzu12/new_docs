The **Partner Integration Details** page allows you to view each partner's products or service and how the integration can benefit your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}. Additionally, it details the technical requirements necessary for the integration.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/9e5MFc-m2kZ6T65_x_D3b_integration-card-detail.png" size="64" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/config/integration-card-detail.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/config/integration-card-detail.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

\{\{Commands.Click}}**See Details** or **See Requirements** on any of the integration cards in the \{\{Buttons-UI.FeaturesSecondary}} to view integration details.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/ueDlHHI_l7dIjYNECDB6i_integration-details-button.png" size="35" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/config/integration-details-button.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/config/integration-details-button.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

Follow the guidelines below when working with partner integration details:

- After you integrate with a \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} partner, the system can show the integrated data in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} if that is the purpose of the integration. However, if the integration is one-directional (pulling \{\{masterVAS.CompanyName-short}} data only), you can view your \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} data in the partner's system.
