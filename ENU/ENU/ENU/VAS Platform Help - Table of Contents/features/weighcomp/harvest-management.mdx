The Harvest Management feature allows you to manage all of your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}'s harvests. Each new harvest is created in the \{\{MorphHelp.WeighComp}} app, and data for each harvest load is captured on the app by a scale attendant. The harvest can then be viewed and managed in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.

You can find this feature under the :Link[WeighComp menu]{label="WeighComp menu" overridedLabel="WeighComp menu" spaceId docId="WFLX7SPMpZfy7zoMyrvRh" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Menu-WeighComp" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../../../Platform/NavPlatform/Menu.htm#Menu-WeighComp" href="../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/navigate-pulse/menu-options.mdx"}.

The relationship between \{\{MorphHelp.WeighComp}}, \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedWatchOnPremise}}, and \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} for harvest management is shown in the following diagram.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/8auh6i4WDdllU0u2J6w1L_harvman.png" size="64" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/WeighComp/HarvMan.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/WeighComp/HarvMan.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

See the topics below when managing your harvest data:

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Enter Harvests

  The harvest data displayed in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} originates in the \{\{MorphHelp.WeighComp}} app. The \{\{MorphHelp.WeighComp}} app is used by the harvest attendant whenever a harvest is underway. The attendant creates the harvest in the app and then uses the app to enter data for each harvest load. Attendants can switch between harvests, or can exit out of harvest \{\{mode}} completely to use the app for spot load WeighComp transactions (i.e., non-harvest transactions). Once a harvest is done, the attendant marks it as complete in the app.

  Load data is sent to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} in real time. Marking the harvest as complete in the app allows \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} user to know that no more loads will be entered for the harvest. The user can then complete the review of the harvest and approve it in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.

  To enter Harvest Management mode, click the **Harvest** button on the main app screen.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/flwihzbHY2OaA4CmCI7rl_harvestbuttonhomescreen.png" size="86" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/WeighComp/HarvestButtonHomeScreen.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/WeighComp/HarvestButtonHomeScreen.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  The system will display the harvest screen.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/rGDZgvFLp_H2gPChiHbB6_harvestscreenmain.png" size="68" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/WeighComp/HarvestScreenMain.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/WeighComp/HarvestScreenMain.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  There are four main steps involved when using the app for harvest management, each of which is described below:

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Step 1: Set your farm inventory

    Setting your farm inventory means creating the assets (i.e., the trucks and trucking companies) you'll be using in all of your harvests. To view your trucks, for example, click the **Trucks** button. To add a new truck, click **Add new**.

    <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/WEQnVXXVYMtWG1f5GZ29R_truckscreen.png" size="60" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/WeighComp/TruckScreen.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/WeighComp/TruckScreen.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

    <hint type="info">
      **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x41;dding trucking companies in addition to adding trucks is helpful in differentiating between trucks with the same name or number from different companies.
    </hint>
  </ExpandableHeading>

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Step 2: Create the harvest

    Once you have set your farm assets, you can start to create harvests. When you create a new harvest, you'll be asked to give it a name.

    <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/yRHoZuZ_Tuc51W1DWko8z_harvestnamescreen.png" size="70" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/WeighComp/HarvestNameScreen.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/WeighComp/HarvestNameScreen.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

    After you've named the harvest, you'll be prompted to choose the vendor and product for the harvest. You'll then be asked to assign the assets for the harvest (i.e., fields, trucks, and location) and set up the harvest's printing parameters.

    Once you've entered all of the required information, click **Create**.
  </ExpandableHeading>

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Step 3: Start the harvest

    Harvests that you've created can be viewed in your harvest list by clicking **Select harvest**.

    <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/XCp2UXpxtBZRD0dqyoljQ_harvestlist.png" size="60" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/WeighComp/HarvestList.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/WeighComp/HarvestList.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

    From here you can start entering data for the loads as they arrive. You'll be prompted to enter weights, field, and location information for the load.

    <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/XA8KJMOTV_6UWjEqE0K7h_loadinfo.png" size="65" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/WeighComp/LoadInfo.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/WeighComp/LoadInfo.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

    Remember that you can pause a harvest at any time to work on a different harvest or go back to the main WeighComp Home screen. You can resume the harvest at a later time from the harvest list.
  </ExpandableHeading>

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Step 4: Complete the harvest

    To complete the harvest and send its data to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, click **Complete Harvest**.

    <hint type="warning">
      **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**&#x4F;nce a harvest is completed, you cannot return to it in the \{\{MorphHelp.WeighComp}} app. Completed harvests can only be viewed in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}. See Harvest Management for more information.
    </hint>
  </ExpandableHeading>
</ExpandableHeading>

    ### Edit Full and Empty Weights

    The full and empty weights originally received by the WeighComp app for pending loads can be manually edited:
