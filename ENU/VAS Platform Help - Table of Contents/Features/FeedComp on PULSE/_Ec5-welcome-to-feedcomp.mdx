Use \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}}'s powerful mobile and web versions to monitor and manage your feed operations whether you're right beside your animal or at your computer! Stay connected and fully control your feed operations no matter where you are! From checking inventory and adjusting feedings to delving into more detailed feeding reports, we've got you covered. Although you can access your data using both versions from any device, here are the recommended uses and main features of the mobile app and web versions:

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Mobile App Version

  Use the [FeedComp App](../mobile/FCM-Home.htm) to easily control all of your feeding tasks while you're walking around the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}! You can complete manual feeding tasks or view simple reports, as well as quickly adjust your ingredients, recipes, or mixing and feeding schedules on-the-fly. You can also check your feed equipment connections, update or keep track of your feed locations, and more. See [Before You Begin: Required Setup](<../../Mobile Apps/FeedComp App/before-you-begin-required-setup.mdx>) to get started, and then check out our [Quick Start Tutorials](<../../Mobile Apps/FeedComp App/quick-start-tutorials.mdx>) to learn more about using the app!

  If you need to adjust feedings while [Editing Restrictions](FCP-Settings-EditingRestrictions.htm) are active, use the mobile app to make changes. See :Link[How does data sync between mobile and web?]{label="How does data sync between mobile and web?" overridedLabel="How does data sync between mobile and web?" spaceId docId="_Ec5VJBYRfErMAWaWiL7o" version="v2" docAnchorId="#sync" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="_Ec5-welcome-to-feedcomp.mdx"} to learn more about syncing your feed data.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Web Version

  Use [FeedComp on PULSE](FCP-Home.htm) to undergo more complex tasks when you're at your computer. You can record and analyze more robust feeding reports to help you make informed decisions about your feed operations. You can also easily manage all of your \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeed}}-related data, edit your user account settings, and more. See [FeedComp on PULSE](FCP-Home.htm) for more information, or see :Link[How does data sync between mobile and web?]{label="How does data sync between mobile and web?" overridedLabel="How does data sync between mobile and web?" spaceId docId="_Ec5VJBYRfErMAWaWiL7o" version="v2" docAnchorId="#sync" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="_Ec5-welcome-to-feedcomp.mdx"} to learn more about syncing your feed data.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## How does feed data sync between mobile and web?

  As long as the app has an active data connection, the app and web versions stay synchronized. If your mobile connection is down, you can continue to feed in offline \{\{mode}}, and the web will update as soon as your data connection is reestablished. To see when your data was last synced, check the top of the mobile app screen, or use the :Link[Refresh option]{label="Refresh option" overridedLabel="Refresh option" spaceId docId="sK8Esbx0pVQzqZ5wDAiMP" version="v2" docAnchorId="#refresh" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../mobile/FCM-UserAccount.htm#refresh" href="../../Mobile Apps/FeedComp App/account-settings.mdx"} to pull down the latest from \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} web. The web version controls all \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} data except for feeding numbers and inventory amounts/usage; the mobile app takes precedence as the source for those details.

  If your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} has set [Editing Restrictions](FCP-Settings-EditingRestrictions.htm), you will not be able to make changes to feedings from web during the restricted times.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## How does data sync between FeedComp and WeighComp?

  If you use \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} and \{\{masterVAS-Products.WC-Platform}}, \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} automatically exchanges data between the two platforms as long as the \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} mobile app has an active connection. This ensures that both \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} and \{\{masterVAS-Products.WC-Platform}} always have the same, accurate inventory data. When you use \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} to manage your feed operations and \{\{masterVAS-Products.WC-Platform}} to manage shipments coming into and leaving your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}, syncing the data between the two platforms also streamlines the operation by reducing manual entry.

  - \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} Data Sync to \{\{masterVAS-Products.WC-Platform}}: \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} sends new or updated ingredient, vendor, and location details to \{\{masterVAS-Products.WC-Platform}}.
  - \{\{masterVAS-Products.WC-Platform}} Data Sync to \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}}: \{\{masterVAS-Products.WC-Platform}} sends new or updated inventory adjustments (including pickup and delivery amounts), costs, \{\{dry matter}}, and vendor details, to \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}}.
</ExpandableHeading>
