  ### Required Setup

  <hint type="warning">

Lactation \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.C-onfiguration-}} settings are also available to \{\{masterVAS-Products.DCU-users-short}} in \{\{masterVAS-Products.OnPremise-lower}} \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC-Platform}} as \{\{Events.1FRESH}}**&#x20;event items**.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Required Permissions

  All users assigned to a role with \{\{RolesPermissions.PermissionNames}} permissions can view this feature.Only users who are assigned a role with \{\{RolesPermissions.PermissionNames}} permissions can edit data or customize settings.

  The ability to edit and customize select fields may also depend on which version of \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} you are using (\{\{masterVAS-Products.DCUnlimited-short}} or \{\{masterVAS-Products.MyDC-short}}). See the topics below to learn more:

  - [Know Your VAS PULSE Version](../../../GetStarted/KnowYourPulse.htm)
  - [Edit Dairy User Permissions](../../../../../features/manage-dairy-users-and-roles/edit-dairy-user-permissions.mdx)

The Lactation \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.C-onfiguration-}} page allows you to control settings and items for new lactations, including resetting items, copying data over as a previous lactation item, or copying details from the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-am}} to her calf. These options automate the data entry required for the new lactation and calf, saving you valuable time and cutting down on typos and errors in the process. You can find this feature under the :Link[DairyComp/MyDC menu]{label="DairyComp/MyDC menu" overridedLabel="DairyComp/MyDC menu" spaceId docId="WFLX7SPMpZfy7zoMyrvRh" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Menu-DairyComp" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../../../NavPlatform/Menu.htm#Menu-DairyComp" href="../../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/navigate-pulse/menu-options.mdx"} > :Link[Settings]{label="Settings" overridedLabel="Settings" spaceId docId="WFLX7SPMpZfy7zoMyrvRh" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Menu-DairyComp" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../../../NavPlatform/Menu.htm#Menu-DairyComp" href="../../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/navigate-pulse/menu-options.mdx"} option on the \{\{Buttons-UI.FeaturesSecondary}} tab.

<hint type="warning">
  **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**&#x49;f your Lactation \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.C-onfiguration-}} is set incorrectly, you may lose important historical lactation data. Use caution when editing these settings to avoid inadvertently clearing the wrong items or copying data to new lactations incorrectly.
</hint>

With the correct permissions,\{\{masterVAS-Products.DCU-users-short}} and \{\{masterVAS-Products.DCP-users-short}} can edit lactation \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.c-onfiguration}} settings, but only \{\{masterVAS-Products.DCU-users-short}} can edit **Items to be Reset** when starting a new lactation, as well as **Items to be copied to a new lactation item** and **Items to be copied from&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-am}}**&#x20;to calf**.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Items to be added together

  This option is intended for advanced users who are capturing and analyzing animal details based on multiple data points.

  For instance, you might add the \{\{age}} at breeding to a body condition score, and push that sum to a custom New Lactation Item.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/94aweXl9yXNKmdlfztSah_settings-lactconfig-addtogether.png" size="42" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/dairycomp/settings-lactconfig-addtogether.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/dairycomp/settings-lactconfig-addtogether.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  You can then use this custom item to build an index that sets benchmarks and points to the optimal age for breeding animals based on their body condition scores.
</ExpandableHeading>

  ## Items to be Reset

  Several items need to be reset when a new lactation begins, including dates (heat date, \{\{due}} date, vet check date, etc.), production data (total milk, total fat, etc.), and \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.s-ire}} details. Select an item from the drop-down list to add it, or click the **X** to remove an item.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/4RHaFkLkRolHvcnTVQDy__settings-lactconfig-itemsreset.png" size="53" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/dairycomp/settings-lactconfig-itemsreset.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/dairycomp/settings-lactconfig-itemsreset.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### What if I see Invalid Items in red?

    \{\{masterVAS-Products.DCU-users-long}} may see **Invalid Items** in your **Items to be Reset** section.

    <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/325lXFzvLS-ChBZ2M0QGZ_settings-lactconfig-invaliditems.png" size="38" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/dairycomp/settings-lactconfig-invaliditems.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/dairycomp/settings-lactconfig-invaliditems.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

    This tells you that items that were previously included as Items to Reset are no longer available in \{\{masterVAS-Products.OnPremise-lower}}\{\{masterVAS-Products.DC-Platform}} and \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}. The reasons for this may vary, but it is likely the item was deleted and is no longer used, or another user may have temporarily removed the item with plans to add it back later.

    Invalid Items do not affect the functionality of resetting items for new lactations. You may opt to click the **X** to remove the items, or leave them as is and they will be returned to the Items to be Reset list as soon as they become available.
  </ExpandableHeading>
</ExpandableHeading>

  ## Items to be copied to a new lactation item

  You'll likely want to save data from the previous lactation when a new lactation begins, including dates and breeding details (times bred, \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.s-ire}}, etc.). \{\{Commands.Click}}**Add Item**, and then choose the **Item** and corresponding **New Lactation Item** to have the data copied to the new lactation.

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### How does the order of items affect new New Lactation items?

    The order of items controls how duplicate New Lactation items are treated. If your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} has New Lactation items used more than once, the last instance of the item becomes the final source for the copied item.
  </ExpandableHeading>
</ExpandableHeading>

  ## Items to be copied from dam to calf

  Calves entering the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} already have multiple data points associated with them based on their \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-am}}. To copy those items to the calf's record, you can add them here.

  For instance, you can add the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-am}}'s \{\{ID}} to copy it to the calf \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-am}} ID (\{\{DID}}), and the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-am}}'s \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.s-ire}} ID (\{\{SID}}) to copy it to the calf maternal \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.g-rands-ire}} (MGSIR). \{\{Commands.Click}}**Add Item** and choose the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-am-}}**&#x20;Item** to copy to a **Calf Item**.
