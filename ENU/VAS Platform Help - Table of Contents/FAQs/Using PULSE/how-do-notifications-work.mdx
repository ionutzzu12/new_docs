The \{\{General.ProductName}} provides **Notifications** to share details on the latest product releases, as well as system messages, general alerts, and notifications. An activity badge :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/O3a0cHEPP0GWkRc0VrWd__notifications-w-badge31x25.png" alt caption size="10"} on this icon at the top of the page indicates the total number of unread messages, and \{\{Commands.click}}ing the  Notifications icon displays links to the most recent messages.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/3B4nRbpot87bY95BNU8oh_nav-main-top-right-notifications-dropdown.png" size="18" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Platform/navigating/nav-main-top-right-notifications-dropdown.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/navigating/nav-main-top-right-notifications-dropdown.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

After new messages are read, the activity badge no longer shows, and messages display in a read (grayed out) format.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/bLsdnVQrfN6pesa52y-jw_nav-main-top-right-notifications-dropdown-read.png" size="25" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Platform/navigating/nav-main-top-right-notifications-dropdown-read.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/navigating/nav-main-top-right-notifications-dropdown-read.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

To access all messages, view the [Message Center](../Features/MessageCenter/MessageCenter.htm), whichThe \{\{General.ProductName}}nowincludes a Message Centerserves to serve as a communication hub and provide an easily-accessible area to view important messages between your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} and from the \{\{General.ProductName}}.

To view all messages, selectSelect **Message Center** from the [User Menu](../Features/Config/UserMenu.htm) in the top right corner of the page.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/k0-O1HtupGpqNDWUXvOit_user-menu-msgcntr.png" size="22" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Platform/users-roles/user-menu-msgcntr.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/users-roles/user-menu-msgcntr.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>
