The company or \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} you are viewing is displayed at the top left of the page. To view a different one, \{\{Commands.click}} the arrow and choose from the list of all your connected companies and \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}}.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/AueCwaRJQNbdogEtLkdtw_choose-dairy-company.png" size="32" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Platform/navigating/choose-dairy-company.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/navigating/choose-dairy-company.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

If no \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}} are available or you don't see the one you're looking for, see [Request Access to a Dairy](<../../Get Started/request-access-to-a-dairy.mdx>).
