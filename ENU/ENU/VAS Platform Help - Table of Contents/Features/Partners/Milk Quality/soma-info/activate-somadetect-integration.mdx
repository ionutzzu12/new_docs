<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Required Permissions

  Only users who are assigned a Role with Integrations Management permissions can connect partner integrations. See [Edit Dairy User Permissions](<../../../../../ENU/VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/features/manage-dairy-users-and-roles/edit-dairy-user-permissions.mdx>) and [Manage Dairy Roles](<../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Manage Dairy Users and Roles/manage-dairy-roles.mdx>) for more information.
</ExpandableHeading>

\{\{Partners.SomaDetect}}'s one-directional integration with \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} allows for bulk \{\{General.CompanyName-short}} animal data, including stall location, \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.t-est-day}} details, and diagnosis events, to be shared with \{\{Partners.SomaDetect}}'s system. Follow these steps to activate the integration with \{\{Partners.SomaDetect}}:

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Step 1: Connect Integration in PULSE

  1. In \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, navigate to **Partners&#x20;**>**&#xA0;Integrations Gallery&#x20;**>**&#xA0;**\{\{Partners.PartnerName}}.
  2. \{\{Commands.Click}}**See Details** or **See Requirements** on the \{\{Partners.PartnerName}}integration card.
  3. Follow the Connection Instructions on the right side of the page to establish the connection:1) Click the **Connection Status** toggle and verify the status switches to **Active**.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/yVoLb7NAnbX1zs-j2jcYx_integration-connected.png" alt="Connection Status: Active" caption size="22"}
     2\) \{\{Commands.Click}}**Save**.
     3\) If necessary, \{\{Commands.click}}**Edit Integration** to make any adjustments or edits and save your changes.

  <hint type="warning">
    **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**&#x49;f your integration does not automatically connect, contact your \{\{Partners.PartnerName}} representative to connect the integration from their system.
  </hint>
</ExpandableHeading>
