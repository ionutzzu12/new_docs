The flow canvas dashboard allows you to visualize data and information for your flows. You can organize the dashboard into tabs and groups, and then add widgets and links to display specific information. You can then make aesthetic choices for the look and feel of your dashboard.

To view the dashboard from the flow canvas, click the **Dashboard&#x20;**&#x69;con.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads.s3.amazonaws.com/SSUUxKZUk9bFTEPNn_6Zo/XCU8haFhKjda2u8MBZ-LR_image.png" size="80" width="1057" height="706" caption="Dashboard icon" position="center" showCaption="true"></Image>

The dashboard tabs allow you to do the following. &#x20;

- **Layout**: Add tabs or links and to launch the [Node-RED](https://nodered.org/) dashboard.&#x20;
- **Site**: Configure [Node-RED](https://nodered.org/) options.
- **Theme**: Configure the style, base color, and font.&#x20;
- **Angular**: Configure colors.&#x20;
