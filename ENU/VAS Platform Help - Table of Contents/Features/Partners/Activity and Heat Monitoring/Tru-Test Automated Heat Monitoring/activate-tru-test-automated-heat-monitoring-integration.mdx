  ### Required Permissions

  Only users who are assigned a Role with Integrations Management permissions can connect partner integrations. See [Edit Dairy User Permissions](../../Platform/Features/Config/UserMgmtRoles/EditUserPermissions.htm) and [Manage Dairy Roles](<../../../Manage Dairy Users and Roles/manage-dairy-roles.mdx>) for more information.
</ExpandableHeading>

Follow the steps below to connect \{\{Partners.Datamars\_Tru-Test}} integration in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}:

1. In \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, navigate to **Partners&#x20;**>**&#xA0;Integrations Gallery&#x20;**>**&#xA0;**\{\{Partners.PartnerName}}.
2. \{\{Commands.Click}}**See Details** or **See Requirements** on the \{\{Partners.PartnerName}}integration card.
3. Follow the Connection Instructions on the right side of the page to establish the connection:1) \{\{Commands.Click}}**Select&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.P-ens-}} to choose \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}} that use this product. Enter the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} numbers and select the correct \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}} from the results list, which lists the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}} in ascending order.
   2\) Click the **Connection Status** toggle and verify the status switches to **Active**.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/yVoLb7NAnbX1zs-j2jcYx_integration-connected.png" alt="Connection Status: Active" caption size="22"}
   3\) \{\{Commands.Click}}**Save**.
   4\) If necessary, \{\{Commands.click}}**Edit Integration** to make any adjustments or edits and save your changes.

<hint type="warning">
  **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**&#x49;f your integration does not automatically connect, contact your \{\{Partners.PartnerName}} representative to connect the integration from their system.
</hint>
