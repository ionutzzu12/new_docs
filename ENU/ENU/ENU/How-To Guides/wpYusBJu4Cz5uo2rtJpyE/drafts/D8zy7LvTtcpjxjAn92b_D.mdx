You can inject the data being calculated to the **DataHub Publish&#x20;**&#x6E;ode. The data is sent to the topic defined in DataHub.

**To publish data to a Topic:**

1. From the navigation panel, select **Flows Manager**.
2. Click the **Expand&#xA0;**&#x69;con next to a selected Flow.
3. Click the **Add** icon.
4. Drag the **Inject** and **Data Publish** nodes to the canvas and wire them together.
5. Double-click the **DataHub Publish Node**.
6. Return to the **Flows pane&#xA0;**&#x62;rowser tab.
7. Navigate to **DeviceHub > Tags**.
8. Click the Copy icon next to a Raw Topic.
9. Return to the **DataHub Publish&#xA0;**&#x62;rowser tab.
10. From the **DataHub Publish&#xA0;**&#x6E;ode dialog box, do the following:
    1. Paste or type the topic name in the Topic field.&#x20;
    2. Enter the name for the node in the Name field, and then click Done.&#x20;
    3. Click **Save**.
11. Click the **Timestamp&#x20;**&#x62;utton.
    The timestamp is injected into the Datahub Publish node and the messages show the timestamp.
