<hint type="warning">
  **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**

  If you use

  \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.radiofrequencyID-short}}

  scanners, make sure your

  \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.AppleOS-TM}}

  device is compatible with your version of the mobile app: 

  - \{\{masterVAS-Products.VASPlatformMobile}}\:Variable\{key="Mobile-UI.MobileAppVersion1" type="variable"}: iOS versions 18.3.2 and lower. Do **not** upgrade to \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.AppleOS-TM}} versions 18.4.0 or greater and be sure to disable automatic updates in \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.AppleOS-TM}} settings.
  - \{\{masterVAS-Products.DConMobile}}\{\{Mobile-UI.MobileAppVersion2}}: All recent iOS versions, including 18.4.0 and above.
</hint>

The first time you connect a scanner to an \{\{MorphHelp.AppleOS-TM}} device, go to your Bluetooth settings to connect it. After the first connection, your device will recognize the scanner and automatically pair and connect (as long as Bluetooth is enabled).

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/bisJSjXi9NiJOOTJ-dxu-_bluetooth-connection-image-ios.jpg" size="56" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/mobile-app/bluetooth-connection-image-IOS.jpg" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/mobile-app/bluetooth-connection-image-IOS.jpg" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

<hint type="info">
  **TIP:&#x20;**&#x49;f you turn on **Airplane Mode** on your \{\{MorphHelp.AppleOS-TM}} device, Bluetooth is turned off automatically. You can turn Bluetooth back on in settings, and it will stay on until you turn it off or toggle Airplane Mode off and on again.
</hint>

Once the scanner is paired with your device, it can scan animals in the mobile app. Tap the **More** button to check your scanner's connection status.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/M9DondHg-ZHNtuW7GILB6_rfid-options-ios.png" size="46" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/mobile-app/rfid-options-ios.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/mobile-app/rfid-options-ios.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  ## I don't see the More button. How do I find it?

  :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/oKazFYT_2nVAqkeaJfHEH_back-button.png" alt caption size="19"}:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/1ZxRnXQSdCm_IDaw6O5wy_home-buttons.png" alt caption size="16"}

  Follow the steps below if you do not see the \{\{Mobile-UI.Feature-Names}} button at the bottom of your screen.

  1. Tap the **Back** arrow at the top left of the screen until you return to one of the main feature screens.
  2. From there, you'll find the feature buttons at the bottom of the screen.
</ExpandableHeading>
