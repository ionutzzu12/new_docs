  ### Required Setup

  - This feature is only available when you’re using this device for feeding. If you change your mind about using a device for feeding, :Link[log out of the app]{label="log out of the app" overridedLabel="log out of the app" spaceId docId="sK8Esbx0pVQzqZ5wDAiMP" version="v2" docAnchorId="#logout" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="FCM-UserAccount.htm#logout" href="../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Mobile Apps/FeedComp App/account-settings.mdx"} and select **Yes** or **No** when asked if the device will be used for feeding.
  - Before you can begin using \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} to feed at your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}, you must complete the required setup. See [Before You Begin: Required Setup](<../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Mobile Apps/FeedComp App/before-you-begin-required-setup.mdx>) to learn more about the required setup order.
  - Devices used for feeding must be connected to a scale. See [Scales](<../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Mobile Apps/FeedComp App/scales.mdx>) to learn more about connecting to your scale.
</ExpandableHeading>

Tap \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeed}} at the bottom of the screen, and then tap \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompBatchFeeding}} to record an unscheduled feed drop to one or more \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}}.

  ## Skip  or Ignore  during Batch Feedings

  \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} allows you to skip or ignore ingredients when loading them into a mixer, and you can also skip or ignore \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}} when dropping the mix when feeding.

  When you're loading ingredients for a mix and dropping the mix at a \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}}, the following options are available:

  - Tap **Ignore**
