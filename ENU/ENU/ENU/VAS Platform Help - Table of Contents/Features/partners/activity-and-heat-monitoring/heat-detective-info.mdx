[UHF Heat Detective by InterPulsUHF Heat Detective by InterPuls Cloud](https://www.milkrite-interpuls.com/products-EN-US.htm) animal sensors monitor reproduction, rumination, eating, and movements to provide deeper insights on heat detection, overall health, and animals requiring urgent attention. \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} displays live data from animal sensors in a \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} dashboard widgetand individual animal CowCardson the \{\{Partners.PartnersUIoption}} tab, providing an up-to-date view of animals requiring health checks or reproduction-related protocols.
