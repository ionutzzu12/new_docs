[Alpha Dairy Consulting](https://alphadairyconsulting.com/) provides holistic nutrition consulting for \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} cattle, with an understanding that the implementation of change requires time and attention to detail. When you connect the integration from the \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}[Integrations Gallery](<../../../../ENU/VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/partners/integrations-gallery.mdx>), your raw herd data is sent to \{\{Partners.PartnerName}}. This is a one-way data share that you can turn on and off with a single click. See [General Integration Instructions](../../Platform/Features/Config/Integrations/GeneralIntegrationInstructions.htm) to learn more about connecting the intergration!
