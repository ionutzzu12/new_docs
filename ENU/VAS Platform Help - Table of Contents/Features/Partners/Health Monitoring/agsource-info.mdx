[AgSource KetoMonitor](https://vas.com/keto-monitor/) is a comprehensive management tool that combines laboratory analysis and individual cow data, collected weekly or on the monthly \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.t-est-day}}, to provide producers with an estimate of a \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}’s monthly \{\{ketosis}} prevalence, and identifying when blood testing or therapeutic treatments should be implemented. This cost-effective management tool will help curb the costly impacts of ketosis.

The \{\{Partners.Keto}} widget allows you to display ketosis test data from your \{\{Partners.PartnerName}} integration directly in your \{\{Buttons-UI.Dashboard}} in the \{\{General.ProductName}}.
