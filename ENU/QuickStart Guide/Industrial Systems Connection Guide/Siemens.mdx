<hint type="info">
  **Note**: You should always start with the Siemens S7 driver in lieu of the model-specific drivers, such as S7-300 or S7-400. The Siemens S7 driver is faster and supports more data types.
</hint>

Refer to the following table of compatible DeviceHub drivers for your PLC series.

<hint type="warning">
  **Important**: [Siemens S7-300 (Gen1)](<Siemens/Gen 1 Drivers/Siemens S7-300 _Gen1_.mdx>) and [Siemens S7-400 (Gen1)](<Siemens/Gen 1 Drivers/Siemens S7-400 _Gen1_.mdx>) is no longer available as of Litmus Edge version 3.11.0. We recommend using [Siemens S7](<Siemens/Gen 2 Drivers/Siemens S7.mdx>).
</hint>
