\{\{General.ProductName}}\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}}s provide a single, highly customizable location for you to monitor your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}. With the ability to view an animal's \{\{CowCards-UI.CC-tab-names}}, \{\{CowCards-UI.CC-tab-names}}, \{\{CowCards-UI.CC-tab-names}}, and \{\{CowCards-UI.CC-tab-names}}, as well as switch easily between data view and graph view, any \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} member can find what they need, whether it's an animal's current status and \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} location or its recent milk weights and lactation cycles:

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/V4DYSaiOZq-8bn56QLzWJ_cowcard-overview.png" size="93" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/cowcards/cowcard-overview.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/cowcards/cowcard-overview.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

When your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} is first set up on \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, each individual animal receives its own \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}}. You can then customize these \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}}s to better support on‑the‑go use while out on the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}. For \{\{masterVAS-Products.DCU-users-long}}, your \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}}s are built directly from your \{\{DC-UI.DC-ProdNameShort}} data, and it remains synchronized when you make changes \{\{masterVAS-Products.OnPremise-lower}} or in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.

See [Animals on Mobile (v1)](<../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Mobile Apps/VAS Platform Mobile App (v1)/animals-on-mobile-v1.mdx>) or [Manage Animals (v2)](../../mobile-apps/dc-on-mobile-v2/manage-animals-v2.mdx) for information on viewing \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}}s in the mobile app.
