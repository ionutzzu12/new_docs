The AI Models page allows you to connect and manage [Generative AI ](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Generative_artificial_intelligence)model integrations within the Analytics platform. This feature enables engineering teams to use their preferred [Large Language Models (LLMs)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Large_language_model) without compromising on ease of use.&#x20;

# Before You Begin

Ensure that you meet the following requirements:&#x20;

- Valid API keys and tokens for the selected AI model provider to authenticate a stable connection.
- Appropriate permissions to access and modify AI model configurations within the Analytics platform.&#x20;

<hint type="info">
  **Important:&#x20;**&#x57;hile multiple providers are available (OpenAI, Cloudflare AI Gateway, OpenAI Assistants  , Grok 3  , Ollama, Google Gemini, Nvidia, and Anthropic), only specific options may be supported with this release.&#x20;
</hint>

# Access AI Models UI

**To access AI Models:**

1. Log in to Litmus Edge.
2. From the Navigation panel, navigate to **Analytics&#x20;**> **Models**.

<Image src="https://api.archbee.com/api/optimize/SSUUxKZUk9bFTEPNn_6Zo/xR3owZKIg0TKlNrqG4Ylx_image.png" size="80" width="1854" height="652" position="flex-start" indent="2" showCaption="false"></Image>

Refer to the actions you can take from the AI Model's page:

| **Action**        | **Details**                                                                                   |
| ----------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| \[1] **Name**     | The label used to identify the specific AI model within the system.                           |
| \[2] **Provider** | The organization or service that offers the AI model, such as OpenAI or Google Gemini.        |
| \[3] **URL**      | The web address used to access the AI model's endpoint for integration.                       |
| \[4] **Model ID** | A unique identifier for the AI model, which distinguishes it from other models in the system. |
| \[5] **Edit**     | - Edit                                                                                        |

- Delete
  See [Modify Connection](<AI Models/Modify Connection.mdx>) for details. |
  \| \[6] **Add Connection** | See [Add Connection](<AI Models/Add Connection.mdx>) for details.                            |
