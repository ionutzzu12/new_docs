A variety of \{\{Partners.ActivityMonitoring}} sensors are available to track behaviors, such as activity, rumination, eating, and inactivity. The resulting data can provide insights on heat detection, overall health, and animals requiring attention.

\{\{masterVAS.CompanyName-short}} captures sensor data as \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} items (and \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-simple}} items for \{\{General.DCUnlimited}} dairies) to support reporting and analysis tasks. Heat and health alerts are also automatically sent to your daily \{\{Buttons-UI.FeaturesSecondary}} for \{\{masterVAS-Products.MyDC-short}}\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}} for actionable results related to health checks or reproduction-related protocols. Start by connecting your integration, [Activate Activity Monitoring Integration](activity-integrate.htm), and then see [Activity Monitoring Reports/Worklists](activity-reports-worklists.htm) to learn more about reporting.
