When \{\{Partners.Zoetis}} returns the samples, the system shows the genomic results in the **Genomics**tab in your animal's \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}}. The data is shown in two widgets in the **Genomics** tab: the **Genomic Results** widget, which shows Council for \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}} Cattle Breeding (\{\{CDCB}}) standard traits, and the **Zoetis Genomic Results** widget, which shows special genomic traits. Different traits are listed depending upon the tests you selected when you [submitted the samples](submit-zoetis-genomics-samples.mdx).
