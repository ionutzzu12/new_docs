The \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}\{\{Buttons-UI.FeaturesSecondary}} tool allows you to view your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} data in reports, as well as create \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}s to manage daily activities at your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}. You can create your own custom reports and \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}s or create them from the many templates provided in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.

The \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}} \{\{Buttons-UI.DC-Reports}} page includes the reports or \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}s you customized from the [Template Gallery](../Features/DairyComp/ReportsWorklists/DC-ReportsGallery.htm), as well as any other custom reports you created using [Report/Worklist Builder](../Features/DairyComp/ReportsWorklists/ReportWorklistBuilder.htm). See [Dairy Reports/Worklists](../Features/DairyComp/ReportsWorklists/ManageReportsWorklists.htm) for more information.
