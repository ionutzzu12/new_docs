After you submit samples for genetic testing to \{\{Partners.Zoetis}} in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, you can view your submitted samples in **Partners** > **Zoetis** > **Submitted Samples**.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/QR6fx1rmQuujjWArK7_QS_zoetis-submitted-samples.png" size="58" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Partners/zoetis/zoetis-submitted-samples.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Partners/zoetis/zoetis-submitted-samples.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

Follow the guidelines below when working with your submitted samples:

- **# Evaluated:** Number of samples that have received genomic results from \{\{Partners.Zoetis}} after testing.
- **# Submitted:** Number of samples that have been submitted to \{\{Partners.Zoetis}}.
- **% Evaluated:** Percentage of samples that have received genomic results from \{\{Partners.Zoetis}} after testing.
- Click **See Submitted Samples** to see the samples and the test selections you submitted.
- Click **+ Submit Samples** at the top right to [submit more samples for testing](submit-zoetis-genomics-samples.mdx).

After \{\{Partners.Zoetis}} returns the genomic results, you can view the data in your animals' \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}}s in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}. See [View Zoetis Genomics Results ](view-zoetis-genomics-results.mdx)to learn more.
