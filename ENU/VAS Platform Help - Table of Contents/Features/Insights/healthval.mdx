The \{\{masterVAS.CompanyName-short}}\{\{Insights.HealthSummaries}}\{\{Insights.Insights}} provides visibility into the diseases affecting your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} and allows you to compare your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} health events to customized benchmarking categories, including local \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}} and \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}} of similar size. At a glance, you can assess your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}'s events and gather insights into the best areas to focus on for improvement.

By tracking and examining health events for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}, you can implement new strategies and protocols for avoiding or treating specific illnesses. Over time, you can lower treatment and production loss costs, and have fewer milk and beef withholds. You can find this feature under the :Link[Insights menu]{label="Insights menu" overridedLabel="Insights menu" spaceId docId="WFLX7SPMpZfy7zoMyrvRh" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Menu-Insights" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../../../NavPlatform/Menu.htm#Menu-Insights" href="../../../NavPlatform/Menu.htm#Menu-Insights"}.

First, [Customize HealthVAL](CustomizeHealthVAL.htm) to select which events to include and set your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}'s profile for benchmarking purposes. Then, [View HealthVAL Reports](HealthVAL/view-healthval-reports.mdx) and begin your analysis!
