The **Change Log** tab on the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}} displays a history of the updates made to an animal from \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}. You can use the **Filter** fields above the table to only see changes that match your criteria.

From the Changes table, you can determine:

- The date and time a change was made
- Who made the change
- The type of change (see table above)
- The type of entry that was changed (Item, Event, etc.)
- A brief description of the change
- Whether the change is still in progress or has already been processed

Click on any row in the table to display the change details in a side panel. Within the side panel, you can click the **View&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}} link to jump to the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}} page associated with the change.
