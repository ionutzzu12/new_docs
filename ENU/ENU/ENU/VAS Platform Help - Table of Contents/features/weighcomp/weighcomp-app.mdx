  ### App Minimum Requirements

  Hardware and software requirements for installing and running \{\{MorphHelp.WeighComp}} can be found on the [VAS website](https://vas.com/technology-specifications/), along with information regarding recommended devices.
</ExpandableHeading>

At this time, the \{\{MorphHelp.WeighComp}} app can only be used on the RuggON-PA-501 Android-based tablet. Once you have your tablet, follow the instructions below to download and set up the app:

<hint type="info">
  **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x41; Wi-Fi, Ethernet, or cellular connection for the tablet is required in order to complete the steps below.
</hint>

1. Add your Google Account in the tablet's Settings.
2. Download the \{\{MorphHelp.WeighComp}} app from the Google Play Store.
3. Once the app has downloaded, enter your \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} login credentials into the app.You only need to login once. After the login has completed successfully, the app will sync with \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} and be authenticated for use.

<hint type="info">
  **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x49;n order for the app to sync and authenticate with \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, a \{\{MorphHelp.WeighComp}} \{\{zone}} integration must be set up. This should have occurred shortly after your purchase of \{\{MorphHelp.WeighComp}}, but if you have trouble with step 3 (above) please check if this zone integration has been set up or contact customer support.
</hint>

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/J9T9Dq-CfoZl1Fes94XvY_ruggon.png" size="49" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/WeighComp/Ruggon.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/WeighComp/Ruggon.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

Once the you have downloaded the app, you can start using it to capture \{\{MorphHelp.WeighComp}} data for spot load transactions or harvests. Proceed to one of the following sections to learn how:

- [Transaction Management](../Platform/WC-TransManage.htm)
- [Harvest Management](harvest-management.mdx)
