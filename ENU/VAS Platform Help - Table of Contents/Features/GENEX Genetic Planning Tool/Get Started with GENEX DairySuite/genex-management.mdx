On the \{\{UDS-general.UDSBrand}}**&#x20;Management** page, you can view your Dairies and Bull list, as well as edit your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}'s currency, manually add bulls, and more. Access the \{\{UDS-general.UDSBrand}} Management tools for \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}'s \{\{UDS-general.CommonProductName}} by selecting \{\{UDS-general.UDSBrand}}**&#x20;Management** from your user menu at the top right of the page. If you have more permissions, you may see additional tools.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Roles and Permissions

  All users can view and use the \{\{UDS-general.UDSBrand}}**&#x20;Management** features in the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airies-}} and **Bull List** tabs. Some roles may see additional tools. If you have questions about your role or permissions, :Link[contact your GENEX admin]{label="contact your GENEX admin" overridedLabel="contact your GENEX admin" spaceId docId="Kk0UeFPEsM0CshK-aRILO" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Contact" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="genex-management.mdx"}.

  The list includes each available role for the \{\{UDS-general.CommonProductName}} in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.

  - \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.C-onsultant-}}: This role allows you to access the genetic planning tool for your assigned dairies.
  - Manager: This role allows you to access the genetic planning tool for your assigned dairies and the dairies assigned to the consultants you manage.
  - Administrator: This role allows you to access the genetic planning tool for all dairies, as well as add or remove \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.c-onsultants}} and assign roles.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## View Dairies

  Use the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airies-}} tab to view all \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}} linked to your account.

  - Filters: You can filter the list by \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.c-onsultant}} to narrow the results or search for a specific \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}.
  - \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airies-}} List: Each entry includes the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} name, \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} code, a list of \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.c-onsultants}} associated with the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}, and the currency used, :Link[which you can update if needed]{label="which you can update if needed" overridedLabel="which you can update if needed" spaceId docId="Kk0UeFPEsM0CshK-aRILO" version="v2" docAnchorId="#currency" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="genex-management.mdx"}.
  - \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}} Details: Click on any \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}'s name to open the [Dairy Profile page](../../../Platform/Features/Dairy/DairyHome/DairyProfile.htm) to see more information about the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## View Bull List

  Open the **Bull List** tab to view bulls that have been manually added in the last 7 days.

  - Filters: By default, the list only shows bulls added in the last 7 days. Clear or adjust the filters to view all bulls from proofs and bulls that have been manually added.
  - Bull List: Each entry includes the bull's registration information, parentage, and the country where the bull information originated from. If the bull was added manually, it also includes the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.c-onsultant}} that added the bull.
  - Bull Details: To view a bull's details, click its **IntRegNum** to open the animal's information in a side panel and click **See More Info** at the bottom right corner of the side panel.
  - Add Bull: You can manually add a bull if the \{\{UDS-general.CommonProductName}} cannot recognize or find it. Click **Add Bull** in the upper right corner and enter the bull's information. See :Link[Manually Add A Bull]{label="Manually Add A Bull" overridedLabel="Manually Add A Bull" spaceId docId="Kk0UeFPEsM0CshK-aRILO" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Bulls" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="genex-management.mdx"} to learn more.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Manually Add a Bull

  If you have the correct role and permissions, you can add bulls that the \{\{UDS-general.CommonProductName}} cannot recognize or find:

  <WorkflowBlock>
    <WorkflowBlockItem>
      In \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, click on your user name in the upper right corner, then select \{\{UDS-general.UDSBrand}}**&#x20;Management** from the drop-down list to open the \{\{UDS-general.UDSBrand}}**&#x20;Management** page.
    </WorkflowBlockItem>

    <WorkflowBlockItem>
      Open the **Bull List** tab, then click **Add Bull** in the upper right corner.
    </WorkflowBlockItem>

    <WorkflowBlockItem>
      Enter the bull's **Sire Information**, then select **Continue**. The following fields are required:\* **IntRegNum:** Animal's identification number. An IntRegNum must consist of 18 characters in this order: a 2-digit breed code, a 3-digit country code, a 1-digit gender code, followed by an identification number. For example, `HO840M000123456789` represents a Holstein (`HO`) from the United States (`840`) that is male (`M`) with an identification number of `000123456789`.

      - **NAAB Code(s):** Animal's identification code assigned by the National Association of Animal Breeders (\{\{NAAB}}). \{\{UDS-general.UDSBrand}}\{\{UDS-general.UDSProduct}} can support up to 6 NAAB codes for a single animal.
      - **Sire:** Animal's sire.
      - **Breed:** Animal's breed. If you don't select a dam, you must select a breed from the drop-down list.
    </WorkflowBlockItem>

    <WorkflowBlockItem>
      Enter the bull's **Genetic Values** information, then select **Continue**. The genetic values are automatically populated based on the animal's parent averages, but you can edit the traits to be more accurate. Use the USA scale to configure trait values in this step.
    </WorkflowBlockItem>

    <WorkflowBlockItem>
      <ExpandableHeading>
        ### Required Fields

        - Milk (lbs)
        - Protein (lbs)
        - Fat (lbs)
        - NM$: Net Merit Index
        - CM$: Cheese Merit Index
        - FM$: Fluid Merit Index
        - Productive Life
        - Daughter Pregnancy Rate
        - Somatic Cell Score
        - Sire Calving \{\{Ease}}
        - Daughter Calving Ease
        - Sire Still Birth
        - Daughter Still Birth
        - Cow Conception Rate
        - Heifer Conception Rate
        - Livability
        - DWP$: Dairy Wellness Profit
      </ExpandableHeading>

      Enter the bull's **Genes & Haplotype** information about its recessive genes and haplotypes, then select **Continue**. Although this information isn't required, adding any known information helps the \{\{UDS-general.CommonProductName}} eliminate poor matches that could promote recessive genes and haplotypes.
    </WorkflowBlockItem>

    <WorkflowBlockItem>
      Review the bull's summary, then click **Confirm** at the bottom right side of the page to add the bull to your Bulls List.
    </WorkflowBlockItem>
  </WorkflowBlock>
</ExpandableHeading>
