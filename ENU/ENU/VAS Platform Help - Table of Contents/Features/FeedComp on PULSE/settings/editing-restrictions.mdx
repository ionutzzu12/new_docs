\{\{Buttons-UI.FeedCompMenu}} allows you to limit when users can edit feed settings from web, so you can restrict editing whenever feedings are in progress. This helps to ensure that your staff, including nutritionists or other outside consultants, do not make changes that impact your active feedings, including recipes, inventory, or schedules.

Expand the sections below to learn more:

1. View Editing Restrictions: View \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompEditingRestrictions}} to see any scheduled blocked times in a calendar view.
2. Add a Blocked Time::Variable\{key="Commands.Click" type="variable"}\{\{Commands.UserActions}} \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Upper}} at the top right of the page to open the side panel and \{\{Commands.UserActions}} information for the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}.
3. Edit or Delete a Blocked Time::Variable\{key="Commands.Click" type="variable"} the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} to \{\{Commands.UserActions}} or delete from the calendar view to open the **Edit blocked time** side panel. From here, you can change the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} details or click the delete button
