If you do not use a \{\{masterVAS.CompanyName-short}} herd management tool, you can still access \{\{UDS-general.GENEX-Pulse}} to gain valuable animal insights and use advanced genetic planning tools to make informed decisions that guide your herd toward its genetic goals:

- If your herd has been migrated from \{\{UDS-general.GENEX-Pulse}}, your data has already been imported into the genetic planning tool and you can proceed to [Create a Genetic Plan](../BuildGeneticPlan/BuildGeneticPlan-intro.htm).
- If your herd is brand new, :Link[contact your GENEX DairySuite admin]{label="contact your GENEX DairySuite admin" overridedLabel="contact your GENEX DairySuite admin" spaceId docId="Kk0UeFPEsM0CshK-aRILO" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Contact" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="UserMgmt.htm#Contact" href="../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/GENEX Genetic Planning Tool/Get Started with GENEX DairySuite/genex-management.mdx"} to create a third-party \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} and follow the steps below to import \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} data so you can use it to start building genetic plans:

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Add Animal

  Follow the steps below to add a single animal from your third-party \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} management system:

  1. Select \{\{UDS-general.GENEX-Pulse}} > **Animals** > **Add Animal** from the main menu bar.
  2. Select whether the animal is a **Cow** or a **Heifer**.
  3. Enter the animal's ID and breed. Enter as much animal information as you can. \{\{UDS-general.GENEX-Pulse}} yields more accurate results when you add more information. Animal ID is required to proceed, but all other fields are essential for creating a quality genetic plan.
  4. Enter the animal's phenotype data (physical traits). Each value must be a whole number between 1 and 10.
  5. Review the animal's summary, then click **Add** save your changes.
</ExpandableHeading>
