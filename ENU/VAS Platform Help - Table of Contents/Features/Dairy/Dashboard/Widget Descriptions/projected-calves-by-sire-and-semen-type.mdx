The \{\{Widgets.ProjectedCalves}}\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} widget identifies the expected number of calves to be born in upcoming months, including data for 7 to 8 months. This information helps to plan for Replacement Heifers that will be added to your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} by month. It also allows you to analyze parentage information for your calves.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/n5pPoHYWxaGWUBURCmVYW_proj-calves-widget.png" size="45" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/dashboard/proj-calves-widget.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/dashboard/proj-calves-widget.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

Reference the following to understand the data in this widget.

Semen Type: Identifies the type of semen used to conceive the calf. Available types are:

- **Sexed**&#xNAN;**&#xA0;(S)**&#xNAN;**:&#x20;**&#x53;emen that has been analyzed and sorted based on the X/Y chromosome in the individual sperm cells. For \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} cows, this type of semen has mostly X-chromosome sperm cells to increase the likelihood of producing heifer calves.
- **Unsexed**&#xNAN;**&#x20;(U)**&#xNAN;**:&#x20;**&#x53;emen that has not been sorted based on the X/Y chromosome in the sperm cells.
- **Unknown**&#xNAN;**&#x20;(?)**&#xNAN;**:&#x20;**&#x53;emen for which the bull \{\{NAAB}} code was not specified.
- **Beef**&#xNAN;**&#x20;(B)**&#xNAN;**:** Semen produced by a beef bull. Beef semen is a lower-cost option and may provide additional advantages, including improved conception rates and lower calf birth weight for calving \{\{ease}}. Heifer and bull calves with a beef \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.s-ire}} are designated for beef production.
