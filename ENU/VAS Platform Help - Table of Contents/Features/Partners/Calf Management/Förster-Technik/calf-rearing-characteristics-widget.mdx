<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Required Setup

  For the \{\{Partners.PartnersUIoption}} tab to display \{\{Partners.Forster}} data, your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} must first activate the integration. See [Activate Förster-Technik Integration](forster-integrate.htm) to learn more.
</ExpandableHeading>

The \{\{Widgets.ForsterCalf}} widget includes important calf data such as birth date, projected \{\{wean}} date, parentage, weight, and feeding details. The widget also includes an option to download the calf's feeding plan as a PDF.

View the example below for more information on the data shown in the widget.

3. Units: Choose the units in which to display data\:Units: Choose the units in which to display data:\* Metric: Display data in Kilograms and Liters.
   - Imperial: Display data in Pounds and Gallons.:::hint\{type="info"}
     **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x54;his setting is independent of the Units selected in your [Account Settings](../../Platform/Features/Config/EditUserAcct.htm) and applies to all users in your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}.
     \:::
4. Calf Weight data. If your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} is equipped with a \{\{Partners.Forster}} half-body scale, this is an actual weight. If not, this value is an estimate based on calf age.\* 4 days: Calf weight at 4 days old
   - \{\{Today}}: Current calf weight
   - ADG: Average daily weight gain
5. Milk Consumption data:\* Total Consumption: MR (milk \{\{replacer}} consumed)
   - Milk: Volume of milk consumed
   - Concentration: Total weight of concentrate consumed
   - Additive: Total weight of additive(s) consumed
6. Allowance data:\* Allowance Ratio: Milk consumed/allowance, in weight and as a percentage
   - \# Dips Milk Intake: Total number of periods during which the calf milk consumption is decreased
   - Avg Breaks/Day: Average number of times per day the calf feeding is interrupted
   - Days on Prescription: Number of days on a prescription and the number of days as a percentage of the feeding plan period. For instance, if a calf has been on a prescription for 5 days of 28 days on a feeding plan, the Days on Prescription is 5 (17.86%).
