## Milk Information

The Milk Information settings display by default. View or enter milk details as follows:

1. IOFC \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompReport-singular}}: Click the link to view the current [Income Over Feed Cost Report](<../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Insights/income-over-feed-cost-report.mdx>).
2. Milk Price: Enter the milk price for a specific timeframe. Milk prices can fluctuate depending on several factors, including the composition of your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}'s bulk milk and market demand. In the US, milk prices are based on CWT (hundredweight), or 100 pounds of milk.:::hint\{type="info"}
   **TIP:&#x20;**&#x46;or the most up-to-date \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompIOFC-short}} value, enter your latest pricing before running the [Income Over Feed Cost Report](<../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Insights/income-over-feed-cost-report.mdx>). \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} will update the price for the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompIOFC-short}} calculation in the next data refresh, which occurs every 24 hours.
   To view the current milk price being used for the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompIOFC-short}} calculation, hover over the info icon next to the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompIOFC-short}} value:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/LfOQCXuzCXjYeddZv3glz_blue-i30x30.png" alt caption size="10"}::inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/mK9zib6Y7qXYjwx6J-70V_fcp-reports-iofc-hover-tip.png" alt caption size="29"}

<ExpandableHeading indent="2">
  ### Click here to learn more about milk components and pricing!

  Milk prices are based on the component levels in your bulk milk, with higher levels generally leading to a higher price. Milk component testing identifies the levels of butterfat, protein, and other solids, and pricing for each component is based on the current market price of butter, nonfat dry milk, cheese, and dry whey.

  See :Link[Components]{label="Components" overridedLabel="Components" spaceId docId="Uu_WXrFMWgGN4yxjTcegV" version="v2" docAnchorId="#components" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../DairyComp/Testdays/DC-TestDays.htm#components" href="../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/DairyComp/MyDC/testdays.mdx"} to learn more about \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.t-est-day}} data in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}!
  \:::
</ExpandableHeading>

3. Milk Weight: Enter the total milk weight produced by the herd for a specific timeframe. If necessary, this value can be used to calculate \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompIOFC-short}}.:::hint\{type="info"}
   **TIP:&#x20;**&#x46;or the most accurate \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompIOFC-short}} value, use your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}'s daily milk values or \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.t-est-day}} data as the basis for the [Income Over Feed Cost Report](<../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Insights/income-over-feed-cost-report.mdx>).
   \:::
4. View History: Click this option to view past price or weight details in a side panel.
5. Estimated Data: For any dates missing price or weight data, \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} estimates values based on previous records.
