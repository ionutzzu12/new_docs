Use the **Historic Trends** dashboard to view inventory history for all \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}} from the past year. Use :Link[one of the many provided metrics]{label="one of the many provided metrics" overridedLabel="one of the many provided metrics" spaceId docId="WHKYfUXtzyCPzzxoC1W6U" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Metrics" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="historic-trends.mdx"} to break down the inventory history further to learn more about your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}}. For example, you can check metrics for specific health events, such as Total \{\{Mastitis}} Incidences, to see whether cases of mastitis have increased or declined in the past year.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/F7NTLu9RWCh23jCc5aHkH_historictrends.png" size="65" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/oneview/historictrends.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/oneview/historictrends.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

Follow the guidelines below when viewing the Inventory dashboard:

<WorkflowBlock>
  <WorkflowBlockItem>
    **Filters:** Click the **Filters** option on the upper left side of the dashboard to open a side panel where you can manage the data you see in the graph:
  </WorkflowBlockItem>

  <WorkflowBlockItem>
    **Facility filters:&#x20;**&#x55;se the **Facility Type** and/or **Facilities** filters to manage the facilities shown in the graph. Facilities are listed by \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} name. By default, the graph includes data from all \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}}.
  </WorkflowBlockItem>

  <WorkflowBlockItem>
    **Date Range filter:&#x20;**&#x55;se the **Date&#x20;****From** and **Date&#x20;****To** filters to only view data from that range. By default, the graph includes data from the previous seven days.
  </WorkflowBlockItem>

  <WorkflowBlockItem>
    **Metric filter:** Select the specific metric you want to view. There are many metrics to choose from by animal type (female animals, bulls, cows, heifers), \{\{age}}, lactation number, health event, \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} change, and breed. For example, you can view the number of bulls, the number of \{\{metritis}} cases, or the number of Holsteins you've had across all \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}} in the past month.
  </WorkflowBlockItem>
