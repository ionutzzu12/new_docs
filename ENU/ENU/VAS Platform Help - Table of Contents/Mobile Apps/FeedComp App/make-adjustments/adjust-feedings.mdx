\{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} allows you to create, adjust, and maintain the feedings included in your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}’s feeding schedule. To follow your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}'s current feeding schedule and feed your animals, see [Feed Using the Feeding Schedule](<../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Mobile Apps/FeedComp App/Feed/feed-using-the-feeding-schedule.mdx>).

All of your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}’s current feedings are listed on the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Upper}}\{\{General.UI-gui}}. The feeder will generally follow the order displayed, moving left to right and top to bottom. See the topics below for help adjusting feedings.

  ## Reorder the Feeding Schedule

  All of your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}’s current feedings are listed on the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Upper}}\{\{General.UI-gui}}. The feeder will generally follow the order displayed, moving left to right and top to bottom. Follow the steps below to change the feeding order:

  1. \{\{Commands.UserActions}}\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Upper}}.
  2. Tap and hold on a feeding and drop it into a new order. Or, tap **Move to** on a feeding, tap or specify the new order, and then tap ` Move`.

  4. Tap `Save` to confirm the new feeding order.

  ### FeedComp Rules for Handling Splits

  - Split feedings with no equipment change (described in Scenario :Link[1]{label="1" overridedLabel="1" spaceId docId="anKARoqEXHHKmUAtRsfzx" version="v2" docAnchorId="#splitfeedings-scenario1" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="adjust-feedings.mdx"}) behave the same was as standard scheduled feedings.
  - If a feeding is in progress or a split is completed, the only change allowed for the feeding is swapping equipment. Other updates can be made after the feeding schedule resets.
  - If the first split has not started when the equipment assigned to a split feeding is changed, the splits are recalculated.
  - Once the first split begins, \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} does not recalculate the splits. Instead, it applies the outcomes described in Scenarios :Link[2]{label="2" overridedLabel="2" spaceId docId="anKARoqEXHHKmUAtRsfzx" version="v2" docAnchorId="#splitfeedings-scenario2" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="adjust-feedings.mdx"}, :Link[3]{label="3" overridedLabel="3" spaceId docId="anKARoqEXHHKmUAtRsfzx" version="v2" docAnchorId="#splitfeedings-scenario3" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="adjust-feedings.mdx"}, and :Link[4]{label="4" overridedLabel="4" spaceId docId="anKARoqEXHHKmUAtRsfzx" version="v2" docAnchorId="#splitfeedings-scenario4" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="adjust-feedings.mdx"} below.

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Scenario 1: Standard Split Feeding

    **When This Happens:&#x20;**&#x41; recipe load is bigger than the mixer capacity, and you approve it to be split before feeding starts.

    **What&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}}**&#x20;Does:**

    - Divides the load into equal parts
    - Recalculates ingredient amounts for each split
    - Updates \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} counts

    **What You See in&#x20;**[Scheduled Feedings](<../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Mobile Apps/FeedComp App/Feed/feed-using-the-feeding-schedule.mdx>)**:**

    - Splits created as planned
    - All splits listed and ready for feeding

    **What You See in&#x20;**:Link[Feeding History]{label="Feeding History" overridedLabel="Feeding History" spaceId docId="EgGjILyt4px-Lp9R18n71" version="v2" docAnchorId="#feed" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="FCM-Reports.htm#feed" href="FCM-Reports.htm#feed"}**:&#x20;**&#x53;plits reflect the actual feeding status (completed, incomplete, aborted, or missing).

    **Why It Matters:&#x20;**&#x59;ou get accurate splits without doing any math by hand. \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} automatically splits the recipe load to safely fit within mixer capacity.
  </ExpandableHeading>

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Scenario 2: Splits Partially Completed, Equipment Changed, New Mixer Larger Than Remaining Load

    **When This Happens:&#x20;**&#x59;ou start with splits, then switch to a mixer larger than what’s left in the load.

    **What&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}}**&#x20;Does:**

    - Keeps completed and/or in-progress splits as is
    - Merges the remaining load into one final split

    **What You See in&#x20;**[Scheduled Feedings](<../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Mobile Apps/FeedComp App/Feed/feed-using-the-feeding-schedule.mdx>)**:**

    - Completed and/or in-progress splits are **Completed**
    - Remaining splits merged into one new split

    **What You See in&#x20;**:Link[Feeding History]{label="Feeding History" overridedLabel="Feeding History" spaceId docId="EgGjILyt4px-Lp9R18n71" version="v2" docAnchorId="#feed" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="FCM-Reports.htm#feed" href="FCM-Reports.htm#feed"}**:&#x20;**&#x53;plits reflect the actual feeding status (completed, incomplete, aborted, or missing).

    **Why It Matters:&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} reorganizes the feeding automatically; no manual adjustment needed.
  </ExpandableHeading>

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Scenario 3: Splits Partially Completed, Equipment Changed, New Mixer Smaller Than Remaining Load but Large Enough for Each Split

    **When This Happens:&#x20;**&#x59;ou switch to a smaller mixer that can’t hold the whole remaining load, but can hold each split.

    **What&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}}**&#x20;Does:**

    - Completed and/or in-progress splits are **Completed**
    - Keeps remaining splits as is

    **What You See in&#x20;**[Scheduled Feedings](<../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Mobile Apps/FeedComp App/Feed/feed-using-the-feeding-schedule.mdx>)**:**

    - Completed and/or in-progress splits are **Completed**
    - Splits not started yet remain separate and ready to feed

    **What You See in&#x20;**:Link[Feeding History]{label="Feeding History" overridedLabel="Feeding History" spaceId docId="EgGjILyt4px-Lp9R18n71" version="v2" docAnchorId="#feed" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="FCM-Reports.htm#feed" href="FCM-Reports.htm#feed"}**:&#x20;**&#x53;plits reflect the actual feeding status (completed, incomplete, aborted, or missing).

    **Why It Matters:&#x20;**&#x53;plits remain accurate without any recalculation required. In this case, you may choose to manually recalculate the remaining splits, depending on how much total load remains and how many \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}} still need to be fed.
