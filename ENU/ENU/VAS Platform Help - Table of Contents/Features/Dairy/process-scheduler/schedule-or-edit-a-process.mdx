Expand the topics below to learn more about scheduling and managing the processes that are included in your calendar on the \{\{Protocols.ProcessScheduler}} page. You can schedule future processes or edit or delete current processes:

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Schedule Processes

  You can manage your scheduled \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}s, reports, and other processes on \{\{Protocols.ProcessScheduler}}, though each type is scheduled a little differently. Expand the sections below to learn more:

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Worklists

    To schedule a recurring report or worklist and add it to \{\{Protocols.ProcessScheduler}}, navigate to the [Dairy Reports/Worklists](../../DairyComp/MyDC/reportsworklists/manage-dairy-reportsworklists.mdx) page and follow the instructions below:

    1. Click the **Schedule** button to open a side panel where you can create an automated schedule for the report or \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}.

    <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/2dnTmWNMdvWN7vj0YBf05_scheduleworklists.png" size="61" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/process-scheduler/ScheduleWorklists.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/process-scheduler/ScheduleWorklists.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false" indent="2"></Image>

    <Paragraph indent="1">
      Use the following guidelines to schedule your report or \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}:\* Select the date and time to start the process.:::hint\{type="warning"}
      **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**&#x53;et the **Day to Start** to a future time, either later \{\{today}} or a future date, as scheduling a process at a time that has passed will not run until the next time the scheduled interval is reached. For example, if you schedule a process to start on Mondays at 5 am, but today is a Monday at 9 am, the enrollment will start next week at 5 am.
      \:::- Set the schedule for the process:1) Set the **Repetition** to "Repeats".
      2\) Set the frequency and interval for the process. For instance, to run a process weekly, set **Every** to "1" and **Intervals** to "Weeks".
    </Paragraph>

    <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
      Click **Schedule**.After you schedule your report or \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}, \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} shows the **Scheduled** button and \{\{Protocols.ProcessScheduler}} includes the report or \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}. To view the generated report or \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}, see [Report History](../../DairyComp/MyDC/reportsworklists/view-report-history.mdx) or [Worklist History](<../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/DairyComp/MyDC/Reports/Worklists/view-worklists-history.mdx>).
    </Paragraph>
  </ExpandableHeading>

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Reports

    To schedule a recurring report and add it to \{\{Protocols.ProcessScheduler}}, select \{\{Buttons-UI.FeaturesMain}}**&#x20;>&#x20;**\{\{Buttons-UI.FeaturesSecondary}}**\*\*\*\***\{\{Buttons-UI.FeaturesSecondary}} from the [menu bar](../../../navigate-pulse/menu-options.mdx) and follow the steps below:

    <hint type="info">
      **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x4F;nly reports on your [Dairy Reports/Worklists](../../DairyComp/MyDC/reportsworklists/manage-dairy-reportsworklists.mdx) page can be scheduled and added to \{\{Protocols.ProcessScheduler}}.
    </hint>

    1. Click the **Schedule** button to open a side panel where you can create an automated schedule for the report or \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}.

    <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/yvARXVR2Stbbp2Qh1gip7_schedule-report-worklist.png" size="66" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/process-scheduler/schedule-report-worklist.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/process-scheduler/schedule-report-worklist.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false" indent="2"></Image>

    <Paragraph indent="1">
      Use the following guidelines to schedule your report or \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}:\* Select the date and time to start the process.:::hint\{type="warning"}
      **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**&#x53;et the **Day to Start** to a future time, either later today or a future date, as scheduling a process at a time that has passed will not run until the next time the scheduled interval is reached. For example, if you schedule a process to start on Mondays at 5 am, but today is a Monday at 9 am, the enrollment will start next week at 5 am.
      \:::- Set the schedule for the process:1) Set the **Repetition** to "Repeats".
      2\) Set the frequency and interval for the process. For instance, to run a process weekly, set **Every** to "1" and **Intervals** to "Weeks".
    </Paragraph>

    <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
      Click **Schedule**.After you schedule your report or \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}, \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} shows the **Scheduled** button and \{\{Protocols.ProcessScheduler}} includes the report or \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}. To view the generated report or \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}, see [Report History](../../DairyComp/MyDC/reportsworklists/view-report-history.mdx) or [Worklist History](<../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/DairyComp/MyDC/Reports/Worklists/view-worklists-history.mdx>).
    </Paragraph>
  </ExpandableHeading>

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Protocol Task Status Alerts

    \{\{Protocols.ProcessScheduler}} allows you to create and manage email and text alerts for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}'s \{\{Protocols.Protocol-common}} tasks. See [Add and Manage Task Status Alerts](add-and-manage-task-status-alerts.mdx) for more information on setting \{\{Protocols.Protocol-common}} task alerts.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Edit Processes

  <hint type="info">
    **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x43;ompleted processes cannot be edited.
  </hint>

  1. Click on a date in the \{\{Protocols.ProcessScheduler}} calendar to open a side panel that displays the date's scheduled processes.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/z92YVa-gI0dOqDj_YM3Br_dailyview.png" size="71" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/process-scheduler/DailyView.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/process-scheduler/DailyView.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false" indent="2"></Image>

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    Click a scheduled process to view its details.
  </Paragraph>

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/Z1bXJS1tkcFiDsHbcEMOr_editprocess.png" size="38" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/process-scheduler/EditProcess.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/process-scheduler/EditProcess.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false" indent="2"></Image>

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    Complete your edits, then click **Save** to apply your changes.
  </Paragraph>
</ExpandableHeading>
