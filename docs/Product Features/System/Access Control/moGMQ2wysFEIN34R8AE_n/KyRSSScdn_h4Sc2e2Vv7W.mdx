You can access your user profile from your user icon. To manage your user profile, click your user icon in the top-right corner and select **User Profile**.&#x20;

![](https://api.archbee.com/api/optimize/SSUUxKZUk9bFTEPNn_6Zo/SMQoXSuHJpAnxQNXqtkfm_image.png)

# Update your Password

From your user profile, you can update your password.&#x20;

# Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (2FA)

To enable Multi-Factor Authentication (2FA):

1. In **Multi Factor Authentication**, click **Enable**.

<Image src="https://api.archbee.com/api/optimize/SSUUxKZUk9bFTEPNn_6Zo/zqjrD8-nrXIKe_WDZxH5d_image.png" size="50" width="619" height="183" position="flex-start" showCaption="false" indent="2"></Image>

2. Login to Litmus Edge again and navigate back to **User Profile**.
3. In **Multi Factor Authentication**, click **Setup**.&#x20;

<Image src="https://api.archbee.com/api/optimize/SSUUxKZUk9bFTEPNn_6Zo/dCxk5XZLVdLM5vu2mArgw_image.png" size="50" width="616" height="183" position="flex-start" showCaption="false" indent="2"></Image>

4. In **Multi Factor Authentication**, click scan the QR with the authenticator app on your mobile device.

<Image src="https://api.archbee.com/api/optimize/SSUUxKZUk9bFTEPNn_6Zo/OBAoLXkz8tE2GwguPhcmz_image.png" size="100" width="616" height="473" position="flex-start" showCaption="false" indent="2"></Image>

5. Enter the code from your authenticator app and click **Submit**.
