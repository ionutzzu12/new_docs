Use the **Process Scheduler** calendar to view all your scheduled activities in one convenient location. The calendar provides a monthly view, and clicking on a specific day provides additional details in a side panel.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/nEopxdGTJuerTUc1bc9-D_processscheduler-wnjan22.png" size="91" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/process-scheduler/ProcessScheduler-WNJan22.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/process-scheduler/ProcessScheduler-WNJan22.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

\{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} supports scheduling \{\{Buttons-UI.DC-WorklistsONLY}} and \{\{Buttons-UI.DC-Reports}} to automatically generate on a daily, weekly, monthly, or even hourly basis. See :Link[Schedule Worklists]{label="Schedule Worklists" overridedLabel="Schedule Worklists" spaceId docId="MX3To8AuTQZu2befIPOA_" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Sched-Worklists" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="ScheduleEditProcess.htm#Sched-Worklists" href="ScheduleEditProcess.htm#Sched-Worklists"} or :Link[Schedule Reports]{label="Schedule Reports" overridedLabel="Schedule Reports" spaceId docId="MX3To8AuTQZu2befIPOA_" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Sched-Reports" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="ScheduleEditProcess.htm#Sched-Reports" href="ScheduleEditProcess.htm#Sched-Reports"} for instructions on adding those processes. After adding the processes to the schedule, you can view the activities on Process Scheduler.

\{\{Protocols.ProcessScheduler}}also allows you to create and manage email and text alerts for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}'s \{\{Protocols.Protocol-common}} tasks. See [Add and Manage Task Status Alerts](AddManageAlerts.htm) for more information on setting \{\{Protocols.Protocol-common}} task alerts.
