<ExpandableHeading>
  ### How do I add this widget to my Dashboard?

  Thiswidget is added to your \{\{Buttons-UI.Dashboard}} automatically after the integration is activated.
</ExpandableHeading>

<hint type="warning">
  **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**&#x54;he page will not load immediately. Please wait a few moments before you refresh the page, so \{\{General.CompanyName-short}} can establish a connection with \{\{Partners.Allflex}}. If the page does not load after a few minutes, contact [VAS Support](<mailto\:support@vas.com?subject=need support with VAS Platform>).
</hint>

\{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} displays live \{\{Partners.Allflex}} sensor data in two \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} dashboard widgets, providing an up-to-date view of animals that require health checks or reproduction-related protocols. View the examples below for more information on the data shown in the dashboard widgets:

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Allflex SCR Heatime Health Alerts

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/KVWQuSvVChDUec8o0V5Rn_allflex-health-alerts-w-table.png" size="88" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Partners/Allflex/Allflex-Health-alerts-w-table.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Partners/Allflex/Allflex-Health-alerts-w-table.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  1. **Partners Dashboard Tab:** To view the \{\{Partners.Allflex}} Dashboard widgets, open the Partners Dashboard tab on your \{\{Buttons-UI.Dashboard}}.
  2. **Allflex Health Alerts:** This dashboard displays the percentage of animals that have a health alert, sorted by \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}}.At a glance, you'll be able to see which \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}} may require more attention if there is an unusually high percentage of health alerts in a \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} that isn't designated as a hospital \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}}.
  3. \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.P-en-}}**&#x20;Details:** Hover over the graph to view details about each \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}}, including the percentage of animals in the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} who have a health alert, the total number of animals in the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}}, and the total number of animals in the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} who have a health alert.
  4. \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.P-ens-}}**&#x20;Filters:** From the drop-down list, you can select the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}} that are shown in the graph. For example, if you only want to see alerts for healthy animals, you may choose to deselect a hospital \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}}, because it will inevitably contain more animals with health alerts.
  5. **Scroll Bar:** Pull the sliders or drag the shaded area to adjust the visible data range.
  6. **Animals with Health Alert:** Click the **Expand** button

  </Paragraph>

  8. **Cow Number:**\{\{Commands.Click}} an animal's Cow Number to view more detailed health information on its [CowCard](allflex-cowcard-widgets.htm).
  9. **Health Index:**\{\{Partners.Allflex}}'s measurement of an animal's health, between 1–100, with 100 being the highest. \{\{Partners.Allflex}} calculates health by comparing an animal's health history with its health history in the past 24 hours.

  ## Allflex Heat Alerts

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/kL9mxxX4jXJ7OyuoC4WSH_allflex-heat-alerts-w-table.png" size="87" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Partners/Allflex/Allflex-Heat-alerts-w-table.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Partners/Allflex/Allflex-Heat-alerts-w-table.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  1. \{\{Partners.Allflex}}**&#x20;Heat Alerts:** This dashboard displays the number of animals with a heat alert, sorted by reproduction status:\* \{\{Preg}}**:** Pregnant animals
     - **Bred:** Bred animals
     - \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.F-resh-cow-}}**:**\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.F-resh-cow-}} animals (recently calved)
     - **No Bred:** Do not breed animals
     - **Dry:** Cows in their dry phase near the end of their pregnancy and preparing to \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.f-reshen}}
     - **OK/Open:** Animal is confirmed not pregnant
  2. **Reproduction Status Details:** Hover over each reproduction status in the graph to view heifer and cow animals counts.
  3. **For&#x20;**\{\{AI}}**:** In this graph, view all animals that are eligible to be bred and have a heat alert. Click the **Expand** button

  </Paragraph>

  4. **Other Heat Alerts:** In this graph, view all animals that have a heat alert but are **not** eligible to be bred. Click the **Expand** button

  </Paragraph>

  5. **Cow Number**: Click an animal's Cow Number to view more detailed heat information on its [CowCard](allflex-cowcard-widgets.htm).
  6. **Heat Index:**\{\{Partners.Allflex}}'s measurement that indicates whether an animal is in heat, between 1–100, with 100 being the highest. \{\{Partners.Allflex}} uses multiple parameters to determine whether an animal with elevated activity is actually in heat.
  7. **Breeding Window:** The amount of time (in hours) that a user has to breed an animal, based on \{\{Partners.Allflex}}'s heat-related calculations.
