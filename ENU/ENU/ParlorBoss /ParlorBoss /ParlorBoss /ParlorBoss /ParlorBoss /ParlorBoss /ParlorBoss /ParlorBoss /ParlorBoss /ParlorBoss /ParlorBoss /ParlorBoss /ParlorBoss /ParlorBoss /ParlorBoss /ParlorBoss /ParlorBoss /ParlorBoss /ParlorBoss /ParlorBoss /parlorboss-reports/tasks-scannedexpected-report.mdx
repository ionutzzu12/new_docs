  ## Search and Filter the Tasks Scanned/Expected Report

  Options to search and filter the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompReportNames}}\{\{Controls.ControlTypes}} are available at the top of the \{\{General.UI-gui}}. Follow the guidelines below when \{\{Commands.UserActions}} the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompReportNames}}\{\{Controls.ControlTypes}}:

  - \{\{Commands.Click}}**Filters** at the top left of the page to limit the data that is displayed. The **Filters** side panel opens.
  - Use the **Date&#x20;****From** and **Date&#x20;****To** filters to only view data from that range.
  - \{\{Commands.UserActions}} a \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Upper}} to limit the \{\{Controls.ControlTypes}} to a specific \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}.
  - \{\{Commands.UserActions}} the **Clear Filter\*\*\*\*s** option to clear all of the filters currently applied.

  If your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} has multiple \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}, \{\{Commands.Click}} the down arrow

  - Total \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompReportNames}}: The total number of \{\{Commands.UserActions}} on \{\{General.Grammar}}specific day.

  ## View Details for a Specific Date

  - Summary: An overall summary is displayed in a **GRAY** bar at the top left of the \{\{General.UI-gui}}:\* Total Affected Shifts: The total number of \{\{Partners.parlor}} shifts that had missed tasks on that day.
    - Total Animals with Missed \{\{Mobile-UI.ListsTab}}: The total number animals with missed tasks.

  - Animal ID: The identifier of the animal with missed \{\{Worklists.taskCommonUse}}s.
  - \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.P-en-}}: The \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} number the animal was in when scanned.
