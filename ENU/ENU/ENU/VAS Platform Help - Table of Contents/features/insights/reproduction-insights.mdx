The \{\{Buttons-UI.ReproductionInsights}} report tracks cow and heifer reproduction across your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} and allows you to evaluate the overall success of your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}'s breeding program. At a glance, you can assess your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} and see which areas need improvement.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/pU61cE7jsaXk580kyIcwO_reproduction-insights.png" size="69" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/insights/reproduction-insights.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/insights/reproduction-insights.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

You can find this feature under the :Link[Insights menu]{label="Insights menu" overridedLabel="Insights menu" spaceId docId="WFLX7SPMpZfy7zoMyrvRh" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Menu-Insights" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../../../NavPlatform/Menu.htm#Menu-Insights" href="../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/navigate-pulse/menu-options.mdx"}.

While similar to the `BREDSUM` feature in \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}}, \{\{Buttons-UI.ReproductionInsights}} allow you to track \{\{KPIs}} you've set and quickly determine whether your actual \{\{Insights.InsightTypes}} fall within your limits.

Click **Set Thresholds** at the top right in the blue bar to view your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}'s current KPIs and edit the limits you want tracked on the \{\{Insights.Reproduction}}\{\{Insights.Insights}}. See :Link[Reproduction Indicators]{label="Reproduction Indicators" overridedLabel="Reproduction Indicators" spaceId docId="Qc6TtLaRXHEUO9aO_uRiu" version="v2" docAnchorId="#reproduction" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../../DairySettings/IndicatorsTab.htm#reproduction" href="../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Dairy/Settings (Dairy)/Performance Indicators/working-with-indicators.mdx"}for more information.
