After you connect your \{\{Partners.parlor}}, milking shifts and \{\{Partners.parlor}} data are available in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} from the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}}, and you can create reports to track and analyze your \{\{Partners.parlor}} data.

Expand the sections below to learn more:

<ExpandableHeading>

  Use the Daily Milk tab on the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}} to view milking shift details for your animals:

  ## View Parlor Items

  After your \{\{Partners.parlor\_system}} is integrated, the following \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} items populate with data from your \{\{Partners.parlor\_system}}: 

  - \{\{Items.M1}}: Today’s milk weight at shift 1
  - \{\{Items.M2}}: Today’s milk weight at shift 2
  - \{\{Items.M3}}: Today’s milk weight at shift 3
  - \{\{Items.M4}}: Today’s milk weight at shift 4
  - \{\{Items.DMLK1}}: Current daily milk total
  - \{\{Items.WMLK1}}: Weekly milk average
  - \{\{Items.EXPDM}}: Expected daily milk weight
  - \{\{Items.EXDEV}}: Expected daily milk deviation percent
  - \{\{Items.MDEV}}: Daily milk deviation from expected
  - \{\{Items.WMDEV}}: Weekly milk deviation percent
  - \{\{Items.PPEN}}: Pen animal was identified in at last milking
  - \{\{Items.MCODE}}: Milking Code, 1-20:::ExpandableHeading

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Build Parlor Reports

  \{\{Partners.Parlor}} reporting can be completed using \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}[Report/Worklist Builder](../../Platform/Features/DairyComp/ReportsWorklists/ReportWorklistBuilder.htm) and the \{\{Partners.parlor}} items listed above. See the sample reports below: 

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Daily Milk Summary

    SUMM1 M2 M3 DMLK1BYPENFORLACT>0 RC\<6
  </ExpandableHeading>

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Milking Code Summary

    SUM BYMCODE
  </ExpandableHeading>

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Missing EID Tag

    LIST ID EID DIM WMLK1 FORDIM>6 WMLK1=0 FDAT>0 DDAT=0

    <hint type="info">
      **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x49;f your \{\{Partners.parlor}} uses an identifier other than \{\{EID}}, be sure to use the correct identifier to build your report.
    </hint>
  </ExpandableHeading>

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Cow Milked in Wrong Pen

    SHOW ID PEN PPEN FOR PPEN\<>PEN
  </ExpandableHeading>

  <hint type="info">
    **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x41;dditional \{\{Partners.Parlor}} insights and analysis tools are planned for a future \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} release. Stay tuned!
  </hint>
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Check Parlor Integration Status & Troubleshoot Errors

  You can quickly check on the connection status of your \{\{Partners.parlor}} integration by clicking on the Integration Status icon at the top right of the page in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/KrfQAZanOEfoZu0NS8wdV_integration-status.png" size="49" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Platform/parlorcomp/integration-status.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/parlorcomp/integration-status.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  See [Integration Status](../../Dairy/integration-status.mdx) to learn more about the Integration Status page and the indicators used. You can also use the [Notifications](<../../../FAQs/Using PULSE/how-do-notifications-work.mdx>) area at the top right of the page in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} to check for errors. Use the guidelines below for troubleshooting potential errors: 

  - Cow Not Found: Make sure the animal was correctly added to \{\{masterVAS-Products.MyDC-short}} and has a valid \{\{Partners.parlor}} identifier assigned. See [Add Animals](../../DairyComp/MyDC/Animals/add-animals.mdx) to learn more about adding one or more animals to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.
  - Other errors:[Contact Us](<../../../Get Started/contact-us.mdx>) for help addressing other errors:\* Could not load milk
    - Failed to send data to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}
    - Unable to fetch animal data from \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}
</ExpandableHeading>
