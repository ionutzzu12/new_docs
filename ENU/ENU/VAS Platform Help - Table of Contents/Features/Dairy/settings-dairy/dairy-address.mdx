<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Required Permissions

  Only users who are assigned to a Role with \{\{RolesPermissions.PermissionNames}} permissions can use this feature. See [Edit Dairy User Permissions](../Config/UserMgmtRoles/EditUserPermissions.htm) and [Manage Dairy Roles](<../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Manage Dairy Users and Roles/manage-dairy-roles.mdx>) for more information.
</ExpandableHeading>

Use the **Address** page to edit the address, phone number, email, or website for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}.. All required fields are marked with the **\*** (asterisk) symbol. See the topics below to learn more:

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/nXsnubcIyY-0lY85xn1YP_dairy-settings-address-tab.png" size="39" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/getting-started/dairy-settings-address-tab.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/getting-started/dairy-settings-address-tab.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

1. Physical Address: The physical address where your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} is located.
2. Phone Number: The telephone number where your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} can be contacted.:::hint\{type="info"}
   **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x59;our \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}'s phone number and email address, as well as its herdcode(s), are only visible to users who are connected to your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}.
   \:::
3. Address Email: The primary email address where your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} can be contacted.
4. Web: The URL address for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}'s website.
5. Click **Save** to apply your changes.
6. Click the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}}**&#x20;Info** tab to edit general information for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}, such as the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} name, \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} type, and \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} description. See [Dairy Info](<../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Dairy/Settings (Dairy)/dairy-info.mdx>) for more information.
7. Click the **Performance Indicators Tab&#x20;**&#x74;o set alert thresholds for common health events that occur across your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}, send notifications to your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} team when your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} exceeds those thresholds, and set the upper and lower limits used to track other \{\{KPIs}} across your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}. See [Performance Indicators](performance-indicators.mdx) for more information.
