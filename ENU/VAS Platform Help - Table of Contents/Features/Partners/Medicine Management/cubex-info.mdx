[MicroTechnologies’ Cubex](https://www.microtechnologies.com/dairy/cubex-qbud) is a wireless inventory system for animal health products that simplifies the management of on-hand inventory and automates the reordering and receiving of supplies. The \{\{Widgets.Cubex}} widget allows you to display drug inventory data from your \{\{Partners.Cubex}} integration directly in your \{\{General.ProductName}}\{\{Buttons-UI.Dashboard}}, including key \{\{masterVAS.CompanyName-short}} animal events, such as diagnoses and treatment protocols.
