\{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp-upper}} is easy to configure so you can start feeding quickly at your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}! First decide which devices will be used for feeding and then check [Before You Begin: Required Setup](before-you-begin-required-setup.mdx).

<hint type="info">
  **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x44;evices used for feeding must be connected to a scale. See [Scales](FCM-ScaleConnections.htm) to learn more about connecting to your scale. Devices not used for feeding will not have all available [Feed](FCM-Feed.htm) options. If you change your mind about using a device for feeding, :Link[log out of the app]{label="log out of the app" overridedLabel="log out of the app" spaceId docId="sK8Esbx0pVQzqZ5wDAiMP" version="v2" docAnchorId="#logout" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="FCM-UserAccount.htm#logout" href="account-settings.mdx"} and select **Yes** or **No** when asked if the device will be used for feeding.
</hint>
