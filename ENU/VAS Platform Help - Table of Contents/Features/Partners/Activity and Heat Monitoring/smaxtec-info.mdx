\{\{General.ProductName}} displays live \{\{Partners.smaXtec}} sensor data and alerts for each animal equipped with a bolus. And, to maintain consistent animal data between the \{\{General.ProductName}} and smaXtec Messenger, \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} also sends animal events to smaXtec every 6 hours.

\{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} also pulls items requiring action into one of two \{\{Partners.smaXtec}}-specific worklists.

View the diagram below to learn more about the two-way data exchange between \{\{General.ProductName}} and \{\{Partners.smaXtec}}.
