  ### Required Permissions

  Only users who are assigned to a Role with \{\{RolesPermissions.PermissionNames}} permissions can use this feature. See [Edit Dairy User Permissions](../Config/UserMgmtRoles/EditUserPermissions.htm) and [Manage Dairy Roles](<../../../../Manage Dairy Users and Roles/manage-dairy-roles.mdx>) for more information.

\{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} automatically processes key performance indicators (\{\{KPIs}}) for the animals across your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} around midnight each night.

This nightly process is specific for \{\{Buttons-UI.PerformanceIndicators}} and is unrelated to the [KPIs and Alerts](../../GetStarted/SetAlerts.htm) feature in [Account Settings](<../../../../User Menu/edit-your-user-account.mdx>).

\{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} does not currently support alerts and notifications for all available indicator types. Use the following instructions to set up \{\{Buttons-UI.HealthIndicators}}, but note that the steps may differ for other indicator types.

Follow the steps below to enable and set up indicators for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}. These instructions assume that you are starting with a \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} that does not already have alerts and indicators set up in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}:

1. Begin by selecting \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}}**&#x20;> Settings** from the \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} menu.
2. Click the \{\{Buttons-UI.PerformanceIndicators}} tab, and make sure the \{\{Buttons-UI.Indicators}} tab and the \{\{Buttons-UI.HealthIndicators}} option are selected.When the [Indicators](../working-with-indicators.mdx) page is displayed, \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} examines your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} and updates the \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-Occurrence}} fields for each \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-OccurrenceType}} found across your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/f1oeu2bhLbixrboxAK1zL_indicators-tab-initial-setup.png" alt caption size="88"}In the example above, \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} found one **Overall&#x20;**\{\{Mastitis}} event and one **Cow Mastitis** event while examining the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}. You can track these events by setting an \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-AlertThresholdValue}} for each event.Since \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-AlertsAndThresholds}} have never been enabled for this \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}, note that the **Alert Threshold** fields are disabled.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/3KQxga9K16bC7nIo-38Xv_indicators-tab-disabled.png" alt caption size="63"}
3. Enable, or turn on, \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-AlertsAndThresholds}} for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} by clicking the **Configure** button, and then in the side panel, toggle the **Status** control for **Indicators and Alerts** to "on" (green).:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/og-JER7IIZCzhyhnGyzk-_indicators-tab-config-btn.png" alt caption size="63"}:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/7fFQPG9R2XCYeBKtv0LLi_indicators-tab-config-status.png" alt caption size="42"}
4. While still in the side panel, use the **Period Preferences** area to select how often you want \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} to examine \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-ThresholdDifferential}}s for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} and send notifications to your team. Note that the **Daily** option is automatically selected for you. This tells \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} that you want to examine \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-ThresholdDifferential}}s and send alerts on a daily basis.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/FLQCdPLdOfOB__2UM7UTT_indicators-tab-config-daily.png" alt caption size="44"}If you prefer \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} to examine \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-ThresholdDifferential}}s and send alerts on a weekly basis, select the **Weekly** option.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/Tqi6_Y4SB33IfN415_wjg_indicators-tab-config-weekly.png" alt caption size="42"}When the **Weekly** option is selected, you must select a **Day of week** to receive alerts. For instance, if you select **Wednesday** in the list, \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} examines \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-ThresholdDifferential}}s and sends alerts every Wednesday.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/M2xtVZ4Gs37HSvrS74CPa_indicators-tab-config-weekly-slct.png" alt caption size="43"}If you decide that alerting your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} team once a week doesn't meet your specific needs, don't worry! You can always return to this side panel later and select the **Daily** option to examine \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-ThresholdDifferential}}s and send alerts on a daily basis.
5. Now that you've specified how often you want \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} to examine \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-ThresholdDifferential}}s for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} and send notifications to your team, you can click **See more details** in the side panel to view the current \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-ThresholdDifferential}} and alert schedule.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/zs0Lwo_D_lxAaE6sSTtMO_indicators-tab-config-schedule-expand.png" alt caption size="31"}See [Indicators Configuration Details](<Step 1: Enable & Set Up Health Indicators/indicators-configuration-details.mdx>) for more information about the \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-ThresholdDifferential}} and alert schedule.
6. Click the **Save** button. \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} displays a message indicating that your changes have been saved.

Proceed to [Step 2: Enter Alert Thresholds](step-2-enter-alert-thresholds.mdx) to continue.
