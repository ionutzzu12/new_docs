Use the **Projections** dashboard to view monthly projections of your inventory, including the number of milking cows, dry cows, calves, and more, for the next six months. Use these projections to make well-informed inventory decisions in the future.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/2iBRAlsVKCYG_mHFMAap2_inventoryprojections.png" size="63" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/oneview/inventoryprojections.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/oneview/inventoryprojections.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

Follow the steps below to continue:

<WorkflowBlock>
  <WorkflowBlockItem>
    **Filters:** Click the **Filters** option on the upper left side of the dashboard to open a side panel where you can manage the data you see in the graph:
  </WorkflowBlockItem>

  <WorkflowBlockItem>
    **Facility filters:&#x20;**&#x55;se the **Facility Type** and/or **Facilities** filters to manage the facilities shown in the graph. Facilities are listed by \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} name. By default, the graph includes data from all \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}}.
  </WorkflowBlockItem>

  <WorkflowBlockItem>
    **Animal group filter:** By default, this graph includes all animal groups. Select the animals groups shown in the graph.
  </WorkflowBlockItem>

  <WorkflowBlockItem>
    **Edit variables:** On the upper right side of the screen, click **Edit Variables** to change the variable data to see possible outcomes for different scenarios. The system opens a side panel where you can edit Culling Rate, Dry Period, Dead on Arrival, Averaged Sexed Accuracy, and Twinning Rate.
  </WorkflowBlockItem>

  <WorkflowBlockItem>
    <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/aAGTTsV_Qw1wl3BCOg1KW_projections-variables.png" size="43" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/oneview/projections-variables.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/oneview/projections-variables.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

    <ExpandableHeading>
      ### Click here to see variable definitions!

      - **Culling Rate:** Percentage of animals that are SOLD or DIED.
      - **Dry Period:** Number of days animals are not milked between lactations.
      - **Dead on Arrival:** Percentage of calves born dead.
      - **Average Sexed Accuracy:** Average percentage of female calves born after conception from sexed semen.
      - **Twinning Rate:** Percentage of births that result in twins. Currently, this variable cannot be changed and is based on an industry standard.
    </ExpandableHeading>

    **References:** Each animal group is represented on the graph by its own colored line.
  </WorkflowBlockItem>

  <WorkflowBlockItem>
    **Tooltips:** Hover over the graph data to view more detailed information, including animal counts and percentages.
  </WorkflowBlockItem>

  <WorkflowBlockItem>
    **95% CI:** The 95% confidence interval (CI) measures the degree of certainty or uncertainty in the system's sampling. A high confidence interval indicates the system has more uncertainty in the accuracy of the projection. A low confidence interval indicates more certainty in the accuracy of the projection.
  </WorkflowBlockItem>
</WorkflowBlock>
