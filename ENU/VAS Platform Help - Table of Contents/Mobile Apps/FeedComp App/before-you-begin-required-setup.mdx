Before you can begin using \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} to feed at your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}, you must complete the required setup in the following order:

1. [Locations](FCM-Locations.htm)
2. [Equipment](FCM-Equipment.htm)
3. [Pens](FCM-Pens.htm)
4. [Ingredients](FCM-Ingredients.htm)
5. [Recipes](FCM-Recipes.htm)
6. [Adjust Feedings](FCM-FeedingSchedule.htm)
7. [Vendors](FCM-Vendors.htm) (if your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} handles deliveries or pickups for [Inventory](FCM-Inventory.htm))
8. [Scales](FCM-ScaleConnections.htm) (if the device is used for feeding)

<hint type="info">
  **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x44;evices used for feeding must be connected to a scale. See [Scales](FCM-ScaleConnections.htm) to learn more about connecting to your scale. Devices not used for feeding will not have all available [Feed](FCM-Feed.htm) options. If you change your mind about using a device for feeding, :Link[log out of the app]{label="log out of the app" overridedLabel="log out of the app" spaceId docId="sK8Esbx0pVQzqZ5wDAiMP" version="v2" docAnchorId="#logout" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="FCM-UserAccount.htm#logout" href="FCM-UserAccount.htm#logout"} and select **Yes** or **No** when asked if the device will be used for feeding.
</hint>
