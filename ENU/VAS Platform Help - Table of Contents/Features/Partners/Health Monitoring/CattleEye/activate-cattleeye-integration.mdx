<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Required Permissions

  Only users who are assigned a Role with Integrations Management permissions can connect partner integrations. See [Edit Dairy User Permissions](../../Platform/Features/Config/UserMgmtRoles/EditUserPermissions.htm) and [Manage Dairy Roles](<../../../Manage Dairy Users and Roles/manage-dairy-roles.mdx>) for more information.
</ExpandableHeading>

Follow these steps to connect the \{\{Partners.PartnerName}} integration and view measurement data in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}:

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Step 1: Connect Integration in PULSE

  1. In \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, navigate to **Partners&#x20;**>**&#xA0;Integrations Gallery&#x20;**>**&#xA0;**\{\{Partners.PartnerName}}.
  2. \{\{Commands.Click}}**See Details** or **See Requirements** on the \{\{Partners.PartnerName}}integration card.
  3. Follow the Connection Instructions on the right side of the page to establish the connection:1) Click the **Connection Status** toggle and verify the status switches to **Active**.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/yVoLb7NAnbX1zs-j2jcYx_integration-connected.png" alt="Connection Status: Active" caption size="22"}
     2\) \{\{Commands.Click}}**Save**.
     3\) If necessary, \{\{Commands.click}}**Edit Integration** to make any adjustments or edits and save your changes.

  <hint type="warning">
    **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**&#x49;f your integration does not automatically connect, contact your \{\{Partners.PartnerName}} representative to connect the integration from their system. If requested, provide your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}} \{\{Id}} and Herdcode listed on the \{\{Partners.PartnerName}} Integration Gallery details page.
  </hint>
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Step 2: Configure Items in DairyComp 305 (DairyComp Unlimited Users Only)

  <hint type="warning">
    **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}} version 25.1 is required to support all :Link[Measurement Items]{label="Measurement Items" overridedLabel="Measurement Items" spaceId docId="-OCLzLD2z8ixp1k_oAajr" version="v2" docAnchorId="#items" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="activate-cattleeye-integration.mdx"}. See [Update DC305 to the Latest Release](https://dc-help.vas.com/FAQs/WebUpdater.htm) \{\{masterVAS-Phrases.RefsToOtherHelpProjects}} for update instructions.
  </hint>

  1. In \{\{DC-UI.DC-ProductName}}, enter `PLATFORM` on the command line.
  2. \{\{Commands.Click}} the \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}**&#x20;Items** tab and expand the **Activity Measurement Alerts**.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/zdYxPRHUqEU_10MbwsDdN_dc305-pulse-items.png" alt caption size="33"}
  3. Verify that locomotion and body condition items exist.
