  ### Complete OvSynch tasks and enter them in PULSE daily

  To reap the full benefits of an \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.ovsynch}} program, be sure to complete your tasks on time and mark them as complete in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} on a daily basis. See [Step 3: Complete OvSynch Tasks](OvsynchTasks.htm) to learn more!

<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Can I automate the enrollment of animals to an OvSynch protocol?

  Yes! \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.ovsynch}} enrollment can be handled manually or automatically depending on how your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} prefers to manage the associated treatments and tasks: 

  - Manual enrollment offers more control and allows you to select specific animals to enroll in a \{\{protocol}}.
  - Automatic enrollment streamlines the process and eliminates the selection process for enrolling animals. Animals that meet your defined criteria are automatically enrolled according to the schedule you set.

  See :Link[Manually Enroll from Dairy Protocols]{label="Manually Enroll from Dairy Protocols" overridedLabel="Manually Enroll from Dairy Protocols" spaceId docId="sSzi86fL-qtVukJuHAfoS" version="v2" docAnchorId="#DairyProtocols" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="OvsynchEnrollment.htm#DairyProtocols" href="OvsynchEnrollment.htm#DairyProtocols"} and :Link[Schedule Automatic Enrollment]{label="Schedule Automatic Enrollment" overridedLabel="Schedule Automatic Enrollment" spaceId docId="sSzi86fL-qtVukJuHAfoS" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Schedule" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="OvsynchEnrollment.htm#Schedule" href="OvsynchEnrollment.htm#Schedule"} to learn more!
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Can an animal be enrolled in more than one OvSynch protocol?

  No. Animals can only be enrolled in a single \{\{Protocols.Protocol-common}} at a time. To begin a new \{\{Protocols.Protocol-common}}, first :Link[deactivate the current protocol]{label="deactivate the current protocol" overridedLabel="deactivate the current protocol" spaceId docId="85pssZnyf8LxkZEO_euLj" version="v2" docAnchorId="#remove" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="OvsynchTasks.htm#remove" href="OvsynchTasks.htm#remove"} and then add a new one. By default, automatic enrollment excludes animals that are already on a \{\{Protocols.Protocol-common}}.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ### What checks are in place to ensure the right animals are enrolled in an OvSynch protocol?

  If you use manual enrollment, the only check the system completes is to verify the animal is not currently on an \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.ovsynch}}\{\{Protocols.Protocol-common}}. For automatic enrollment, the following additional checks are in place: 

  - If a \{\{Protocols.Protocol-common}} is set for 1st Service Synchronization, animals that have previously been on an \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.ovsynch}}\{\{Protocols.Protocol-common}} are automatically excluded.
  - If you defined Additional Criteria for your \{\{Protocols.Protocol-common}}, only animals that meet all selected conditions are automatically enrolled.

  See [Step 2: OvSynch Enrollment & Scheduling](OvsynchEnrollment.htm) to learn more!
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## What happens when I Skip a task?

  You can skip treatments that are not set to Required in your \{\{Protocols.Protocol-common}} setup. Reasons for skipping a \{\{Worklists.taskCommonUse}} include signs of illness or a change in breeding plans for that animal based on other events. Skipping a \{\{Worklists.taskCommonUse}} marks it as

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/eyRtmG2LxSLVuGPhwa-Vd_status-skipped99x30.png" size="10" caption="Skipped" alt="Skipped" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/tasks/status-skipped_99x30.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/tasks/status-skipped_99x30.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="true"></Image>

  in the event side panel. A skipped \{\{Worklists.taskCommonUse}} is considered **Done** and it moves from the \{\{Worklists.OpenTasksTodoTasks}} page to the \{\{Worklists.OpenTasksDoneTasks}} page.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ### How should I handle overdue tasks?

  Be sure to address any Overdue tasks as soon as possible. To quickly locate Overdue tasks, go to \{\{Buttons-UI.FeaturesMain}}**&#x20;>&#xA0;**\{\{Buttons-UI.DC-Reports}}**&#x20;> Open Worklists** and click the \{\{Protocols.OpenTasks}} list to open it. Then set the Overdue filter t&#x6F;**&#x20;Only Overdue**.
