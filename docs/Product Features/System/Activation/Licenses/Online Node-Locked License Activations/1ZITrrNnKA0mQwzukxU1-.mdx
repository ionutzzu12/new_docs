You can activate a license key received from Litmus by navigating to **System&#x20;**>**&#x20;Activation&#x20;**>**&#x20;License**.&#x20;

# Before You Begin

Before completing the steps below, make sure you do the following:

- Obtain the license activation key from Litmus.&#x20;
- If your current license is expired, also obtain the *Data & Device Management Endpoint* value from Litmus.&#x20;
- Synchronize the time for the Litmus device. See :Link[Time Synchronization Issues]{label="Time Synchronization Issues" spaceId="JWQMp_kvm0WsA_Fpjt1v_" docId="oocqie-QUk2u6B9r5MuUN" version="v2" docAnchorId loadingMethod="dynamic" overridedLabel} to learn more.&#x20;

# Activate License Online

Refer to the appropriate steps depending on if your current license is expired or not.&#x20;

## Activate a License Online if Your Current License is Not Expired

**To activate a license if your current license is not expired:**

1. Navigate to **System&#x20;***>&#x20;***Activation**.&#x20;
2. Click the **License&#x20;**&#x74;ab.&#x20;
3. From the *Node-Locked License* section, click **Add License**.
   The *Add a New License* form appears.
   ![](https://archbee-image-uploads.s3.amazonaws.com/SSUUxKZUk9bFTEPNn_6Zo/gIfBapS31Ml4aYU2QMqYU_add-license-button.png)
4. From the *Online License Activation* tab, paste or enter the license activation key obtained from Litmus in the **Activation Key** field.&#x20;
5. Click **Submit**.
   The license is activated and appears in the *License* pane.

## Activate a License Online if Your Current License is Expired

**To activate a license if your current license is expired:**

1. Log in to Litmus Edge. &#x20;
2. Click **License Settings**.&#x20;
   ![](https://archbee-image-uploads.s3.amazonaws.com/SSUUxKZUk9bFTEPNn_6Zo/aiX34ZzOv5Xny0ls9fBTn_license-settings-button.png)
3. In the *Node-Locked License* section, click **Add License**.
   ![](https://archbee-image-uploads.s3.amazonaws.com/SSUUxKZUk9bFTEPNn_6Zo/zNqFHTkHP8afB-ZXWVNj4_add-license-button-2.png)
4. Under *Online License Activation*, paste or enter the license activation key obtained from Litmus in the **Activation Key** field.
   ![](https://archbee-image-uploads.s3.amazonaws.com/SSUUxKZUk9bFTEPNn_6Zo/ybLcpFq_-yf29Iy4SNkHy_activation-key.png)
5. Click **Submit**.
   The license is activated and appears in the *License&#x20;*&#x70;ane.
