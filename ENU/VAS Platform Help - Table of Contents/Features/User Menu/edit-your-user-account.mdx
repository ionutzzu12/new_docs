If you're not already on your **Account&#x20;****Settings** page, click \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} logo button in the top left corner of the window, and \{\{Commands.click}} the **Account Settings** button to the far right of your User Account home page.

**OR**

Select **Account Settings** from the [User Menu](UserMenu.htm) in the top right corner of the page.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/ecq-YsGRfRV3m8X9-g7v3_user-menu-acct.png" size="22" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/users-roles/user-menu-acct.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/users-roles/user-menu-acct.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

This opens your **Account Settings** page. Follow the guidelines below to edit your user account.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/dzYWdztI-j9vIZIb_N8P__user-profile-illustrated.png" size="38" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/users-roles/user-profile-illustrated.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/users-roles/user-profile-illustrated.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

1. Account: Use the Account tab to add a picture and your mobile phone number to your profile. Add an account photo by uploading it directly to \{\{General.ProductName-short}} or selecting it from a Gravatar account.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-doc-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/yRXE7gDqL4-AmUaOAIykd_capture1.bmp" alt caption}
2. Password: Change your \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} password.
3. User Profile: Adjust general account information. The options on this page are defined in #6–9 below.
4. Address: Edit your physical address, phone number, email address, and website.
5. Alerts: Set \{\{UDS-general.KPI-long}}s (\{\{KPIs}}) for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}. See [How do I set KPIs and alerts?](../../GetStarted/SetAlerts.htm) to learn more!
6. Date Format: Change \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} date and time format. The settings you select here will also be applied in the mobile app.
7. Units: The unit of measure you select here applies to all features in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} (widgets, \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}}s, graphs, tables, etc.). Once you save this change, it will also be applied to the mobile app upon your next login:\* Select **Imperial System** to display units in Pounds, Gallons, Fahrenheit, and Ounces.
   - Select **Metric System** to display units in Kilograms, Liters, Celsius, and Milligrams.
8. Language: Change the language that is displayed in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}. Once you change the language and save it here, it will change in the mobile app the next time you open it.:::hint\{type="info"}
   **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x46;or \{\{masterVAS-Products.DCUnlimited}}, the \{\{General.ProductName-short}} language setting does **not** change the data fields that are pulled from \{\{masterVAS-Products.OnPremise-lower}} \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC-Platform}}. When \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} synchronizes data from the \{\{masterVAS-Products.OnPremise-lower}} \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC-Platform}}, it will be displayed in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} the same way it was entered in the \{\{masterVAS-Products.OnPremise-lower}} \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC-Platform}}.
   \:::
9. Am I discoverable? Set your user account to be visible or not visible to other \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} users. If you change this field to **No, I don't want to be visible to other users**, your name will no longer be displayed when other \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} users search for a user.
