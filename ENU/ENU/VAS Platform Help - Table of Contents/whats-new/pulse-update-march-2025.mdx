ENHANCEMENTActivity Monitoring Integrations are now available! We heard you loud and clear: you want to make data from your \{\{Partners.ActivityMonitoringPartner}} available in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} to support reporting and \{\{worklist}} activities, and \{\{masterVAS.CompanyName-short}} is excited to announce that we've added multiple \{\{Partners.ActivityMonitoring}} partners to support this request!

First check the [Integrations Gallery](../Features/partners/integrations-gallery.mdx) to see if your \{\{Partners.ActivityMonitoringPartner}} is available, and then follow the steps in [Activate Activity Monitoring Integration](<../Features/Partners/Activity and Heat Monitoring/activity-monitoring-overview/activate-activity-monitoring-integration.mdx>) to get started. If your \{\{Partners.ActivityMonitoringPartner}} is not available, check the [Upcoming Integrations](<../../ENU/VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/partners/integrations-gallery/upcoming-integrations-and-beta-testing.mdx>) page or [Contact Us](<../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Get Started/contact-us.mdx>) to inquire about adding a new partner to \{\{masterVAS.CompanyName-short}}\{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} integrations.

ENHANCEMENT`MyDC ONLY` Automatic heat and health tasks! Your \{\{Partners.ActivityMonitoringPartner}} can now automatically send heat and health alerts to your \{\{masterVAS-Products.MyDC-short}}\{\{Protocols.OpenTasks}} list. See [Process Open Tasks (MyDC Only)](../Features/DairyComp/MyDC/task-lists-mydc-only/process-open-tasks-mydc-only.mdx) to learn more about working with your \{\{Protocols.OpenTasks}}.

## Bug Fixes

- `MyDC ONLY`\{\{Events.23MEASURE}} event metric value fix. \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} now handles metric height and weight values as expected. Previously, metric values were converted to imperial values and included in the remark for the event.
