<hint type="info">
  **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x41;s a security measure, if only one user in a \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} is assigned \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} \{\{MorphHelp.User\_Management\_UI\_item}} permissions, that user cannot be removed from the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} and cannot leave the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}. \{\{MorphHelp.User\_Management\_UI\_item}} permissions must be assigned to at least one other user account if the only user with that permission level wishes to leave the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}. See [Edit Dairy User Permissions](../Features/Config/UserMgmtRoles/EditUserPermissions.htm) for more information.
</hint>

If you no longer wish to be connected to a \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}, your \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-omepage}} provides an option to leave it:

1. Click the \{\{General.ProductName}} logo button in the top left corner of the window.
2. In the **My Connections** list on the right side of your User Account \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-omepage}}, click the Options button

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/wIXwfkZ07oPcd_rKpJE8J_options-green-small41x29.png" size="10" caption="Options button" alt="Options button" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/icons/options-green-small_41x29.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/icons/options-green-small_41x29.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="true" indent="2"></Image>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  next to the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} name that you wish to leave.
</Paragraph>

3. Click the **Leave&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}} button over to the right.The system displays a confirmation message at the bottom of the screen.
4. At the bottom of the screen, click the **Yes, Leave** confirmation button to leave the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/X4dzOvEOZGI5juysqGDVc_leave-dairy-illustrated.png" alt caption size="75"}

<hint type="warning">
  **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**&#x42;e certain that you want to leave the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} because once you do, your access to it will be entirely removed from your \{\{General.ProductName}} account. At that point, you must be reconnected to that \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} before you can view it again.
</hint>
