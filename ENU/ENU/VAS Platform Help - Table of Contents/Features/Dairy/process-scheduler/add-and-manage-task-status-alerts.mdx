<hint type="info">
  **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x55;se this topic to learn more about \{\{Protocols.Protocol-common}} task status alerts, which are based on your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} protocols and scheduling setup. \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} also offers an option to set \{\{KPI}}-based alerts for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}. See [How do I set KPIs and alerts?](../../../GetStarted/SetAlerts.htm) to learn more about KPI-based alerts.
</hint>

You can receive a daily email or text that provides a quick status update about the day's scheduled processes. For example, you can set up a daily alert that summarizes the progress of your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}'s scheduled \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.protocolTasks}}. In a short message, the system tells you how many tasks were completed, are overdue, and other details.

Expand the sections below to learn more:

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Add Alerts

  1. Click the **Add Scheduled Process** button

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/-y4ii8eHG-8CGAypMbRCj_scheduled-process-add125x25.png" size="10" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/icons/scheduled-process-add_125x25.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/icons/scheduled-process-add_125x25.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false" indent="2"></Image>

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    in the upper right corner of the Process Scheduler page.
  </Paragraph>

  2. Select \{\{Protocols.Protocol-singular}}**&#x20;task status alert** from the side panel on the right to open the setup side panel.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/rG6tHcmt63O4EfyZ4RguA_addalert.png" size="57" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/process-scheduler/AddAlert.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/process-scheduler/AddAlert.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false" indent="2"></Image>

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    Use the following guidelines to schedule your alert:\* **Timeframe Preferences:** Enter the time you want to receive your daily alert.
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    \{\{Protocols.Protocols}}**&#x20;to Check:** Select the \{\{Protocols.Protocol-common}}s that should be included in the alert.
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    **Users to Notify:** Select the users that need to receive the alert and indicate whether the user should receive the alert in an email, text message, or both.
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    Click **Save** to add the alert.
    The \{\{Protocols.ProcessScheduler}} calendar now includes the alert and you should now receive a daily alert at the designated time.
  </Paragraph>
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Manage Alerts

  <hint type="info">
    **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x43;ompleted alerts cannot be edited or deleted.
  </hint>

  1. Click on a date in the \{\{Protocols.ProcessScheduler}} calendar to see the date's scheduled processes in a side panel.
  2. Click an alert to view its details.
  3. Edit or delete your alert:1) To edit your alert, update the alert details and click **Save** to apply your changes.
     2. To delete your alert, click the trash icon

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/LpD9BwgjpcA94KYTX2Y5v_trashcan-gray-on-white22x25.png" size="10" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/icons/trashcan-gray-on-white_22x25.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/icons/trashcan-gray-on-white_22x25.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false" indent="3"></Image>

  <Paragraph indent="2">
    in the upper right corner and click **Delete** in the confirmation pop-up window.
  </Paragraph>
</ExpandableHeading>
