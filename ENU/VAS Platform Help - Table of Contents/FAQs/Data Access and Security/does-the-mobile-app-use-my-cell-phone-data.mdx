We find this question especially important for users who have a limited amount of data on their cell phone plan or who have prepaid cell phone plans, such as TracFone®.

\{\{General.CompanyName-short}} understands that you need access to your data regardless of whether you have an internet connection. The same consideration goes for users who have pre-paid for their cell phone minutes and do not want to use those minutes to refresh data on \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} mobile app.

For this reason, \{\{General.CompanyName-short}} has designed the mobile app to work in either online or offline \{\{mode}} and thus does not need to use your cell phone data while you are out in the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}.

If you own a prepaid device and want to ensure that the \{\{General.ProductName}} mobile app does not use your prepaid data, you can use the app settings within your device to restrict the app from using a data connection or to refresh data only when a Wi-Fi connection is available.

All actions required by \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} mobile app can be performed over a Wi-Fi connection, from downloading and installing the app to refreshing \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} data on a regular basis. Data on the mobile app refreshes automatically when the device is within Wi-Fi range, so any devices that are in Wi-Fi range on the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}—or anywhere with an established Wi-Fi connection—will receive the data refreshes
