[Zoetis](https://www.zoetisus.com/index.aspx) uses your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}'s genomic samples and their genomic testing tools to compile comprehensive genomic data. The genomic sample testing results build and provide insights to help optimize your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} management practices. When you enable this integration, you can submit and check your samples using \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}. Then, as soon as \{\{Partners.Zoetis}} returns the results, you can view your animal's genomic data directly in your \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}}s, or you can utilize the genomic data in your reports.
