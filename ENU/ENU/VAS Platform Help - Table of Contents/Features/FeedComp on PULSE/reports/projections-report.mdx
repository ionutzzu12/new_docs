\{\{Commands.UserActions}} the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompReportNames}}\{\{Controls.ControlTypes}} to view on-hand and future amounts of ingredients needed to accommodate upcoming feedings. Set the **Duration** to view projected ingredient amounts needed for feedings during the selected time period (daily, weekly, monthly, etc.), or check individual ingredients for upcoming **Run Out Dates.**

<ExpandableHeading indent="2">
  ## Search, Sort, and Filter the Projections Report

  Options to search and filter the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompReportNames}}\{\{Controls.ControlTypes}} are available at the top of the \{\{General.UI-gui}}. Follow the guidelines below when \{\{Commands.UserActions}} the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompReportNames}}\{\{Controls.ControlTypes}}:

  - \{\{Commands.Click}}**Filters** at the top left of the page to limit the data that is displayed. The **Filters** side panel opens.
  - Select a **Duration** to view data for a specific time frame.
  - Type text in the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Upper}}**\*\*\*\* Name** field to search the list of \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} by name.
  - \{\{Commands.UserActions}} an\*\*\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Upper}} \*\***Type** to limit the \{\{Controls.ControlTypes}} to a specific type of \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}. Multiple \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} can be \{\{Commands.UserActions}}. You can also type text directly in the field to search for \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}\{\{Controls.ControlTypes}}by name.
  - \{\{Commands.UserActions}} a \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Upper}} to limit the \{\{Controls.ControlTypes}} to a specific \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}. Multiple \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} can be \{\{Commands.UserActions}}. You can also type text directly in the field to search for \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} by name.
  - Check the **Show only premixes** box to limit the report to premix recipes. See :Link[Premixes]{label="Premixes" overridedLabel="Premixes" spaceId docId="S9LobXeMznI0BVBh155FJ" version="v2" docAnchorId="#premixes" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="FCP-Recipes.htm#premixes" href="../../../../../Recipes/Recipes/Recipes/Recipes/Recipes/Recipes/Recipes/Recipes/Recipes/Recipes/Recipes/Recipes/Recipes/Recipes/Recipes/Recipes/Recipes/Recipes/Recipes/Recipes/recipes.mdx"} to learn more.
  - \{\{Commands.UserActions}} the **Clear Filter\*\*\*\*s** option to clear all of the filters currently applied.
  - Select an option in **Sort By** at the top right of the page to change how the page is sorted.
  - Select an option in **View Rows Per Page** to change how many records are displayed on the page, and click the navigation options to jump to a specific page.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading indent="2">
  ## Change the Report View

  Select the preferred view for the \{\{Controls.ControlTypes}} at the top right of the \{\{General.UI-gui}}:

  - \{\{Commands.Click}}**Units** to view \{\{Controls.ControlTypes}} data in pounds or kilograms (based on the unit of measure specified for your [user account](<../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/User Menu/edit-your-user-account.mdx>)).
  - \{\{Commands.Click}}**Tons** to view \{\{Controls.ControlTypes}} data in tons.
</ExpandableHeading>

  ## View Ingredient Cards

  Follow the guidelines below when \{\{Commands.UserActions}}\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}\{\{Controls.ControlTypes}}:

  - \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Upper}}\{\{Controls.ControlTypes}}: The \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}\{\{Controls.ControlTypes}} is displayed at the top left of the \{\{Controls.ControlTypes}}.
  - Type: The ingredient type is displayed at the top right of the card in a color-coded label.:::ExpandableHeading\{customAnchorSlug="ingred-types"}

  ### Click here to learn more about ingredient types!

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    Dry Forag&#x65;**:&#x20;**&#x56;egetative material, such as stems, leaves, and stalks of plants, in a dried state that is used for feed.
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    Fresh Forage&#x73;**:&#x20;**&#x41; crop of cultivated plants or plant parts that are grazed or harvested as feed.

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    Silag&#x65;**:&#x20;**&#x50;reserved forage for feed, like pasture grass, alfalfa, grains, and corn, that is produced by fermenting plant matter.

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    Energ&#x79;**:&#x20;**&#x48;igh-calorie feed, such as corn, sorghum, barley, and wheat.

    Protei&#x6E;**:&#x20;**&#x48;igh-protein feed, such as soy and canola.

    Vitamins and Mineral&#x73;**:&#x20;**&#x53;upplements added to feed to prevent deficiencies in \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} cows that can impact health and milk production.
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    Additive&#x73;**:&#x20;**&#x50;roducts added to feed to enhance feed quality, make the feed more digestible, and improve health and performance.

  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    Wate&#x72;**:&#x20;**&#x57;ater added to a feed mixture to improve daily milk yield.

  - \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompLocation-singular}}: The name of the location where the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} is currently \{\{Commands.UserActions}}.
  - Projected Usage: A projection of the total ingredient amount needed to feed your animals over the **Duration** filter currently selected.
  - Run Out Date: The projected date when a tracked ingredient will run out. If the ingredient is not tracked, a "-" (dash) is displayed. If a feeder uses too much/little of an ingredient or the inventory is not accurate, this date may be incorrect. Be sure to check your actual inventory if this date seems unreasonable.
  - On-Hand Amount: \{\{General.Grammar}} current ingredient amount available for feeding at your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}. If the ingredient is not tracked, "Not tracked" is displayed.
