## View Event History

1. Filters: \{\{Commands.Click}}**Filters** at the top left of the page to limit the data that is displayed. The **Filters** side panel opens. You can filter the list by \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-OccurrenceType}} type, \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}}, and more. After you select the filters, click **Apply** in the bottom right corner to filter the list. If you choose a single \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-OccurrenceType}} filter, you can include \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-OccurrenceType}}-specific items in the list.
2. ID: The animal identifier. Click an animal's \{\{ID}} to view its details in a side panel, including its recent event history. From the side panel, you can also add an \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-OccurrenceType}} for that animal or click **Go To Events Page** to :Link[view the Events page on its CowCard]{label="view the Events page on its CowCard" overridedLabel="view the Events page on its CowCard" spaceId docId="sp1fQUQTHg-WLIRXAer4X" version="v2" docAnchorId="#GoToEventsPage" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../../Events/Events.htm#GoToEventsPage" href="../../Events/Events.htm#GoToEventsPage"}.
3. Default items: These item are shown in the list by default, including the animal's pen, lactation number, and reproduction status. You can customize the items included in the list.
4. Customize items: You can customize the items shown in the list: click the **Options** button

5. Export \{\{Worklists.ListType}}s: You can print and export the \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-OccurrenceType}} history as PDF or CSV for further analysis or for record keeping purposes. Click **Export&#x20;**\{\{Worklists.ListType}}**s** at the top right of the page to print and export the \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-OccurrenceType}} history.
6. Add events: In the animal details side panel, click **Add events** to add an \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-OccurrenceType}} for that animal. In the side panel, select the \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-OccurrenceType}} from the drop-down list, enter the \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-OccurrenceType}} details, and click **Save** to add the \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-OccurrenceType}}.
7. Go To \{\{Worklists.ListType}}s Page: In the animal details side panel, click **Go To Events Page** to :Link[view the Events page on its CowCard]{label="view the Events page on its CowCard" overridedLabel="view the Events page on its CowCard" spaceId docId="sp1fQUQTHg-WLIRXAer4X" version="v2" docAnchorId="#GoToEventsPage" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../../Events/Events.htm#GoToEventsPage" href="../../Events/Events.htm#GoToEventsPage"}.
