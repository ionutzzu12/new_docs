  ## Assign Alternate Recipes

  If your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} runs out of an ingredient, your feeding technician can select an \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} to keep your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} fed until the ingredient is restocked. An \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} can be used to add \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompWeighback-lower}} ingredients to a feed mix and help you reduce waste.

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ## Click here to learn more about alternate recipes!

    Follow the guidelines below when \{\{Commands.UserActions}}\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}:

    - A recipe cannot have more than one \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} assigned.
    - A recipe can be assigned as an \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} to multiple other recipes.
    - Inactive recipes cannot be assigned as an \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}.
    - Two recipes cannot be alternates for each other. For example, if recipe #4 is assigned as an \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} to recipe #3, recipe #3 cannot also be assigned as an \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} to recipe #4.
    - \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompRecipes}} currently using an alternate cannot be **activated** as an \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} in another recipe. For example, if recipe #1 is assigned to and activated in recipe #2, and if recipe #1 is then assigned to recipe #5, recipe #1 cannot be activated in recipe #5 until recipe #1 is deactivated in recipe #2.
    - \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompRecipes}} currently replacing other recipes cannot **activate** their \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}. For example, if recipe #1 is assigned to and activated in recipe #2, and if recipe #4 is then assigned to recipe #1, recipe #4 cannot be activated in recipe #1 until recipe #1 is deactivated in recipe #2.
  </ExpandableHeading>

  To assign alternate recipe options that can replace a recipe if the original recipe can't be made:

  1. Click **Assign&#x20;**\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompAlternateRecipe-singular}} in the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompAlternateRecipe-singular}} section of the page. The **Add&#x20;**\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompAlternateRecipe-singular}} side panel opens.
  2. \{\{Commands.Click}} the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} to \{\{Commands.UserActions}} in **Assign&#x20;**\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompAlternateRecipe-singular}}.
  3. \{\{Commands.UserActions}}\{\{Controls.ControlTypes}} at the bottom right of the side panel. When an alternate is active, that recipe's row is green in the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompAlternateRecipe-singular}} table.
</ExpandableHeading>
