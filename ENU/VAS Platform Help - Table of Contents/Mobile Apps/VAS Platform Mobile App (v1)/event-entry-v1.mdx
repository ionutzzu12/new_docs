  ### Required Permissions

  Only users who are assigned to a Role with \{\{RolesPermissions.PermissionNames}} permissions can use this feature.If your account only has \{\{RolesPermissions.PermissionNames}} permissions, you will **not** be able to use this feature. \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} allows you to create events, add animals to them, and process your changes—all from the mobile app while you are out on the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}. Add events to one or more animals, either by searching for them manually or using [a scanner](work-with-rfid-scanners-v1.mdx). When you finish adding animals to an event, tap \{\{Mobile-UI.EventProcessChangesv1}} to send your updates back to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}and then on to \{\{DC-UI.DC-ProdNameShort}} (\{\{Mobile-UI.ConnectionRequired}}).

See [Event Definitions](<../../Features/Manage Your Animals/Events/hHY8-event-definitions.mdx>) for a full list of standard\{\{masterVAS-Products.MyDC-short}} events available in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}. \{\{masterVAS-Products.DCUnlimited-short}}\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}} may have additional custom events.
