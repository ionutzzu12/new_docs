\{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} — the world's most trusted \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} management solution — is available in two versions: \{\{General.DCUnlimited-short}} and \{\{masterVAS-Products.MyDC-short}}. Though both versions include the ability to access your data from any current device with a web browser, there are some differences you should know to make the most of your \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.

Expand the sections below to learn more:

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## DairyComp Unlimited

  \{\{masterVAS-Products.DCUnlimited-short}}includes \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-long}}, \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, and the mobile app. With \{\{masterVAS-Products.DCUnlimited-short}}, you can enter \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} data into \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-long}} and then review that data or generate reports using \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} or the mobile app. With \{\{masterVAS-Products.DCUnlimited-short}}, you also have access to our full list of partner-product integrations, dashboard widgets, and additional reporting options at no additional cost.

  For \{\{masterVAS-Products.DCUnlimited-short}} support, you can reach us by phone, email, text, or chat. Contact [VAS Support](http://platform-docs.vas.com/default.htm#cshid=1004) for more details.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## MyDC on PULSE

  \{\{masterVAS-Products.MyDC-short}} includes \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} and the mobile app. Data entry and retrieval are performed entirely through \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} using a web browser or the mobile app. \{\{masterVAS-Products.MyDC-short}} offers a powerful solution at a lower per-animal cost and can be integrated with \{\{masterVAS.CompanyName-short}}\{\{masterVAS-Products.WC-Platform}} and \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedWatchPlatform}} software. You also have a suite of structured reports and partner-product integrations available through \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.

  For \{\{masterVAS-Products.MyDC-short}} support, you can reach us by phone, email, text, or chat. Contact [VAS Support](http://platform-docs.vas.com/default.htm#cshid=1004) for more details.
</ExpandableHeading>
