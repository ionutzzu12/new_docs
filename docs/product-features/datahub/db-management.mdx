You can manage the timeaseries database, called `tsdata` , from the *DB Management pane&#x20;*&#x6F;f the DataHub module. Your edge device communicates with this database through port `8086`.

# Access DataHub DB Management UI

**To access DB Management:**

1. Log in to Litmus Edge.
2. From the *Navigation&#x20;*&#x70;anel, navigate to **DataHub&#x20;**>**&#x20;DB Management**.
   The *DB Management* pane appears.
