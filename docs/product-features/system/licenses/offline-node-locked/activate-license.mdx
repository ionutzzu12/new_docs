If your Litmus Edge doesn't have an internet connection, you can activate a license offline.&#x20;

# Before You Begin

Before completing the steps below, make sure you do the following:

- Have an Ethernet connection for your first bootup of the Litmus Edge device after installation.
- Synchronize the time for the Litmus device. See [Time Synchronization Issues](../../../../troubleshooting/system-troublehsooting/time-synchronization-issues.mdx) to learn more.&#x20;
- Have access to a browser that is connected to the internet to complete this activation.
- Obtain the license key from Litmus that will be used to activate the license.&#x20;
- If your Internet browser is on a separate computer, use a text file to copy the Activation Key and Offline Activation Key so you can transport the information.

# Activate License Offline

Refer to the appropriate steps depending on if your current license is expired or not.&#x20;

## Activate a License Offline if Your Current License is Not Expired

**To activate a license without an internet connection if your current license is not expired:**

1. Navigate to **System&#x20;***>&#x20;***Activation**.&#x20;
2. Click the **License&#x20;**&#x74;ab.&#x20;
3. From the *Node-Locked License* section, click **Add License**.
   The *Add a New License* form appears.
   ![](https://archbee-image-uploads.s3.amazonaws.com/SSUUxKZUk9bFTEPNn_6Zo/iBr9N4al-D0t69wK1s_AN_add-license-button.png)
4. Click the **Offline License Activation** tab.
   The *Activation Key&#x20;*&#x61;nd *Offline Activation Key* fields appear.
   ![](https://archbee-image-uploads.s3.amazonaws.com/SSUUxKZUk9bFTEPNn_6Zo/LfAxsSWTNK3x2A3RFc1jm_offline-license-key.png)
5. Paste or enter the license key obtained from Litmus in the **Activation Key** field. Then, click **Generate Request**.
   The *Offline Activation Request&#x20;*&#x76;alue appears.
   ![](https://archbee-image-uploads.s3.amazonaws.com/SSUUxKZUk9bFTEPNn_6Zo/55xejQBqFEUctV1JeXTC1_generate-request.png)
6. Click the **Copy&#x20;**&#x69;con for the *Offline Activation Request*.
   ![](https://archbee-image-uploads.s3.amazonaws.com/SSUUxKZUk9bFTEPNn_6Zo/yBa5b3dWZcAz1CRudlGCB_copy-offline-request.png)
7. Click **Open License Portal**.&#x20;
   The *Licensing Portal* opens in a new browser tab.&#x20;
8. In the **Activation Request** field paste th&#x65;*&#x20;Offline Activation Request&#x20;*&#x63;opied in step 5.&#x20;
9. Click th&#x65;**&#x20;I'm not a robot** check box. Then, click **Make License**.&#x20;
10. Click the **Copy&#x20;**&#x69;con for the *Activation Result*.&#x20;
11. Return to the browser tab with Litmus Edge and paste the *Activation Result&#x20;*&#x69;n the **Offline Activation Key** field.&#x20;
12. Click **Submit**.&#x20;
    Your license is now activated.&#x20;

## Activate a License Offline if Your Current License is Expired

**To activate a license without an Internet connection if your current license is expired:**

1. Log in to Litmus Edge. &#x20;
2. Click **License Settings**.&#x20;
   ![](https://archbee-image-uploads.s3.amazonaws.com/SSUUxKZUk9bFTEPNn_6Zo/M8h-6ZRfkhaPAR7zJprY9_license-settings-button.png)
3. From the *Node-Locked License* section, click **Add License**.
   The *Add a New License* form appears.
   ![](https://archbee-image-uploads.s3.amazonaws.com/SSUUxKZUk9bFTEPNn_6Zo/lRVzYpS3xufwwtVqlvqJK_add-license-button-2.png)
4. Click the **Offline License Activation** tab.
   The *Activation Key* and *Offline Activation Key* fields appear.
   ![](https://archbee-image-uploads.s3.amazonaws.com/SSUUxKZUk9bFTEPNn_6Zo/0drXb72dDQg3SFrjzJAAJ_offline-license-key-2.png)
5. Paste or enter the license key in the **Activation Key** field. Then, click **Generate Request**.
   Th&#x65;*&#x20;Offline Activation Request* value appears.
   ![](https://archbee-image-uploads.s3.amazonaws.com/SSUUxKZUk9bFTEPNn_6Zo/EAtMwYfeBajHjOci3GAqh_generate-request-2.png)
6. Click the **Copy&#x20;**&#x69;con for the *Offline Activation Request*.
   ![](https://archbee-image-uploads.s3.amazonaws.com/SSUUxKZUk9bFTEPNn_6Zo/FXs3-Ya5URzoN4lqwoNgf_offline-license-key-3.png)
7. Click **Open License Portal**.&#x20;
   The *Licensing Portal* opens in a new browser tab.&#x20;
8. In the **Activation Request** field paste th&#x65;*&#x20;Offline Activation Request&#x20;*&#x63;opied in step 5.&#x20;
9. Click th&#x65;**&#x20;I'm not a robot** check box. Then, click **Make License**.&#x20;
10. Click the **Copy&#x20;**&#x69;con for the *Activation Result*.&#x20;
11. Return to the browser tab with Litmus Edge and paste the *Activation Result&#x20;*&#x69;n the **Offline Activation Key** field.&#x20;
12. Click **Submit**.&#x20;
    Your license is now activated.&#x20;
