<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Required Permissions

  To use this feature, your \{\{General.ProductName}} user account must be assigned to a Role with \{\{RolesPermissions.PermissionNames}} permissions. See [Edit Dairy User Permissions](../manage-dairy-users-and-roles/edit-dairy-user-permissions.mdx) and [Manage Dairy Roles](<../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Manage Dairy Users and Roles/manage-dairy-roles.mdx>) for more information.
</ExpandableHeading>

\{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} includes a **My Subscriptions** page that allows you to freely cancel and resume your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} subscriptions at any time.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/JinAWhJBHZgiSEE_SdoRD_mysubs-dairycards.png" size="43" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/users-roles/MySubs-dairycards.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/users-roles/MySubs-dairycards.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Click here to learn more about your subscription details!

  - \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}} \{\{ID}}**:** Unique identification number assigned to your subscription in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.
  - **Herdcode:** Unique alpha-numeric identifier assigned to your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}.
  - **Status:** Current subscription status will show either **Active**, **Suspended**, or **Past&#x20;**\{\{Due}}.
  - **Next Billing Date:** Date when your subscription will be charged.
  - **Subscription End:** Date when your subscription period ends, typically the day before your Next Billing Date.
  - **Subscription Owner:** Name of the subscriber who is billed for the subscription.
  - **Product:** Subscribed \{\{masterVAS.CompanyName-short}} product.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## View or Edit Subscriptions

  Select **My Subscriptions** from the [User Menu](<../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/features/user-menu.mdx>) in the top right corner of the page to view your subscriptions.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/Z2YYB8RRVxj9h38PXKwI2_user-menu-sub.png" size="24" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../ContentShare/MySubscriptions/user-menu-sub.png" githubPath="Content/ContentShare/MySubscriptions/user-menu-sub.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  - An active subscription with no payment issues is shown in a card with a light blue header.
  - A user-suspended subscription has a light gray header and the status is "Suspended".
  - A subscription with a payment issue has a red header.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/8WbN_J103gB2cyvRWNEOU_suspendedsubscards.png" size="75" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../ContentShare/MySubscriptions/suspended_subs_cards.png" githubPath="Content/ContentShare/MySubscriptions/suspended_subs_cards.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  Click the **Edit** button to open the billing information page where you can edit payment methods, billing and shipping information, or view invoices.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/5vpvbOES5J_EDQTAXVDjg_billing-info.png" size="50" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../ContentShare/MySubscriptions/billing-info.png" githubPath="Content/ContentShare/MySubscriptions/billing-info.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  You can click on an invoice to view invoice details and download invoices or receipts in PDF format.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/9xtCV1nkeOpppFjXelbVU_view-invoice.png" size="54" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../ContentShare/MySubscriptions/view-invoice.png" githubPath="Content/ContentShare/MySubscriptions/view-invoice.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Suspend or Resume Subscriptions

  On the **My Subscriptions** page, you can suspend your subscription and resume it when you're ready. \{\{masterVAS.CompanyName-short}} requires that you pay any outstanding invoices before you can resume a subscription.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Subscription Payment Issues

  If there is an issue with your \{\{masterVAS-Products.MyDC-short}} subscription, such as an overdue payment, a red banner displays at the bottom of your \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} homepage. Click in the banner to open the **My Subscriptions** page and view more details.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/FhbGjPWD54936g9SvmDaX_mydc-sub-issue.png" size="63" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../ContentShare/MySubscriptions/mydc-sub-issue.png" githubPath="Content/ContentShare/MySubscriptions/mydc-sub-issue.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  If an invoice remains unpaid after 60 days, \{\{masterVAS.CompanyName-short}} will suspend your \{\{masterVAS-Products.MyDC-short}} subscription. The subscription card will show as "Suspended" and you can click in the gray warning box to pay the outstanding invoice.

  <hint type="info">
    **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x4F;nly users with \{\{RolesPermissions.PermissionNames}} permissions can see the red \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} subscription cards indicating a payment issue.
  </hint>
</ExpandableHeading>
