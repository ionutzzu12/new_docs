<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Required Permissions

  Only users who are assigned to a Role with \{\{RolesPermissions.PermissionNames}} permissions can use this feature. See [Edit Dairy User Permissions](../Features/Config/UserMgmtRoles/EditUserPermissions.htm) and [Manage Dairy Roles](manage-dairy-roles.mdx) for more information.
</ExpandableHeading>

The \{\{MorphHelp.User\_Management\_UI\_item}} feature allows you to add new users and invite them to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, to your company, and to your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}}. This topic explains how to add and invite users to your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/fGZUZvUy-n8MTxH10N5zh_user-add-dairy.png" size="57" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Platform/users-roles/user-add-dairy.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/users-roles/user-add-dairy.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

You can find this feature under the :Link[Dairy menu]{label="Dairy menu" overridedLabel="Dairy menu" spaceId docId="WFLX7SPMpZfy7zoMyrvRh" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Menu-Dairy" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../NavPlatform/Menu.htm#Menu-Dairy" href="../../../ENU/VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/navigate-pulse/menu-options.mdx"}. If you need to invite a user to your company, see :Link[Add and Invite a New User to Your Company]{label="Add and Invite a New User to Your Company" overridedLabel="Add and Invite a New User to Your Company" spaceId docId="ZBxNM9nH4pjlw1ThsUy51" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Invite_a_User_to_your_Company" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../Features/Company/CompanyUserMgmt.htm#Invite_a_User_to_your_Company" href="../Features/Company/CompanyUserMgmt.htm#Invite_a_User_to_your_Company"}.

1. \{\{Commands.Click}} the **Add New User** button at the top right side of the **Users** tab.If you are connected to more than one \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}, be aware that new users are added and invited to the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} currently selected.
2. Enter the user's **Email** address.If a \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} account already exists for the email address entered, a message is displayed. Click the link in the message to preview the user account for the email address entered.
3. In the **Roles** list, check the boxes to select the roles to assign to the user's account.The roles selected control how much or how little a user can do with the data in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}. You can edit role assignments at any time; see [Edit Dairy User Permissions](../Features/Config/UserMgmtRoles/EditUserPermissions.htm) for more information. Each role checked is displayed in the **Roles Assigned** list. You can click the **X** on the right side of a role to unassign it.
4. Click the **Invite User** button at the bottom right side of the page.

\{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} sends the user an invitation email, which they can then use to create a \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} account (if they don't already have one). If they already have a \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} account, they will be able to access your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} once they accept your invitation.
