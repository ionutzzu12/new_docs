  ## Track Ingredients at a Location

  When an ingredient location is tracked, its inventory is actively managed in \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}}. Tracking begins when inventory is added to a location, ensuring stock levels are continuously monitored and accurately reflected in the system. Follow the steps below to continue:

  1. \{\{Commands.Click}} the **Details** link at the top right of an\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} card to \{\{Commands.UserActions}} the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}'s details. The \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Upper}} \{\{Controls.ControlTypes}}\{\{General.UI-gui}} opens.
  2. \{\{Commands.Click}}\{\{Commands.UserActions}} \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Upper}} at the top right of the page. The \{\{Commands.UserActions}} \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Upper}}\{\{General.UI-gui}} opens, and all locations associated with the ingredient are displayed under \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompLocations}}.
  3. \{\{Commands.Click}} the **Track this&#x20;**\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompLocation-singular}} toggle to track the ingredient in the location.:::hint\{type="info"}
     **NOTE:&#x20;**&#xA;\{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}}
     &#x20;adds a new Adjustment transaction to the&#x20;
     :Link[Transactions]{label="Transactions" overridedLabel="Transactions" spaceId docId="J6muKg_EJrb83_p29HqUP" version="v2" docAnchorId="#transactions" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="FCP-Inventory.htm#transactions" href="../../../Inventory/Inventory/Inventory/Inventory/Inventory/Inventory/Inventory/Inventory/Inventory/Inventory/Inventory/Inventory/Inventory/Inventory/Inventory/Inventory/Inventory/Inventory/Inventory/Inventory/inventory.mdx"}

       \:::
  4. In **On-Hand Amount**, enter \{\{General.Grammar}} current ingredient amount available at the location, and then \{\{Commands.UserActions}} the **Unit** or **Ton** option.
