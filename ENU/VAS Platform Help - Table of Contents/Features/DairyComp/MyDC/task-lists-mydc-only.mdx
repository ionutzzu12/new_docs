Use \{\{Buttons-UI.FeaturesSecondary}} to consolidate all required animal activities for the current day, including all \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.ovsynch}}\{\{Protocols.Protocol-common}}s, wellness checks, and any other scheduled \{\{Worklists.taskCommonUse}}s. Use [Task Builder](<Task Lists (MyDC only)/build-a-task-list.mdx>) to generate a custom \{\{Worklists.taskCommonUse}} list, or choose one of your saved lists from the [Task Lists Gallery](TaskListsGallery.htm). [Process Open Tasks (MyDC Only)](ProcessOpenTasks.htm) to review and complete the open tasks on your list. Then use the [Task Lists History](TaskListsHistory.htm) page to view completed tasks and ensure that all tasks followed \{\{Protocols.Protocol-common}}.

You can find this feature under \{\{Buttons-UI.FeaturesMain}} > \{\{Buttons-UI.FeaturesSecondary}}.
