Existing **AI Model** connections can be updated or removed as required.

# Update Connections

1. Locate the connection you wish to modify in the *AI Model's* list.

<Image src="https://api.archbee.com/api/optimize/SSUUxKZUk9bFTEPNn_6Zo/rmadew4y9hRMKZy0zW_1S_image.png" size="80" width="1827" height="423" position="flex-start" showCaption="false" indent="2"></Image>

2. To edit connection details:
   - Click the three dots icon next to the connection and click **Edit**.
   - Update the necessary fields such as **API&#x20;**&#x6B;ey, **URL**, or **Model ID**.
   - Click **Save&#x20;**&#x6F;r **Update**.

<Image src="https://api.archbee.com/api/optimize/SSUUxKZUk9bFTEPNn_6Zo/Ripe82QbQEbp2z0tG0kHh_image.png" size="50" width="981" height="617" position="flex-start" showCaption="false" indent="3"></Image>

<Paragraph listStyleType="disc" listStart="4" indent="2">
  After making changes, run the verification process again to confirm configuration integrity.
</Paragraph>

3. To remove a connection:&#x20;
   - Click the three dots icon next to the connection and click **Remove**.
   - Confirm the deleting by using the **Delete&#x20;**&#x6F;ption to delete the connection.
