smaXtec worklists are generated based on event data pulled from \{\{Partners.smaXtec}} every 6 hours. If a health or reproduction \{\{worklist}} already exists, any new or updated animal information is added to the existing list. If no worklist exists, a new list is generated.

## smaXtec: Health List

This worklist captures all health-related events, including the following.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/7U_fH1r1HEyK4EH3TswTJ_smaxtec-worklists-healthlist.png" size="63" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Partners/smaxtec-worklists-healthlist.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Partners/smaxtec-worklists-healthlist.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

### Activity

- Drop in activity
- Increase in activity

### Drinking

- Insufficient water intake
- Increase in drinking cycles
- Reduced drinking cycles

### Feeding\* (premium bolus only)

- Feed efficiency
- Risk of \{\{acidosis}}

### pH Variation\* (premium bolus only)

- Increase in average pH
- Drop in average pH
- Increase in 12h pH
- Drop in 12h pH

### Heat Stress

\{\{Partners.smaXtec}} uses Temperature-Humidity Index (THI) data to track heat-related stress on the animal. The higher the value, the greater the heat stress.

- THI ≥ 72 Heat stress
- THI ≥ 78 Heat stress
- THI ≥ 82 Heat stress

### Temperature

- Temperature increase
- Temperature drop

## smaXtec: Reproduction List

This worklist captures all reproduction-related events, including the following.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/F-xa_IR3W2pF4ktnOkflQ_smaxtec-worklists-reprolist.png" size="64" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Partners/smaxtec-worklists-reprolist.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Partners/smaxtec-worklists-reprolist.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

### Calving

- Dry off
- Indication of imminent calving
- Detection of a calving
- Confirmation of a calving
- Abortion of a pregnancy which can be a late abort

### Fertility

- Detection of a heat
- Inseminated of an animal
- A positive or negative pregnancy result
- Oestrus (heat)
