You can manage \{\{masterVAS-Products.AllProductNames}}\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} on the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompButtons}}\{\{General.UI-gui}}. \{\{Mobile-UI.ActiveMobile}}\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}is displayed by default. Tap the \{\{Mobile-UI.InactiveMobile}} filter to see more.

Expand the sections below to learn more:

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Create a Delivery or Pickup Transaction

  <hint type="info">
    **TIP:&#x20;**&#x42;efore you create a delivery or pickup, a [Vendor](<../../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Mobile Apps/FeedComp App/Make Adjustments/Inventory/vendors.mdx>) must be assigned to the [Ingredient](FCM-Ingredients.htm). See [Before You Begin: Required Setup](<../../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Mobile Apps/FeedComp App/before-you-begin-required-setup.mdx>) to learn more about the required setup order.
  </hint>

  1. Tap on the ingredient to create a transaction for it.
  2. Tap `Create Transaction` at the bottom of the screen.
  3. \{\{Commands.UserActions}} the transaction **Type**: **Delivery** or**Pickup**.
  4. \{\{Commands.UserActions}} the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} details, \{\{Commands.UserActions}}`Save` at the bottom of the screen.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Make a Physical Adjustment on Inventory

  1. Tap on the ingredient to make an adjustment.
  2. Tap **Physical Inventory Adjustment** at the bottom of the screen.
  3. \{\{Commands.UserActions}} the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} details, \{\{Commands.UserActions}}`Save` at the bottom of the screen.
</ExpandableHeading>
