  ## Create New Location

  \{\{Commands.Click}}\{\{Commands.UserActions}} \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Upper}} at the top right of the page to open the side panel and \{\{Commands.UserActions}} information for the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}.

  - The \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Upper}}**&#x20;Name**\{\{Commands.UserActions}} must be unique and cannot match the name of existing \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}.
  - \{\{Commands.Click}} the \{\{Mobile-UI.ActiveMobile}} \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Upper}} toggle to \{\{Commands.UserActions}} the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}.
  - Once you've added the information, click **Save**.
</ExpandableHeading>

    3. \{\{Commands.Click}}**Save**.

<ExpandableHeading indent="2">
  ## View Ingredients at a Location

  1. \{\{Commands.Click}} the name of the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} to \{\{Commands.UserActions}}. The \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Upper}}\{\{General.UI-gui}} opens, and all ingredients associated with the location are displayed under \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompIngredients}}.
  2. Click the arrow button **>** on the right side of an ingredient to view the ingredient's details on the [Ingredients](FCP-Ingredients.htm) page.

  <hint type="info">
    **TIP:&#x20;**&#x4E;eed to move ingredients to a new location? See :Link[Transfer Ingredients to Other Locations]{label="Transfer Ingredients to Other Locations" overridedLabel="Transfer Ingredients to Other Locations" spaceId docId="45h2OeK29wT6G1rvc-0qq" version="v2" docAnchorId="#transfer" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="45h2-locations.mdx"} for more information.

<ExpandableHeading indent="2">
  ## Transfer Ingredients to Other Locations

  1. \{\{Commands.Click}} the location from which the ingredient needs to be moved. The \{\{Commands.UserActions}} \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Upper}}\{\{General.UI-gui}} opens, and all ingredients associated with the location are displayed under \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompIngredients}}.
  2. Check the box for one or more \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} to \{\{Commands.UserActions}}. Or, check the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Upper}}**&#x20;Name** box to select all \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}.
  3. Click **Transfer&#x20;**\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Upper}}**(s)**

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/8UL-rj3gMo-yptPuyNEpc_transfer.png" size="10" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/feedcomp-img/icons/transfer.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/feedcomp-img/icons/transfer.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false" indent="2"></Image>

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    . The \{\{Commands.UserActions}} \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Upper}}\{\{General.UI-gui}} opens.
  </Paragraph>

  4. Select the new \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}, \{\{Commands.Click}}**Save**.

  <hint type="info">
    **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x49;f the location selected already contains the ingredient being transferred, \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} will automatically update the \{\{DM%}} and the **Cost** for the ingredient.
  </hint>
