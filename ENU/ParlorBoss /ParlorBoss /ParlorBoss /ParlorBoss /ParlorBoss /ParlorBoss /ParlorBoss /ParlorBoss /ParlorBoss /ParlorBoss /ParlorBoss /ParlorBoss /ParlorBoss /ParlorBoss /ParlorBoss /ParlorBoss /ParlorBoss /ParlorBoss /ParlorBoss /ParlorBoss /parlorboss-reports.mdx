  ### Required Setup

  <hint type="warning">
    **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Products.ParlorBoss}} version 2.8.9 or higher must be installed and integrated with \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-simple}} at your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} for reporting to be available. Contact Us to get started! After the integration is activated, configure your \{\{Partners.parlor}} and milking shifts in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}. The \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompButtons}}\{\{General.UI-gui}} allows you to view \{\{masterVAS-Products.ParlorBoss}} efficiency and animal identification data in \{\{masterVAS-Products.AllProductNames}} to help optimize your \{\{Partners.parlor}} operations. This information can help you review your management strategies to improve efficiencies for specific \{\{Partners.parlor}}s, shifts, or \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}}.
