Before you can include any \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} genomic data in your \{\{DC-UI.DC-ProdNameShort}} reports, you must first add \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} genomic items to \{\{DC-UI.DC-ProdNameShort}}. Follow the steps below to continue:

<hint type="warning">
  **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**&#x4F;nly add genomic items that you frequently use for reports. The item table in \{\{DC-UI.DC-ProdNameShort}} has limited space. You can view all genomic items on your animals' \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}}s in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} at any time.
</hint>

1. In \{\{DC-UI.DC-ProdNameShort}}, enter **PLATFORM** in the command line to open the \{\{General.CompanyName-short}} \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}**&#x20;Setup** window.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/VwWEv_rPzInFkC-bWbx4c_zoetis-pulse-items.png" size="47" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Partners/zoetis/zoetis-pulse-items.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Partners/zoetis/zoetis-pulse-items.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false" indent="2"></Image>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  Click the **Pulse Items** tab.
</Paragraph>

3. Click the **+ Genomics** button to expand the list.
4. Map or add a genomic trait:\* **Map Existing Item:** To map a genomic trait to an existing \{\{DC-UI.DC-ProdNameShort}} item, double-click the trait. Select the existing item, then click **OK** to confirm your selection to map the trait to the item.
   - **Add New Item:** To create a new item for a genomic trait in \{\{DC-UI.DC-ProdNameShort}}, check the box for the trait. Click **Create (1) Items**, then confirm your selection to add the item to the \{\{DC-UI.DC-ProdNameShort}} database.

After you store the items, proceed to [Step 3: Map Genomic Items in PULSE](step-3-map-genomic-items-in-pulse.mdx) to map the genomic items in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.
