<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Required Setup

  To generate \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}s based on your \{\{Partners.PartnerName}} measurement values, your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} must first activate the integration. See [Activate Nedap SmartSight Integration](smartsight-integrate.htm) to learn more.
</ExpandableHeading>

Use \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}'s [Report/Worklist Builder](../../Platform/Features/DairyComp/ReportsWorklists/ReportWorklistBuilder.htm) or \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}} to generate reports or \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}s based on locomotion or BCS values.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Sample Report to View Average BCS by Pen

  SUMPEN BCSFORBCS>0BYPEN
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Sample Worklist Command

  List of animals with a locomotion score of 50 or greater \{\{today}} or yesterday by \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}}:

  LIST ID PEN LMSFD LMSFT LMSVFOR LMSV>50 LMSFD>-2BYPEN
</ExpandableHeading>

<hint type="warning">
  **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**&#x49;f generating the \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}} in \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}}, be sure to add the `\K` switch to send the list to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.
</hint>

See [Create To-Do Lists](../../Platform/Features/DairyComp/ReportsWorklists/CreateToDoLists.htm) to learn more about creating custom \{\{Worklists.ToDoCommonUse}}s. After you create your \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}, you'll then be able to [Complete Tasks (v1)](<../../../../Mobile Apps/VAS Platform Mobile App (v1)/Tasks & Worklists (v1)/complete-tasks-v1.mdx>) or [Complete Tasks (v2)](../../Platform/DC-Mobile/MobAppTasks.htm).
