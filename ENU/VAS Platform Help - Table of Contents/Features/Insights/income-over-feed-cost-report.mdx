    ### How are pens with multiple types included in the calculations?

    \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.P-ens-}} with multiple types are counted under the highest priority type to avoid double-counting.\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.P-en-}} type priorities are as follows:

    1. Milking
    2. Bullpen
    3. \{\{AI}}
    4. Dry
    5. Hospital
    6. Calf
    7. Heifer
    8. User

    For example, if a \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} is both an AI and Heifer \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}}, it will be included in the AI \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} type calculation.
    \:::

    \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompIOFC-short}} by Pen: View feed cost and milk production details in a table format:\* IOFC: Daily income for the pen. This calculation is based on the current Milk Price setting, Daily Milk Production, and \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} Feed Cost data from \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}}. Non-milking \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}} have a negative value.

    - Daily Milk Production: Volume of milk produced by the pen per day. The source for this data is controlled by the :Link[Milk Weight Source]{label="Milk Weight Source" overridedLabel="Milk Weight Source" spaceId docId="PLJ_MjgZ7i2z-J5HAcxqq" version="v2" docAnchorId="#source" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="income-over-feed-cost-report.mdx"} option at the top of the report.

    <hint type="info" indent="2">
      **TIP:&#x20;**&#x46;or the most accurate \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompIOFC-short}} value, use your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}'s daily milk values or \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.t-est-day}} data as the basis for this report.
    </hint>

    <Paragraph indent="1">
      Revenue: Income from milk production for the pen. This calculation is based on the current :Link[Milk Price]{label="Milk Price" overridedLabel="Milk Price" spaceId docId="K77SQAyesiT4G_MfVPVsv" version="v2" docAnchorId="#price" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../../Insights/InsightsSettings.htm#price" href="../../Insights/InsightsSettings.htm#price"} setting and Daily Milk Production. Non-milking \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}} have $0 revenue.
    </Paragraph>

    - Daily Feed Cost: Cost of feed for the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} from \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}}, which is based on the :Link[Actual Load Amounts]{label="Actual Load Amounts" overridedLabel="Actual Load Amounts" spaceId docId="d3RdNSQfqdWmCWum10l6J" version="v2" docAnchorId="#actual" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="FCP-Reports-FeedHistory.htm#actual" href="FCP-Reports-FeedHistory.htm#actual"} and \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompIngredient-singular}} costs for completed \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeedings}}. See [Feed History Report](FCP-Reports-FeedHistory.htm) to learn more.
    - Feeding Count: Total number of animals being fed in the pen.
