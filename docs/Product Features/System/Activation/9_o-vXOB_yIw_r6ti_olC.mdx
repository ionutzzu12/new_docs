To deactivate a device, navigate to **System&#x20;**> **Activation**. From the *Cloud Activation* section, click **Deactivate**.

![](https://archbee-image-uploads.s3.amazonaws.com/SSUUxKZUk9bFTEPNn_6Zo/WPl0yKrMb82Y4AK6kL_hB_image.png)

<hint type="warning">
  **Important**:**&#xA0;**&#x41; system reboot is required after device deactivation.&#x20;
</hint>
