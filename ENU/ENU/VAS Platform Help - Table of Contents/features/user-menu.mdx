Your User Menu is located at the top right of \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}. Click the drop‑down arrow to view your options.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/QXUYTx1LoQ54pckI9MoAa_user-menu.png" size="22" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/users-roles/user-menu.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/users-roles/user-menu.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

- **My Home:** Navigate to your User Account \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-omepage}}.
- **My Subscriptions:** Navigate to your [My Subscriptions](../MySubscriptions/MySubscriptions.htm) page.
- **Message Center:** Navigate to the [Message Center](<../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/User Menu/message-center.mdx>).
- **Account Settings:** Edit your \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} user account settings. See [Edit Your User Account](<../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/User Menu/edit-your-user-account.mdx>) for more details.
- **Log Out:** Log out of the \{\{General.ProductName-short}}.
