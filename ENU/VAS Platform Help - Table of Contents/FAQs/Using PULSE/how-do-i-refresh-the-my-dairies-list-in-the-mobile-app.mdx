The \{\{Mobile-UI.MyDairiesList}} list is displayed when you first log in to the app, and you can return to it at any time. Tap on the \{\{Mobile-UI.MoreTab}} tab at the bottom right of the screen, and then tap \{\{Mobile-UI.SwitchDairyInfo}}

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/5rBKpzy7q707GIONY7HMJ_switchdairy-icon26x25.png" size="10" caption="Switch to Another Dairy icon" alt="Switch to Another Dairy icon" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Platform/dc-mobile-img/switchdairy-icon_26x25.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/dc-mobile-img/switchdairy-icon_26x25.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="true"></Image>

.

If the \{\{Mobile-UI.MyDairiesList}} list does not appear to be up to date, swipe down from the top of the screen to reload the list.
