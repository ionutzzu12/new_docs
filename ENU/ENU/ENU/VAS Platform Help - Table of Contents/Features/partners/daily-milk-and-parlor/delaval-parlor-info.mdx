  ### Can't find this feature? Make sure it's included in your subscription!

  This feature/option is available only for \{\{masterVAS-Products.DCP-users-short}}. \{\{masterVAS-Products.DCU-users-short}} will not see it in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} because this functionality is controlled in \{\{masterVAS-Products.OnPremise-lower}}\{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-simple}} rather than in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.

  Click [here](<../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Get Started/know-your-vas-pulse-version.mdx>) to learn more about the different versions of \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.
</ExpandableHeading>

\{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}\{\{Partners.Parlor}} integrations allow \{\{masterVAS-Products.MyDC-short}}\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}} to capture data from their milk sensors. This allows you to view daily milk and shift data details in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} for analysis tasks. See [Connect Parlor Integration and Configure Shifts (MyDC)](<../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Partners/Parlors/connect-parlor-integration-and-configure-shifts-mydc.mdx>) to get started, and then [View Parlor Data and Build Reports](<../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Partners/Parlors/view-parlor-data-and-build-reports.mdx>). After your \{\{Partners.Parlor}} data is received by \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, you can also view milk data on your animals' [Daily Milk](<../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Manage Your Animals/daily-milk.mdx>)\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}} tab.
