  ## Search and Filter the Parlor Speed Report

  Options to search and filter the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompReportNames}}\{\{Controls.ControlTypes}} are available at the top of the \{\{General.UI-gui}}. Follow the guidelines below when \{\{Commands.UserActions}} the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompReportNames}}\{\{Controls.ControlTypes}}:

  - \{\{Commands.Click}}**Filters** at the top left of the page to limit the data that is displayed. The **Filters** side panel opens.
  - Use the **Date&#x20;****From** and **Date&#x20;****To** filters to only view data from that range.
  - \{\{Commands.UserActions}} a \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Upper}} to limit the \{\{Controls.ControlTypes}} to a specific \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}.
  - \{\{Commands.UserActions}} the **Clear Filter\*\*\*\*s** option to clear all of the filters currently applied.

<ExpandableHeading indent="2">
  ## View Report Summary

  An overall summary is displayed in a **GRAY** bar at the top left of the \{\{General.UI-gui}} based on the current **Filters**.

  - Fastest Turn: The fastest turn of the \{\{Partners.parlor}}.
  - Avg Time per Turn: The average amount of time recorded for all turns of the \{\{Partners.parlor}}.
  - Avg Cows per Hour: The average number of cows milked per hour.
  - Avg Parlor Visit Time: Average time from \{\{Partners.parlor}} entry to departure per cow, including any multiple turns. This time also includes all animal prep time, post-dipping, and gate sorting activities as cows leave the \{\{Partners.parlor}}.
  - Fastest Turn/Avg Parlor Visit: Compares the fastest turn of the \{\{Partners.parlor}} to the average \{\{Partners.parlor}} visit time per cow. The higher the percentage, the more efficiently your cows are making their way through the \{\{Partners.parlor}}. If you notice this value decreasing, you may want to evaluate animal prep routines, milking equipment attachment methods, and other \{\{Partners.parlor}} activities for potential improvement opportunities.

  \{\{Partners.Parlor}} speed is also summarized in a table below the **GRAY** bar.
</ExpandableHeading>
