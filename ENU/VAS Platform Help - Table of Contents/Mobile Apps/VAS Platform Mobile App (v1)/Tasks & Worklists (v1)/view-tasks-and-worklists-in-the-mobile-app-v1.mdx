  ### I don't see the Tasks button. How do I find it?

  If you do not see the \{\{Mobile-UI.Feature-Names}} button at the bottom of your screen, tap the Back arrow at the top left of the page until you return to one of the main feature screens. From there, you'll find the feature buttons at the bottom of the screen.

  :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/pZ-fdT6OopdTlK4swZj7h_back-button.png" alt caption size="51"}:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/fPssjiPFN6FBGrFPY_OV1_home-buttons.jpg" alt caption size="45"}
</ExpandableHeading>

Follow the guidelines below when working with mobile lists:

- Get \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}} updates from \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}: You can tap the green **Refresh Lists** button to view updates that other users may have processed from other devices.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/wszxGn8X0NY2khjy73vMA_mob-wklst-refresh.png" size="36" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/mobile-app/worklists/mob-wklst-refresh.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/mobile-app/worklists/mob-wklst-refresh.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false" indent="2"></Image>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  Send animal updates to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}: To process animals, open a list and select a \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}}. From there, you can search for animals and process their tasks. You can also tap on an animal to view its details at the bottom of the screen.
</Paragraph>

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/aTNm40xM3q7tH8nQ6IpWc_working-with-lists.png" size="58" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/mobile-app/worklists/working-with-lists.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/mobile-app/worklists/working-with-lists.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false" indent="2"></Image>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  View animals by \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} number: In a mobile \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}, the animals are displayed by the animal's \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} number. You can view animals by each \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}}, or you can view all animals across all \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}}. To view animals across all \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}}, simply select the **All&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.P-ens-}} option.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/nVaVL7mijrue7HAtAp0tR_all-pens.png" alt caption size="85"}When you select **All&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.P-ens-}} for an \{\{Protocols.OpenTasks}} list, the system lists all \{\{protocol}} tasks for all animals in all \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}}. When you select **All&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.P-ens-}} for a \{\{Worklists.HospListCommonUse}}, \{\{Worklists.ToDoCommonUse}}, or \{\{Worklists.VetListCommonUse}}, the system lists all animals in all \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}} that are included on the list.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/FPBrPwZraBY4Ombac-6IG_allpens-opentasks.png" alt caption size="39"}:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/OjJfU_7ZTjcn7r8FWIrbo_allpens-hosplist.png" alt caption size="36"}
</Paragraph>
