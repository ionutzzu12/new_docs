This section lists all of our current milk quality partners. Their products can provide information about milk deliveries or more detailed insights about the milk produced by the animals, such as fat and protein percentages or other bacteria data.

After you activate an integration with one of the following partners, \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} displays animal data received from the partners' products.
