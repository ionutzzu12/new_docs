You can submit samples for genetic testing to the \{\{Partners.ZoetisGenomics}} directly from \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}. Open **Partners** > **Zoetis** > **Submit Samples** to submit one sample at a time or multiple samples at once.

On the Sample Submission Dashboard page, the system lists all the samples that are eligible for submission. Follow the steps below to continue:

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/srR10Zr_m_pA28tlq5B42_zoetis-submit-samples.png" size="85" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Partners/zoetis/zoetis-submit-samples.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Partners/zoetis/zoetis-submit-samples.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

<hint type="info">
  **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x54;he system will not display records that do not have a GSAMP (Genomic Sample Number) and a GSDAT (Genomic Sample Date), which you set up in [Step 1: Create Genomic Items in DC305](<Activate Zoetis Genomics Integration/DC Unlimited Subscribers: Activate the Integration/step-1-create-genomic-items-in-dc305.mdx>).
</hint>

<WorkflowBlock>
  <WorkflowBlockItem>
    **Herdcode:&#x20;**&#x53;elect the sampled herd's herdcode.
  </WorkflowBlockItem>

  <WorkflowBlockItem>
    **Time Interval:&#x20;**&#x53;elect the time shown in the results: Last 30 Days, Last 60 Days, or All Dates.
  </WorkflowBlockItem>

  <WorkflowBlockItem>
    **GSDAT:&#x20;**&#x53;elect the dates you collected the samples.
  </WorkflowBlockItem>

  <WorkflowBlockItem>
    **Select Animal Samples:&#x20;**&#x43;heck the boxes next to animal samples you want to submit. Check the box next to **ID** to select all the samples.
  </WorkflowBlockItem>

  <WorkflowBlockItem>
    **Sample Type:&#x20;**&#x53;elect the animal sample type from the drop-down list: Allflex Tissue Sample, Caisley Tissue Sample, Hair, Blood, or Semen.
  </WorkflowBlockItem>

  <WorkflowBlockItem>
    **Tests:&#x20;**&#x43;heck the box underneath the tests you want administered to each sample.
  </WorkflowBlockItem>

  <WorkflowBlockItem>
    <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/B2zfs148QOgnk-DgKZM5O_zoetis-test-info.png" size="50" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Partners/zoetis/zoetis-test-info.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Partners/zoetis/zoetis-test-info.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

    Note that tests with an exclamation icon are add-on tests only and cannot be selected unless you select the base test first. Hover over the exclamation icon next to any add-on test to learn which base tests need to be selected before you can select the add-on test:

    - **Tests:&#x20;**&#x42;eta Casein A2, Clarifide, or Clarifide Plus
    - **Add-On Tests:&#x20;**&#x42;VD Test, Brachyspina, CVM, or HP

    <hint type="info">
      **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x49;f you don't know which tests you need to select, contact your \{\{Partners.Zoetis}} representative.
    </hint>

    **Edit Multiple Animal Samples:&#x20;**&#x59;ou can edit multiple animal samples at once by clicking **Edit** at the top right corner. In the side panel, make your selections and save your edits.
  </WorkflowBlockItem>

  <WorkflowBlockItem>
    <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/Fe6fUURg9EZsPB2VezDvB_zoetis-edit-muli-samples.png" size="41" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Partners/zoetis/zoetis-edit-muli-samples.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Partners/zoetis/zoetis-edit-muli-samples.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

    **Submit Samples:&#x20;**&#x43;lick **Submit** to submit the samples or click **Save Draft** to save your changes without submitting them.

    If you select **Save Draft**, your selections will be available the next time you open the Submission Sample Dashboard.
  </WorkflowBlockItem>
</WorkflowBlock>

After you submit your sample selections through \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, \{\{Partners.Zoetis}} will send you a packing slip that you need to print and send with the samples.

You can check the status of your submitted samples on \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}. See [View Zoetis Genomics Submissions ](zoetis-genomics-submissions.htm)to learn more.
