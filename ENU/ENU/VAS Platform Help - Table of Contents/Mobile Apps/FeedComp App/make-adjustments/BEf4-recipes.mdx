  1. \{\{Commands.Click}} the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} to \{\{Commands.UserActions}}.
  2. \{\{Commands.UserActions}}`Edit Recipe` at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Tap **+ Add/Edit&#x20;**\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Upper}} at the bottom of the screen.
  4. Select each ingredient you want to \{\{Commands.UserActions}} to the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}.:::hint\{type="warning"}
     **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**&#x41; \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompWeighback-lower}} ingredient must be the first ingredient selected when adding ingredients for a recipe's loading sequence. See :Link[Create New Ingredient]{label="Create New Ingredient" overridedLabel="Create New Ingredient" spaceId docId="gSxIDSwxFHSGq07jw2Vek" version="v2" docAnchorId="#add" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="FCM-Ingredients.htm#add" href="FCM-Ingredients.htm#add"} to learn how to create a \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompWeighback-lower}} ingredient, or \{\{Commands.UserActions}} :Link[Filler Load]{label="Filler Load" overridedLabel="Filler Load" spaceId docId="d2HWr0rpbBTW5tg47euCM" version="v2" docAnchorId="#fillerload" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="FCM-Weighbacks.htm#fillerload" href="FCM-Weighbacks.htm#fillerload"} to learn how to use a \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompWeighback-lower}} ingredient in a \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFillerLoad-lower}}.
     \:::
  5. \{\{Commands.UserActions}}`Save and Continue` at the bottom of the screen.
  6. If multiple \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}s were selected, tap and hold on an \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}, drop it into a new \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} order, \{\{Commands.UserActions}}`Next` at the bottom of the screen.
  7. \{\{Commands.UserActions}} the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} details, \{\{Commands.UserActions}}`Add` at the bottom of the screen.:::hint\{type="info"}
     **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x49;f multiple ingredients were selected, `Add` is disabled until details are added for all ingredients.
     \:::
  8. \{\{Commands.UserActions}}`Save` at the bottom of the screen.
</ExpandableHeading>

  ## Adjust Ingredient Order

  To adjust the ingredient mixing order for a \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}:

  1. Tap on the recipe to update the ingredient order.
  2. \{\{Commands.UserActions}}`Edit Recipe` at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Tap **+ Add/Edit&#x20;**\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Upper}} at the bottom of the screen.
  4. Select each ingredient you want to \{\{Commands.UserActions}} to the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}.
  5. \{\{Commands.UserActions}}`Save and Continue` at the bottom of the screen.
  6. If multiple \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}s were selected, tap and hold on an \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}, drop it into a new \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} order, \{\{Commands.UserActions}}`Next` at the bottom of the screen.
  7. \{\{Commands.UserActions}} the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} details, \{\{Commands.UserActions}}`Add` at the bottom of the screen.:::hint\{type="info"}
     **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x49;f multiple ingredients were selected, `Add` is disabled until details are added for all ingredients.
     \:::
  8. \{\{Commands.UserActions}}`Save` at the bottom of the screen
