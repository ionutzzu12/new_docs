  ### Required Permissions

  To access and use \{\{UDS-general.UDSBrand}}\{\{UDS-general.UDSProduct}}, you must first be added by your administrator or manager and assigned to specific \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}}. See [GENEX Management](<../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/GENEX Genetic Planning Tool/Get Started with GENEX DairySuite/genex-management.mdx>) to learn more about user access.

  <hint type="warning">
    **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**&#x44;o **NOT** use the Request Access button :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/X8Sxc2XLRUtVwCZCx9WBW_request-access144x30.png" alt caption="Request Access button" size="10"} on the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}'s profile page to request access to the \{\{UDS-general.CommonProductName}}. That option sends a message to the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}'s owner and may cause confusion.
  </hint>
</ExpandableHeading>

\{\{UDS-general.UDSBrand}}'s \{\{UDS-general.UDSProduct}} is a \{\{UDS-general.CommonProductName}} that allows you to improve and adjust specific traits in a \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}'s \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}. By creating a customized genetic plan, you can select ideal \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.s-ires}} and mating partners to refine the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} (or a segment of the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}) in many different ways.

Maybe the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} wants to focus on increasing production and decreasing the risk of \{\{mastitis}} in first-lactation cows. Or perhaps the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}'s goal is to improve conception rates. Maybe they're aiming to increase the fat content of their milk. Whatever the unique goals are for the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}, \{\{UDS-general.UDSBrand}}'s \{\{UDS-general.UDSProduct}} guides you through the phases below to get you there!
