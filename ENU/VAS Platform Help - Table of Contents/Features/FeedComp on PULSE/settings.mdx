Follow the guidelines below when \{\{Commands.UserActions}} the \{\{masterVAS-Products.AllProductNames}}\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Upper}}\{\{Controls.ControlTypes}}:

1. Search for Settings: Use the **Search** field at the top left to find a specific Settings page.
2. Select an Option: Settings for the option selected are displayed on the right side of the page.:::ExpandableHeading\{customAnchorSlug="list-dropdown"}
