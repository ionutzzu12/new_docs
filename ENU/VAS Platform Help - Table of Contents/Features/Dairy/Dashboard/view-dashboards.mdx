Each dashboard provides different information to help you better manage your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}. Learn more about each dashboard type:

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Dairy Dashboards

  \{\{Buttons-UI.Dashboard}}**s** provide easy‑to‑read graphs and illustrations of essential \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} information. The point‑in‑time, heads-up display helps you see where your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} stands \{\{today}}, what has happened in the recent past, and where it might be in the next few months. These dashboards are only available when you are viewing a \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}; for information on company-level dashboards, see :Link[Company Dashboards]{label="Company Dashboards" overridedLabel="Company Dashboards" spaceId docId="OWa0u7ECouAiZddWpVuYu" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Company" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="view-dashboards.mdx"}.

  ## Company Dashboards

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Where do I find my Company View?

    **Company features are only available when viewing a company in&#x20;**\{\{General.ProductName-short}}**, not when viewing a&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}**.** To access company features, a member of the company must invite you to connect with it. See [Invite a User to Your Dairy](<../../Manage Dairy Users and Roles/invite-a-user-to-your-dairy.mdx>) for more information.

    <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/-1RWzJXwq_CihZI38KrJF_nav-co-dashboard.png" size="45" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/dashboard/company/nav-co-dashboard.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/dashboard/company/nav-co-dashboard.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>
