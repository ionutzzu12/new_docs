<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Required Permissions

  Only users who are assigned to a Role with \{\{RolesPermissions.PermissionNames}} permissions can use this feature. See [Edit Dairy User Permissions](../Config/UserMgmtRoles/EditUserPermissions.htm) and [Manage Dairy Roles](<../../../../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Manage Dairy Users and Roles/manage-dairy-roles.mdx>) for more information.
</ExpandableHeading>

On the [Alert Groups](<../../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Dairy/settings-dairy/performance-indicators/working-with-alert-groups.mdx>) page, you can easily delete an alert group by clicking on an alert group to open a side panel that displays the alert group details.

:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/2xySWoPOCFHf6sYQDeiWC_indicators-tab-alerts-subtab-edit-intro.png" alt caption size="75"}:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/6fbQCx7RYZH8CcV7XPz4h_indicators-tab-alerts-subtab-delete.png" alt caption size="53"}

Click the delete button

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/LpD9BwgjpcA94KYTX2Y5v_trashcan-gray-on-white22x25.png" size="10" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/icons/trashcan-gray-on-white_22x25.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/icons/trashcan-gray-on-white_22x25.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

in the upper right corner of the side panel, and then click the **Yes, Delete** button in the pop-up confirmation window to complete the deletion.

<hint type="info">
  **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x41;ny changes made to an alert group affect all scheduled occurrences of the alert group.
</hint>

The [Alert Groups](<../../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Dairy/settings-dairy/performance-indicators/working-with-alert-groups.mdx>) page is displayed and \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} displays a message indicating that the alert group was deleted.
