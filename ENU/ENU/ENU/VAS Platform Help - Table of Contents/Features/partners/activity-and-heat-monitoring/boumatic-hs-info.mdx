  ### Required Setup

  For the \{\{Partners.PartnersUIoption}} tab to display heat and health data and generate alerts, \{\{masterVAS-Products.DCUnlimited}}\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}} must first configure \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC-Platform}} to activate the integration. See [Activate Integration](<../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Partners/Activity and Heat Monitoring/UHF Heat Detective by InterPuls/activate-integration.mdx>) to learn more.
</ExpandableHeading>

[BouMatic HeatSeeker](https://boumatic.com/us_en/) animal sensors monitor reproduction, rumination, eating, and movements to provide deeper insights on heat detection, overall health, and animals requiring urgent attention. \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} displays live data from animal sensors in a new \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} dashboard widget and individual animal CowCards on the \{\{Partners.PartnersUIoption}} tab, providing an up-to-date view of animals requiring health checks or reproduction-related protocols.

<hint type="warning">
  **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**&#x59;our animal **Preferred Identifier** must match the \{\{ID}} item in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} to correctly identify your animals in the partner’s system. Either set your :Link[Preferred Identifier]{label="Preferred Identifier" overridedLabel="Preferred Identifier" spaceId docId="87n3BKxetaoNmZIUQMas_" version="v2" docAnchorId="#naming" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../../Platform/Features/DairyComp/Settings/DC-AnimalConfiguration.htm#naming" href="../../Platform/Features/DairyComp/Settings/DC-AnimalConfiguration.htm#naming"} to **ID** in [Settings](<../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/DairyComp/MyDC/Fo2o-settings.mdx>), or confirm that your selected identifier and ID item values match.
</hint>
