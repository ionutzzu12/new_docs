For 65 years, [Allflex Livestock Intelligence](https://www.allflex.global/na/) has been involved in the livestock \{\{ID}} business. As the leading provider of ID solutions and monitoring with real-time data, \{\{Partners.Allflex}} continues to improve the way millions of cows are being managed every day. \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} displays live data from animal sensors in a \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} dashboard widget and individual animal CowCards on the \{\{Partners.PartnersUIoption}} tab, providing an up-to-date view of animals' rumination and activity levels.
