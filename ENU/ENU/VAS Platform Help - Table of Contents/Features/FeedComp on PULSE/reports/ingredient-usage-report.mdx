<ExpandableHeading indent="2">
  ## Search and Filter the Ingredient Usage Report

  Options to search and filter the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompReportNames}}\{\{Controls.ControlTypes}} are available at the top of the \{\{General.UI-gui}}. Follow the guidelines below when \{\{Commands.UserActions}} the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompReportNames}}\{\{Controls.ControlTypes}}:

  - \{\{Commands.Click}}**Filters** at the top left of the page to limit the data that is displayed. The **Filters** side panel opens.
  - Use the **From** and **To** filters to only view data from that range.
  - \{\{Commands.UserActions}} an\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Upper}}**&#x20;Name** to limit the \{\{Controls.ControlTypes}} to a specific \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}. Multiple \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} can be \{\{Commands.UserActions}}. You can also type text directly in the field to search for \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} by name.
  - \{\{Commands.UserActions}} an\*\*\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Upper}} \*\***Type** to limit the \{\{Controls.ControlTypes}} to a specific type of \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}. Multiple \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} can be \{\{Commands.UserActions}}. You can also type text directly in the field to search for \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}\{\{Controls.ControlTypes}}by name.
  - \{\{Commands.UserActions}}a \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Upper}} to limit the \{\{Controls.ControlTypes}} to a specific \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}. You can type text directly in the field to search for \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} by name.
  - \{\{Commands.UserActions}} the **Clear Filter\*\*\*\*s** option to clear all of the filters currently applied.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading indent="2">
  ## View Report Details

  Follow the guidelines below when \{\{Commands.UserActions}}\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}\{\{Controls.ControlTypes}}:

  - \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompIngredient-singular}}: The name of the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}.
  - Type: The \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} type in a color-coded label.:::ExpandableHeading\{customAnchorSlug="ingred-types"}

  ### Click here to learn more about ingredient types!

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    Dry Forag&#x65;**:&#x20;**&#x56;egetative material, such as stems, leaves, and stalks of plants, in a dried state that is used for feed.
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    Fresh Forage&#x73;**:&#x20;**&#x41; crop of cultivated plants or plant parts that are grazed or harvested as feed.
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    Silag&#x65;**:&#x20;**&#x50;reserved forage for feed, like pasture grass, alfalfa, grains, and corn, that is produced by fermenting plant matter.
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    Energ&#x79;**:&#x20;**&#x48;igh-calorie feed, such as corn, sorghum, barley, and wheat.
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    Protei&#x6E;**:&#x20;**&#x48;igh-protein feed, such as soy and canola.
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    Vitamins and Mineral&#x73;**:&#x20;**&#x53;upplements added to feed to prevent deficiencies in \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} cows that can impact health and milk production.
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    Additive&#x73;**:&#x20;**&#x50;roducts added to feed to enhance feed quality, make the feed more digestible, and improve health and performance.
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompWeighback-singular}}**:&#x20;**\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeed}} leftover from a prior feeding that can be added to a \{\{recipe}} and help reduce waste.
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    Wate&#x72;**:&#x20;**&#x57;ater added to a feed mixture to improve daily milk yield.
    \:::
  </Paragraph>

  - \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompLocation-singular}} Name: The name of the location where the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} is currently \{\{Commands.UserActions}}.
  - Usage (Units): The total \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} amount used during the time frame currently selected in the **From** and **To** filters in pounds or kilograms based on your account settings in \{\{masterVAS-Products.AllProductNames}}. See [Edit Your User Account](<../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/User Menu/edit-your-user-account.mdx>) to learn more.
  - Usage (Tons): The total \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} amount used during the time frame currently selected in the **From** and **To** filters in tons.
</ExpandableHeading>
