  ### Required Permissions

  Only users who are assigned to a Role with \{\{RolesPermissions.PermissionNames}} permissions can use this feature. See [Edit Dairy User Permissions](edit-dairy-user-permissions.mdx) and [Manage Dairy Roles](<../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Manage Dairy Users and Roles/manage-dairy-roles.mdx>) for more information.
</ExpandableHeading>

This topic explains how to cancel or resend an invite to a \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} user.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/cT9FQWQahI87cOspq8Axg_user-cancel-resend-invite-dairy.png" size="74" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/users-roles/user-cancel-resend-invite-dairy.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/users-roles/user-cancel-resend-invite-dairy.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

You can find this feature under the :Link[Dairy menu]{label="Dairy menu" overridedLabel="Dairy menu" spaceId docId="WFLX7SPMpZfy7zoMyrvRh" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Menu-Dairy" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../../../NavPlatform/Menu.htm#Menu-Dairy" href="../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/navigate-pulse/menu-options.mdx"}. If you need to cancel or resend an invite to a company user, see :Link[Cancel or Resend an Invite to a Company User]{label="Cancel or Resend an Invite to a Company User" overridedLabel="Cancel or Resend an Invite to a Company User" spaceId docId="ZBxNM9nH4pjlw1ThsUy51" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Cancel_or_Resend_an_Invite_to_a_Company_User" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../../Company/CompanyUserMgmt.htm#Cancel_or_Resend_an_Invite_to_a_Company_User" href="../company-view/manage-company-users-and-roles.mdx"}.

1. In the **Access Pending** list on the **Users** tab, expand the details of the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} user whose invite you want to cancel or resend.
2. Click the **Cancel Invite** button at the bottom left side of the page to cancel the invite to the user.ORClick the **Resend Invite** button at the bottom left side of the page to resend the invite to the user.
