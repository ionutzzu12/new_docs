Use the \{\{Insights.ConceptionRateTab}} page to examine the factors that impact your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}'s conception rate. \{\{Commands.Click}}**Filters** at the top left of the page to limit the data that is displayed Select a specific **Parameter**, such as technician, stud code, heat interval, etc., to view specific results by category.

The gray box at the top of the page summarizes your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}'s reproduction data based on your selected **Filters** and **Parameter**:

- Total: The total number of conceptions that occurred.
- Overall Conception Rate: The \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}'s overall conception rate, displayed as a percentage.
- Overall Confidence Interval: A summarized percentage of the 95th percentile confidence interval. In the example above, the overall confidence interval is 36-40%. This means that you can be 95% confident in a conception rate between 36%-40%.

The **Parameter** option allows you to drill down into your conception rate data so that you can easily identify your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}'s top performers. You can choose from the following options:

- Lactation Group: View data sorted by the lactation status of cows in your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}. This parameter is not available for heifers.
- Technician: View data sorted by the breeding technicians on your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} team.
- Stud Code: View data sorted by the individual stud codes assigned to \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.s-ires}} in your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}.
- Breeding Code: View data sorted by the different types of breeding events that occur across your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}.
- Breed: View data sorted by the individual \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.s-ire}} breeds within your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}.
- Month: View data sorted by calendar month.
- Breeding Cycle: View data sorted by transitions in your animals' breeding cycles.
- Heat Interval: View data sorted by the number of days in your animals' heat cycles.
- Week Day: View data sorted by each day of the week.
- Times Bred: View data sorted by the number of times your animals were bred.
- \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.S-ires-}}: View data sorted by \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.s-ires}} used by the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}. Once selected, additional filter options, such as breed, stud code, semen type, etc., are available.

The graphed data helps to visualize your overall conception rate as well as the confidence interval:

- References: Each reference is color-coded to match a specific bar or line on the graph.
- The black "wick" represents a 95% confidence interval (CI) for the \{\{Insights.InsightDataType}} shown by the bar. For instance, if the wick reflects a 95% CI of 27-35, you can be 95% confident in a \{\{Insights.InsightDataType}} between 27%-35%.
