The \{\{Buttons-UI.FeaturesTertiary}} page allows you to view and manage all of the technicians working with your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}. You can quickly see who they are, if they are active, and the \{\{General.ProductName}} user associated with them (if any). You can find this feature under the :Link[DairyComp/MyDC menu]{label="DairyComp/MyDC menu" overridedLabel="DairyComp/MyDC menu" spaceId docId="WFLX7SPMpZfy7zoMyrvRh" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Menu-DairyComp" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../../../NavPlatform/Menu.htm#Menu-DairyComp" href="../../../../../../ENU/VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/navigate-pulse/menu-options.mdx"} > :Link[Settings]{label="Settings" overridedLabel="Settings" spaceId docId="WFLX7SPMpZfy7zoMyrvRh" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Menu-DairyComp" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../../../NavPlatform/Menu.htm#Menu-DairyComp" href="../../../../../../ENU/VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/navigate-pulse/menu-options.mdx"} option on the \{\{Buttons-UI.FeaturesSecondary}} or \{\{Buttons-UI.FeaturesSecondary}} tabs. Your updates will apply to the \{\{Buttons-UI.FeaturesTertiary}} list regardless of which tab you use to access it.

  ## Edit a Technician

  1. Click on the applicable technician in the list.A side panel appears in which you can make your changes.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/xOmmKS15k0fLK3uCytPln_dc-dairytechs-edit.png" alt caption size="72"}
  2. Click in any of the following fields to change their values:\* **Active technician**: Toggle the technician between active and inactive.
     - \{\{Tech}}**&#x20;name**: Edit the name of the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} technician.
     - **User**: Assign a specific \{\{General.ProductName}} user to the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} technician. By default, this field is left as unassigned.To revert back to the original values for the technician, click **Reset**.
  3. When you are done editing the technician, click **Save**.A message appears at the bottom of the page indicating that your changes have been saved.
</ExpandableHeading>
