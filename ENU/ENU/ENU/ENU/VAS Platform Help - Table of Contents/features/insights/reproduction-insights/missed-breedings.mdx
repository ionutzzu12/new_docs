The animals that currently have not been bred beyond their bred eligible date can be viewed on the \{\{Insights.MissedBreedingsTab}} page. \{\{Commands.Click}}**Filters** at the top left of the page to limit the data that is displayed

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/1A9ESrx_1uSq9x4Udqk61_reproduction-cows-missedbreedings.png" size="77" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/insights/reproduction-cows-missedbreedings.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/insights/reproduction-cows-missedbreedings.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

The gray boxes at the top of the page summarize your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}'s reproduction data based on your selected **Filters**:

- \{\{VWP}}: Identifies the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}'s VWP setting. Animals are included in the Missed Breedings report if their VWP has ended and they have not yet been bred. You can set your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}’s limit for the number of animals with missed breedings using the **Missed Breedings - Animal Count** threshold on the [Performance Indicators](<../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Dairy/settings-dairy/performance-indicators.mdx>) page.
- Times Bred: The number of times bred is always 0.

Once the data is displayed, \{\{Commands.UserActions}} the green **Export as CSV** button

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/zn2pf0OAXXl9XFdkEO1Ld_export.png" size="10" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/icons/export.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/icons/export.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

at the top right of the page to save the data as a CSV file. If you applied filter(s), the data will print with the current filter(s) applied. Click on an animal's \{\{ID}} in the table to open a side panel and view its details. See [View Animal Details](<../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/DairyComp/MyDC/animals/view-animal-details.mdx>) to learn more about the information displayed in the side panel.
