Before you can use \{\{UDS-general.UDSBrand}}\{\{UDS-general.UDSProduct}} in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, you must first sign up for an account. If you have already been directed to a login screen and clicked the **Sign Up** button, you can start at Step 2 below.

1. Go to [https://platform.vas.com/login](https://platform.vas.com/login), and click the **Sign Up** button.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/pKnHVsHwcVbpMz5lIsAtG_sign-up-button.png" alt caption size="16"}
2. Enter your contact email and click the next **Sign Up** button.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/5WR8NGRHXshnJH5Krytuj_sign-up-email.png" alt caption size="32"}At this point, \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} sends a welcome email to the address you entered. Click the **Done** button to continue.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/iZuyqsjMiUUEjgg2gWLxT_sign-up-confirm.png" alt caption size="31"}
3. Check your email for a message entitled **Welcome to the&#x20;**\{\{General.ProductName}}.
4. Open the email and click the **Continue Sign Up** button.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/ogojxR_ePkWKMLAo_K_hR_sign-up-welcome-email.png" alt caption size="29"}You will be directed back to the account set‑up screen, where you can finish entering your user profile details and set your password.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/iUEEm2jhSLU3-ie23ZCvX_sign-up-profile.png" alt caption size="44"}:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/O9eoL3qP7TXctjt20EfPG_sign-up-password.png" alt caption size="31"}After you \{\{Commands.click}} the **Save Password** button, the system displays a message confirming that your account is now ready to use.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/QA5AcdfAE7JIVVaBksaI2_sign-up-complete.png" alt caption size="27"}
5. \{\{Commands.Click}}**Continue** to review and accept \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} End User Subscription Agreement (EUSA) and Privacy Policy. Once you accept the agreements, you can begin using \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.You may not see much the first time you log in because you must have access to at least one \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} to use \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}. You can use the search field to find and connect to a new \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/lNKI46ZYPOuwJul3vbpXT_sign-up-first-signin.png" alt caption size="48"}
