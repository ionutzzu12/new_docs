<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Required Setup

  For the \{\{Partners.PartnersUIoption}} tab to display heat and health data and generate alerts, \{\{masterVAS-Products.DCUnlimited}}\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}} must first configure \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC-Platform}} to activate the integration. See [Activate Integration](activate-integration.mdx) to learn more.
</ExpandableHeading>

Animal alerts are generated based on sensor data pulled from the partner system and sent to \{\{DC-UI.DC-ProductName}} and \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} every hour. To view animals with alerts, check the [Dashboard Widget](nedap-dashboard-widgets.htm) and click the animal's Cow Number to view its CowCard.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Heat Alerts

  The partner system sends the following heat alerts to \{\{Buttons-UI.FeaturesMain}} and \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}:

  - Heat detected date and time
  - Suspicious heat detected date and time

  Heat alerts are marked on the CowCard graph in red or yellow, and hovering over the marking provides additional detail.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/c1FbUsqtGQFwdE61bo8UA_nedap-alert-heat.png" size="30" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Partners/nedap/nedap-alert-heat.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Partners/nedap/nedap-alert-heat.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Health Alerts

  The partner system sends the following health alerts to \{\{Buttons-UI.FeaturesMain}} and \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}:

  - Eating, when total eating time is too low
  - Inactive, when total time spent not eating or ruminating is too high
  - Lying, when the total time spent lying down is too high

  Health alerts are marked on the CowCard graph as a red bar, and hovering over the bar provides additional detail.
