Follow these steps to view your \{\{Partners.Allflex}} health and reproduction data and alerts in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}:

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Step 1: Connect Integration in PULSE

  1. In \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, navigate to **Partners&#x20;**>**&#xA0;Integrations Gallery&#x20;**>**&#xA0;**\{\{Partners.PartnerName}}.
  2. \{\{Commands.Click}}**See Details** or **See Requirements** on the \{\{Partners.PartnerName}}integration card.
  3. Follow the Connection Instructions on the right side of the page to establish the connection:1) \{\{Commands.Click}}**Add New Alias**, then complete the following fields:\* **Alias:** Enter your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}} Name. If you have multiple \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}}, we recommend you use different names for each alias.
     - **Registration Key**&#xNAN;**:** Copy or enter your software registration key from your \{\{Partners.PartnerName}} account. Each device used to access \{\{Partners.PartnerName}} has a unique registration key.

  ## Step 2: Configure Items in DairyComp 305 (DC Unlimited Users Only)

  1. In \{\{DC-UI.DC-ProductName}}, enter `PLATFORM` in the command line.
  2. Click the **Pulse Items** tab and expand \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.Allflex}}.
  3. If necessary, map or add an item.      2\) **Add New Item:** To create a new item, check the box for that item. Click **Create (1) Items**, then click **Yes** to confirm your selection.
  4. \{\{Commands.Click}}**OK** to save your changes.
