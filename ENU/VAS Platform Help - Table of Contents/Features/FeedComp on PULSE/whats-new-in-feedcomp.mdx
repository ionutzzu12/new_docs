  ## Tips, Known Issues, and Workarounds

  The \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} team is working hard to bring you new features and enhancements, but some updates may not be fully ready or supported yet. Review the list of tips, known issues, and workarounds for \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}}:

  - Uninstall and redownload \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} if the app won't open: Uninstall your current version of \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}}, then download the latest version. You can search for \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} in your device's app store or tap these links from your device: [Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.vas.mobile.feednext) or [iOS](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/feedcomp/id6481264460).
  - Use online \{\{mode}} to ensure data is synced and saved correctly: Whenever possible, use online mode in the \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp-upper}} when creating or editing ingredients or recipes.
  - Use standard workflows to ensure the best performance: To ensure that the current version of \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} works correctly, avoid mixing units, editing during feeding, or switching devices mid-task.
  - Keep the app open during feedings: For the best experience, keep the app open during feedings to ensure that feeding processes are not disrupted and everything runs smoothly.
  - Sync your data if completed feedings are still marked incomplete: Make sure you sync the app data before manually completing a record from the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeedHistory}} report. Some feedings may appear **Incomplete** until the data is fully synced. This helps prevent losing or overwriting data that is already saved in the app.
  - Check your \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeedingSchedule}} if you have multiple split feedings: If you have multiple split feedings, check your \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeedingSchedule}} to make sure that your feedings are ordered correctly. Reorder the feedings if needed.
  - Set the mixer's Drop Tolerance value to at least 1: If you use a \{\{FeedComp.ScaleTopcon}} and the \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp-upper}} will not complete a drop or automatically advance to the next \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}}, make sure that the \{\{mixer}}'s Drop Tolerance is set to a minimum value of **1**. If you use **0** for the Drop Tolerance value, the \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp-upper}} may not complete drops correctly. To update your mixer's Drop Tolerance, tap \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompAdjustments}} at the bottom of the \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp-upper}} screen, tap \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompEquipment}}, and select the mixer to make your changes.
