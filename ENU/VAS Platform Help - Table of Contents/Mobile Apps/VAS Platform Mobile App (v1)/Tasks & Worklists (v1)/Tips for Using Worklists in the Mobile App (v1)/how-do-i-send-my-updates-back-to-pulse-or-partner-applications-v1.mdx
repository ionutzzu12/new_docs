You can process your \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}} updates to send them from your mobile devices to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}(and \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}} for \{\{masterVAS-Products.DCUnlimited}}\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}}) anytime. Follow the steps below to process your pending \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}} changes.

:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/5Pg2jAIxNUsX96xDhnIdA_return-to-list-main.png" alt caption size="56"}:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/M4huDR4FIC5O1GQcxbtiN_process-changes.png" alt caption size="57"}

1. \{\{Commands.Click}} the arrow to the left of the list name to go back to the main list.
2. From there, \{\{Commands.click}} the \{\{Worklists.ProcessChanges}} button to send the updates back to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} or \{\{General.CompanyName-short}} integrated partner product (\{\{Mobile-UI.ConnectionRequired}}).

<ExpandableHeading>
  ### What happens when I Process Changes?

  - When you process changes, it completes all animals that are marked as \{\{Worklists.DoneUI}} and are in the status \{\{Worklists.Received}}. Once their status changes to \{\{Worklists.Processing}} in the mobile app, you can no longer undo them. The animals' status changes to PROCESSED, and the updates are sent from \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} to mobile devices, as well as \{\{DC-UI.DC-ProdNameShort}} (for \{\{masterVAS-Products.DCUnlimited}}\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}}).
  - As long as your device maintains an active internet connection, the mobile app will send processed changes back to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}and \{\{DC-UI.DC-ProdNameShort}}approximately every 10 minutes. If you do not have an active internet connection, you cannot process changes. You will need to tap the **Process Changes** button again once your connection is restored.
  - If you are using the mobile app in offline \{\{mode}}, you can still mark animals as \{\{Worklists.DoneUI}} and then tap the \{\{Worklists.ProcessChanges}} button once your device is back online.
  - For \{\{masterVAS-Products.DCUnlimited}}\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}}: When \{\{DC-UI.DC-ProdNameShort}} receives notification that one or more animals on a list is \{\{Worklists.DoneUI}}, it kicks off any corresponding \{\{Worklists.OnDone}} or \{\{Worklists.PostProcess}} routine(s) for those animals.
</ExpandableHeading>
