Use \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}'s [Report/Worklist Builder](../../Platform/Features/DairyComp/ReportsWorklists/ReportWorklistBuilder.htm) or \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}} to generate reports or \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}s for animals with activity data that may indicate they need to be checked or bred. Connected \{\{Partners.ActivityMonitoring}} systems send data to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} and \{\{DC-UI.DC-ProductName}} (for \{\{General.DCUnlimited}} dairies) at a frequency controlled by the \{\{Partners.ActivityMonitoringPartner}}. Contact your \{\{Partners.ActivityMonitoring}} representative with any questions or issues with device data being sent to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Sample report to capture heat activity

  List of animals showing all heat activity data for yesterday and \{\{today}} by \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}}:

  LISTID PEN DIM RPRO TBRD DSLH ACDAT ACTIM ACLEVFORACDAT>-2BYPEN
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Sample report to capture health and other data

  List of animals showing all health and activity data for yesterday and today by \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}}:

  LISTID PEN DIM LACT DXDAT DXTIM DXLEV RUM EAT INAFORDXDAT>-2BYPEN
</ExpandableHeading>

<hint type="warning">
  **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**&#x49;f generating the \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}} in \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}}, be sure to add the `\K` switch to send the list to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.
</hint>
