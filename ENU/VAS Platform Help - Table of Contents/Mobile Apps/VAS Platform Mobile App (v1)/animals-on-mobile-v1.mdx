The \{\{Mobile-UI.AnimalsTab}} page in the mobile app allows you to view animals in your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}. When you tap the \{\{Mobile-UI.AnimalsTab}} button at the bottom of the mobile app, it opens to a list of all animals in your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}. From there, use the search field at the top to find a specific animal, or use the Filters option to narrow the list by \{\{RPRO}} code. Tap on any animal in the list to view its CowCard.

  Follow the guidelines below when finding and viewing animals.

  1. Tap the \{\{Mobile-UI.AnimalsTab}} tab to view a list of all animals in your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}.\* The list is sorted from lowest to highest \{\{ID}} number.
     - Scroll up and down to view the list, and use the Search and Filter options defined below to narrow the list.
  2. There are two main ways to find an animal in the mobile app: the Search bar and the Animals page. You can use the Search bar at any time by tapping the search icon at the top of the page or the \{\{Buttons-UI.Search}} button on the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.H-omepage-}}.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/Nu6M2MmAeHItirwPCCs1S_search-3.png" alt caption size="20"}:::ExpandableHeading

  ## Add an Animal

  You can easily add a new animal to your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} by tapping the **+ Add New Animal** option at the top of the \{\{Mobile-UI.AnimalsTab}} page and then entering identification information for the animal. Once the animal is added, you can view it within your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}.

  Follow these steps to add an animal:

  1. From the \{\{Mobile-UI.AnimalsTab}} page, tap **+ Add New Animal**.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/1JZufL2Css1fqld0QKRC9_herd-add-animal.png" alt caption size="27"}
  2. Choose the **Animal Type** and add required details.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/apCrMNBhSpMW2hGvWgAY-_herd-add-animal-details.png" alt caption size="66"}:::hint\{type="info"}
     **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x59;ou must enter information in all the required fields, which are marked with an asterisk (\*). Depending on the Animal Type selected, different fields are available and required.
     \:::
  3. Enter other optional animal details. The \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-am-}}**&#x20;Cow Number**, \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-am-}} \{\{ISO}}**&#xA0;ID**, \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-am-}}**&#x20;Name**, and \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.S-ire-}} fields are searchable. Start typing in these fields to search for matching animals.
  4. Tap **Save**.
  5. Confirm the animal details and tap **Add Animal**. If you need to correct any of the information you've entered, tap the back button to return to the data entry page.The system displays a message at the bottom of the page stating that the animal was added.

  <hint type="info">
    **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x49;f you do not have an active internet connection or are using the app in offline \{\{mode}}, added animals will sync to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} as soon as your device is back online.
  </hint>
</ExpandableHeading>

  ## Filter the Animals List

  Follow these steps to filter the animals list:

  1. Tap the \{\{Mobile-UI.Filters}} option

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/w4b6HOs65ckjEhzxoJLzY_herd-tab-filter-icon.png" size="10" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Platform/mobile-app/herd-tab-filter-icon.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/mobile-app/herd-tab-filter-icon.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false" indent="2"></Image>

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    at the top right of the \{\{Mobile-UI.AnimalsTab}} page to open the \{\{Mobile-UI.FiltersList}} page.
  </Paragraph>

  2. In the \{\{Mobile-UI.FiltersList}} page, choose one or more RPRO code filters.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/KZxoG8SHa4wgyvDpcsgaf_animals-tab-filters.png" alt caption}Note the following information on the \{\{Mobile-UI.FiltersList}} page:\* The number of animals in each RPRO condition is displayed beneath each RPRO code so that you can see how many animals will be in the list when you apply its respective filter.
     - The total number of animals that will be displayed in the filtered list is shown at the bottom left of the page.
  3. Tap the \{\{Mobile-UI.ApplyChanges}} button at the top right of the \{\{Mobile-UI.FiltersList}} page to apply your selected filters.
  4. Review your filtered list in the \{\{Mobile-UI.AnimalsTab}} page.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/d-0_JX3rl46ic5NaQj_1E_herd-tab-filter-applied.jpg" alt caption size="35"}

  <hint type="info">
    **NOTE:**

    - The codes you select in the Filters list will be the only ones displayed in the Animals list. Be sure to tap **Apply** at the top right of the \{\{Mobile-UI.FiltersList}} page to apply your filters.
    - When you apply one or more filters, the animals with *non-selected* codes will no longer be displayed in the list.
    - To remove all filters, open the \{\{Mobile-UI.FiltersList}} page and tap the **Clear Filters** option at the bottom right. This action clears all filters and displays the full list of animals again.
    - After applying your filters, the top left of the Animals page adjusts the "Viewing" numbers. As shown in the 2nd example above, after applying the filter to show only the animals with the RPRO code of OK/OPEN, you are now viewing only 179 out of the total 5278 animals in the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}.
  </hint>
</ExpandableHeading>

  ## Update Items on Mobile CowCards

  Tap any animal in the \{\{Mobile-UI.AnimalsTab}} page to view its \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}} and update items.

  <hint type="info">
    **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x4F;nly stored item values can be changed. Calculated item values (such as \{\{Age}}), as well as certain restricted items, cannot be edited.
  </hint>

  Follow these steps to edit items: 

  1. Tap the **Edit Items** button at the bottom of the page.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/gjNwlFEJDHEI3lp2xq03-_herd-tab-edit-items.png" alt caption size="35"}
  2. Scroll to find the item to edit and tap the item name.
  3. Depending on the item type, the app opens a page that allows you to change the date or text, or to select an item from a list.
  4. Enter or choose the correct item.
  5. If necessary, update additional items for this animal. Updated items remain highlighted in green until you tap **Done**.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/piP3oH9r0Ecd7SFnVxgMJ_herd-tab-edit-items-done.png" alt caption size="27"}
  6. Tap **Done** to save your changes.

  <hint type="info">
    **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x49;f you do not have an active internet connection or are using the app in offline mode, your item updates will sync to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} as soon as your device is back online.
  </hint>
</ExpandableHeading>

  ## Add an Event to a Mobile CowCard

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Required Permissions

    Only users who are assigned to a Role with \{\{RolesPermissions.PermissionNames}} permissions can use this feature. If your account only has \{\{RolesPermissions.PermissionNames}} permissions, you will **not** be able to use this feature. See [Edit Dairy User Permissions](../Features/Config/UserMgmtRoles/EditUserPermissions.htm) and [Manage Dairy Roles](<../../Features/Manage Dairy Users and Roles/manage-dairy-roles.mdx>) for more information.
  </ExpandableHeading>

  Tap any animal on the \{\{Mobile-UI.AnimalsTab}} screen to view its \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}}. Follow the steps below to continue:

  <WorkflowBlock>
    <WorkflowBlockItem>
      Tap **Add Event** at the bottom of the screen.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/9KjwuA3V-B8f8FBkg6dWU_herd-tab-add-event.png" alt caption size="28"}
    </WorkflowBlockItem>

    <WorkflowBlockItem>
      On the **Add Event** screen, scroll to the event you want to add and tap the event name.The mobile app opens the event-specific screen. All required fields are marked with the **\*** (asterisk) symbol.
    </WorkflowBlockItem>

    <WorkflowBlockItem>
      Tap **Save**.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/iR3ASn2O5UVhKpmAo0o91_event-specific-page1.png" alt caption size="27"}Once added, the event is displayed on the \{\{CowCards-UI.CC-tab-names}} tab of the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}}.

      <hint type="info">
        **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x53;aved events display on the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}} after a brief delay if you have \{\{masterVAS-Products.MyDC-short}}. If you have \{\{masterVAS-Products.DCUnlimited-short}}, saved events display after the system synchronizes with the \{\{masterVAS-Products.OnPremise-lower}} \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC-Platform}}. If you do not have an active internet connection or are using the app in offline mode, added events will sync to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} as soon as your device is back online.
      </hint>
    </WorkflowBlockItem>
  </WorkflowBlock>
