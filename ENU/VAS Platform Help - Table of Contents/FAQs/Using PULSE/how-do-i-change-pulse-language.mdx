Your **Account Settings** allow you to choose a different language for the \{\{General.ProductName}}. The language you select in your Account Settings will be applied when you log in to both the web and mobile versions of \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}. Once you save your language selection in the web version, you must then restart the mobile app and re-enter your credentials to apply the language change there.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Click here to view the supported languages.

  \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} currently supports the following languages:

  - English
  - Español (Spanish)
  - Français (French)
  - Italiano (Italian)
  - Deutsch (German)
  - Polski (Polish)
  - Pусский (Russian)
  - 中文 (Chinese)
  - Čeština (Czech)
  - Português (Portuguese)

  <hint type="info">
    **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x54;he current **Français** option is Canadian French.
  </hint>
</ExpandableHeading>

Follow these guidelines when changing the language:

<WorkflowBlock>
  <WorkflowBlockItem>
    If you're not already on your **Account&#x20;****Settings** page, click \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} logo button in the top left corner of the window, and \{\{Commands.click}} the **Account Settings** button to the far right of your User Account home page.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/nAf0uJAS7dMuTp2ngmncr_vas-logo-open-user-acct.png" alt caption size="22"}:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/HxDcPsmEhxhcUD2T2wxYh_open-acct-settings.png" alt caption size="57"}**OR**Select **Account Settings** from the [User Menu](../Features/Config/UserMenu.htm) in the top right corner of the page.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/ecq-YsGRfRV3m8X9-g7v3_user-menu-acct.png" alt caption size="22"}
  </WorkflowBlockItem>

  <WorkflowBlockItem>
    On the **Account Settings** page, select the **User Profile** tab at the top of the page.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/IYI3BwT_8kjrZwJG6oP6D_user-profile-language.png" alt caption size="43"}
  </WorkflowBlockItem>

  <WorkflowBlockItem>
    Locate the **Language** field toward the bottom of the page and choose a setting from the drop-down list.
  </WorkflowBlockItem>

  <WorkflowBlockItem>
    Click the **Save** button to apply the language setting to the web version of \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.
  </WorkflowBlockItem>

  <WorkflowBlockItem>
    If you use the mobile app as well, restart the app and re-enter your credentials to apply the language setting there.

    <hint type="info">
      **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x46;or \{\{masterVAS-Products.DCUnlimited}}, the \{\{General.ProductName-short}} language setting does **not** change the data fields that are pulled from \{\{masterVAS-Products.OnPremise-lower}} \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC-Platform}}. When \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} synchronizes data from the \{\{masterVAS-Products.OnPremise-lower}} \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC-Platform}}, it will be displayed in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} the same way it was entered in the \{\{masterVAS-Products.OnPremise-lower}} \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC-Platform}}.
    </hint>

    [\< Return to Using PULSE](FAQs-Using-Platform.htm)
  </WorkflowBlockItem>
</WorkflowBlock>
