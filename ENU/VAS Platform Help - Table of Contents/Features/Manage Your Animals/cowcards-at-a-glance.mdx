The numerous ways to open and view a \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}} in \{\{General.ProductName}} ensure you can always access your animals' information in just a few clicks. To make navigating \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}}s much easier, you should familiarize yourself with the features below.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/H0o1Jh5EyDwph1bg1gv0s_cowcard-overview-new.png" size="86" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/cowcards/cowcard-overview-new.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/cowcards/cowcard-overview-new.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

1. **Animal&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.O-verview-}}**:** The bar at the top of the page provides a brief summary of the animal, such as its current \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} and reproductive code.
2. \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}}**&#x20;Page Tabs:** These tabs are where you'll find all of the animal's details beyond the summary. By default, \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}}s open to the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.O-verview-}} page. Note that the tabs you see here may vary depending on your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}'s \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}[Partner integrations](../partners.mdx) and [subscriptions](../../GetStarted/KnowYourPulse.htm).
3. **Page Details:** This section of the page displays data for the selected tab. The \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.O-verview-}} page, for example, displays the animal's most recent events, a summary of its items, and more.
