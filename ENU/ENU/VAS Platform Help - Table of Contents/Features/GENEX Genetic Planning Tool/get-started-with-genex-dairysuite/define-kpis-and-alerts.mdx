\{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} can alert you when the following \{\{UDS-general.KPI-short}}s fall below or exceed your designated limits:

- Conception rates for cows and heifers
- Animal counts by reproduction status: Open, Pregnant, Dry
- Population counts for a variety of specific animal types, including heifers, breeding-\{\{age}} heifers, departing animals, and new heifers
