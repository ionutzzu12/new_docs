<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Required Permissions

  Only users who are assigned a Role with Integrations Management permissions can connect partner integrations. See [Edit Dairy User Permissions](../../Platform/Features/Config/UserMgmtRoles/EditUserPermissions.htm) and [Manage Dairy Roles](<../../../Manage Dairy Users and Roles/manage-dairy-roles.mdx>) for more information.
</ExpandableHeading>

Follow these steps to activate the integration and view data on your [Bacteria](bacteria-widget-details.mdx) and [Milk Quality](milk-quality-widget-details.mdx) widgets:

1. Log into your \{\{Partners.Ever.AgBrand}} account, select **Integrations**, and click the toggle to activate the \{\{General.ProductName}} integration for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/u3Tl5bGweWX4uvH744-c1_dashboard-toggle.png" alt caption size="26"}:::hint\{type="warning"}
   **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**&#x59;ou must have administrator access in your creamery app to be able to connect the integration. Contact your creamery if you need to adjust your access level.
   \:::
2. In \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, navigate to **Partners&#x20;**>**&#xA0;Integrations Gallery&#x20;**>**&#xA0;**\{\{Partners.PartnerName}}.
3. \{\{Commands.Click}}**See Details** or **See Requirements** on the \{\{Partners.PartnerName}}integration card.
4. Follow the Connection Instructions on the right side of the page to establish the connection:1) \{\{Commands.Click}}**Add New Alias**, then complete the following fields:\* **Alias:** Enter your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}} Name. If you have multiple \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}}, we recommend you use different names for each alias.
   - **Producer Code:** Copy or enter your code from your \{\{Partners.PartnerName}} account. Your Producer Code is included in the weekly summary email from your creamery.

<Paragraph listStyleType="decimal" listStart="2" indent="2">
  \{\{Commands.Click}}**Save**.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph listStyleType="decimal" listStart="3" indent="2">
  Click the **Connection Status** toggle and verify the status switches to **Active**.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/yVoLb7NAnbX1zs-j2jcYx_integration-connected.png" alt="Connection Status: Active" caption size="22"}
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph listStyleType="decimal" listStart="4" indent="2">
  If necessary, \{\{Commands.click}}**Edit Integration** to make any adjustments or edits and save your changes.
</Paragraph>

<hint type="warning">
  **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**&#x49;f your integration does not automatically connect, contact your \{\{Partners.PartnerName}} representative to connect the integration from their system.
</hint>

Once activated, the widgets are automatically added to your [Dairy Dashboard](../../Platform/Features/Dashboards/DashboardsOverview.htm).
