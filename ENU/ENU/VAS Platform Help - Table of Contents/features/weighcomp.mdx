The \{\{MorphHelp.WeighComp}} add-on to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} captures transaction information for feed, milk, or cattle shipments coming into and leaving the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}. Working in conjunction with \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedWatchOnPremise}}, \{\{MorphHelp.WeighComp}} tracks product, vendor, and location information for each transaction, eliminating entries written "by hand" and reducing clerical errors. You can find this feature under the [main menu bar](../navigate-pulse/menu-options.mdx).

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## How does data sync between FeedComp and WeighComp?

  If you use \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} and \{\{masterVAS-Products.WC-Platform}}, \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} automatically exchanges data between the two platforms as long as the \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} mobile app has an active connection. This ensures that both \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} and \{\{masterVAS-Products.WC-Platform}} always have the same, accurate inventory data. When you use \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} to manage your feed operations and \{\{masterVAS-Products.WC-Platform}} to manage shipments coming into and leaving your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}, syncing the data between the two platforms also streamlines the operation by reducing manual entry.

  - \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} Data Sync to \{\{masterVAS-Products.WC-Platform}}: \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} sends new or updated ingredient, vendor, and location details to \{\{masterVAS-Products.WC-Platform}}.
  - \{\{masterVAS-Products.WC-Platform}} Data Sync to \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}}: \{\{masterVAS-Products.WC-Platform}} sends new or updated inventory adjustments (including pickup and delivery amounts), costs, \{\{dry matter}}, and vendor details, to \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}}.
</ExpandableHeading>

Additionally, the \{\{MorphHelp.WeighComp}} Harvest Management feature allows you to view and manage all of your harvests, as well as the ability to approve harvests, view and edit pending harvest loads, and approve loads.

Both spot load transactions and harvest load data is captured via an [easy to use cloud-based app](../App/WC-AppWorkflow.htm). This data is then sent to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, where it can be viewed and managed.
