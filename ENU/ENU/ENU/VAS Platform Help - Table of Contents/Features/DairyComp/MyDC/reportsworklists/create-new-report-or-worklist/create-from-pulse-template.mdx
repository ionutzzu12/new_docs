The **Template&#x20;**\{\{Buttons-UI.GalleryTab}} page provides all standard reports and \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}s that are available for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} within \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}. Note that reports are shaded in **blue** and \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}s are shaded in **green**.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/7FmGs38zf2U8Ytq-niIv9_reports-gallery.png" size="85" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/dairycomp/Reports/reports-gallery.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/dairycomp/Reports/reports-gallery.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

You can find this feature under the :Link[DairyComp/MyDC > Reports/Worklists menu]{label="DairyComp/MyDC > Reports/Worklists menu" overridedLabel="DairyComp/MyDC > Reports/Worklists menu" spaceId docId="WFLX7SPMpZfy7zoMyrvRh" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Menu-DairyComp-Reports" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../../../NavPlatform/Menu.htm#Menu-DairyComp-Reports" href="../../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/navigate-pulse/menu-options.mdx"} on the \{\{Buttons-UI.FeaturesSecondary}} tab.

You can customize any standard report or \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}} available in the gallery! \{\{Commands.Click}} the **Customize** button to open the report in [Report/Worklist Builder](build-custom-report-or-worklist.mdx). After your standard report is customized and saved, the report is added to the [Dairy Reports/Worklists](<../../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/DairyComp/MyDC/reportsworklists/manage-dairy-reportsworklists.mdx>)page.

See [Build Custom Report or Worklist](build-custom-report-or-worklist.mdx) to learn more about customizing standard reports and \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}s.
