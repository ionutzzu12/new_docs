\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeed}} left over from a prior feeding is called a "\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompWeighback-lower}}" in \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}}. \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompWeighbacks}} may also be called "refusals" or "cleanups." \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} allows you to capture \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompWeighbacks-lower}} so that the leftover feed can be fed to animals in other \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}} or locations, included as a \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFillerLoad-lower}} in a recipe, or sold off farm. You can manage \{\{masterVAS-Products.AllProductNames}}\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} on the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompButtons}}\{\{General.UI-gui}}.

\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompWeighbacks}} help you reduce waste by adding leftover feed to existing recipes, and are typically picked up the morning after the scheduled feeding. When you create a \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompWeighback-lower}} from one of yesterday’s scheduled feedings, \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} automatically recalculates the \{\{dry matter}} intake and updates the :Link[DM Intake Report]{label="DM Intake Report" overridedLabel="DM Intake Report" spaceId docId="EgGjILyt4px-Lp9R18n71" version="v2" docAnchorId="#dmintake" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="FCM-Reports.htm#dmintake" href="FCM-Reports.htm#dmintake"}. For example, if you pick up 200 pounds of unused feed from a \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}}, the dry matter intake for that \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} is readjusted to subtract 200 pounds from the prior day’s feeding, which helps reduce costs and keep your ingredient amounts more precise.

  ## Filler Load

  A \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFillerLoad-lower}} is type of \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompWeighback-lower}} collection that reuses leftover feed from the previous day and adds it into a scheduled feeding for the current day. A predetermined \{\{recipe}} with a \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompWeighback-lower}} as the first ingredient must be active in a scheduled feeding for it to populate in the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFillerLoad-lower}} feeding flow. A \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompWeighback-lower}} for the day is loaded first during this flow, and the following ingredient amounts are recalculated based on dry matter targets.

  A \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFillerLoad-lower}} will only allow scheduled feeding drops. Users may pick up a \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompWeighback-lower}} from locations or \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}}. Dry matter intake amounts will auto adjust for the previous day based on collected \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompWeighback-lower}} if collecting from individual \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}}.
