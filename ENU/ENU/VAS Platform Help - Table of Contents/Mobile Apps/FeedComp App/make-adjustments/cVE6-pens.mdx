  ## Synchronize Pen Data from DairyComp via PULSE (DC Unlimited subscribers)

  Since :Link[FeedComp synchronizes data with PULSE]{label="FeedComp synchronizes data with PULSE" overridedLabel="FeedComp synchronizes data with PULSE" spaceId docId="7vBYkI2WZAhfwKLZ9e7qE" version="v2" docAnchorId="#sync" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="FCM-Welcome.htm#sync" href="../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Mobile Apps/FeedComp App/welcome-to-feedcomp.mdx"}, \{\{masterVAS-Products.DCU-users-short}} can pull the latest animal counts from \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-simple}} (via \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}) into the app. Follow the steps below to continue:

  1. \{\{Commands.Click}} the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} to \{\{Commands.UserActions}}.
  2. \{\{Commands.UserActions}}`Edit Pen` at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Check the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompSyncCounts}} box, \{\{Commands.UserActions}}`Save` at the bottom of the screen.

  <hint type="warning">
    **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**&#x57;hen the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompSyncCounts}} box is checked, the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.P-en-}}**&#x20;Count** field **can**not**** be updated for this \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}}. If your device data connection is active, \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} data is synchronized from \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-simple}} (via \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}). If you do not have an active connection, \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} data will sync from \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-simple}} (via \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}) as soon as your device is back online. See :Link[Update Pen Count]{label="Update Pen Count" overridedLabel="Update Pen Count" spaceId docId="cVE6URg-HUF0olITJooo_" version="v2" docAnchorId="#pencounticons" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="cVE6-pens.mdx"} to learn more.
  </hint>
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Add Feedings to a Pen

  1. \{\{Commands.Click}} the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} to \{\{Commands.UserActions}}.
  2. \{\{Commands.UserActions}}`Edit Pen` at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Tap **+ Add Feeding** at the bottom of the screen.
  4. Update the **Mix Name**, select the \{\{Mixer}} and the \{\{Recipe}}, and then tap **Add&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.P-en-}} in the middle of the screen.
  5. Select the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}}, \{\{Commands.UserActions}}`Next` at the bottom of the screen.
  6. If more than one \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} was selected, tap and hold on a \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} and drop it into a new order, \{\{Commands.UserActions}}`Next` at the bottom of the screen.
  7. Enter the ingredient **Amount**, select \{\{DM/HD}} or \{\{AF}}, \{\{Commands.UserActions}}`Add` at the bottom of the screen.
  8. \{\{Commands.UserActions}}`Save` at the bottom of the screen.
</ExpandableHeading>
