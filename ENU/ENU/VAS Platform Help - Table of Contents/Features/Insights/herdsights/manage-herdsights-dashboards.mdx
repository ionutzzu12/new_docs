  ## View Dashboards

  After your \{\{Insights.KPIViews-only}} and \{\{Insights.KPIDashboards-lower-only}} are set up, you can check them whenever you need to track or measure specific details. Your \{\{Insights.KPIDashboards-lower-only}} refresh every 24 hours.  Follow the guidelines below when viewing your \{\{Insights.KPIDashboards-lower-only}}: 

  - Click **Show More Info** to open the data details in a new page. The data displays in a table format.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/gZ6jGVJGaYOm8CKMxkQei_kpi-dashboard-details.png" alt caption size="67"}

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/wIXwfkZ07oPcd_rKpJE8J_options-green-small41x29.png" size="10" caption="Options button" alt="Options button" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/icons/options-green-small_41x29.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/icons/options-green-small_41x29.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="true"></Image>

<ExpandableHeading indent="2">
  ## Add and Manage Dashboards

  Follow the steps below to add and manage your \{\{Insights.KPIDashboards-lower-only}}:

  1. Go to \{\{Insights.HerdSights}}**&#x20;>&#xA0;**\{\{Insights.KPIVIew-upper}}**&#x20;> My&#x20;**\{\{Insights.KPIVIew-upper}} to make sure the \{\{Insights.KPIViews-only}} you need are available for your dairy. See [Build Views](Views.htm) to learn more about \{\{Insights.KPIViews-only}}.
  2. \{\{Commands.Click}} the Options button

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/wIXwfkZ07oPcd_rKpJE8J_options-green-small41x29.png" size="10" caption="Options button" alt="Options button" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/icons/options-green-small_41x29.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/icons/options-green-small_41x29.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="true" indent="2"></Image>

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    at the top right of the \{\{Buttons-UI.FeatureType}} and select **Create New Dashboard**.
  </Paragraph>

  3. Provide a descriptive **Name** at the top left of the page. Then click **My&#x20;**\{\{Insights.KPI}}\{\{Insights.KPIVIew-upper}} to begin selecting the \{\{Insights.KPIViews-only}} for your dashboard. As you add \{\{Insights.KPIViews-only}}, the **Preview** on the right updates.
  4. Drag and drop your \{\{Insights.KPIViews-only}} to set their order from top to bottom of the page.
  5. When your \{\{Insights.KPIDashboard-lower}} is set to your liking, click **Save** at the top right of the page.
  6. If you'd like, reorder your dashboards by dragging and dropping \{\{Insights.KPIDashboard-lower}} tabs.
  7. If you no longer need a \{\{Insights.KPIDashboard-lower}}, click the Options button

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/wIXwfkZ07oPcd_rKpJE8J_options-green-small41x29.png" size="10" caption="Options button" alt="Options button" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/icons/options-green-small_41x29.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/icons/options-green-small_41x29.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="true" indent="2"></Image>

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    and select **Delete Current&#x20;**\{\{Insights.KPIDashboard-singular}}.:::ExpandableHeading\{customAnchorSlug="sm-add"}
  </Paragraph>

<ExpandableHeading indent="2">
  ## Customize a Dashboard

  Follow the steps below to customize an existing \{\{Insights.KPIDashboard-lower}}:

  1. \{\{Commands.Click}} the Options button

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/wIXwfkZ07oPcd_rKpJE8J_options-green-small41x29.png" size="10" caption="Options button" alt="Options button" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/icons/options-green-small_41x29.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/icons/options-green-small_41x29.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="true" indent="2"></Image>

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    at the top right of the \{\{Buttons-UI.FeatureType}} and select **Customize Current Dashboard**.
  </Paragraph>

  2. Click **My**\{\{Insights.KPI}}\{\{Insights.KPIVIew-upper}} and check or uncheck the \{\{Insights.KPIViews-only}} you'd like to add or remove from your dashboard. As you change which \{\{Insights.KPIViews-only}} are checked, the **Preview** on the right updates.
  3. If necessary, drag and drop your \{\{Insights.KPIViews-only}} to set their order from top to bottom of the page.
  4. When your \{\{Insights.KPIDashboard-lower}} is set to your liking, click **Save** at the top right of the page.:::ExpandableHeading\{customAnchorSlug="sm-add"}
