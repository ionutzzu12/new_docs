<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Required Permissions

  To use this feature, your \{\{General.ProductName}} user account must be assigned to a Role with \{\{RolesPermissions.PermissionNames}} permissions. See [Edit Dairy User Permissions](../../Config/UserMgmtRoles/EditUserPermissions.htm) and [Manage Dairy Roles](../../Config/UserMgmtRoles/ManageRoles.htm) for more information.
</ExpandableHeading>

After [customizing your settings](CustomizeHealthVAL.htm), view your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} health data in clear, easy-to-follow graphs on the [HealthVAL](HealthVAL.htm) page. Use the reports and graphs to identify and analyze trends, review \{\{Insights.CalfMortality}} data, or check the \{\{Insights.HealthIndexAnalysis}} for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}. The reports include up to 12 months of data to allow you to consider seasonal or weather-related differences in your analysis.
