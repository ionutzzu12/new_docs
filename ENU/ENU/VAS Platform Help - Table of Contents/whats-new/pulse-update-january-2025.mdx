ENHANCEMENT\{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}\{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}s now process events or status changes in the order completed.\{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}now processes \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}} updates for animals in the order they are completed, and the last event or status change supersedes previous animal updates. Previously, the first event or status change for the animal took precedence. For instance, if an animal is on a \{\{Worklists.VetListCommonUse}} for a pregnancy check and is marked as \{\{Events.6PREG}}, and is later determined to have aborted, \{\{Events.12ABORT}} is the final status and animal details adjust accordingly. See [Complete Tasks (v1)](<../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Mobile Apps/VAS Platform Mobile App (v1)/Tasks & Worklists (v1)/complete-tasks-v1.mdx>) or [Complete Tasks (v2)](<../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Mobile Apps/DC on Mobile (v2)/complete-tasks-v2.mdx>) to learn more about processing \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}s from mobile, or see [Process Open Worklist in PULSE](../../Features/DairyComp/ReportsWorklists/ProcessOpenWorklistsWeb.htm) for help with processing \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}s in web.

ENHANCEMENT\{\{Partners.HerdVision}} integration is now available! \{\{masterVAS.CompanyName-short}} is committed to partnering with other technology leaders in the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} industry, and we are excited to announce the general availability of the \{\{Partners.HerdVision}}\{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} integration!

[HerdVision](http://www.herdvision.co.uk/) is a versatile 2D and 3D imaging system that operates reliably in all lighting conditions and ensures continuous \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} monitoring. This system integrates seamlessly with existing \{\{Partners.parlor}} setups or cattle races and incorporates an Electronic Identification (\{\{EID}}) reader to track individual cows’ daily changes with unmatched accuracy. Data transmission options like WiFi, Ethernet, and 4/5G offer flexibility for farms of any scale or location. See [HerdVision Overview](<../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Partners/Health Monitoring/herdvision-info.mdx>) to learn more.

ENHANCEMENT`MyDC ONLY`New \{\{Events.3003BIRTH}} event! \{\{masterVAS-Products.MyDC-short}}\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}} have even more powerful event data entry at their fingertips. The new \{\{Events.3003BIRTH}} event is automatically added when calves are born as part of their \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-am}}'s \{\{Events.1FRESH}} event. This automates the addition of new animals to your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} and simplifies how your calves' parentage is tracked. See [BIRTH Event (MyDC only)](<../Features/Manage Your Animals/Events/hHY8-event-definitions/birth-event-mydc-only.mdx>) to learn more about this new event!
