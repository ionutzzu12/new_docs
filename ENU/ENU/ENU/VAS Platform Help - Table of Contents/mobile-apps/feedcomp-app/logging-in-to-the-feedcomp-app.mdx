The first time you log in to the app, \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} asks if you are using your device for feeding, and the login process differs based on your selection.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## What happens if I choose Yes, it is for Feeding?

  If you choose \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeedYes}}, the following occurs:

  1. You are prompted to choose the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} that you want to work with.:::ExpandableHeading

  ### What if I choose the wrong dairy?

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    If you select the wrong \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}, don't worry! You can always change the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} once you complete the login process. See :Link[Switch to Another Dairy]{label="Switch to Another Dairy" overridedLabel="Switch to Another Dairy" spaceId docId="sK8Esbx0pVQzqZ5wDAiMP" version="v2" docAnchorId="#dairy" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="FCM-UserAccount.htm#dairy" href="../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Mobile Apps/FeedComp App/account-settings.mdx"} to learn more.
    \:::
  </Paragraph>

  2. Once a \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} is selected, you are prompted to select a \{\{zone}} integration.:::ExpandableHeading

  ### Click here to learn more about zone integrations!

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompZone-singular}} integrations can only be used by one device at a time. When a specific zone integration is selected, \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} provides your device a specific login token; and, if the zone integration selected during the login process has been activated on another device, \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} will automatically log that device out of the app.
    \:::
  </Paragraph>

  3. After the zone integration is selected, select your user name and enter your PIN code.
  4. After completing the steps above, the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompHome}} is displayed.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## What happens if I choose No, it's not for Feeding?

  If you choose \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeedNo}}, the following occurs:

  1. You are prompted to enter your PIN code.
  2. Once your PIN code entered, you are asked to choose a \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} to work with.:::ExpandableHeading

  ### What if I choose the wrong dairy?

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    If you select the wrong \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}, don't worry! You can always change the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} once you complete the login process. See :Link[Switch to Another Dairy]{label="Switch to Another Dairy" overridedLabel="Switch to Another Dairy" spaceId docId="sK8Esbx0pVQzqZ5wDAiMP" version="v2" docAnchorId="#dairy" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="FCM-UserAccount.htm#dairy" href="../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Mobile Apps/FeedComp App/account-settings.mdx"} to learn more.
    \:::
  </Paragraph>

  3. After completing the steps above, the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompHome}} is displayed.
</ExpandableHeading>

The \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} app will remember your choice, and you will not be asked again the next time you log in. If you change your mind about using a device for feeding, :Link[log out of the app]{label="log out of the app" overridedLabel="log out of the app" spaceId docId="sK8Esbx0pVQzqZ5wDAiMP" version="v2" docAnchorId="#logout" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="FCM-UserAccount.htm#logout" href="../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Mobile Apps/FeedComp App/account-settings.mdx"} and select **Yes** or **No** when asked if the device will be used for feeding.
