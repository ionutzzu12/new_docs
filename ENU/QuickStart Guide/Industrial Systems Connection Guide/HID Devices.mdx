Human Interface Devices (HID) are USB-connected peripheral devices that can be polled for data. Examples include scanners used for barcodes and QR codes. To connect these devices to Litmus Edge, you can use the [HID Device (Gen1)](<HID Devices/HID Device _Gen1_3_.mdx>) serial DeviceHub driver.&#x20;
