<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Required Permissions

  Only users who are assigned a Role with Integrations Management permissions can connect partner integrations. See [Edit Dairy User Permissions](../../Platform/Features/Config/UserMgmtRoles/EditUserPermissions.htm) and [Manage Dairy Roles](<../../../Manage Dairy Users and Roles/manage-dairy-roles.mdx>) for more information.
</ExpandableHeading>

Follow these steps to integrate \{\{Partners.smaXtec}} with \{\{General.CompanyName-short}} and allow bolus data and alerts to display on the CowCard Partners tab and to generate health and reproduction worklists:

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Step 1: Capture Organisation Id

  - From the \{\{Partners.smaXtec}} Messenger app, view your Dashboard.
  - From the browser URL, copy the alpha-numeric Organisation \{\{ID}}. The ID includes all numbers and characters after `organisation=` in the URL. For instance, if the URL is `https://messenger.smaxtec.com/dashboard?organisation=1a2b3c456789101112131415`, the Organisation ID is: 1a2b3c456789101112131415.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/pbNaZ7We8cxt8M0aAIpV2_smaxtec-messengerurl.png" size="45" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Partners/smaxtec-messengerURL.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Partners/smaxtec-messengerURL.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  - Click **OK** to save your changes.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Step 2: Configure Items in DairyComp 305 (DairyComp Unlimited Users Only)

  To store item information produced by the smaXtec alerts, you must first create and map items in \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}}:

  1. In \{\{DC-UI.DC-ProductName}}, enter `PLATFORM` in the command line.
  2. Click the **Pulse Items** tab and expand \{\{Partners.smaXtec}}.
  3. If necessary, map or add an item. Expand :Link[smaXtec Items]{label="smaXtec Items" overridedLabel="smaXtec Items" spaceId docId="Mxq4jzY6YUCiz_ITpoFu8" version="v2" docAnchorId="#items" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="activate-smaxtec-integration.mdx"} to learn more about each item:1) **Map Existing Item:** To map an item to an existing \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}} item, double-click the item. Select the existing \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}} item, then click **OK** to confirm your selection.
     2\) **Add New Item:** To create a new item, check the box for that item. Click **Create (1) Items**, then click **Yes** to confirm your selection.
  4. Click **OK** to save your changes.
