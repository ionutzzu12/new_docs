<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Required Setup

  For the \{\{Partners.PartnersUIoption}} tab to display \{\{Partners.Forster}} data, your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} must first activate the integration. See [Activate Förster-Technik Integration](forster-integrate.htm) to learn more.
</ExpandableHeading>

The \{\{Widgets.ForsterDrinkSpeed}} widget shows how quickly milk was consumed by the calf per day. View the example below to for more information on the data shown in the widget.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/cZ3GJTWvw4i8ldMHcj2lm_drink-speed-widget.png" size="49" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Partners/forster/drink-speed-widget.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Partners/forster/drink-speed-widget.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

1. Units: Choose the units in which to display data\:Units: Choose the units in which to display data:\* Metric: Display data in Kilograms and Liters.
   - Imperial: Display data in Pounds and Gallons.:::hint\{type="info"}
     **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x54;his setting is independent of the Units selected in your [Account Settings](../../Platform/Features/Config/EditUserAcct.htm) and applies to all users in your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}.
     \:::
2. **Speed:&#x20;**&#x57;eight/minute of milk consumed.
