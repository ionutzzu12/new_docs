\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.H-erds-}} with a meter configured for daily milk readings can view an animal's daily milk in a table and a graph on the \{\{CowCards-UI.CC-tab-names}} tab in the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}} . You can click the **Bar Graph** or **Line Graph** buttons to change the graph style.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Daily Lactation Bar Graph

  This graph allows you to compare a recorded milk weight against other milkings from the same day and the weights from other days. Hover over a bar on the graph to view additional data.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/yM5kCPyDZNodsL_vW79lb_cc-dailymilk-prodcow15932-avgs.png" size="72" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/cowcards/cc-dailymilk-prodcow15932-avgs.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/cowcards/cc-dailymilk-prodcow15932-avgs.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  Each shift is color-coded. If the animal is identified while entering the parlor, but its actual milk weight for a shift is not captured by the parlor equipment, the shift average is added as an estimated milk weight. On the bar graph, estimated milk weights are represented by diagonal stripes. If an animal fails to be identified in the parlor for a shift, no data is included for that shift.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Weekly Lactation Line Chart

  Similar to the graph found on the [Lactations](CC-Lactations.htm) page, each point on the milk line represents the average milk weight recorded over the course of a week. Check a box under **References** to a second line and y-axis to the line graph. Only two data sets are allowed on the graph at any time, and the milk line is always included as a basis of comparison.

  Hover over a point on the graph to view data associated with a specific \{\{DIM}}.
