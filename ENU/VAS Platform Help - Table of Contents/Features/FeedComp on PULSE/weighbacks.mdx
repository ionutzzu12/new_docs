\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeed}} left over from a prior feeding is called a "\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompWeighback-lower}}" in \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}}. \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompWeighbacks}} may also be called "refusals" or "cleanups." You can manage \{\{masterVAS-Products.AllProductNames}}\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} on the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompButtons}}\{\{General.UI-gui}}. You can find this feature under the :Link[FeedComp Menu]{label="FeedComp Menu" overridedLabel="FeedComp Menu" spaceId docId="WFLX7SPMpZfy7zoMyrvRh" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Menu-FeedComp" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../../../NavPlatform/Menu.htm#Menu-FeedComp" href="../../../ENU/VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/navigate-pulse/menu-options.mdx"}.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/f4fHyTkkFH3L7VVuvT_zB_fcp-weighbacks.png" size="97" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/feedcomp-img/web/fcp-weighbacks.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/feedcomp-img/web/fcp-weighbacks.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompWeighbacks}} help you reduce waste by adding leftover feed to existing recipes, and are typically picked up the morning after the scheduled feeding. When you create a \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompWeighback-lower}} from one of yesterday’s scheduled feedings, \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} automatically recalculates the \{\{dry matter}} intake and updates the [DM Intake Report](Reports/dm-intake-report.mdx). For example, if you pick up 200 pounds of unused feed from a \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}}, the dry matter intake for that \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} is readjusted to subtract 200 pounds from the prior day’s feeding, which helps reduce costs and keep your ingredient amounts more precise.

<hint type="info">
  **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x50;lease note that you can only create, edit, or delete \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompWeighback-singular}}-Only Pick Ups in \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedCompWeb}}. You can create :Link[Filler Loads]{label="Filler Loads" overridedLabel="Filler Loads" spaceId docId="d2HWr0rpbBTW5tg47euCM" version="v2" docAnchorId="#fillerload" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../mobile/FCM-Weighbacks.htm#fillerload" href="../mobile/FCM-Weighbacks.htm#fillerload"} in the \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp-upper}}.
</hint>

  ## View Weighback Details

  \{\{Commands.Click}} on a \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Upper}} \{\{Controls.ControlTypes}} in the table to open the \{\{General.UI-gui}} and view its details. Follow the guidelines below when \{\{Commands.UserActions}}\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}\{\{Controls.ControlTypes}}:

  - \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompEquipment}}: The name of the equipment used for the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} is displayed at the top left of the side panel.
  - Pick Up Date: The \{\{Controls.ControlTypes}} when the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} was \{\{Commands.UserActions}}.
  - Pick Up Time: The \{\{Controls.ControlTypes}} when the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} was \{\{Commands.UserActions}}.
  - Feeder: The name of the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} who completed the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompWeighback-lower}}.
  - \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompLocation-singular}}: The name of the location where the first ingredient was picked up for the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompWeighback-lower}}.
  - \{\{Controls.ControlNames}}%: The dry matter percentage for the first ingredient was picked up for the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompWeighback-lower}}.
  - \{\{Controls.ControlNames}} Amount: The as-fed amount for the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}.
  - DM Amount: The dry matter amount for the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}.
  - Pick Ups: A list of \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}} and locations where the ingredients were picked up for the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompWeighback-lower}}.
  - Drops: A list of \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}} and locations where the ingredients were dropped off for the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompWeighback-lower}}.
</ExpandableHeading>
