  \{\{Commands.Click}}\{\{Commands.UserActions}} \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Upper}} at the top right of the page to open the side panel and \{\{Commands.UserActions}} information for the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}.

  - The \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Upper}}**&#x20;Name**\{\{Commands.UserActions}} must be unique and cannot match the name of existing \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}.
  - If "Mobile Mixer" is selected in \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompEquipment}}**&#x20;Type**, you can \{\{Commands.UserActions}} the **Capacity**and **Acknowledgment Threshold**. If the weight of an ingredient drops below the threshold set while mixing a \{\{recipe}}, \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} displays an alert that requires attention before adding the next ingredient to the \{\{mixer}}. This ensures that ingredients are recorded correctly when their amounts are too small to be recognized by the scale while mixing.
