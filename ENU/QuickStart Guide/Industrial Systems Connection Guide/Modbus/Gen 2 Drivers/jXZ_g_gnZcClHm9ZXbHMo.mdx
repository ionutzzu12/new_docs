Use this driver instead of the :Link[Modbus ASCII]{label="Modbus ASCII" spaceId="JWQMp_kvm0WsA_Fpjt1v_" docId="TBvlUfWx-qLZRUw_05yxl" version="v2" docAnchorId loadingMethod="dynamic" overridedLabel} driver when multiple stations are connected to the RS-485 bus. The **Station ID** setting can be customized at the tag level with this driver. The device connection status is defined by the last received response.

Review the following information for setting up and configuring the Modbus ASCII Advanced driver.&#x20;
