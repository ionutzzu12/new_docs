<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Required Setup

  - This feature is only available when you’re using this device for feeding. If you change your mind about using a device for feeding, :Link[log out of the app]{label="log out of the app" overridedLabel="log out of the app" spaceId docId="sK8Esbx0pVQzqZ5wDAiMP" version="v2" docAnchorId="#logout" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="FCM-UserAccount.htm#logout" href="../account-settings.mdx"} and select **Yes** or **No** when asked if the device will be used for feeding.
  - Before you can begin using \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} to feed at your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}, you must complete the required setup. See [Before You Begin: Required Setup](../before-you-begin-required-setup.mdx) to learn more about the required setup order.
  - Devices used for feeding must be connected to a scale. See [Scales](../scales.mdx) to learn more about connecting to your scale.
  - Bluetooth must be enabled on both loading and feeding devices.
</ExpandableHeading>

Use \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompLoaderView}} to monitor and manage loads from a loader device. \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompLoaderView}} allows you to connect your loading device to your feeding device, so any operations on your feeding device are mirrored on your loading device. When \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompLoaderView}} is enabled on a loading device, you can advance or pause feedings and view real-time feeding details while loading is in progress.

<hint type="info">
  **TIP:&#x20;**&#x54;o use this feature, Bluetooth must be enabled on both loading and feeding devices.
</hint>

Expand and follow the steps in the sections below to enable and start using \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompLoaderView}}:

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Add Loader Equipment to FeedComp

  Before you can use \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompLoaderView}}, add loader equipment to the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompEquipment}} list in \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} if you have not already done so:

  1. Go to the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompAdjustments}} screen.
  2. Tap `Create Equipment` at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Enter the loader name.
  4. Select **Loader** as the equipment type.
  5. Tap **Save** at the bottom of the screen.

  After you finish adding loader equipment to \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}}, :Link[connect the loader equipment to FeedComp]{label="connect the loader equipment to FeedComp" overridedLabel="connect the loader equipment to FeedComp" spaceId docId="wNiKGL-uRhT810QXhF33p" version="v2" docAnchorId="#connectequip" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="loader-view.mdx"}.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Connect Loader Equipment to FeedComp

  After you :Link[add loader equipment]{label="add loader equipment" overridedLabel="add loader equipment" spaceId docId="wNiKGL-uRhT810QXhF33p" version="v2" docAnchorId="#addequip" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="loader-view.mdx"} to the Equipment list, connect the loader equipment to \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}}:

  1. Go to :Link[Account   Settings > Settings]{label="Account   Settings > Settings" overridedLabel="Account   Settings > Settings" spaceId docId="sK8Esbx0pVQzqZ5wDAiMP" version="v2" docAnchorId="#equipment" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="FCM-UserAccount.htm#equipment" href="../account-settings.mdx"}.
  2. Select the loader equipment.
  3. Save your selection and exit **Settings**.

  After you finish connecting loader equipment, you can now :Link[connect the loading device to the feeding device]{label="connect the loading device to the feeding device" overridedLabel="connect the loading device to the feeding device" spaceId docId="wNiKGL-uRhT810QXhF33p" version="v2" docAnchorId="#connect" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="loader-view.mdx"} and start using \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompLoaderView}}.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Connect a Loading Device to a Feeding Device

  <hint type="warning">
    **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**

    \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompLoaderView}}

    will not work if you do not complete the following requirements:

    - Loader equipment must be \[added]\(#addequip) and :Link[connected]{label="connected" overridedLabel="connected" spaceId docId="wNiKGL-uRhT810QXhF33p" version="v2" docAnchorId="#connectequip" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="loader-view.mdx"} to \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}}.
    - The feeding device must be connected to a scale.
    - A feeding must be started on the feeding device.
  </hint>

  After you complete the requirements above, follow the steps below to enable \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompLoaderView}}, so you can connect your loading device to your feeding device:

  1. Tap \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeed}} at the bottom of the screen.
  2. Tap \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompLoaderView}}.
  3. Tap `Connect Now` at the bottom of the screen.
  4. Select the system you're connecting to: \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.AppleOS-TM}} or \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.AndroidOS}}.
  5. Select the feeding device, and then tap `Connect` at the bottom of the screen.
  6. After your device is successfully connected to the feeding device, tap `Continue` at the bottom of the screen.

  After establishing the connection, you can view and manage :Link[loading processes from your loading device.]{label="loading processes from your loading device." overridedLabel="loading processes from your loading device." spaceId docId="wNiKGL-uRhT810QXhF33p" version="v2" docAnchorId="#load" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="loader-view.mdx"}
</ExpandableHeading>
