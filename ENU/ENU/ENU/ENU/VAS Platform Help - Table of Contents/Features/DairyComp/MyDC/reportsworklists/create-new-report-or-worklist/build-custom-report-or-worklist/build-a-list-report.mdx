A LIST report in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}generates a listing of animals with certain items and conditions.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/KlA8PR2f3r2e_sS2xXAln_cmdbuilder-list-example.png" size="57" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/cmdline/cmdbuilder-LIST-example.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/cmdline/cmdbuilder-LIST-example.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

Follow the steps below to continue:

1. Use the search field at the top of the items list to find items by name or description. For example, searching for "\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.s-ire}}" finds all associated items in your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}: \{\{LSIR}}, MGSIR, \{\{PSIRC}}, \{\{SIR1}}, etc.:::ExpandableHeading\{customAnchorSlug="SM-1"}

2. To build your report, drag and drop items from the items list onto the **Report Output** area. Reorder the columns by dragging and dropping the item names

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/lFWM3M7d9I3Oxkmc0JjJJ_cmdbuilder-edit-item58x25.png" size="10" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/cmdline/cmdbuilder-edit-item_58x25.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/cmdline/cmdbuilder-edit-item_58x25.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false" indent="2"></Image>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  or click the **x** next to an item name to delete it.:::ExpandableHeading\{customAnchorSlug="SM-2"}
</Paragraph>

3. To set filters, drag and drop items from the items list into the **Filters** area, and then choose an **Operator** from the drop-down and set a **Value** for the operator.:::ExpandableHeading\{customAnchorSlug="SM-3"}

4. To customize how items are sorted on your report, drag and drop items from the items list into the **Sorting** area, and then choose the sort order:\* A - Z: Ascending sort (low to high, earliest to latest)
   - Z - A: Descending sort (high to low, latest to earliest)
5. In the **Switches** area, select to include alive, dead, or all animals in your report.
6. Click **Save and Execute** to build your report and display the results of the report on the [ReportWorklistResults.htm](<../../../../../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/DairyComp/MyDC/Reports/Worklists/Create New Report or Worklist/see-reportsworklists-results.mdx>)[Reports/Worklists Results](<../../../../../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/DairyComp/MyDC/Reports/Worklists/Create New Report or Worklist/see-reportsworklists-results.mdx>) page.\{\{masterVAS-Products.DCU-users-short}} can also preview the command as the report is being built.:::ExpandableHeading\{customAnchorSlug="SM-4"}
