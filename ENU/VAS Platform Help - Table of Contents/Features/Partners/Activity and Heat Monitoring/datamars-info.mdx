Their \{\{Partners.Datamars\_Tru-Test}} products monitor individual animals' heat and health behavior and provide accurate insights about animal activity. \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} will display live data from \{\{Partners.Datamars\_Tru-Test}} in a future release.
