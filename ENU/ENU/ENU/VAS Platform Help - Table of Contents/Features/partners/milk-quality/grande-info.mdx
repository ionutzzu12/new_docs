[Grande Cheese](https://www.grande.com/) provides milk plant data for participating \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} producers. The \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}\{\{Buttons-UI.Dashboard}} widgets display delivery details and bacteria data for up to 28 days, including the following details:

- Amount of milk delivered
- Milk composition details, including fat and protein percentages
- Milk testing results, including somatic cell count (\{\{SCC}}), coliform count, lab pasteurized count (LPC), and standard plate count (SPC) data
