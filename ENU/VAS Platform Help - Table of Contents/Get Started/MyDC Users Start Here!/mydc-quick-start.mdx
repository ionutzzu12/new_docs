Welcome, \{\{masterVAS-Products.DCP-users-long}}! Follow the steps below to get started with \{\{masterVAS-Products.MyDC-short}}:

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Step 1: Log in on web & mobile

  1. Begin by [signing up](../sign-up.mdx) for \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.
  2. [Log in](LoginToPlatform.htm).
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Step 2: Get started with MyDC!

  After you log in, \{\{Commands.click}} the **Get Started with&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Products.MyDC-short}} button on the upper right side of the page to add your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/cfxaEL8ZInCm0MQNeNxr0_mydc-get-started.png" size="79" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Platform/getting-started/MyDC-get-started.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/getting-started/MyDC-get-started.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  \{\{masterVAS-Products.MyDC-short}} walks you through your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} setup, provides options to import your animal data, and allows you to begin your \{\{masterVAS-Products.MyDC-short}} subscription.

  <hint type="info">
    **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x49;f your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} has already been added, click the **Connect to a New&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}} button to request access. See [Request Access to a Dairy](../request-access-to-a-dairy.mdx) to learn more!
  </hint>

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Check out these tips before you begin!

    - \{\{Did}} you know you can automate your data entry process? Here's how:\* If you're an \{\{Partners.AgSource}} customer, transfer your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} database and latest animal records automatically using an \{\{RAC}} during step *2 - Identify your&#x20;*\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}}. Find this code on the bottom right corner of your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.H-erd-}} Summary or contact your \{\{Partners.AgSource}} representative for help.
      - Use an \{\{STF}} from your \{\{DRPC}} to import your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} database and latest animal records. If you have a \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.t-estday}} approaching and prefer to hold off and import the latest file, you can complete the setup process first and then come back to \{\{masterVAS-Products.MyDC-short}} to complete the upload. Be sure to select **Yes** in step *4 - Tell us about your&#x20;*\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}}*'s Data* to indicate your plan to upload an STF.
    - If your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} has a herdcode, have it handy! You can find this 8-digit number in your DRPC communications or \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.t-estday}} results. Contact your DRPC if you need help finding your herdcode.
    - If your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} is tax exempt, have your 9-digit exemption number or taxpayer \{\{ID}} ready. You will also need to upload a copy of your certificate, which can be a photo of the actual document. Use your phone to quickly snap a photo and upload it in step *5 - Tax exemptions*!
    - If you have a promotion code, have it ready and add it in step *6 - Estimate your cost*.
    - Have your credit card on hand to set up your subscription.
  </ExpandableHeading>

  After the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} setup process completes, you are ready to import your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} data or add your animals. Expand the sections below to learn more:

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Import herd data from AgSource

    If your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} uses \{\{Partners.AgSource}} for milk testing services and you entered your RAC in step *2 - Identify your&#x20;*\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}} of the setup wizard, \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} will automatically import your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} and notify you by email when your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} data is available.
  </ExpandableHeading>

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Import an STF

    If you selected **Yes** in step *4 - Tell us about your&#x20;*\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}}*'s Data* to indicate your plan to upload an STF, go to the **Quick Actions** section of your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-omepage}} and click **Upload** to import your data.

    <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/nOWVAigDlBLt5fmzeemOQ_mydc-get-started-upload-stf.png" size="58" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Platform/getting-started/MyDC-get-started-upload-STF.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/getting-started/MyDC-get-started-upload-STF.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

    \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} will import your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} data and then notify you by email when your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} is available. This may take up to one business day depending on the size of your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}.
  </ExpandableHeading>

  <ExpandableHeading>

See :Link[Add Animals]{label="Add Animals" overridedLabel="Add Animals" spaceId docId="14xYkFBw518uSYQl2REiD" version="v2" docAnchorId="#AddAnimal" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../Features/DairyComp/Animals/AddAnimals.htm#AddAnimal" href="../../Features/DairyComp/MyDC/Animals/add-animals.mdx"} to continue!

  ## Step 3: Check your settings

  \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} includes a [Settings](../../Features/DairyComp/MyDC/Fo2o-settings.mdx) page where you can view and edit various options for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}. We suggest you check the following settings when you're getting started:

  - Your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}'s :Link[Default Breed]{label="Default Breed" overridedLabel="Default Breed" spaceId docId="87n3BKxetaoNmZIUQMas_" version="v2" docAnchorId="#general" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../Features/DairyComp/Settings/DC-AnimalConfiguration.htm#general" href="../Features/DairyComp/Settings/DC-AnimalConfiguration.htm#general"}.
  - Your :Link[Preferred Identifier]{label="Preferred Identifier" overridedLabel="Preferred Identifier" spaceId docId="87n3BKxetaoNmZIUQMas_" version="v2" docAnchorId="#naming" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../Features/DairyComp/Settings/DC-AnimalConfiguration.htm#naming" href="../Features/DairyComp/Settings/DC-AnimalConfiguration.htm#naming"}, such as ID or barn name.
  - Default [Event Definitions](<../../Features/DairyComp/MyDC/Settings/General Settings/event-definitions.mdx>) and [Item Definitions](../Features/DairyComp/Settings/DC-ItemDefinitions.htm). You can edit the descriptions for events and items, or you can also deactivate events your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} does not plan to track.
  - Your [Lactation Configuration](../Features/DairyComp/Settings/DC-LactConfiguration.htm) settings, which control prompted items for \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.f-resh-cow}} cows and new calves.

  ## You're all set! Now put PULSE to work for you!

  Check out the topics below to start using \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} to manage your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}!

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Manage users and create teams

    See [Invite a User to Your Dairy](<../../Features/Manage Dairy Users and Roles/invite-a-user-to-your-dairy.mdx>) to add new users to your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} and [Manage Dairy Users and Roles](<../../../ENU/VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/features/manage-dairy-users-and-roles.mdx>) to learn more about managing your users.

    You can also group \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} users into Teams, which allows you to set the types of tasks the users typically perform. For instance, you may have a Breeding team that handles all insemination activity. You can add users to the Breeding team and then set their associated events (BRED, ABORT, \{\{DNB}}, \{\{PREG}}) to include the team. See [Teams](../Features/DairyComp/Settings/DC-Teams.htm) and [Event Definitions](<../../Features/DairyComp/MyDC/Settings/General Settings/event-definitions.mdx>) to learn more!
  </ExpandableHeading>

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Get familiar with the CowCard!

    A \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} CowCard contains detailed status and event information about each animal in your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}, including reproduction status and days in milk, as well as any milk and meat withholds. Use CowCards to quickly access animal details.

    <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/V4DYSaiOZq-8bn56QLzWJ_cowcard-overview.png" size="93" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Platform/cowcards/cowcard-overview.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/cowcards/cowcard-overview.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

    CowCards use the following common terms, which are known as "items" in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}: 

    - \{\{LACT}}: Lactation number
    - \{\{RPRO}}: Reproductive code (or status)
    - \{\{DIM}}: Days in milk in current lactation
    - \{\{DSLH}}: Days since last heat
    - \{\{DCC}}: Days carrying calf/pregnant
    - \{\{DUE}}: Date due to \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.f-reshen}} (if pregnant)

    See [View Animal Details on CowCards](../Features/CowCards/WorkingWithCowCards.htm) to learn more!

    <hint type="info">
      **TIP:&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}}s are your primary source for animal data, and you can set which details to include, in your preferred layout. See [Items](<../../Features/Manage Your Animals/items.mdx>) to learn more about setting \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} to display your animal data. You can also use :Link[mobile CowCards]{label="mobile CowCards" overridedLabel="mobile CowCards" spaceId docId="GCoRUf_dQxgxqRXNhqmR-" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Explore" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../MobileApp/MobHerd.htm#Explore" href="../../Mobile Apps/VAS Platform Mobile App (v1)/animals-on-mobile-v1.mdx"} and [Manage Animals (v2)](<../../../ENU/ENU/VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/mobile-apps/dc-on-mobile-v2/manage-animals-v2.mdx>) while out on the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}!
    </hint>
  </ExpandableHeading>

    You can also define a Default Remark field for your protocols, which allows you to capture custom details for specific health events.
