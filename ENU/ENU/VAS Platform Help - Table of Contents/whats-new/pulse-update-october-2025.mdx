NEW FEATURE Meet \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}}: a simplified and smarter feeding program. Built to cut waste, boost mixing accuracy, and optimize the most costly and complex area of \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} management, \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} takes the guesswork (and paperwork!) out of your feed operations.

\{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}}'s intuitive mobile and web apps make feeding effortless: quickly check inventory and adjust feedings, use built-in reports to track feeder performance and projections, leverage automatic \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} count updates or step-by-step mixin guidance, and more. Use the powerful, yet easy-to-use tool anywhere, online or offline, with only two pieces of \{\{tech}}: your tablet or phone and a Bluetooth-enabled scale head.

Ready to start your smarter feeding journey? [Contact Us](<../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Get Started/contact-us.mdx>) to get started and explore [FeedComp (mobile)](../mobile-apps/feedcomp-app.mdx) and [FeedComp (web)](../../Features/FeedComp/web/FCP-Home.htm) to learn more.

## Bug Fixes

- \{\{MorphHelp.FeatureIntegrations}} product in \{\{masterVAS-Products.WC-Platform}}\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}}: Resolved an issue where \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedWatchPlatform}} was not listed on the\{\{masterVAS-Products.WC-Platform}}**&#x20;> Settings > Product** page for \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}} with both integrations.
- \{\{masterVAS-Products.WC-Platform}} load summaries: When filtering harvest loads by truck or field, summary details are now shown as expected.
- \{\{masterVAS-Products.WC-Platform}} timezone fix: Date filtering was adjusted to reflect the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}'s timezone, not a user's local timezone.
- Update animal data fix: \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} no longer flags deleted animals as duplicates when importing sire mating data.
- Embryo transfer breed: Addressed an unexpected embryo breed error when entering \{\{Events.3000TRANSFER}} events.
- `MyDC ONLY`\{\{Events.23MEASURE}} date fix: Dates for \{\{Events.23MEASURE}} events are now being recorded correctly when choosing a previous date.
- `MyDC ONLY`FDAT/BDAT restriction update: \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} restrictions on requiring a calf's birthdate (BDAT) and a dam's \{\{Events.1FRESH}} date (FDAT) to match were relaxed to allow for manual updates as needed in \{\{masterVAS-Products.MyDC-short}}\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}}.
- `MyDC ONLY`ONOV tasks cleared for new lactations: ONOV tasks are now automatically cleared when starting a new lactation.
