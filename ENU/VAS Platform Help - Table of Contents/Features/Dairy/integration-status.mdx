  ### How do I check the connection status between DairyComp and PULSE in DairyComp 305?

  In \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}}, you can check the connection status by clicking the **Connection Status** tab at the bottom of the \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-simple}} screen. To learn more, see [Connection Status](https://dc-help.vas.com/default.htm#cshid=1089)\{\{masterVAS-Phrases.RefsToOtherHelpProjects}}.
</ExpandableHeading>

Quickly check the connection status between \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} and all of your on-premise applications, like \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-simple}}, on the **Integration Status**\{\{Buttons-UI.FeatureType}}. Click the Integration Status button

  ## Troubleshooting Guidelines

  Follow the steps below to troubleshoot any communication or data sync errors. After you resolve your issue, it may take a few minutes for the Communication Status to change to a healthy heartbeat and it may take longer for data to synchronize:

  - Check your internet connection. Verify that the on-premise computer has an active internet connection. If necessary, restart your modem, router, or other equipment, and ensure your internet provider is not experiencing an outage. The Communication Status may take a few minutes to change to a healthy heartbeat.
  - Restart your on-premise computer. Contact your manager or office staff to verify that the \{\{masterVAS-Products.OnPremise-lower}} app is running normally. If it is not, restart your on-premise computer.
  - Make sure that you have the latest version of your \{\{masterVAS-Products.OnPremise-lower}} applications. If \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} detects that your \{\{masterVAS-Products.OnPremise-lower}} software is out of date, the system will display caution and warning messages to inform you that an \{\{masterVAS-Products.OnPremise-lower}} software update is either recommended or required; older versions of some apps may stop syncing data. See :Link[Update Your VAS Software]{label="Update Your VAS Software" overridedLabel="Update Your VAS Software" spaceId docId="Fd8QuwzdEFx_q7JKcYm7K" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Update" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="integration-status.mdx"} to learn more about updating your out-of-date software. See :Link[Offline DairyComp Integration]{label="Offline DairyComp Integration" overridedLabel="Offline DairyComp Integration" spaceId docId="Fd8QuwzdEFx_q7JKcYm7K" version="v2" docAnchorId="#offline" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="integration-status.mdx"} for actions to take when your \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}} connection is suspended or reloading.\* Orange caution messages indicate that even though your \{\{masterVAS-Products.OnPremise-lower}} software is still compatible with \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, but it should be updated to support recent \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} feature updates.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/yEBh09r8o8JImewJ0fFeu_zone-caution.png" alt caption size="88"}
    - Red warning messages indicate that your \{\{masterVAS-Products.OnPremise-lower}} software is too out-of -date to synchronize properly with \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}. In this case, \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} ceases to synchronize your data until you update to a supported version of the \{\{masterVAS-Products.OnPremise-lower}} software for the respective \{\{zone}}.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/9Ruwfhue3YvimuhwK6pv5_zone-warning.png" alt caption size="88"}

  If the errors continue, [Contact Us](../../GetStarted/ContactUs.htm).

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Offline DC305 Integration (DairyComp Unlimited Dairies Only)

  If your \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}} connection goes down, \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} warns you and specifies the actions you need to take. This ensures you don't inadvertently edit outdated animal records and keeps your \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} data current. \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} detects three different offline modes to safeguard your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} data: 

  - Obsolete integration: Your \{\{masterVAS-Products.OnPremise-lower}}\{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}} software is too out-of-date to synchronize properly. Your herd data in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} and mobile app features will remain read-only until you update \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}}. See [Update DC305 to the Latest Release](https://dc-help.vas.com/default.htm#cshid=1009) in the \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}}\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-elp}} for detailed instructions.
  - Suspended integration: Your connection to \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}} has been down for more than 60 days. \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} and mobile app features will remain read-only until the connection is re-established. [Contact Us](../../GetStarted/ContactUs.htm) to reload your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} from \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}}.
  - Reloading integration::Variable\{key="masterVAS.CompanyName-short" type="variable"} Support is reloading your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}. \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} and mobile app features will remain read-only until the reload completes. This may take up to an hour, depending on the size of your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.c-owfile}} and current server traffic.
</ExpandableHeading>

  ## Request Export to Equipment (FeedWatch Only)

  Click the **Send Export** button to send changes you've made in \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedWatchPlatform}} (such as an ingredient \{\{dry matter}} percentage (\{\{DM%}}) adjustment) to the feeders immediately.

  <hint type="info">
    **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x43;hanges made in \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedWatchPlatform}} are automatically sent to the \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedWatchOnPremise}} computer. In order for those changes to be sent from the computer to the feeders immediately (so they can used for feeding), you need to click Send Export.
  </hint>
</ExpandableHeading>
