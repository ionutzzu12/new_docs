This section lists all of our current health monitoring partners. Their products can provide health-related information about the animals, like \{\{lame}} and \{\{trim}} event details, to help you monitor and manage each animal's fitness.

After you activate an integration with one of the following partners, \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} displays animal data received from the partners' products.
