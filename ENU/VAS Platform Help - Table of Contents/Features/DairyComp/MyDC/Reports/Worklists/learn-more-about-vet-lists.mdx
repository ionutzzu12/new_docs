\{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}\{\{Worklists.VetListUI-Web}} provide you a flexible workflow where you can select follow‑up events for each animal and save animals in a queue when they require additional actions before processing.

\{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} mobile app delivers the same audible cues and sounds that many customers are familiar with from the \{\{PCC}} product, which helps your veterinarian and \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} workers quickly identify the required \{\{Mobile-UI.VetCodeSingular}}s for the animals they scan against the \{\{Worklists.VetListCommonUse}}.

Use \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} or \{\{DC-UI.DC-ProdNameShort}} to create your \{\{Worklists.VetListCommonUse}}s with a special VETLIST command. For \{\{DC-UI.DC-ProdNameShort}} lists, adding the \K switch to the commands sends them to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}. The flexibility of the system allows you to schedule the \{\{Worklists.VetListCommonUse}}s to generate on days the veterinarian is scheduled to visit, or to generate and send them on an as-needed basis.

You can then process your lists in the mobile app and [monitor them in the web version of PULSE](../../../MobileApp/Worklists/MonitorListsMob.htm). See the topics below to learn more:

- [Complete Tasks (v1)](<../../../../../Mobile Apps/VAS Platform Mobile App (v1)/Tasks & Worklists (v1)/complete-tasks-v1.mdx>)
- [Complete Tasks (v2)](../../../DC-Mobile/MobAppTasks.htm)
