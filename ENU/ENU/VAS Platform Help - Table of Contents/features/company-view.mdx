Use **Company View** to see essential information for all \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}} within your company. Monitor your company's progress and statistics using two powerful tools: use [Company Dashboards](<../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Company/company-dashboards.mdx>) to view key animal data for all \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}}, such as Cow Stats, or use [OneView](<../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Company/oneview.mdx>) to view past, present, and future inventory data for all \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}} in easy-to-follow graphs.

**Company features are only available when viewing a company in&#x20;**\{\{General.ProductName-short}}**, not when viewing a&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}**.** To access company features, a member of the company must invite you to connect with it. See [Invite a User to Your Dairy](<../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Manage Dairy Users and Roles/invite-a-user-to-your-dairy.mdx>) for more information.
