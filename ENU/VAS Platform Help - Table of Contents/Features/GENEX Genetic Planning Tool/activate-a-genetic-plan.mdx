After you build your genetic plan and review the generated [reports](Reports-GenPlan.htm), you must activate the plan to finalize it and share it with the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Activate the Plan

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/M4LrEK_TUef5vUmAoBU_D_reports-genex-activate.png" size="64" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/GenPlanning/Reports/reports-genex-activate.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/GenPlanning/Reports/reports-genex-activate.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  1. Click **Activate and Send to&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/iLQWcyW7UBA6JRDZac1GC_activate-plan-button165x30.png" size="10" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/GenPlanning/activate-plan-button_165x30.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/GenPlanning/activate-plan-button_165x30.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false" indent="2"></Image>

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    at the bottom right side of the window. If you would like to modify the plan, \{\{Commands.click}}**Edit Plan**
  </Paragraph>

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/ZiLz5n4xZ0_Q9gKcZRr3C_edit-plan-button85x30.png" size="10" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/GenPlanning/edit-plan-button_85x30.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/GenPlanning/edit-plan-button_85x30.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false" indent="2"></Image>

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    .
  </Paragraph>

  2. From the pop-up window, select the data you want to send to \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-long}} and \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}:1) Top three sire choices for each female in the plan
     2\) Semen type of choice for each female in the plan
     3\) Female index values (for the plan's selected breed only)
     4\) Female ranking (for the plan's selected breed only)
     5\) Progeny index values:::ExpandableHeading

  ### Can't select one of the options?

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    If an option is not available, the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} has not configured the required items. Based on their \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC-Platform}} subscription, the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} must complete the following steps to configure the items:
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    \{\{masterVAS-Products.MyDC-short}} customers: Contact [VAS Support](http://platform-docs.vas.com/default.htm#cshid=1004).
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    \{\{masterVAS-Products.DCUnlimited}} customers: Follow the steps in :Link[Configure Items in DC305 (DairyComp Unlimited Users Only)]{label="Configure Items in DC305 (DairyComp Unlimited Users Only)" overridedLabel="Configure Items in DC305 (DairyComp Unlimited Users Only)" spaceId docId="bOFIx0XLID5V8_6mI2RS6" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Configure" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="activate-a-genetic-plan.mdx"}.
    \:::
  </Paragraph>

  3. Click **Activate and Send to&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} to activate the plan.
     After the plan is activated, you will no longer be able to make changes or adjustments to the plan.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Configure Items in DC305 (DairyComp Unlimited Users Only)

  <hint type="info">
    **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x54;he following steps are only for \{\{masterVAS-Products.DCUnlimited}} users. If you have a \{\{masterVAS-Products.MyDC-short}} subscription, contact [VAS Support](http://platform-docs.vas.com/default.htm#cshid=1004) to configure your items.
  </hint>

  Some genetic planning information must be configured before you can send the data to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} and \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}}. Follow the steps below to configure the required \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} items in \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}}:

  1. In \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-long}}, enter `PLATFORM `\{\{masterVAS-Phrases.CmdLineEntry}} to open the \{\{masterVAS.CompanyName-short}}**&#xA0;**\{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}**&#x20;Setup** window.
  2. Open the \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}**&#x20;Items** tab and expand **Everything Else** to see the list of configurable items.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/f7EiEGPvKsMTBpAJKf1iC_configure-items-setup.png" size="51" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/GenPlanning/Reports/configure-items-setup.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/GenPlanning/Reports/configure-items-setup.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false" indent="2"></Image>

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    Select the \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} item you want to configure.
  </Paragraph>

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/mvCNaPcrZ1G0i3lXSgJUZ_configure-items-map.png" size="59" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/GenPlanning/Reports/configure-items-map.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/GenPlanning/Reports/configure-items-map.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false" indent="2"></Image>

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    Map or add the \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} genetic planning items:1) **Map Existing Item:** To map the \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} item to an existing \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}} item, click **Select item for \[**\{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}**&#x20;item]** at the bottom of the window. Select the \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}} item and click **OK**.
    2\) **Add New Item:** To create a new \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}} item for the \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} item, check the box next to the \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} item and click **Create (1) Items** at the bottom of the window. Click **OK** to add the new item to the \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}} database.
  </Paragraph>
</ExpandableHeading>
