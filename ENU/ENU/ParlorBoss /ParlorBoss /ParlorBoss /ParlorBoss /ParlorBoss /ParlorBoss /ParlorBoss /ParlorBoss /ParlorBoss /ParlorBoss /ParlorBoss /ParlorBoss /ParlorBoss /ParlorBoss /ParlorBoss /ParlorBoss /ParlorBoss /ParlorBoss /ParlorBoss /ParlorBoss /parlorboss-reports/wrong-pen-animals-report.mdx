\{\{Commands.UserActions}} the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompReportNames}}\{\{Controls.ControlTypes}} to \{\{Commands.UserActions}} by \{\{Partners.parlor}}, date, and shift. The report details can help you make corrections in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} or \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}} (for \{\{masterVAS-Products.DCUnlimited-short}}\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}}) to ensure animal data is accurate and up to date.

![](https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/dTRLvmnspu36JXeT6oDAZ_pb-reports-wrongpenanimals.png)

<hint type="info">
  **NOTE:&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Products.ParlorBoss}} identifies animals that are in the wrong \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} if an animal's \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} is different from other animals in the \{\{Partners.parlor}}. However, if a milking shift is transitioning from one \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} to the next, \{\{masterVAS-Products.ParlorBoss}} expects to have animals from both \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}} in the \{\{Partners.parlor}} at the same time. In this case, animals are not flagged as being in the wrong \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}}.
</hint>

<ExpandableHeading indent="2">
  ## Search and Filter the Wrong Pen Animals Report

  Options to search and filter the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompReportNames}}\{\{Controls.ControlTypes}} are available at the top of the \{\{General.UI-gui}}. Follow the guidelines below when \{\{Commands.UserActions}} the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompReportNames}}\{\{Controls.ControlTypes}}:

  - \{\{Commands.Click}}**Filters** at the top left of the page to limit the data that is displayed. The **Filters** side panel opens.
  - Use the **Date&#x20;****From** and **Date&#x20;****To** filters to only view data from that range.
  - \{\{Commands.UserActions}} a \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Upper}} to limit the \{\{Controls.ControlTypes}} to a specific \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}.
  - \{\{Commands.UserActions}} the **Clear Filter\*\*\*\*s** option to clear all of the filters currently applied.
</ExpandableHeading>

  ## View Details for a Specific Date

  \{\{Commands.Click}} on a \{\{Controls.ControlTypes}} in the table to open the \{\{General.UI-gui}} and view its details. Follow the guidelines below when \{\{Commands.UserActions}}\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}\{\{Controls.ControlTypes}}:

  - Date: The date of the \{\{Controls.ControlTypes}} is displayed at the top left of the \{\{General.UI-gui}}.
  - Summary: An overall summary is displayed in a **GRAY** bar at the top left of the \{\{General.UI-gui}}:\* Total Affected Shifts: The total number of \{\{Partners.parlor}} shifts that had animals in the wrong pen on that day.
    - \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompReportNames}}: The total number of \{\{Commands.UserActions}} on \{\{General.Grammar}} day.
  - Shift: The shift number.
  - Animal ID: The identifier of the animal that entered the \{\{Partners.parlor}}.
  - Actual \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.P-en-}}: The \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} number that the animal was in when scanned.
  - Correct \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.P-en-}}: The \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} number on the animal's record. See [MOVE Event](../Events/MoveEvent.htm) to learn more about correcting the animal's \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} if needed.
