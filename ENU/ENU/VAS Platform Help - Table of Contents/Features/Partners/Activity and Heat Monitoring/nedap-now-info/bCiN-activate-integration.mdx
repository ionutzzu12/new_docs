<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Required Permissions

  Only users who are assigned a Role with Integrations Management permissions can connect partner integrations. See [Edit Dairy User Permissions](../../Platform/Features/Config/UserMgmtRoles/EditUserPermissions.htm) and [Manage Dairy Roles](<../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Manage Dairy Users and Roles/manage-dairy-roles.mdx>) for more information.
</ExpandableHeading>

Follow these steps to integrate the partner system with \{\{General.CompanyName-short}} and allow health and reproduction data and alerts to display in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}:

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Step 1: Nedap Now Setup

  Prior to activating your integration, you must first fulfill the following requirements: 

  - Ensure your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} has a company in \{\{Partners.NedapNow-platform}}.
  - Ensure that your company is configured with farms and locations in \{\{Partners.NedapNow-platform}}.
  - Connect your \{\{Partners.Nedap-parent}} Velos device to \{\{Partners.NedapNow-platform}}.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Step 2: Configure Items in DairyComp 305 (DairyComp Unlimited Users Only)

  <hint type="warning">
    **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**&#x49;f you have \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}} version lower than 23.10.909, you must update \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}} to the latest release version before you can configure \{\{Partners.NedapNow-product}} items. See [Update DC305 to the Latest Release](https://dc-help.vas.com/FAQs/WebUpdater.htm) \{\{masterVAS-Phrases.RefsToOtherHelpProjects}} for update instructions.
  </hint>

  To receive animal alerts in \{\{DC-UI.DC-ProductName}} and \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, you must first create and configure several items:

  1. In \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-long}}, enter `PLATFORM` in the command line.
  2. Click the **Pulse Items** tab and expand **Nedap**.
  3. If necessary, map or add an activity measurement alert:\* **Map Existing Item:** To map an activity measurement alert to an existing \{\{DC-UI.DC-ProdNameShort}} item, double-click the alert. Select the existing item, then click **OK** to confirm your selection to map the alert to the item.
     - **Add New Item:** To create a new item for an activity measurement alert in \{\{DC-UI.DC-ProdNameShort}}, check the box for the alert. Click **Create (1) Items**, then confirm your selection to add the item to the \{\{DC-UI.DC-ProdNameShort}} database.

  ## Step 3: Configure Worklist Commands in DairyComp 305 (DairyComp Unlimited Users Only)

  To generate partner worklists using the new items and send them to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, you must configure the \{\{worklist}} commands in \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}}. In \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}}, access Command Abbreviations (\{\{ALTER}}\3) and create or modify the required lists. <ExpandableHeading>
  ## Step 4: Connect Integration in PULSE

  Follow the steps below to establish the connection to the partner system:

  1. In \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, navigate to **Partners&#x20;**>**&#xA0;Integrations Gallery&#x20;**>\{\{Partners.NedapNow-product}}.
  2. \{\{Commands.Click}}**See Details** or **See Requirements** on the \{\{Partners.NedapNow-product}}integration card.
  3. Follow the Connection Instructions on the upper right side of the page to establish the connection:1) \{\{Commands.Click}}**Add New Alias**, then complete the following fields:\* **Alias:** Enter your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}} Name. If you have multiple \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}}, we recommend you use different names for each alias.
     2\) \{\{Commands.Click}}**Select&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.P-ens-}} to choose \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}} that use \{\{Partners.NedapNow-product}}. Enter the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} numbers and select the correct \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}} from the results list, which lists the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}} in ascending order.
     3\) \{\{Commands.Click}}**Save**.
     4\) Click the **Connection Status** toggle and verify the status switches to **Active**.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/yVoLb7NAnbX1zs-j2jcYx_integration-connected.png" alt="Connection Status: Active" caption size="22"}
     5\) \{\{Commands.Click}}**Authorize integration**, then complete the necessary fields on the \{\{Partners.NedapNow-platform}} website that automatically opens.
     6\) If necessary, \{\{Commands.click}}**Edit Integration** to make any adjustments or edits and save your changes.

  <hint type="warning">
    **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**&#x49;f your integration does not automatically connect, contact your \{\{Partners.NedapNow-product}} representative to connect the integration from their system.
  </hint>
</ExpandableHeading>
