<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Required Setup

  For the \{\{Partners.PartnersUIoption}} tab to display locomotion and body condition score data, your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} must first activate the integration. See [Activate Nedap SmartSight Integration](activate-nedap-smartsight-integration.mdx) to learn more.
</ExpandableHeading>

\{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} displays locomotion and body condition score and alert data in the animal's \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}} on the \{\{Partners.PartnersUIoption}} tab. Scores are available in the \{\{Partners.Measurements}} widget for each visit to the milking \{\{Partners.parlor}} for up to 180 days.

Follow the guidelines below when viewing the \{\{Partners.PartnerName}}\{\{Partners.Measurements}} widget.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/6kE9v4ayofXbQX5U63BTo_measurements-widget.png" size="52" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Partners/cattle-eye/measurements-widget.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Partners/cattle-eye/measurements-widget.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

1. \{\{Partners.PartnersUIoption}}**&#x20;Tab:&#x20;**&#x54;o view the widget, select your measurement integration company from the \{\{Partners.PartnersUIoption}} tab on the animal's \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}}.
2. **View:&#x20;**&#x43;hoose to view widget data for the last 7, 30, or 180 days.
3. **Detail:&#x20;**&#x48;over over the graph data to view the measurement data at a specific date and time.
4. **Alerts**: Hover over the alert icon to view score details. Contact \{\{Partners.PartnerName}} for more details on how their system scores body condition or locomotion.
5. \{\{Partners.Measurements}}**:&#x20;**&#x54;he widget displays data from \{\{Partners.PartnerName}}. Check the boxes to select which data type displays.
