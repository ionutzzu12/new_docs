<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Required Permissions

  Only users who are assigned a Role with Integrations Management permissions can connect partner integrations. See [Edit Dairy User Permissions](../../Platform/Features/Config/UserMgmtRoles/EditUserPermissions.htm) and [Manage Dairy Roles](<../../../Manage Dairy Users and Roles/manage-dairy-roles.mdx>) for more information.
</ExpandableHeading>

Follow these steps to integrate the partner system with \{\{General.CompanyName-short}} and allow health and reproduction data and alerts to display in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}:

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Step 1: Nedap-Bi Setup

  Prior to activating your integration, you must first establish a sync with your on-premise equipment and create a Nedap-Bi account. Contact your partner system representative and request the following: 

  - A scheduled sync with Nedap-Bi. This will allow \{\{General.CompanyName-short}} to pull refreshed data into \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.
  - Assistance with creating a Nedap-Bi account. Be sure to note your credentials, as you will need them when connecting the integration in :Link[Step 4]{label="Step 4" overridedLabel="Step 4" spaceId docId="WGTKjCeVTG2lx_IcSFZOM" version="v2" docAnchorId="#step4" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="activate-integration.mdx"}.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Step 2: Configure Items in DairyComp 305 (DairyComp Unlimited Users Only)

  To receive animal alerts in \{\{DC-UI.DC-ProductName}} and \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, you must first create and configure several items as follows:

  1. In \{\{DC-UI.DC-ProdNameShort}}, access Item Definitions (\{\{ALTER}}\2) and create or modify the required items to store alerts. Expand :Link[Required Items]{label="Required Items" overridedLabel="Required Items" spaceId docId="WGTKjCeVTG2lx_IcSFZOM" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Items" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="activate-integration.mdx"} below to learn more about each item.
  2. In \{\{DC-UI.DC-ProductName}}, enter `PLATFORM` in the command line.
  3. Click the **Pulse Items** tab and expand \{\{Partners.Nedap-parent}}.
  4. If necessary, map or add an item. Expand :Link[Required Items]{label="Required Items" overridedLabel="Required Items" spaceId docId="WGTKjCeVTG2lx_IcSFZOM" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Items" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="activate-integration.mdx"} below to learn more about each item:1) **Map Existing Item:** To map an item to an existing \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}} item, double-click the item. Select the existing \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}} item, then click **OK** to confirm your selection.
     2\) **Add New Item:** To create a new item, check the box for that item. Click **Create (1) Items**, then click **Yes** to confirm your selection.
  5. \{\{Commands.Click}}**OK** to save your changes.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Step 3: Configure Worklist Commands in DairyComp 305 (DairyComp Unlimited Users Only)

  Follow these steps to generate partner worklists using the new items and send them to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.

  1. In \{\{DC-UI.DC-ProdNameShort}}, access Command Abbreviations (ALTER\3) and create or modify the required \{\{worklist}} commands. Expand :Link[Worklist Commands]{label="Worklist Commands" overridedLabel="Worklist Commands" spaceId docId="WGTKjCeVTG2lx_IcSFZOM" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Commands" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="activate-integration.mdx"} below to learn more about each required worklist.
  2. Alert items automatically push to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}. No additional commands are needed to send the alerts or lists to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.
     If present, delete the MILK\VHU1 command from Command Abbreviations (ALTER\3). This command was used to import alert items to the on-premise integration and is no longer needed.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Step 4: Connect Integration in PULSE

  Before you can activate your integration, you must first have the Nedap-Bi credentials you established as part of :Link[Step 1]{label="Step 1" overridedLabel="Step 1" spaceId docId="WGTKjCeVTG2lx_IcSFZOM" version="v2" docAnchorId="#step1" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="activate-integration.mdx"}, as well as all required equipment installed on-site. Follow these steps to establish the connection to the partner system:

  1. In \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, navigate to **Partners&#x20;**>**&#xA0;Integrations Gallery&#x20;**>**&#xA0;**\{\{Partners.PartnerName}}.
  2. \{\{Commands.Click}}**See Details** or **See Requirements** on the \{\{Partners.PartnerName}}integration card.
  3. Follow the Connection Instructions on the right side of the page to establish the connection:1) \{\{Commands.Click}}**Add New Alias**, then complete the following fields:\* **Alias:** Enter your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}} Name. If you have multiple \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}}, we recommend you use different names for each alias.
     2\) \{\{Commands.Click}}**Authorize integration**, then complete the following steps on the \{\{Partners.PartnerName}} website that automatically opens.
     3\) \{\{Commands.Click}}**Select&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.P-ens-}} to choose \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}} that use this product. Enter the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-en}} numbers and select the correct \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}} from the results list, which lists the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}} in ascending order.
     4\) Click the **Connection Status** toggle and verify the status switches to **Active**.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/yVoLb7NAnbX1zs-j2jcYx_integration-connected.png" alt="Connection Status: Active" caption size="22"}
     5\) \{\{Commands.Click}}**Save**.
     6\) If necessary, \{\{Commands.click}}**Edit Integration** to make any adjustments or edits and save your changes.

  <hint type="warning">
    **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**&#x49;f your integration does not automatically connect, contact your \{\{Partners.PartnerName}} representative to connect the integration from their system.
  </hint>
