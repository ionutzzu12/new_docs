  ## Save a Report/Worklist

  Save reports and \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}s that you run frequently to your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}} \{\{Buttons-UI.DC-Reports}} page for easy reuse.You can save reports to your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}} \{\{Buttons-UI.DC-Reports}} page in two different ways:

  - From the [Template Gallery](DC-ReportsGallery.htm)\{\{Commands.Click}} the **Customize** button to open the report in [Report/Worklist Builder](<../../../../../ENU/VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/DairyComp/MyDC/reportsworklists/create-new-report-or-worklist/build-custom-report-or-worklist.mdx>)..
  - After running a report from the [Command Line](../../../../features/command-line.mdx) (\{\{masterVAS-Products.DCU-users-short}} only):1) \{\{Commands.Click}} the **Edit** button to open the [Report/Worklist Builder](<../../../../../ENU/VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/DairyComp/MyDC/reportsworklists/create-new-report-or-worklist/build-custom-report-or-worklist.mdx>)\{\{Buttons-UI.FeatureType}}. Enter the report/\{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}} details and the **Next** button to open the **Contents** screen on the [Report/Worklist Builder](<../../../../../ENU/VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/DairyComp/MyDC/reportsworklists/create-new-report-or-worklist/build-custom-report-or-worklist.mdx>) page.
    2\) \{\{Commands.Click}} the **Save and Execute** button to save the report/\{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Schedule a Report/Worklist

  After a report or \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}} is saved to the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}} \{\{Buttons-UI.DC-Reports}} page, \{\{masterVAS-Products.DCP-users-short}} can automate the generation of a report or \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}} and add it to the [Process Scheduler](<../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Dairy/process-scheduler.mdx>) calendar. Follow these steps on the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}}\{\{Buttons-UI.DC-Reports}} page to schedule a report or \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}} to run automatically:

  1. Click the **Schedule** button to open a side panel where you can create an automated schedule for the report or \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/yvARXVR2Stbbp2Qh1gip7_schedule-report-worklist.png" size="66" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/process-scheduler/schedule-report-worklist.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/process-scheduler/schedule-report-worklist.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false" indent="2"></Image>

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    Use the following guidelines to schedule your report or \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}:\* Select the date and time to start the process.:::hint\{type="warning"}
    **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**&#x53;et the **Day to Start** to a future time, either later \{\{today}} or a future date, as scheduling a process at a time that has passed will not run until the next time the scheduled interval is reached. For example, if you schedule a process to start on Mondays at 5 am, but today is a Monday at 9 am, the enrollment will start next week at 5 am.
    \:::- Set the schedule for the process:1) Set the **Repetition** to "Repeats".
    2\) Set the frequency and interval for the process. For instance, to run a process weekly, set **Every** to "1" and **Intervals** to "Weeks".
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
    Click **Schedule**.After you schedule your report or \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}, \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} shows the **Scheduled** button and \{\{Protocols.ProcessScheduler}} includes the report or \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}. To view the generated report or \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}, see [Report History](view-report-history.mdx) or [Worklist History](<../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/DairyComp/MyDC/Reports/Worklists/view-worklists-history.mdx>).
  </Paragraph>
</ExpandableHeading>

  You can run reports or \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}s for today or up to 30 days in the future. This option allows you to collect additional data and gives you even more choices when planning tasks and managing your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}.

  1. \{\{Commands.Click}} the **See Results** button to open the options window.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/2oQUpgGaNa9JAYOIBXlIs_dairy-reports-report.png" alt caption size="29"}:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/MvbaQ-qG59YkcYljOseAK_report-results-options.png" alt caption size="31"}
  2. The date field defaults to today's date. \{\{Commands.Click}} on the date field to change it.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/tRfIljpjXlLhVcubJZhna_date-picker.png" alt caption size="38"}:::hint\{type="info"}
     **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x59;ou cannot select a date in the past, but you can select a date up to 30 days in the future.
