<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Required Setup

  - This feature is only available when you’re using this device for feeding. If you change your mind about using a device for feeding, :Link[log out of the app]{label="log out of the app" overridedLabel="log out of the app" spaceId docId="sK8Esbx0pVQzqZ5wDAiMP" version="v2" docAnchorId="#logout" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="FCM-UserAccount.htm#logout" href="../account-settings.mdx"} and select **Yes** or **No** when asked if the device will be used for feeding.
  - Before you can begin using \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} to feed at your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}, you must complete the required setup. See [Before You Begin: Required Setup](../before-you-begin-required-setup.mdx) to learn more about the required setup order.
  - Devices used for feeding must be connected to a scale. See [Scales](FCM-ScaleConnections.htm) to learn more about connecting to your scale.
</ExpandableHeading>

Tap \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeed}} at the bottom of the screen, and then tap \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeedingSchedule}}to follow your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}'s current feeding schedule and feed your animals. To create, adjust, and maintain the feedings included in your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}’s feeding schedule, see [Adjust Feedings](FCM-FeedingSchedule.htm).

All of your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}’s current feedings are listed on the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Upper}}\{\{General.UI-gui}}. The feeder will generally follow the order displayed, moving left to right and top to bottom. See the topics below for help with feeding using the feeding schedule.

<hint type="warning">
  **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeedings}} that exceed the capacity of the \{\{mixer}} are identified with an overcapacity icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/8SaUIB8YwsB_ACr3yfcqe_overcapacity26x25.png" alt caption size="10"}. </hint>

  ## Premix Scheduled Feedings

  A premix is the same as a scheduled feeding, except you'll select a load size instead of selecting \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}}. A premix isn't like a normal recipe mix that is dropped to a sequence of \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}}; instead, a premix is batched for use in other recipes. See :Link[Create New Recipe]{label="Create New Recipe" overridedLabel="Create New Recipe" spaceId docId="BEf4613IZizflWCA9t_BN" version="v2" docAnchorId="#add" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="FCM-Recipes.htm#add" href="FCM-Recipes.htm#add"} and :Link[Batch Feedings]{label="Batch Feedings" overridedLabel="Batch Feedings" spaceId docId="M-EzYH8LWpkOvEuNlA5bI" version="v2" docAnchorId="#batchfeedings" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="FCM-BatchFeeding.htm#batchfeedings" href="FCM-BatchFeeding.htm#batchfeedings"} for more information.

  ## Mix Overnight Loads

  Overnight loads optimize morning feeding routines by allowing feeding technicians to mix a feed recipe the night before, pause the feeding and store the mix in the equipment overnight, and resume the feeding the next morning. This feature also allows feeding technicians to pause the loading sequence at any time while mixing a recipe and add the remaining ingredients that complete the recipe the next morning.

  <hint type="info">
    **TIP:&#x20;**&#x4F;vernight loads are identified with an overnight load icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/pzwR08lR-vu687sF_kna7_overnightload22x25.png" alt caption size="10"} and enabled for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} in \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompSettings}}. See :Link[Enable Overnight Loads]{label="Enable Overnight Loads" overridedLabel="Enable Overnight Loads" spaceId docId="sK8Esbx0pVQzqZ5wDAiMP" version="v2" docAnchorId="#overnightload" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="FCM-UserAccount.htm#overnightload" href="../account-settings.mdx"} to learn more.
  </hint>

  Follow these steps to work with overnight loads:

  1. \{\{Commands.UserActions}}\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Upper}} at the bottom of the screen.
  2. \{\{Commands.Click}} the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} to \{\{Commands.UserActions}}.
  3. If the mix for this feeding was started yesterday and paused before all ingredients were added, \{\{Commands.UserActions}}\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Upper}} at the bottom of the screen.
  4. Start (or continue) loading the ingredients.
  5. \{\{Commands.UserActions}}`Resume Tomorrow` at the bottom of the screen to pause until tomorrow.
  6. To resume the load, open the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeedingSchedule}}, select the overnight load that was paused, and resume.

  <hint type="warning">
    **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**&#x4F;nce an overnight load is started in :Link[equipment connected to a scale]{label="equipment connected to a scale" overridedLabel="equipment connected to a scale" spaceId docId="sK8Esbx0pVQzqZ5wDAiMP" version="v2" docAnchorId="#equipment" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="FCM-UserAccount.htm#equipment" href="../account-settings.mdx"}, all feedings (except for the overnight load) are disabled for that equipment until the overnight load is completed. If you connect to and attempt to feed using equipment containing an overnight load, an overnight load notification with a red alert icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/zFuxFy12vBPd_L3Mh2reG_alert-triangle-red.png" alt caption size="10"} is displayed.
  </hint>
