<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Required Permissions

  Only users who are assigned to a Role with \{\{RolesPermissions.PermissionNames}} permissions can use this feature. See [Edit Dairy User Permissions](../Config/UserMgmtRoles/EditUserPermissions.htm) and [Manage Dairy Roles](<../../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Manage Dairy Users and Roles/manage-dairy-roles.mdx>) for more information.
</ExpandableHeading>

The \{\{Buttons-UI.AlertGroups}} page allows you to select the events you want \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} to track and the team members you want to notify when your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} exceeds the alert thresholds.

<hint type="info">
  **NOTE:&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} does not currently support alerts and notifications for all available indicator types.
</hint>

You cannot add or edit alert groups until \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-AlertsAndThresholds}} are enabled in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} and at least one \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-AlertThresholdValue}} is entered on the [Indicators](<../../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Dairy/Settings (Dairy)/Performance Indicators/working-with-indicators.mdx>) page for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/KUNJ_YHM0EwMTrlJ5v2wx_indicators-tab-alerts-subtab-disabled.png" size="58" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/getting-started/indicators-tab-alerts-subtab-disabled.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/getting-started/indicators-tab-alerts-subtab-disabled.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

See [Step 1: Enable & Set Up Health Indicators](Step1Indicators.htm) and [Step 2: Enter Alert Thresholds](<../../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Dairy/Settings (Dairy)/Performance Indicators/Set Up Health Indicators and Alerts/step-2-enter-alert-thresholds.mdx>) for instructions.

Once \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-AlertsAndThresholds}} are enabled for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} and at least one \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-AlertThresholdValue}} is entered on the [Indicators](<../../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Dairy/Settings (Dairy)/Performance Indicators/working-with-indicators.mdx>) page for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}, you can complete the following tasks on the \{\{Buttons-UI.AlertGroups}} page:

- Add a new alert group.
- Edit or delete an existing alert group.
- Include one or more \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-EventIndicator}}s to track across your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} in an alert group.
- Select one or more members of your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} team to notify when a tracked event occurs.
- Select the time of day when \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} examines \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-ThresholdDifferential}}s for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} and notifies your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} team.

<hint type="info">
  **NOTE:&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} sends notifications for alert groups only by text or email. Alert group notifications are not added to the [Message Center](<../../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/User Menu/message-center.mdx>) or to the [Notifications button](<../../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/FAQs/Using PULSE/how-do-notifications-work.mdx>) as a new activity badge.
</hint>
