# Manage Edge Devices

The *Manage&#x20;*&#x73;ection gives you the option to reboot and reset Litmus Edge.&#x20;

- **Reboot**: You can reboot the Litmus Edge device.&#x20;
- **Reset**: Revert the edge device to its factory settings. All edge device configurations are removed and Litmus Edge returns to its original state before any configurations were created.

<hint type="danger">
  **Important Factory Reset Behavior:&#x20;**&#x50;erforming a factory reset on a Litmus Edge device will revert it to its original state.&#x20;

  The following occurs during a factory reset:

  - All configurations, user settings, and stored data are permanently erased.
  - The device license remains intact and is not removed during the reset.
  - The device will require reconfiguration and reactivation after the reset, except for the retained license.
</hint>
