Not all concepts or features available in Workato is available in \{\{Primary.Product-Neurons-iPaaS}}. You will notice that some of the hyperlinks displayed in the iPaaS feature in ITSM Configuration console do not work which could be due to the following limitations:

- \{\{Primary.Company}}\{\{Primary.Product-Neurons-iPaaS}} does not include [Workato’s API platform.](https://docs.workato.com/api-management.html#api-platform)
- \{\{Primary.Company}}\{\{Primary.Product-Neurons-iPaaS}} does not support [Audit Log Streaming](https://docs.workato.com/features/activity-audit-log-streaming.html).
- \{\{Primary.Company}}\{\{Primary.Product-Neurons-iPaaS}} does not include Workato [FileStorage](https://docs.workato.com/features/workato-filestorage.html) or [SQL Transformation.](https://docs.workato.com/features/sql-transformations.html)
- \{\{Primary.Company}}\{\{Primary.Product-Neurons-iPaaS}} does not support recipe concurrency beyond 5.
- \{\{Primary.Company}}\{\{Primary.Product-Neurons-iPaaS}} does not use or support Workato’s [user and role management concept](https://docs.workato.com/user-accounts-and-teams/role-based-access/). Only ITSM Administrators can access the capability to create, manage and monitor integration flows (recipes), therefore, it is irrelevant to use an additional user management and role concept.
