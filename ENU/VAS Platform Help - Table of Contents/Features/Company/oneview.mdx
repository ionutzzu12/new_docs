\{\{Buttons-UI.OneView}} dashboards in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} allow you to view all your inventory data for all of your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}} in one convenient location. At a glimpse, view your inventory statistics — past, present, and future — across all \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}} to guide your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} management decisions. You can also monitor health trends and other changes in the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}. Use \{\{Buttons-UI.OneView}}'s easy-to-follow graphs and various view and filter options to quickly find inventory information.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/0dVOJ0nAKrMgI2XGd0kX9_oneview-intro.png" size="46" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/oneview/oneview-intro.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/oneview/oneview-intro.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

Navigate to your [Company View](../CompanyIntro.htm), then click the \{\{Buttons-UI.OneView}} tab, and select a view from the drop-down list to start viewing your company's inventory. Select [Inventory](Inventory.htm) to view your present inventory, select [Historic Trends](HistoricTrends.htm) to view your past inventory, or select [Projections](Projections.htm) to see future inventory predictions.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Where do I find my Company View?

  **Company features are only available when viewing a company in&#x20;**\{\{General.ProductName-short}}**, not when viewing a&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}**.** To access company features, a member of the company must invite you to connect with it. See [Invite a User to Your Dairy](<../Manage Dairy Users and Roles/invite-a-user-to-your-dairy.mdx>) for more information.
