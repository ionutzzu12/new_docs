[CattleEye](https://cattleeye.com/) turns your basic security camera into a powerful identification and monitoring tool that checks for lameness when your animals enter or leave the parlor. These measurements provide valuable insights into the health of each animal, and are shared on your animals' CowCards in the \{\{General.ProductName}} after each parlor visit. You can also use custom items to generate [CattleEye Reports/Worklists](<../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Partners/Health Monitoring/CattleEye/cattleeye-reportsworklists.mdx>) that allow you and your hoof trimmer to efficiently identify and treat \{\{lame}} animals.
