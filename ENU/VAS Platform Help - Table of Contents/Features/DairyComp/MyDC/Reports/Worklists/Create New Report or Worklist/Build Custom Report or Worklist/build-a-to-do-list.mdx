\{\{Worklists.ToDoUI-Web}} Lists include animals that may need to be checked, moved, or treated. After the required task is completed, the animal is marked as \{\{Worklists.DoneUI}}. Use these actionable lists to help manage your work on farm and keep track of completed activities electronically.

Begin by selecting **Build Report/**\{\{Worklist}} option from the \{\{Buttons-UI.FeaturesMain}} menu, clicking the button at the top of the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}}\{\{Buttons-UI.DC-Reports}} page, or clicking **Customize** from the Template \{\{Buttons-UI.GalleryTab}} page. Then use the two-step \{\{Buttons-UI.ReportListBuilder}} to create or edit your \{\{Worklists.ListName}}.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/Iiqieqm3mnUZUhUqZ5Ow9_builder-todo-example2.png" size="90" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/dairycomp/worklists/builder-todo-example2.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/dairycomp/worklists/builder-todo-example2.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

1. Enter a **Name** for your worklist.
2. Select \{\{Worklists.ListType}} for the report **Type**.
3. Enter a worklist **Description**.
4. Select **Tags** that apply to your list. Setting tags for your list will allow you to use filters to find your list on the [Dairy Reports/Worklists](ManageReportsWorklists.htm) page.
5. Click **Next**.
6. Use the search field at the top of the items list to find items by name or description. For example, searching for "\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.s-ire}}" finds all associated items in your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}: \{\{LSIR}}, MGSIR, \{\{PSIRC}}, \{\{SIR1}}, etc.:::ExpandableHeading\{customAnchorSlug="SM-1"}

8. Use the **Mobile List Output** field to preview how your \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}} will look in the mobile app.
9. To set filters, drag and drop items from the items list into the **Filters** area, and then choose an **Operator** from the drop-down and set a **Value** for the operator.:::ExpandableHeading\{customAnchorSlug="SM-3"}

10. To customize how items are sorted on your report, drag and drop items from the items list into the **Sorting** area, and then choose the sort order:\* A - Z: Ascending sort (low to high, earliest to latest)
    - Z - A: Descending sort (high to low, latest to earliest)
11. In the **Switches** area, select to include alive, dead, or all animals in your report.
12. Click **Save and Execute** to build your report and display the results of the report on the [ReportWorklistResults.htm](../see-reportsworklists-results.mdx)[Reports/Worklists Results](../see-reportsworklists-results.mdx) page.\{\{masterVAS-Products.DCU-users-short}} can also preview the command as the report is being built.
    View your report and click the **Create Open Worklist** button to generate the \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}. See [Create Worklist from Report](BuildWorklistReport.htm) to learn more about converting a report into a \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}.
