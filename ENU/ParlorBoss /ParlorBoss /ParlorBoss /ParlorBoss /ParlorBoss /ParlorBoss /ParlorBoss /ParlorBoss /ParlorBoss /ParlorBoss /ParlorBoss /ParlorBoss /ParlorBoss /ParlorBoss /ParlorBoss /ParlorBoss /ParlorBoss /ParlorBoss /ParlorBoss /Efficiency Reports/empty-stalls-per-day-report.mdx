  ## View Report Details

  - Shifts: The total number of animals that entered the \{\{Partners.parlor}} during \{\{General.Grammar}}specific \{\{Partners.parlor}} shift. <ExpandableHeading indent="2">
  ## View Details for a Specific Date

  \{\{Commands.Click}} on a \{\{Controls.ControlTypes}} in the table to open the \{\{General.UI-gui}} and view its details. Follow the guidelines below when \{\{Commands.UserActions}}\{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}}\{\{Controls.ControlTypes}}:

  - Date: The date of the \{\{Controls.ControlTypes}} is displayed at the top left of the \{\{General.UI-gui}}.
  - Summary: An overall summary is displayed in a **GRAY** bar at the top left of the \{\{General.UI-gui}}:\* Total Empty Stalls: The total number of empty stalls recorded for all \{\{Partners.parlor}} shifts that occurred on the day.
    - Avg. Empty Stalls per Shift: The average number of empty stalls recorded for all \{\{Partners.parlor}} shifts that occurred on the day.
  - Shift: The shift number.
  - Empty Stalls: The identifier of each \{\{Partners.parlor}} stall that was recorded empty during each shift on the day.
  - Shift Total: The total number of \{\{Partners.parlor}} stalls recorded empty for each specific shift on the day.
</ExpandableHeading>
