  ## KPI Descriptions

  ### Reproduction

  - **Cow Conception Rate:&#xA0;**% of positive pregnancies / total number of inseminations for cows (\{\{LACT}} > 1)
  - **Heifer Conception Rate:** % of positive pregnancies / total number of inseminations for heifers (LACT = 0)
  - **Open Animals:** Count of animals confirmed not pregnant
  - **Pregnant Animals:** Count of animals confirmed to be pregnant
  - **Dry Animals:** Count of animals in their dry phase

  ### Population

  - **Number of heifers:** Count of all heifers in the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}
  - **Number of breeding-age heifers:** Count of breeding-age heifers in the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}
  - **Number of milking cows:** Count of milking cows in the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}
  - \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.H-erd-}}**&#x20;population without bulls:** Count of all female animals in the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}
  - **Number of departing cows:** Count of all cows leaving the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} (sold or died)
  - **Number of departing heifers:** Count of all heifers leaving the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} (sold or died)
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Email Notifications

  You will receive **up to one Alert email per day** for each \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} with Reproduction and Population Alerts enabled in your Account Settings. \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} evaluates your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} data against your Reproduction and Population \{\{UDS-general.KPI-short}}s after midnight on a daily basis. If any \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} attributes fall below a lower limit or exceed an upper limit, an Alert email is sent in the early morning hours to the email address you use to log in to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.
