The \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}\{\{Buttons-UI.FeaturesSecondary}} tool allows you to view your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} data in reports, as well as create \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}s to manage daily activities at your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}. You can create your own custom reports and \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}s or create them from the many templates provided in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.

<hint type="info">
  **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x49;n addition to generating reports and \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}s in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, \{\{masterVAS-Products.DCUnlimited}} \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}} can also generate reports and \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}s in on-premise \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}} and send them to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}. See [Send Worklists from DC305 to PULSE (DC Unlimited only)](SendListsToPlatform.htm) to learn more about \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}} \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}s.
</hint>

Easily access and monitor your \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}s in the \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} web interface, or process them out on the farm by using the mobile app, which allows pairing with \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.radiofrequencyID-short}} scanners to make the work go even faster. All \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}} updates are automatically synchronized between \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} and the mobile app!
