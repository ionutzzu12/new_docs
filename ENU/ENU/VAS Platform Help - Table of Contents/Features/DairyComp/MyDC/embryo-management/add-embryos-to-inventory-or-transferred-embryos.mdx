\{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} allows you to add \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.f-resh}} or frozen embryos to inventory to streamline embryo transfer tasks while out on the farm and using the mobile app. Use the batch import feature to quickly add multiple embryos, such as those harvested from an \{\{embryo flush}}, or add individual embryos or multiple identical embryos as needed. You can also add transferred embryos directly to the [Transferred Embryos](EmbryoTransferred.htm) page using the same batch import process and file.

You can find this feature under the :Link[DairyComp/MyDC]{label="DairyComp/MyDC" overridedLabel="DairyComp/MyDC" spaceId docId="WFLX7SPMpZfy7zoMyrvRh" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Menu-DairyComp" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../../../NavPlatform/Menu.htm#Menu-DairyComp" href="../../../../navigate-pulse/menu-options.mdx"} > :Link[Embryos]{label="Embryos" overridedLabel="Embryos" spaceId docId="WFLX7SPMpZfy7zoMyrvRh" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Menu-DairyComp-Embryos" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../../../NavPlatform/Menu.htm#Menu-DairyComp-Embryos" href="../../../../navigate-pulse/menu-options.mdx"} menu. The options to add embryos are available from the menu or the Embryo Management tabs.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Before You Add Embryos to Inventory

  Prior to entering your embryos to inventory, be sure to have all required details. Verify that the lab report or other listing that accompanied the delivery of your embryos is handy and available for reference. Note that the following details are required to add embryos to inventory:

  - \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.S-ire-}}\{\{NAAB}} code or \{\{ISO}}\{\{ID}}
  - Donor ID, Name, or ISO ID
  - Fertilization Date
  - Frozen Date (for frozen embryos)

  In addition to the required items, other information that is useful when preparing to add embryos includes: 

  - Embryo breed
  - Straw or Mating details
  - Embryo counts
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Embryo Requirements for Transfer

  \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} limits the transfer of embryos to those with the highest potential for success. Embryos must meet the following requirements to qualify for transfer: 

  - Embryo transfers are typically done with 4 to 8-day-old embryos. In \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, you can only enter data for embryos that are less than 12 days old (\{\{EAGE}} \< 12). As noted in :Link[Enter Completed Transfers as Soon as Possible!]{label="Enter Completed Transfers as Soon as Possible!" overridedLabel="Enter Completed Transfers as Soon as Possible!" spaceId docId="2xvgnC4YJ3R3jlH2DvE81" version="v2" docAnchorId="#dataentry" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="add-embryos-to-inventory-or-transferred-embryos.mdx"}, you must be prompt with your data entry to capture embryo transfers in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.
  - Must be Grade 1 - 3: \* **1** Good
    - **2** Fair
    - **3** Poor
  - Must be Stage 4 -9: \* **4** Morula
    - **5** Early Blastocyst
    - **6** Blastocyst
    - **7** Expanded Blastocyst
    - **8** Hatched Blastocyst
    - **9** Expanded and Hatched Blastocyst
</ExpandableHeading>

       - Donor
       - Embryo Breed
       - Fertilization Date

If you change the extension, the file upload will fail, resulting in an "Invalid File Type" error: :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/eltvWmUQDd3N1FcNkorkq_add-animals-batch-file-error116x30.png" alt caption size="10"}.

    <ExpandableHeading indent="2">
      ### Embryo Details

      - \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.S-ire-}}: The bull whose semen was used to fertilize the donor's egg to produce the embryo. If this column shows **Pool**, it means that an \{\{oocyte}} was fertilized by \{\{IVF}} from a pool containing the semen of multiple sires.
      - Donor: TThe female who provided the embryo or oocyte (sometimes referred to as the donor dam).
      - Embryo Breed: The embryo breed. Select from the standard list of NAAB breeds.
      - Fertilization Date: Date the donor egg was fertilized using the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.s-ire}} semen.
      - Recovery Type: The recovery type used:\* **Flush** is used for embryos
        - **IVF** for in vitro fertilization (\{\{oocytes}})
        - **Other** for neither of the above
      - Tank: The liquid nitrogen storage tank containing the cane with the embryo's straw.
      - Incubator: The warming unit containing the embryo's straw or mating.
      - Cane: The container holding the embryo's straw.
      - Mating: A description of the embryo mating details, such as \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.s-ire}}, donor, and fertilization date. May contain multiple embryos.
      - Straw number: Number of the straw holding the embryo. Canes contain multiple straws and each straw holds a single embryo.
      - Stage: The stage at which the embryo was harvested:\* **1** Unfertilized
        - **2** Simple Celled
        - **3** Early Morula
        - **4** Morula
        - **5** Early Blastocyst
        - **6** Blastocyst
        - **7** Expanded Blastocyst
        - **8** Hatched Blastocyst
        - **9** Expanded and Hatched Blastocyst
      - Grade: The quality of the embryo:\* **1** Good
        - **2** Fair
        - **3** Poor
        - **4** Dead (or Degenerating)
      - Lab ID: Identifier for the lab from which the embryo was purchased.
      - Lab Embryo ID: Identifier for the embryo from the lab. This value differs from the \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} Embryo ID.
      - Embryo Owner: Name or company that owns the embryo.
      - Frozen Date: Date the embryo was frozen.
      - Freeze Method: The method used to freeze the embryo:  \* **Direct Transfer (DT)** is the process of using high levels of cryoprotectant to quickly cool an embryo to freezing. A common cryoprotectant used for this process is ethylene glycol.
        - **Slow** is the process of using low levels of cryoprotectant and a programmable freezing machine to slowly lower the temperature of the embryo to freezing.
      - Recipient: The animal that received the embryo transfer. Including the Recipient and Transfer details automatically adds a \{\{Events.3000TRANSFER}} event to the recipient animal. See :Link[Import Transferred Embryos]{label="Import Transferred Embryos" overridedLabel="Import Transferred Embryos" spaceId docId="2xvgnC4YJ3R3jlH2DvE81" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Transferred" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="add-embryos-to-inventory-or-transferred-embryos.mdx"} for more information.
      - Transferred \{\{Tech}}: The embryo technician who completed the transfer.
      - Transferred Date: Date the embryo was transferred to the recipient.
      - Breeding Code: The code associated with the breeding activity. Check your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}'s [Breeding Codes](../Settings/DC-BreedCodes.htm) for more details on each code.
        \:::
    </ExpandableHeading>

    4. Drag and drop the completed template file into the upload box, or click **browse** to navigate to your file.
    5. Select the **Date Format**.
    6. \{\{Commands.Click}}**Continue** to preview and confirm the data.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Import Transferred Embryos

  In addition to importing embryos to inventory, you can use the same template file to import completed transfers. Follow the :Link[Import Embryos in Batch]{label="Import Embryos in Batch" overridedLabel="Import Embryos in Batch" spaceId docId="2xvgnC4YJ3R3jlH2DvE81" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Batch" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="add-embryos-to-inventory-or-transferred-embryos.mdx"} steps and be sure to include the required details in your template file: 

  - Recipient: The animal that received the embryo transfer.
  - Transferred Date: Date the embryo was transferred to the recipient.

  You can also include optional details if your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} tracks them: 

  - Transferred Tech: The embryo technician who completed the transfer.
  - Breeding Code: The code associated with the breeding activity. Check your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}'s [Breeding Codes](../Settings/DC-BreedCodes.htm) for more details on each code.

  After the import, the embryo is added directly to the [Transferred Embryos](EmbryoTransferred.htm) tab. \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} also automatically adds a TRANSFER event to the recipient's \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}} for the Transferred Date you specified.
</ExpandableHeading>
