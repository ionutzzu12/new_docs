<hint type="info">
  The **Alerts** module is not available OOTB. This workspace appears only if your administrator has configured it for your role.
</hint>

The **Alerts** workspace allows you to view important notices, including:

- System or application errors (such as performance issues or application shutdowns)
- Warnings
- Informational messages.

Alerts help you proactively identify and address issues before they affect users.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Accessing Alert workspace

  1. Log in to \{\{Primary.Product}}.
  2. From the left menu, select **Alerts**.

  The list of alerts display showing key details such as the category, status, description.

  3. Click a number below to display the corresponding set of alerts on the page.
</ExpandableHeading>

  **Sort Alerts**

  1. Click **Filter & Sort** dropdown list.
  2. Choose one of the following sort orders:
     - **Ascending**
     - **Descending**
  3. Choose a field to sort by:
     - **Category**
     - **Status**
     - **Description**
  4. Click **Apply**, to sort the list.
  5. To remove sorting, click **Reset All**.

  **Search Alerts**

  1. In the **Search** box, enter a keyword.
  2. Click **Enter**.

  The list updates and matching alerts are displayed.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>

  1. In the **Alerts** workspaces, click **All** from dropdown list.
  2. Click the **Tile View** :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/csed4og1EtUkkNRzw9fke_tile-view-ssm-3022x19.png" alt caption} icon to display alerts as tiles (or bricks).

  3. Click the **List View** :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/mdEwqk1zlmXNy7KFNm01s_list-record-view-ssm-3018x18.png" alt caption} to view the records in list records view.
