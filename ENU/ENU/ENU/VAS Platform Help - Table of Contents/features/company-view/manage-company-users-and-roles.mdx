<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Required Permissions

  Only users who are assigned the \{\{RolesPermissions.RoleNames}} Role in the company can use this feature. See :Link[Edit Company User Permissions]{label="Edit Company User Permissions" overridedLabel="Edit Company User Permissions" spaceId docId="ZBxNM9nH4pjlw1ThsUy51" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Edit_Company_User_Permissions" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="manage-company-users-and-roles.mdx"} for more information.
</ExpandableHeading>

\{\{MorphHelp.User\_Management\_UI\_item}} features in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} allow you to manage user accounts and roles by company and \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}. Company**User** permissions are controlled entirely through **Role** assignments.

:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/QVzUDnzSBVs_JQnQ_V9gE_user-view-all.png" alt caption size="71"}:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/wZZgBV7__BqaiPxN3x2cH_roles-company.png" alt caption size="69"}

User connections at the company level differ from \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}-level connections. This section includes instructions for managing company user permissions and roles. For instructions on how to edit your personal user account, see [Edit Your User Account](<../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/User Menu/edit-your-user-account.mdx>). Or, see [Manage Dairy Users and Roles](<../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/features/manage-dairy-users-and-roles.mdx>) to learn more about \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} user accounts and roles.

**Users cannot request access to a company like they can for a&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}**.** Instead, a user who is assigned the \{\{RolesPermissions.RoleNames}} Role in the company must invite other users to connect with the company. To request company Owner access, please [Contact Us](<../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Get Started/contact-us.mdx>).

Expand the sections below to learn more:

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Add and Invite a New User to Your Company

  1. Navigate to [Company View](<../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/features/company-view.mdx>) and select \{\{Buttons-UI.UserMgmtMenu}} from the **Configure Company** menu at the top right of the page.
  2. \{\{Commands.Click}} the **Add New User** button at the top right side of the **Users** tab.
  3. Enter the user's **Email** address.If a \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} account already exists for the email address entered, a message is displayed. Click the link in the message to preview the user account for the email address entered. Click the **Confirm and Assign Roles** button to confirm the email address entered and continue to assign roles for the new user account.
  4. In the **Roles** list, check the boxes to select the roles to assign to the user's account.The roles selected control how much or how little a user can do with the data in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}. You can edit role assignments at any time; see :Link[Edit Company User Permissions]{label="Edit Company User Permissions" overridedLabel="Edit Company User Permissions" spaceId docId="ZBxNM9nH4pjlw1ThsUy51" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Edit_Company_User_Permissions" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="manage-company-users-and-roles.mdx"} for more information. Each role checked is displayed in the **Roles Assigned** list. You can click the **X** on the right side of a role to unassign it.
  5. Click the **Invite User** button at the bottom right side of the page.

  \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} sends the user an invitation email, which they can then use to create a \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} account (if they don't already have one). If they already have a \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} account, they will be able to access your :Link[company‑level features]{label="company‑level features" overridedLabel="company‑level features" spaceId docId="gaDbO-G_WjIdAOfSxm4IH" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Company" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../Dashboards/DashboardsOverview.htm#Company" href="../Dashboards/DashboardsOverview.htm#Company"} once they accept your invitation.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Cancel or Resend an Invite to a Company User

  1. Navigate to [Company View](<../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/features/company-view.mdx>) and select \{\{Buttons-UI.UserMgmtMenu}} from the **Configure Company** menu at the top right of the page.
  2. In the **Access Pending** list on the **Users** tab, expand the details of the company user whose invite you want to cancel or resend.
  3. Click the **Cancel Invite** button at the bottom left side of the page to cancel the invite to the user.ORClick the **Resend Invite** button at the bottom left side of the page to resend the invite to the user.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Remove a Company User

  1. Navigate to [Company View](<../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/features/company-view.mdx>) and select \{\{Buttons-UI.UserMgmtMenu}} from the **Configure Company** menu at the top right of the page.
  2. In the **Users** list on the **Users** tab, expand the details of the company user that you want to remove.
  3. Click the **Remove User** button at the bottom left side of the page.

  Once these steps are complete, the user's \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} account will still be active, but the user will no longer be able to view details for the company.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Edit Company User Permissions

  1. Navigate to [Company View](<../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/features/company-view.mdx>) and select \{\{Buttons-UI.UserMgmtMenu}} from the **Configure Company** menu at the top right of the page.
  2. In the **Users** list on the **Users** tab, expand the details of the company user that you want to update.
  3. Click the **Edit Roles** button at the bottom right side of the page.
  4. In the **Available Roles** list, check the boxes to select additional **Roles** to assign to the user's account.The roles selected control how much or how little a user can do with the data in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}. Each role checked is displayed in the **Roles Assigned** list. You can click the **X** on the right side of a role to unassign it.
  5. \{\{Commands.Click}} the **Save** button at the bottom right side of the page.

  See :Link[View Company User Roles]{label="View Company User Roles" overridedLabel="View Company User Roles" spaceId docId="ZBxNM9nH4pjlw1ThsUy51" version="v2" docAnchorId="#View_Company_Roles" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="manage-company-users-and-roles.mdx"} to review the permissions assigned to each company role.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## View Company User Roles

  The **Roles** tab contains the following information:

  - All roles that are currently available for this company.
  - The number of permissions that are currently assigned to each role.
  - The status of each role (active/inactive).

  Follow the steps below to continue:

  1. Navigate to [Company View](<../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/features/company-view.mdx>) and select \{\{Buttons-UI.UserMgmtMenu}} from the **Configure Company** menu at the top right of the page.
  2. On the **Roles** tab, expand the details of a role to view the specific permissions that are assigned to it.:::ExpandableHeading
