As your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} completes \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.ovsynch}} tasks, you'll want to track progress and verify compliance to your active protocols. You also have the option of setting daily alerts on your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.protocolTasks}}. Follow the steps below to monitor the progress of \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.ovsynch}} tasks or use alerts to help you stay on track:

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Monitor Tasks from Web

  Select \{\{Buttons-UI.FeaturesMain}}**&#x20;>&#xA0;**\{\{Buttons-UI.DC-Reports}}**&#x20;> Open Worklists** and click the \{\{Protocols.OpenTasks}} list to open it and check progress.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/b2zNRaOsHvH9dO10auaN0_web-monitor-progress.png" size="43" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/tasks/web-monitor-progress.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/tasks/web-monitor-progress.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Monitor Tasks from Mobile

  Tap **Lists** and then the **Refresh Lists** button to make sure you have the latest \{\{Worklists.worklistCommonUse}}s. View your \{\{Protocols.OpenTasks}} list to check progress.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/wz5Yd2oVox1i9O-EewTXg_mobile-monitor-progress.png" size="39" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/dairycomp/protocols/mobile-monitor-progress.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/dairycomp/protocols/mobile-monitor-progress.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Set and Manage Task Alerts

  To help you and your team stay on top of \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.ovsynch}} tasks that need to be completed each day, you can set email or text alerts to notify you daily or throughout the day. See [Add and Manage Task Status Alerts](../../Dairy/ProcessScheduler/AddManageAlerts.htm) to learn more about managing alerts for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.ovsynch}} tasks.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Deactivate/Reactivate an OvSynch Protocol

  You can deactivate or reactivate an \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.ovsynch}}\{\{Protocols.Protocol-common}} for an animal as necessary. Expand the sections below to learn more:

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Manually deactivate (or reactivate) a protocol

    If you determine an animal needs to be removed from the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.ovsynch}} protocol, click the **Active&#x20;**\{\{Protocols.Protocol-singular}}**&#x20;for this animal** toggle to cancel active \{\{Protocols.Protocol-common}} tasks and then click **Deactivate Tasks** to confirm.

    <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/gTP3OX6zAbObQkNRLzEMM_protocol-side-panel-deactivate-all-open-tasks.png" size="72" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/dairycomp/protocols/protocol-side-panel-deactivate-all-open-tasks.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/dairycomp/protocols/protocol-side-panel-deactivate-all-open-tasks.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

    If necessary, click the toggle again to reactivate the \{\{Protocols.Protocol-common}}.

    <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/bw8eQQFGfh8FDjlOsB7T-_protocol-side-panel-reactivate.png" size="35" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/dairycomp/protocols/protocol-side-panel-reactivate.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/dairycomp/protocols/protocol-side-panel-reactivate.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>
  </ExpandableHeading>

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Add a disenrolling event

    If you add a disenrolling event for the \{\{Protocols.Protocol-common}}, the animal’s \{\{Protocols.Protocol-common}} tasks are automatically deactivated. If you later delete the disenrolling event, you have the option to reactivate the \{\{Protocols.Protocol-common}}.

    <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/xxSNl-hOw5dLNi9liflQ8_delete-event-reactivate-protocol.png" size="60" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/cowcards/delete-event-reactivate-protocol.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/cowcards/delete-event-reactivate-protocol.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>
  </ExpandableHeading>

  <hint type="warning">
    **IMPORTANT:&#x20;**&#x49;f the animal has missed required \{\{Protocols.Protocol-common}} tasks or some tasks were already completed, you will not be able to reactivate the \{\{Protocols.Protocol-common}}. The animal can be added at the next scheduled enrollment.
