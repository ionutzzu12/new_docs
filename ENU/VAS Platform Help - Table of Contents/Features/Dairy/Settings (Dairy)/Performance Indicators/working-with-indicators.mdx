The \{\{Buttons-UI.Indicators}} page allows you to view or adjust health and reproduction indicators for the animals in your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}.

  ## Health Indicators

  Select the \{\{Buttons-UI.HealthIndicators}} option to view your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}'s alert threshold settings for health-related events. You can also complete the following tasks:

  - Enable \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-AlertsAndThresholds}} for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}.
  - Enter the \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-AlertThresholdValue}}s that trigger \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} to notify your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} team when common events occur across your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}.
  - Select how often you want \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} to examine \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-ThresholdDifferential}}s for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} and send notifications to your team.

  When the page is displayed, \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} examines your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} and updates the \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-Occurrence}} fields for each \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-OccurrenceType}} found across your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/f1oeu2bhLbixrboxAK1zL_indicators-tab-initial-setup.png" size="88" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/getting-started/indicators-tab-initial-setup.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/getting-started/indicators-tab-initial-setup.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  In the example above, \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} found one **Overall&#x20;**\{\{Mastitis}} event and one **Cow Mastitis** event while examining the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}. You can track these events by setting an \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-AlertThresholdValue}} for each event.

  <hint type="info">
    **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x59;ou cannot enter \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-AlertThresholdValue}}s until \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-AlertsAndThresholds}} are enabled in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/P7lOyqqkMxkHj-wsbyMOK_indicators-tab-intro.png" alt caption size="64"}See [Step 1: Enable & Set Up Health Indicators](Step1Indicators.htm) for instructions.
  </hint>

    ### Click here to learn more about Health Indicators!

    Follow the guidelines below when working with the \{\{Buttons-UI.Indicators}} page.

    <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/j7E0hsI4sb0kZTYoNkkcN_indicators-tab-view.png" size="96" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/getting-started/indicators-tab-view.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/getting-started/indicators-tab-view.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

    1. Click the \{\{Buttons-UI.AlertGroups}} tab to select the events you want \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} to track and the team members you want to notify when your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} exceeds the alert thresholds. See [Working with Alert Groups](AlertGroupsTab.htm) for more information.
    2. Updated: The date and time when \{\{KPI}}'s were last updated by \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} and the schedule \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} follows when examining \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-ThresholdDifferential}}s.
    3. Click the **Configure** button to open a side panel and enable \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-AlertsAndThresholds}} for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}. See [Step 1: Enable & Set Up Health Indicators](Step1Indicators.htm) for instructions.
    4. Count Of Occurrence: The total number of times an event exceeded the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}'s **Alert Threshold** value since \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} last checked.
    5. Alert Threshold: The \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-AlertThresholdValue}}s that trigger \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} to notify your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} team when common events occur across your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}.
  </ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Reproduction Indicators

  Select the \{\{Buttons-UI.ReproductionIndicators}} option to view or adjust health and reproduction indicators for the animals in your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}. When the page is displayed, \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} examines your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} and updates the \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-Occurrence}} fields for each \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-OccurrenceType}} found across your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}. You can set unique limits for heifers and cows.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/0Kv6abJOSCEua3pRz_0y__indicators-tab-reproduction-view.png" size="82" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/Platform/getting-started/indicators-tab-reproduction-view.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/getting-started/indicators-tab-reproduction-view.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  You can set either an upper or lower limit, or set both to capture a range of acceptable values.

  <hint type="info">
    **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x43;hanges made in a field on this page are automatically saved when clicking or tabbing into another field or navigating to a different page.
  </hint>

  \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} does not currently support alerts and notifications for \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-ReproductionIndicators}}. However, you'll see the \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-ReproductionIndicators}} you're currently tracking on the \{\{Buttons-UI.ReproductionInsights}} report, which tracks cow and heifer reproduction across your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}.
