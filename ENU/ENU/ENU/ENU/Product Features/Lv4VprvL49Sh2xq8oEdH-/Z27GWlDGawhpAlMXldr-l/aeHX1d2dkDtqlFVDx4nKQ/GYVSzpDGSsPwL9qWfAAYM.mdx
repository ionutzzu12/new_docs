Review the available role permissions and the specific areas of Litmus Edge they can view/modify.&#x20;

<hint type="info">
  **Note**: Consider the following:

  - If a role has a Modify permission, any relevant View permission will be automatically added to the role as well.&#x20;
  - Click **Individual Permissions** for DeviceHub, Flows Manager, or Marketplace to view granular permissions for each component.&#x20;
</hint>

There are several default permissions that apply to all users. See the "Default User Permissions" section in [Users](<../../../../../Product Features/Lv4VprvL49Sh2xq8oEdH-/Z27GWlDGawhpAlMXldr-l/aeHX1d2dkDtqlFVDx4nKQ.mdx>) for details.&#x20;
