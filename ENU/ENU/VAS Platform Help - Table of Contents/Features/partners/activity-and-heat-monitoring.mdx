This section lists all of our current activity and heat monitoring partners. Their products can provide information about an animal's monitor reproduction, rumination, eating, and movements to provide deeper insights on heat detection, overall health, and animals requiring urgent attention.

After you activate an integration with one of the following partners, \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} displays animal data received from the partners' products.
