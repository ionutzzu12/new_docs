If you haven't already, you'll need to set up the \{\{PlatformContentSnippets.PerformanceIndicators-ReproductionIndicator}}s you want to track for your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}. Click **Set Thresholds** at the top right in the blue bar to view your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}'s current \{\{KPIs}} and edit the limits you want tracked on the \{\{Insights.Reproduction}}\{\{Insights.Insights}} (\{\{RolesPermissions.PermissionNames}} permissions required). See :Link[Reproduction Indicators]{label="Reproduction Indicators" overridedLabel="Reproduction Indicators" spaceId docId="Qc6TtLaRXHEUO9aO_uRiu" version="v2" docAnchorId="#reproduction" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../../DairySettings/IndicatorsTab.htm#reproduction" href="../../Dairy/Settings (Dairy)/Performance Indicators/working-with-indicators.mdx"}for more information.

Follow the guidelines below when using this page:

- \{\{Commands.Click}}**Filters** at the top left of the page to limit the data that is displayed. You can view data for the last 90, 180, or 365 days. If farms where the animals originated are set up on the [Source Farms](<../../DairyComp/MyDC/Settings/General Settings/source-farms.mdx>) page in [Settings](../../DairyComp/Settings/DC-Settings.htm), you can also filter by source farm when viewing heifer data. Once a filter is selected, the current filter applied is displayed next to the **Filters** option. Click the **X** on a specific filter to remove it. Or, \{\{Commands.UserActions}} the **Clear Filter** option to clear all of the filters currently applied. You can find the following reproduction values in the center of the page:\* \{\{Insights.ConceptionRateTab}}: The \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}'s overall conception rate, displayed as a percentage. Click **See More Info** to view \{\{Insights.InsightDataType}} details on the [Conception Rate](Reproduction-ConceptionRate.htm) page.
  - \{\{Insights.PregnancyRiskTab}}: Your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}'s overall \{\{Insights.InsightDataType}}, displayed as a percentage. A higher \{\{Insights.InsightDataType}} indicates a more efficient breeding program. Click **See More Info** to view \{\{Insights.InsightDataType}} details on the [Pregnancy Risk](Reproduction-PregnancyRisk.htm) page.
  - \{\{DIM}}/\{\{Age}} at First Breeding: The average DIM for cows. For heifers, the age in days at first insemination. Click **See More Info** to view \{\{Insights.InsightDataType}} details on the [Time of First Breeding](Reproduction-TimeFirstBreeding.htm) page.
  - \{\{Insights.MissedBreedingsTab}}: The total number of animals that currently have not been bred beyond their bred eligible date. Click **See More Info** to view \{\{Insights.InsightDataType}} details on the [Missed Breedings](Reproduction-MissedBreedings.htm) page.
  - Actual \{\{VWP}}: The number of days after calving before a cow is eligible for insemination. The actual VWP is a calculated value based on the 5th percentile of breedings over 14 months of breeding data. See :Link[Pregnancy Risk]{label="Pregnancy Risk" overridedLabel="Pregnancy Risk" spaceId docId="VCBpqYKN4AQK4EHb3EPZ4" version="v2" docAnchorId="#VWP" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="Reproduction-PregnancyRisk.htm#VWP" href="Reproduction-PregnancyRisk.htm#VWP"} and :Link[Time of First Breeding]{label="Time of First Breeding" overridedLabel="Time of First Breeding" spaceId docId="2nq1fyh0pyKxOP1MKwbZ8" version="v2" docAnchorId="#ActualVWP" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="Reproduction-TimeFirstBreeding.htm#ActualVWP" href="Reproduction-TimeFirstBreeding.htm#ActualVWP"} for more information.
  - Services per Conception: The average number of inseminations required for conception. See [Conception Rate](Reproduction-ConceptionRate.htm) for more information.
  - Service Rate: The breeding-eligible-animals that were actually inseminated during a 21-day cycle, displayed as a percentage. See :Link[Pregnancy Risk]{label="Pregnancy Risk" overridedLabel="Pregnancy Risk" spaceId docId="VCBpqYKN4AQK4EHb3EPZ4" version="v2" docAnchorId="#ServiceRate" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="Reproduction-PregnancyRisk.htm#ServiceRate" href="Reproduction-PregnancyRisk.htm#ServiceRate"} for more information.
  - Abort Rate: The percentage of animals that were confirmed pregnant but later lost the pregnancy. See :Link[Pregnancy Risk]{label="Pregnancy Risk" overridedLabel="Pregnancy Risk" spaceId docId="VCBpqYKN4AQK4EHb3EPZ4" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Abort" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="Reproduction-PregnancyRisk.htm#Abort" href="Reproduction-PregnancyRisk.htm#Abort"} for more information.
