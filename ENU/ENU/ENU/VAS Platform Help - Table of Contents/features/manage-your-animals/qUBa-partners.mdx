Open this tab on the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}} to access data from any of your integrated \{\{General.ProductName}} partner products. Within the **Partners** tab, you’ll find additional tabs for each partner integration, as illustrated in the example below. The data presented on each partner-specific tab will vary depending on the product. If you have questions about a specific partner integration, each tab contains a **Need help with** link you can click for more information.
