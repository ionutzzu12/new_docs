\{\{masterVAS.CompanyName-short}} is preparing several enhancements for our valued customers! Please check back in the new year for the latest updates. We wish you and yours all the best this holiday season!

## Bug Fixes

- \{\{CowCards-UI.CC-tab-names}} tab fix. \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} now supports adding a \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-am}} to a calf that was born in a previous lactation.
- SCC testday calculation issue. \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} is now calculating SCC testday results correctly for the herd and individual animals.
- DUE issues for heifers. We have addressed an issue where heifers without a confirmed pregnancy would show DUE14 and DUE21 dates in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} instead of blank (null) values.
- `MyDC ONLY`\{\{Events.1FRESH}} event lactation issues. We have addressed unexpected issues with the \{\{Events.1FRESH}} event: a new lactation starts and data for FDAT, CDAT, DDAT, RPRO, DIM, DSLH, DCC, and DUE are now updated correctly. If a \{\{Events.1FRESH}} event is deleted, the previous lactation is restored.
- `MyDC ONLY`GSAMP expanded to 17 characters. \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} can now handle 17 characters for genomic sample numbers.
