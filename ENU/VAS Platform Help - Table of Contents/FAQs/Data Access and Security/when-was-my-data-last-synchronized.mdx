You can quickly see when your data was last synchronized on the **Integration Status**\{\{Buttons-UI.FeatureType}}. Click the Integration Status button at top right side of \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} to open the \{\{Buttons-UI.FeatureType}}.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/MBcAE-H43mZLgp1kLX9a4_intstatusicon-nav.png" size="54" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Platform/intstatusicon-nav.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/intstatusicon-nav.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

This page contains a card for each of your integrated, \{\{masterVAS-Products.OnPremise-lower}} products, letting you quickly see the last time the data was synced for each product.
