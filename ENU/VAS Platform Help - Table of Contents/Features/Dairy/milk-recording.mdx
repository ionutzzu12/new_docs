The \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}\{\{General.DCPro-milk}} feature allows \{\{masterVAS-Products.MyDC-short}}\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}} to share their latest animal records with their DRPC. This task is typically done on a monthly or bi-monthly basis, when your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} completes their scheduled milk testing. After the DRPC \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.t-estday}} results are returned to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, you can view the results on individual animals' \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}}s, on the [Testdays](../CowCards/CC-TestDays.htm) tab.

Follow the steps below to connect the integration and begin using the \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}\{\{General.DCPro-milk}} feature:

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Step 1: Connect the integration

  1. In \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, navigate to **Partners&#x20;**>**&#xA0;Integrations Gallery** and locate your DRPC.
  2. \{\{Commands.Click}}**See Details** or **See Requirements** on your DRPC's card.
  3. Review the Connection Instructions on the right side of the page. Then click the **Connection Status** toggle and verify the status switches to **Active**.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/yVoLb7NAnbX1zs-j2jcYx_integration-connected.png" alt="Connection Status: Active" caption size="22"}
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Step 2: Manage animal records on testday

  \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} allows you to manage \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} data on \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.t-estday}}, including sharing the animal records that have changed since the previous \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.t-estday}} with your DRPC. The updated records file includes animals added to the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}, new animal events, and, if selected, milk weight details.

  Follow the steps below when working with your milk tester to collect samples and submit your latest animal records. The steps required depend on your DRPC:

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Step 3: View your testday results

  \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} automatically imports your DRPC \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.t-estday}} results from the latest file (\{\{DNL}} format) when it becomes available, usually within a few days.

  After your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.t-estday}} data is imported, you can view a [herd-level summary](../DairyComp/MyDC/testdays.mdx) of the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.t-estday}} results or individual animal results from the [CowCard](../CowCards/CC-TestDays.htm).
</ExpandableHeading>
