The \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}\{\{Protocols.Protocols}} feature allows \{\{masterVAS-Products.DCP-users-short}} to define custom animal treatments for reproduction activities or health events. These treatments include \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.protocolTasks}}, which are a series of treatments that are scheduled and follow a prescribed order. \{\{Protocols.Protocols}} may be defined for reproduction purposes or to treat an illness or other physical condition. Defining \{\{Protocols.Protocol-common}}s with the assistance of your vet or other specialist and complying with the protocol improves your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}'s overall health as well as reproduction outcomes.

Customize reproduction \{\{Protocols.Protocol-common}} templates from the **Gallery**, or view available health \{\{Protocols.Protocol-common}} templates from the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}}**&#x20;Protocols** page. You can also build custom \{\{Protocols.Protocol-common}}s based on your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}'s needs.
