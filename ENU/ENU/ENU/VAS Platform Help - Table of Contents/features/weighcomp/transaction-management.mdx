The Transaction Management feature allows you to view a list of all transactions that have taken place in \{\{MorphHelp.WeighComp}}, both pending and previously approved. The Transaction Management page displays detailed information about every transaction, such as its type (i.e., delivery or pickup), its vendor, and its product (for example, rolled wheat). Additionally, you can quickly enter new transactions or approve pending transactions directly on the page.

You can find this feature under the :Link[WeighComp menu]{label="WeighComp menu" overridedLabel="WeighComp menu" spaceId docId="WFLX7SPMpZfy7zoMyrvRh" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Menu-WeighComp" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../../../Platform/NavPlatform/Menu.htm#Menu-WeighComp" href="../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/navigate-pulse/menu-options.mdx"}.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Enter WeighComp Transactions

  Data entry for spot load transactions is done via the \{\{MorphHelp.WeighComp}} app by the truck driver, whether the transaction is inbound (a delivery) or outbound (a pickup). Additionally, authorized users can enter transactions directly on \{\{masterVAS-Products.WC-Platform}} in \{\{General.ProductName}}. However, the \{\{masterVAS-Products.WC-Platform}} app provides a step-by-step interface so that truck drivers can easily record deliveries and pickups of feed inventory. Once a transaction is completed in the \{\{masterVAS-Products.WC-Platform}} app, the data is sent to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.

  The relationship between \{\{MorphHelp.WeighComp}}, \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedWatchOnPremise}}, and \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} for transaction management is shown in the following diagram:

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/lmc-qDt3qId8uGgAO9qy0_transman.png" size="57" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/WeighComp/TransMan.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/WeighComp/TransMan.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  For example, once a truck arrives at the feed scale for a delivery, the driver inputs basic information about the transaction (name, product type, vendor, etc.) into the app, records the full and empty weights for the load, and then finalizes the transaction. The data for that transaction is transferred to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} via a cloud-based connection. At that point, the driver is done and is free to move on to other work.

  ## Add a New Transaction

  You can easily add a new WeighComp transaction to your records on \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}. Click the New Transaction button

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/PasCIKvW3GpZk7qfCX05U_addtransaction114x25.png" size="10" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/WeighComp/AddTransaction_114x25.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/WeighComp/AddTransaction_114x25.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  at the upper right corner of the screen to open a side panel where you can create a new transaction.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/XMY_WPGTPtuSRxlOyqjin_transsidepanel-add.png" size="44" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/WeighComp/TransSidePanel-Add.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/WeighComp/TransSidePanel-Add.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  Follow the guidelines below when you enter information for new transactions:

  - You can manually enter a ticket number for the transaction. If you prefer, you can leave the ticket number blank.
  - The fields in this side panel are the same as those shown in the Transactions list. Refer to :Link[View WeighComp Transactions]{label="View WeighComp Transactions" overridedLabel="View WeighComp Transactions" spaceId docId="ekw9j1KL_O4tzm2hyoQLO" version="v2" docAnchorId="#The" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="transaction-management.mdx"} for a description for each of these fields.
  - Remember to mark the transaction as a delivery or a pickup transaction.
  - You can manually enter information for the **DM%** or **AF Cost/Ton** fields or you can click **Import DM% and Cost from&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedWatchPlatform}} to directly import the percentage of dry matter or the cost of the product from \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedWatchPlatform}}. See :Link[Import Dry Matter and Cost Data]{label="Import Dry Matter and Cost Data" overridedLabel="Import Dry Matter and Cost Data" spaceId docId="ekw9j1KL_O4tzm2hyoQLO" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Import" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="transaction-management.mdx"} to learn more.

  When you're finished, click **Save Draft**. The system displays a message at the bottom of the page stating that your new transaction has been added.

  The list now displays your new transaction, which you can now edit or approve. See :Link[Edit and Approve Transactions]{label="Edit and Approve Transactions" overridedLabel="Edit and Approve Transactions" spaceId docId="ekw9j1KL_O4tzm2hyoQLO" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Editing" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="transaction-management.mdx"} to learn more.

  ## Edit and Approve Transactions

  Click on any pending transaction to edit its details in a side panel. You can also edit the start and end weights of a pending transaction and approve pending transactions from the side panel.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/E1_DU2qSfbLx7m12ESfr5_transsidepanel.png" size="38" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/WeighComp/TransSidePanel.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/WeighComp/TransSidePanel.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  When editing a pending transaction, you can use the **Save Draft** button to save your changes before actually approving the transaction. A new transaction can't be approved until all the fields (except **Field&#x20;**\{\{ID}} and **Truck ID**) are completed. To approve the transaction, click **Approve**. Approved transactions are considered done and cannot be edited.

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Edit Transaction Details

    1. Click on a pending transaction to display its side panel.
    2. In the side panel, click in the field(s) of the detail(s) to change and enter the new information.
    3. When you're finished editing the information, click **Save Draft**.The system displays a message at the bottom of the page indicating that your changes have been saved.
  </ExpandableHeading>

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Edit Start and End Weights

    The start and end weights originally received by the WeighComp app for pending transactions can be manually edited:

    1. Click on a pending transaction to display its side panel.
    2. In the side panel, click the edit icon

    <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/0NEgqyaM4Qdt3vIYmstxD_manualediticon.png" size="10" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/WeighComp/ManualEditIcon.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/WeighComp/ManualEditIcon.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false" indent="2"></Image>

    <Paragraph indent="1">
      for the start weight or end weight.
    </Paragraph>

    3. Enter a new start or end weight value.
    4. When you're finished editing the information, click **Save Draft**The system displays a message at the bottom of the page indicating that your changes have been saved.
  </ExpandableHeading>

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Import Dry Matter and Cost Data

    You can import the dry matter percentage (DM%) and cost data for a pending transaction directly from \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedWatchPlatform}}. Doing so provides the most current data available for those values (which can be viewed from the [Ingredients](<../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/FeedWatch/ingredients.mdx>) page). Dry matter and cost data can be added to individual transactions, or to multiple transactions using the bulk edit feature.

    <hint type="info">
      **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x49;n order to import dry matter and cost data, the transaction must have a product and location defined. Additionally, the selected location must have data assigned to it. You can view all available locations and their data in the [Location DM% Adjustments](<../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/feedwatch/location-dmpercent-adjustments.mdx>) page.
    </hint>

    1. Click on a pending transaction to display its side panel.
    2. If necessary, assign **Product** and **Location** values to the transaction.
    3. Click **Import DM% and Cost from&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedWatchPlatform}}.The newly imported values appear in the **DM%** and **AF Cost/Ton** fields.
    4. Click **Save Draft**.
  </ExpandableHeading>

  ## Reconcile Approved Transactions

  Reconciling a transaction is a way to note that an approved transaction has been reviewed in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}. This is an optional step to electronically verify that the desired loads for a product were actually brought to (or picked up from) the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}.

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/CWN_x4XsdMXo4nuQcZF6w_reconcile.png" size="97" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/WeighComp/Reconcile.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/PlatformAddons/WeighComp/Reconcile.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false"></Image>

  1. Transactions that have been reconciled are displayed in the **Reconciled** column of the Approved tab.
  2. In the Approved tab, check the boxes for the transactions you want to reconcile.
  3. In the blue indicator bar that appears, click **Reconcile**.

  <hint type="info">
    **NOTE:&#x20;**&#x41;lternatively, you can click **Non-Reconcile** to undo transactions that have already been reconciled.
  </hint>
</ExpandableHeading>

</hint>
