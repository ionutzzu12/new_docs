Use the \{\{Insights.TimeOfFirstBreedingTab}} page to examine your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}'s monthly breeding statistics at the time of first insemination. \{\{Commands.Click}}**Filters** at the top left of the page to limit the data that is displayed.

The gray boxes at the top of the page summarize your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}'s reproduction data based on your selected **Filters**:

- Actual \{\{VWP}}: The number of days after calving before a cow or heifer is eligible for insemination based on the bottom 5th percentile of \{\{DIM}} for cows or \{\{age}} for heifers. The actual VWP is a calculated value based on the 5th percentile of breedings over 14 months of breeding data.
- Stated VWP: The number of days that should pass before another reproductive event is entered for cows based on your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}'s standard :Link[voluntary wait period]{label="voluntary wait period" overridedLabel="voluntary wait period" spaceId docId="Jcq4yrtkoAtv19uGQB89c" version="v2" docAnchorId="#vwp" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../../DairyComp/Settings/DC-ReproConstraints.htm#vwp" href="../../DairyComp/MyDC/Settings/Reproduction Settings/reproduction-constraints.mdx"} policy.For heifers, the minimum age before entering a \{\{Events.5BRED}} event based on your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}'s :Link[minimum age for heifer breeding]{label="minimum age for heifer breeding" overridedLabel="minimum age for heifer breeding" spaceId docId="Jcq4yrtkoAtv19uGQB89c" version="v2" docAnchorId="#min-age-heifer-breeding" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../../DairyComp/Settings/DC-ReproConstraints.htm#min-age-heifer-breeding" href="../../DairyComp/MyDC/Settings/Reproduction Settings/reproduction-constraints.mdx"} converted to days.The **Stated VWP** and the **Actual VWP** allow you to see if your breeding program is deviating from your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}'s standard policy.
- AVG DIM/Age: The average DIM for cows. For heifers, the age in days at first insemination based on the average age at first breeding.

The graphed data helps to visualize your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}}'s monthly breeding statistics:

- References: Each reference is color-coded to match a specific bar or line on the graph. Uncheck a box to remove a plot line from the graph.
- Hover over an animal's data point on the graph to view additional details. Or, click the animal's data point on the graph to open a side panel and view even more details about the animal, including recent events. See [View Animal Details](../../DairyComp/Animals/AnimalDetails.htm) to learn more about the information displayed in the side panel.
- Darker data points on the graph indicate that more than one animal is plotted at the same point.
- Scroll down to view the graphed data in a table format.

Once the data is displayed, \{\{Commands.UserActions}} the green **Export as CSV** button

at the top right of the page to save the data as a CSV file. If you applied filter(s), the data will print with the current filter(s) applied.
