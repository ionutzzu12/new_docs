[Förster-Technik](https://www.foerster-technik.com/) provides a variety of calf-rearing tools and applications focused on feeding and calf growth, with a philosophy that optimally-raised calves are your future healthy and high-producing \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} cows. \{\{Partners.Forster}}'s \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} integration allows for \{\{General.CompanyName-short}} calf data, including \{\{Events.1FRESH}} event details and \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.s-ire}} and \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-am}} parentage, to be shared with \{\{Partners.Forster}}'s system.
