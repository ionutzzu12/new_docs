If you collected genomic samples [and did not use a Pocket CowCard scanner](zoetis-integrate-step5.htm) to automatically assign the genomic sample data to an animal, you can manually assign the genomic sample data to an animal using \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, \{\{masterVAS-Products.DConMobile}}, or \{\{DC-UI.DC-ProdNameShort}}.

To assign genomic sample data to an animal, you must update the animal's genomic sample items (GSAMP and GSDAT) in its \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}}:

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Update Genomic Items in PULSE

  1. In \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, open the animal's \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}}.
  2. In the **Items** tab, click the **GSAMP** field, enter the animal's genomic sample \{\{ID}}, and save your changes.
  3. Click the **GSDAT** field, enter the date you collected the sample, and save your changes.
  4. Repeat these steps for every animal you sampled.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Update Genomic Items in DC on Mobile

  1. In \{\{masterVAS-Products.DConMobile}}, open the animal's \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}}.
  2. In the **Items** tab, tap **Edit Items** at the bottom of the page.
  3. In the **GSAMP** field, enter the animal's genomic sample ID.
  4. In the **GSDAT** field, enter the date you collected the sample.
  5. Tap **Done** to save your changes.
  6. Repeat these steps for every animal you sampled.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Update Genomic Items in DC305 (DC Unlimited Subscribers Only)

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### How do I know which PULSE version I'm using?

    Check the top of your \{\{General.ProductName-short}} page to see if your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} has \{\{masterVAS-Products.DCUnlimited-short}} or \{\{masterVAS-Products.MyDC-short}}.

    Click [here](../../../Platform/GetStarted/KnowYourPulse.htm) to learn more about the different versions of \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.
  </ExpandableHeading>

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Update Items for One Animal

    1. In \{\{DC-UI.DC-ProdNameShort}}, open the animal's \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.cowcard}}.
    2. In the **Items** tabs, double-click the **GSAMP** field and enter the animal's genomic sample ID.
    3. Double-click the **GSDAT** field and enter the date you collected the sample.
    4. Repeat these steps for every animal you sampled.
  </ExpandableHeading>

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Update Items for Multiple Animals

    1. In \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}}, enter `GSDAT=%DATE_SAMPLE GSAMP` in the command line.
    2. Enter the date you collected the animal samples.
    3. Enter the animal ID and the genomic sample ID for the sampled animal. Repeat this step for every animal you sampled.
    4. Press **Enter** twice on your keyboard to complete the batch entry.
  </ExpandableHeading>
</ExpandableHeading>

After you update the genomic sample items, you can submit the genomic samples to \{\{Partners.Zoetis}} in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}. See [Submit Zoetis Genomics Samples](zoetis-genomics-submit-samples.htm) to get started.
