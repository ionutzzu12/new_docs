  ### Required Setup

  - Before you connect the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.APR250long}}, \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.AWR300long}}, or \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.APR600long}}, download the latest firmware from the [Allflex website](https://www.allflexsa.com/products/readers/game-livestock/).
  - If you purchased the scanner from a third party, verify with the seller that the scanner has all of the necessary \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.c-onfiguration}} files installed before you continue.

  <ExpandableHeading>
    ### Navigation Tips

    - The display is **not** a touchscreen; options are selected using the **Arrow** buttons.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/DjXc5HyW5l2FVn-GBr6MA_allflex-navigation.png" alt caption size="19"}
    - To confirm a selection, you must press the **Enter** button
