  <hint type="info">

  ## Enable Sounds

  \{\{masterVAS-Products.FeedComp}} can play helpful sounds when while adding ingredients to the mixer or dropping loads at \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.p-ens}}. These sounds can notify your technician when a specific ingredient weight is reached while mixing or a load of feed is almost done dropping at a \{\{pen}}. Sounds allow technicians to feed more efficiently because they won't need to look at the scale and/or mobile device while feeding.

  1. \{\{Commands.Click}} the \{\{FeedComp.FeedCompFeatures-Lower}} to \{\{Commands.UserActions}}.
  2. In **Unit Amount** under **Audio Trigger Weight**, enter the scale weight that will activate a sound.
  3. Tap `+ Add Sound` at the bottom of the screen.
  4. Tap

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/cowgMcOahTcEzMjjjY1w8_path.png" size="10" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/feedcomp-img/icons/Path.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/feedcomp-img/icons/Path.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false" indent="2"></Image>

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    to preview each sound available, and then tap the preferred sound to enable it.
  </Paragraph>

  5. \{\{Commands.UserActions}}`Add` at the bottom of the screen.
  6. Under **Audio Settings**, tap

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/cowgMcOahTcEzMjjjY1w8_path.png" size="10" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../../../Resources/Images/Platform/feedcomp-img/icons/Path.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Platform/feedcomp-img/icons/Path.png" position="flex-start" showCaption="false" indent="2"></Image>

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    to play the sound selected. If it is too loud, tap and drag on the **Volume** bar to change the volume. Or, tap **Mute Sound** to turn the sound off.
  </Paragraph>

  7. \{\{Commands.UserActions}}`Save` at the bottom of the screen.
</ExpandableHeading>
