\{\{Insights.HerdSights}} bring you actionable data to directly support your efforts to reduce feed costs and waste, keep your herd healthy, and improve milk production and quality. Build custom views and dashboards quickly with the help of prebuilt templates, and easily adjust the frequency of how your most critical \{\{Insights.KPIs}} are reported to best fit your evolving analysis needs. You can find this feature under the :Link[Insights menu]{label="Insights menu" overridedLabel="Insights menu" spaceId docId="WFLX7SPMpZfy7zoMyrvRh" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Menu-Insights" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../../../NavPlatform/Menu.htm#Menu-Insights" href="../../../NavPlatform/Menu.htm#Menu-Insights"}.
