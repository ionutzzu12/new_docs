Using the Favorite option you can easily access your most frequently accessed Knowledge Articles, Service Catalogs, and Service Requests by adding them to Favorite.

<hint type="info">
  If the Favorite option is not available for you, then it has not been enabled by your Administrator. Reach out to your Administrator to get it enabled.
</hint>

## Adding and Removing a Favorite

### To add a record to Favorites

1. Log in to Self Service Mobile portal.
2. Open the Knowledge/Service Catalog/Service Request workspace.
3. Open a record or from the list view itself click the **Add to Favorite** icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/SuUCKyOJOVoqVudduq7Tx_favorites-disabled.png" alt caption} for any of the record that you wish to add to Favorite.

### To remove a record from Favorites

4. Open the Knowledge/Service Catalog/Service Request workspace.
5. Open a record or from the list view itself click the **Remove from Favorite** icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/Ao-Riba-CkGwLQU2XknWZ_favorites-selected.png" alt caption} for any of record that you wish to remove from Favorites.

<hint type="info">
  - The Favorite option is also available in the **Search** workspace. You can add or remove records as Favorite from the **Search** workspace as well.
  - When you click the **Add to Favorite** icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/SuUCKyOJOVoqVudduq7Tx_favorites-disabled.png" alt caption}, the icon toggles to **Remove from Favorite** icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/Ao-Riba-CkGwLQU2XknWZ_favorites-selected.png" alt caption}.
</hint>
