<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Can't find this feature? Make sure it's included in your subscription!

  This feature/option is available only for \{\{masterVAS-Products.DCU-users-short}}. \{\{masterVAS-Products.DCP-users-short}} will not see it in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.

  Click [here](../../Platform/GetStarted/KnowYourPulse.htm) to learn more about the different versions of \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Required Permissions

  Only users who are assigned a Role with Integrations Management permissions can connect partner integrations. See [Edit Dairy User Permissions](../../Platform/Features/Config/UserMgmtRoles/EditUserPermissions.htm) and [Manage Dairy Roles](<../../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/Manage Dairy Users and Roles/manage-dairy-roles.mdx>) for more information.
</ExpandableHeading>

\{\{General.CompanyName-short}} offers \{\{General.DCUnlimited}}\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}} the option to quickly and easily share their most recent \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}}\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.c-owfile}} backup with their \{\{Partners.PartnerName}}s using the [Integrations Gallery](<../../../../../VAS Platform Help - Table of Contents/Features/partners/integrations-gallery.mdx>) in \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}. This automatically provides access to your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-erd}} data to simplify file sharing when needed — no need to download or upload your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.c-owfiles}} manually! If at any point you decide to stop sharing your latest \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.c-owfile}}, you can turn off the connection with a single click.

Follow the steps below to connect the integration and begin using the \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}}\{\{masterVAS-Integrations.Cowfile}} feature:

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Step 1: Configure DairyComp 305 to backup to the cloud

  You can configure your \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}} system to back up its current \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.c-onfiguration}} and setup data. This process is sometimes referred to as "backing up to the cloud."

  To learn how to configure your system to create regular backups, follow the steps in [Back DC305 Up to the Cloud](https://dc-help.vas.com/default.htm#cshid=1027) in the \{\{masterVAS-Products.DC305-short}}\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-elp}}.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Step 2: Connect the integration in PULSE

  1. In \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}, navigate to **Partners > Integrations Gallery** and locate your \{\{Partners.PartnerName}}.
  2. \{\{Commands.Click}}**See Details** or **See Requirements** on your partner's card.
  3. Review the Connection Instructions on the right side of the page. Then click the **Connection Status** toggle and verify the status switches to **Active**.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/yVoLb7NAnbX1zs-j2jcYx_integration-connected.png" alt="Connection Status: Active" caption size="22"}
  4. If you decide to stop sharing your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.c-owfile}} with your \{\{Partners.PartnerName}}, click the toggle to disable the connection.
</ExpandableHeading>
