<ExpandableHeading>
  ### Required Permissions

  Only users who are assigned to a Role with \{\{RolesPermissions.PermissionNames}} permissions can use this feature. See [Edit Dairy User Permissions](../Features/Config/UserMgmtRoles/EditUserPermissions.htm) and [Manage Dairy Roles](../Features/Config/UserMgmtRoles/ManageRoles.htm) for more information.
</ExpandableHeading>

When users request to connect with your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}, \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} adds their request to the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}}[User Management](../Features/Config/UserMgmtRoles/UserMgmtIntro.htm) page.

Any member of your \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} who has \{\{MorphHelp.User\_Management\_UI\_item}} permissions can accept or decline a connection request. You can find this feature under the :Link[Dairy menu]{label="Dairy menu" overridedLabel="Dairy menu" spaceId docId="WFLX7SPMpZfy7zoMyrvRh" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Menu-Dairy" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="../NavPlatform/Menu.htm#Menu-Dairy" href="../NavPlatform/Menu.htm#Menu-Dairy"}.

Follow the steps below to continue:

1. In the **Access Pending** list on the **Users** tab, expand the user who requested to connect with the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}.
2. Click the **Accept** or **Decline** buttons at the bottom of the expanded row.\{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} sends the user an email to confirm that their request has either been accepted or declined. If accepted, the user can then log into \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} and view all data for the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} to which they are now connected.
