To log in, you must be assigned the Self Service user role. Ask your administrator for the login URL.

1. To login:

<Paragraph indent="1">
  a. **On a mobile device**
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  Enter the login URL provided by your administrator in the **Tenant URL** field. or tap **Scan Tenant QR**, and allow camera access when prompted.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  b. **On a desktop application**
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  Open the browser and enter URL provided by your administrator.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The login page is displayed.
</Paragraph>

2. Enter your **username**, and **password** and then tap or click **Login**.
3. Select the **Self Service** role and tap or click **Submit**.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The Self Service UI is displayed.
</Paragraph>

## Changing your Profile Settings

You can update your password, language, and profile picture through the **Profile Settings** menu.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Change your password

  <hint type="info">
    Passwords are case sensitive.
  </hint>

  1. Log in to the Self Service Management portal.
  2. Click :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/-k1Jz8ryehcfVEXRCSlK1_user-icon17x17.png" alt caption} icon and from the dropdown list, click **Profile Settings**.

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    The **My Settings** page is displayed.
  </Paragraph>

  <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/dPRQ5TGM3ctqzSBQ5LuWE_password-settings.png" position="flex-start" size="40" indent="1" initialPath="../../Resources/images/password-settings.png" githubPath="self/Content/Resources/images/password-settings.png"></Image>

  3. On the **My Settings** page, select **Password**.
  4. Enter your **Old Password**, **New Password**, and **Confirm Password**.
  5. Click **Update Password**.
  6. Log out, and log in again using your new password.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Reset your password

  <hint type="info">
    This feature is only available for users authenticated through internal authorization. It does not work for external login providers.
  </hint>

  1. On the login page, click **Forgot password?**.
  2. Enter your **username** or **email address** and then click **Submit**.

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    A one-time reset link is sent to your registered email address.
  </Paragraph>

  3. Open your email and click the reset link. This link expires in **20 minutes** or as configured by your administrator.
  4. Click the reset link.

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    The **Reset Password** page is displayed.
  </Paragraph>

  5. Enter your **User ID**, **New Password** and **Confirm Password**.
  6. Click **Submit**.

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    If you are unsure of your **User ID**, contact your organization's administrator.
  </Paragraph>
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Change your language preference

  The default language is English (United States). If your administrator provides permission, you can change the interface language. The selected language remains same until you change it.

  1. Click :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/-k1Jz8ryehcfVEXRCSlK1_user-icon17x17.png" alt caption} icon and from the dropdown list, click **Profile Settings**.

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    The **My Settings** page is displayed.
  </Paragraph>

  2. Select **General Setting**.
  3. Select the language, and then click **Save Changes**.

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    The application refreshes with the selected language.
  </Paragraph>
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Update your profile picture

  1. Click :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/-k1Jz8ryehcfVEXRCSlK1_user-icon17x17.png" alt caption} icon and from the dropdown list, click **Profile Settings**.

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    The **My Settings** page is displayed.
  </Paragraph>

  2. Select **General Setting**.
  3. Click profile picture :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/KD-5IukMR8Ue-KvrU1-_h_user-icon18x18.png" alt caption} icon.

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    Th&#x65;**&#x20;Edit Profile** dialog box is displayed.
  </Paragraph>

  4. In the **Edit Profile** dialog box, click **Select File** select an image file and click **Save**.

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    The profile picture appears next to your username.
  </Paragraph>
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Change your role

  1. Click :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/-k1Jz8ryehcfVEXRCSlK1_user-icon17x17.png" alt caption} icon and from the dropdown list, click **Profile Settings**.

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    The **My Settings** page is displayed.
  </Paragraph>

  2. Select **General Setting**.
  3. Click **Change Role**. From the dropdown list, select the role.

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    The Home page updates based on the selected role.
  </Paragraph>
</ExpandableHeading>
