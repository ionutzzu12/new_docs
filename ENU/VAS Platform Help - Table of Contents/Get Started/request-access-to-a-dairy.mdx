The \{\{General.ProductName-short}} currently allows you to request access to \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}} but **not** to companies. To access company features, a member of the company must invite you to connect with it. See [Invite a User to Your Dairy](InviteUserToDairy.htm) for more information.

To connect to a new \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}, follow these steps:

1. If you're not already on your **Account&#x20;****Settings** page, click \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}} logo button in the top left corner of the window, and \{\{Commands.click}} the **Account Settings** button to the far right of your User Account home page.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/nAf0uJAS7dMuTp2ngmncr_vas-logo-open-user-acct.png" alt caption size="22"}:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/HxDcPsmEhxhcUD2T2wxYh_open-acct-settings.png" alt caption size="57"}**OR**Select **Account Settings** from the [User Menu](../Features/Config/UserMenu.htm) in the top right corner of the page.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/ecq-YsGRfRV3m8X9-g7v3_user-menu-acct.png" alt caption size="22"}
2. \{\{Commands.Click}} the **Connect to New&#x20;**\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.D-airy-}} button on the top right of the **My Connections** list to locate the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} you want to access.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/viAYe9RWFYtfHwY4YLC6F_find-dairy.png" alt caption size="57"}
3. As you start typing, all matches are displayed. Select a \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} from the list.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/pvE3MZPWwsAkHMWi52mvD_find-dairy-search.png" alt caption size="58"}You will be directed to \{\{masterVAS-Products.Pulse-only}}\{\{masterVAS-Terminology.h-omepage}} of the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} you selected (see example below).
4. \{\{Commands.Click}} the **Request Access** button.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/YFchCbBT_A2JtI5SnmVQh_request-access-button.png" alt caption size="57"}At this point, the system emails a request to all users at that \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} who are assigned to the **Owner** role. Any of those users can then grant you access to the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}. You will receive an email notification when your request has been accepted. For more information on the Owner role, see [Manage Dairy Users and Roles](../Features/Config/UserMgmtRoles/UserMgmtIntro.htm).After you are connected to a \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}}, it displays in your **My Connections** list with a colored bar to indicate if the \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airy}} is equipped with \{\{General.DCUnlimited-short}} or \{\{masterVAS-Products.MyDC-short}}. If you have company-level access, your connected \{\{masterVAS-Terminology.d-airies}} are listed below the company name.:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/QjalyvC_Eyr95bGir3p2_/JndJHnCtejrNuWuDgZs2m_my-connections.png" alt caption size="57"}
